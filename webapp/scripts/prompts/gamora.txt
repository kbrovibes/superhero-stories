Gamora has vivid green skin and dark, sharp eyes. Her long dark hair falls past her shoulders. She wears a fitted dark jacket in deep burgundy and charcoal over dark pants, with a worn leather sword belt at her hip holding a long scabbard for the blade Godslayer. Her expression is calm and steady — intelligent, watchful, and quietly kind. She holds herself with the upright, balanced posture of a master swordfighter entirely at ease.
