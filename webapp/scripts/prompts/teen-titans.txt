Five young heroes stand on the roof of Titans Tower, a giant steel T rising behind them. Robin is at the center in red and green, cape snapping, confident stance. Starfire floats beside him radiating golden light, long red hair streaming, wide joyful smile. Cyborg stands tall on the right, half-metal body gleaming, red optical sensor glowing. Raven stands left in a dark blue cloak, calm and watchful. Beast Boy crouches in front, green-skinned and grinning, one hand mid-transform into a cheetah's paw.
