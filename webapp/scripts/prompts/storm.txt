Ororo Munroe stands tall with striking white hair that flows loose around her shoulders. Her eyes glow solid white when her powers are active, giving her a calm, radiant intensity. She wears a white bodysuit with a flowing white cape and gold accents at the collar and cuffs. Her skin is a rich dark brown. Her expression is composed and warm — quietly powerful, like the moment before a storm breaks. Small arcs of lightning flicker at her fingertips and around the hem of her cape. She holds herself with the stillness of someone completely at home in the open sky.
