T'Challa opened Wakanda's borders on a morning when the sky was still pink over the Golden City, and the decision changed everything. For generations the hidden kingdom had kept its vibranium and its brilliance to itself. Now the Avengers came through the force field with battered ships and a desperate plan, and T'Challa met them at the city's edge with open arms. Wakanda would not hide from this. The world's fight was Wakanda's fight, and T'Challa had decided that mattered more than safety.

In the palace lab, Princess Shuri worked with the focused calm of someone built for impossible problems. Vision lay on her table, and inside his forehead glowed the Mind Stone — yellow, warm, one of the six Infinity Stones Thanos needed to complete his plan. If she could extract it cleanly, the stone could be destroyed and Vision could live. Outside the city, Thanos's army pressed against the force field like a wave. General Okoye stood at the front of the Dora Milaje, Wakanda's elite guard, and did not step back once. Natasha Romanoff fought beside her — precise, cool, moving like water — and the two of them together were something the army had not anticipated.

Steve Rogers moved through the battle using vibranium arm guards Shuri had made for him — sleek and curved, built after she'd watched how he fought, extending his reach without slowing him down. Bruce Banner piloted the Hulkbuster armor, because the Hulk had decided not to come out after Thanos beat him in Asgard. Bruce understood. He also discovered, pushing through the army in Tony's enormous suit, that he was more capable inside it than he'd expected.

Then the sky opened and Thor arrived. He descended in a column of lightning with Stormbreaker in one hand and Groot beside him, and when he landed, the shockwave pushed Thanos's entire advance back for a full minute. Every Avenger on the field felt that pause like a gift and used it.

Wanda Maximoff held the line with her scarlet energy, stretched between the army and Vision, buying Shuri every second she could. Every hero gave everything they had. The Mind Stone glowed yellow in the lab behind them. The stakes were enormous. The team was together, and together — as they had always found — was the only way through.
