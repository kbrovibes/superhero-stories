After the Snap, the world became a very quiet place. Half of all the people — and animals, and birds, and creatures across every planet — were simply gone. The Avengers who remained did their best to hold things together. Steve Rogers sat in circles with sad, grieving people, listening and helping as best he could. Natasha Romanoff stayed at the Avengers Compound, watching the screens and keeping in touch with every hero left in the world.

The others found their own ways through the sorrow. Tony Stark moved to a small house in the woods with Pepper Potts, and their daughter Morgan was born — a small, perfect gift in a very difficult time. Thor went to the village of New Asgard in Norway and tried to forget. Bruce Banner made a remarkable discovery: he figured out how to be both Bruce and the Hulk at the same time — gentle, smart, and green. And Clint Barton, having lost his whole family to the Snap, put on a dark disguise and became the Ronin, hunting criminals across the world in his grief.

Then one day, Scott Lang tumbled out of the Quantum Realm — a tiny, strange universe inside the universe — and he had spent what felt like five hours in there. But five years had passed on Earth. And in those five hours, Scott had had an idea. The Quantum Realm could be used to travel through time.

The Avengers called it the Time Heist. They would go back to three moments in history and borrow the Infinity Stones before Thanos ever found them. Tony solved the hardest part of the puzzle. The team split up and slipped into the past. Thor saw his mother one more time. Tony spoke with his father. Steve glimpsed a world he had lost.

But on the icy cliffs of Vormir, Natasha and Clint faced a terrible rule: the Soul Stone demands a soul. They each tried to be the one to fall. Natasha was faster and braver. And Clint came home with the stone, and with tears on his face that never quite dried. Bruce Banner put on the new gauntlet — red and gold, made for a human hand — and snapped his fingers. Across the world, the lost came back.
