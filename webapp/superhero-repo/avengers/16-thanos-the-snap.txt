While the Battle of Titan raged a hundred million miles away, Earth fought its own desperate stand in Wakanda. The Vision lay on a stone slab in Shuri’s laboratory, the yellow Mind Stone shining in his forehead. If she could pull the stone free without ending him, Thanos would never complete his collection. Captain America stood on the edge of the great Wakandan plain with Black Panther, Black Widow, Bucky, Falcon, Okoye, and the entire Dora Milaje, watching a wall of Outriders pour out of Thanos’s landing rings. "We don’t trade lives, Vision," Steve Rogers said, and the Avengers ran into the field.

The battle was thunderous and clean. Thor, Rocket, and Groot dropped from the sky with the newly forged Stormbreaker, the axe ringing like a bell as it carved through the alien army. Wanda Maximoff lifted dozens of Outriders into the air with a sweep of her crimson hands. Shuri ran her trembling fingers across the Mind Stone’s casing, pulling thread after thread of code free, almost there, almost there. And then a portal opened in the middle of the trees, and Thanos himself stepped through, calm as a tide.

Wanda made the hardest choice of her life. With one hand she held the Mad Titan back; with the other she destroyed the Mind Stone in Vision’s forehead, ending the synthezoid she loved to save the universe. The yellow gem shattered into bright dust. For one breath, the heroes had won. Then Thanos raised the gauntlet, twisted the Time Stone, and ran the moment backwards. The gem reassembled itself. Vision rose, terrified. Thanos lifted him up gently, almost tenderly, and pulled the Mind Stone from his forehead. Six stones now burned in the gauntlet. The Mad Titan looked up at the sky, smiled a small, tired smile, and said, "I am inevitable."

Thor arrived a heartbeat later in a storm of lightning and drove Stormbreaker into Thanos’s chest. The Mad Titan’s eyes widened in pain. "You should have gone for the head," he whispered. And then he closed his gauntleted fist and snapped his fingers. The sound was not loud. It was a single, dry click, and the world went quiet. Across Wakanda, across Earth, across every star in every galaxy, half of all living beings turned to drifting grey ash and were gone. Bucky vanished while staring at Steve. Spider-Man held Tony Stark on Titan and crumbled in his arms. Half the Avengers dissolved into the wind. And Thanos, using the gauntlet one final time, opened a portal and walked away to a quiet planet to watch the sun rise.
