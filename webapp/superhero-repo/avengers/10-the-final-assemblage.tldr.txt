• A past-timeline Thanos follows Nebula's memories through the time portal; his warship Sanctuary II bombards the Avengers Compound to rubble
• Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor — the "Big Three" — rise from the wreckage and fight Thanos three-on-one
• Steve Rogers lifts Mjolnir and wields it alongside his vibranium shield — the first time he channels Thor's lightning
• Thanos calls his full army: Chitauri, Outriders, Black Order fill the horizon
• Doctor Strange opens portals across the battlefield; all snapped-away heroes return — Wakandans, Guardians, sorcerers of Kamar-Taj, Ravagers, Asgardians
• Captain America says "Avengers… Assemble" and two armies collide
• Captain Marvel punches through Sanctuary II; Wanda Maximoff nearly breaks Thanos with grief-fueled power; Spider-Man protects the gauntlet
• Thanos seizes the gauntlet and prepares to snap — Tony Stark steals the stones using nanotech and says "I am… Iron Man"
• Tony Stark snaps his fingers; Thanos and his entire army crumble to dust
• Tony Stark dies from the energy; Steve Rogers returns the stones to their timelines and stays in the past to live his life; passes the shield to Sam Wilson
