Two battles. Two planets. One chance. Are you listening? Because this one matters a lot.

On the dead planet Titan — the ruins of Thanos's own home world — something extraordinary was happening. Iron Man, Doctor Strange, Spider-Man, and the Guardians of the Galaxy had all ended up in the same place at exactly the same time, which was either very lucky or part of a plan nobody had explained yet. Doctor Strange raised the Eye of Agamotto and looked forward through fourteen MILLION possible futures, searching every single one for the path where the heroes won.

He found one. Just one.

"We have a plan," he said, without sharing what the winning future looked like.

The plan was brilliant: Mantis, whose power was to calm any mind she touched, would climb onto Thanos's shoulders. While he was dazed, the others would drag the golden Infinity Gauntlet off his enormous fist. Tony's armor gripped it. Peter Parker's webs pinned it. The glove was SLIDING — they were SO close —

Then Star-Lord learned that Gamora, the person he loved most, was gone forever. His grief broke through like a wave, and he struck Thanos across the face. The gauntlet slammed back on. Everything collapsed.

Thanos RIPPED A PIECE OF THE MOON out of orbit and dropped it in chunks. Then Doctor Strange traded the Time Stone — the Eye of Agamotto — for Tony's life, and Thanos stepped through a portal with five stones on his fist.

Meanwhile: Wakanda. King T'Challa had opened the borders of his kingdom to shelter Vision while Princess Shuri tried to safely remove the Mind Stone from his forehead. Outside, Captain America, Black Widow, Bucky Barnes, Falcon, and the Dora Milaje warrior-women stood in a line as Outrider monsters thundered toward them.

Then Thor arrived like a comet — BOOM — Stormbreaker ringing across the sky, Rocket and Groot right behind him. The army broke apart.

For one bright moment, hope felt real.

Thanos stepped through a portal.

Wanda Maximoff did the most painful thing she had ever done: she destroyed the Mind Stone herself — taking Vision with it — to keep Thanos from having it. The stone shattered. Silence.

Thanos used the Time Stone. The moment reversed. The Mind Stone rebuilt itself. He lifted Vision, took the stone gently, and six colors blazed from the completed Infinity Gauntlet.

Thor drove Stormbreaker into Thanos's chest with everything he had.

"You should have gone for the head," Thanos whispered.

He snapped his fingers. One quiet click. And half of all living things — on Earth and across every star in the sky — simply dissolved away on the wind. Quietly. All at once.
