• The Sokovia Accords — an international treaty placing the Avengers under government oversight — divides the team
• Tony Stark supports the Accords (accountability); Steve Rogers opposes them (freedom of conscience)
• Bucky Barnes (the Winter Soldier) is framed for a terrorist bombing at a United Nations summit
• Airport battle in Germany: Team Iron Man vs. Team Captain America, with Spider-Man (recruited by Stark) and Black Panther (seeking justice for his father) joining the fray
• Secret HYDRA base in Siberia: Tony learns Bucky killed his parents while under mind control — fights Steve in a raw, emotional clash
• Steve defeats Tony to protect Bucky; leaves his vibranium shield behind — symbolically resigning from "the system"
• Avengers fracture: Steve's faction operates underground as global fugitives; Tony remains at the Avengers Compound
• Thanos begins collecting Infinity Stones — Power Stone from Xandar; Space Stone (Tesseract) from Loki aboard the Asgardian refugee ship
• Bruce Banner crashes into the Sanctum Sanctorum warning Doctor Strange and Tony: "Thanos is coming"
• Tony Stark, Doctor Strange, Peter Parker sent into deep space; Steve Rogers and allies travel to Wakanda
