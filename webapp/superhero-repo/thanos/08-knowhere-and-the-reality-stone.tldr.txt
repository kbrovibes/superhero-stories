• Knowhere: a city built inside the severed skull of an ancient Celestial, drifting at the edge of the universe
• The Collector — Taneleer Tivan — built an extraordinary museum there with one of everything from a thousand galaxies
• The Reality Stone (also called the Aether) hid in his collection — red and liquid, able to reshape matter into any form
• Thanos sent the Black Order in first, then walked calmly through the chaos to the sealed case
• The Aether crystallized from liquid into a faceted red gem in his palm — the third stone in the gauntlet
• The Guardians of the Galaxy arrived too late; Thanos bent the world around Gamora with the stone's power
• He departed with three stones set in the gauntlet, heading toward Vormir and the hardest price of all
