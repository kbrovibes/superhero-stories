• After the snap Thanos retreated to a small unnamed green planet he called the Garden — his chosen retirement
• He hung the ruined Infinity Gauntlet on a post, grew vegetables, and watched sunrises; the gauntlet was burned and cracked by the snap's energy
• Twenty-three days later, the surviving Avengers — Thor, Captain America, Iron Man, Nebula, War Machine, Rocket, Captain Marvel — traced the stones' energy trail to the Garden
• They found a farmhouse and a thin, quiet man with a burned arm; no fortress, no warlord
• Thanos told them he had used the stones a second time to destroy them — no reversal was possible
• Thor raised Stormbreaker; the Mad Titan was gone — not triumphantly, just finally
• Five years of mourning followed before Scott Lang and the Time Heist brought everyone back
