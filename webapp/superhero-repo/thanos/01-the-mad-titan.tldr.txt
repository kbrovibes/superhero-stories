• Thanos born on Titan — a purple moon of Eternals — and watched his world die of overpopulation and famine
• Proposed halving Titan's population at random to save the rest; council called him mad and rejected the plan
• Titan fell to ruin; Thanos left on a starship with the conviction he was right
• Traveled the galaxy with the Black Order, "culling" worlds including Zen-Whoberi, Praxius IX, and icy moons of Aakon
• On Zen-Whoberi, took a green-skinned girl named Gamora as an adopted daughter; raised her to be the deadliest woman in the galaxy
• Also took Nebula — a blue-and-silver girl — rebuilding her body piece by piece as harsh training
• Lived not in a palace but in a wooden hut on a barge ship at the edge of the galaxy
• Six ancient Infinity Stones — Space, Mind, Reality, Power, Time, Soul — waited in the dark for the only being patient and strong enough to collect them all
