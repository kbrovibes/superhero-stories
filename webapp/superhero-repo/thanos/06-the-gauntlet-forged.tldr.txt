• Thanos needed a vessel strong enough to hold all six Infinity Stones — no ordinary material would survive
• He traveled to Nidavellir, the great dwarf forge built around a neutron star, ruled by dwarf-king Eitri
• Eitri's forge smelted Uru metal — the same material as Mjolnir — using the star's extreme heat and gravity
• Thanos demanded Eitri forge the Infinity Gauntlet, threatening the lives of two hundred dwarves
• Eitri opened the star ring; his smiths worked three days without rest to build the golden gauntlet
• The gauntlet had six stone settings and was the finest object Eitri had ever made
• Thanos spared the dwarves but destroyed the forge mechanism — no weapon could ever be made there again
• He left Eitri alone on a cooling ring with burned hands
• Thanos departed with the gauntlet gleaming on his fist, six empty settings waiting
