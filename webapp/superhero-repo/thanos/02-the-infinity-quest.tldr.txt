• Six Infinity Stones — Space (blue), Mind (yellow), Reality (red), Power (purple), Time (green), Soul (orange) — each controls a law of reality
• Thanos commissions dwarf-king Eitri of Nidavellir to forge the Infinity Gauntlet from solid Uru metal
• Power Stone (purple sphere inside the Orb) retrieved from Xandar after the Black Order destroys the Nova Corps
• Space Stone (Tesseract — glowing blue cube) surrendered by Loki aboard the Asgardian refugee ship to save Thor
• Reality Stone (red, liquid-like) taken from the Collector deep in Knowhere
• Mind Stone (yellow gem) lives in the forehead of Vision on Earth
• Time Stone (green) worn by Doctor Strange as the Eye of Agamotto
• Soul Stone on frozen cliffs of Vormir — guarded by a hooded figure with one rule: "A soul for a soul" — Thanos sacrifices Gamora and claims it
• Five stones glowing on the gauntlet; only Mind Stone and Time Stone remain before the Snap
