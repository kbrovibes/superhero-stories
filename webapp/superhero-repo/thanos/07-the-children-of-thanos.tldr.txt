• The Black Order was Thanos's inner circle — a family he built through conquest, believing his devotion to them was love
• Corvus Glaive: the most loyal, carrying a glaive that could cut through anything, including Infinity Stone housings
• Proxima Midnight: wielded a star-forged spear that split into three tracking shafts mid-flight; challenged Captain America
• Ebony Maw: telekinetic tactician who used words and mind-power rather than brute force; called Thanos "my father"
• Cull Obsidian (Black Dwarf): the largest, with near-impenetrable armor and a chain-axe — his arrival meant battle was serious
• Gamora and Nebula: daughters taken from conquered worlds, raised and scarred by Thanos, each finding her own path away
• Together they culled a hundred worlds, believing in Thanos's mission
• In the final battle over Wakanda, the Black Order fought until the assembled Avengers dissolved them one by one into dust
