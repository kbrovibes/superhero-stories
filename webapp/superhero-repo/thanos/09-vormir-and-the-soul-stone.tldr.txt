• The Soul Stone waited on the barren cliffs of Vormir, a world with an orange sky at the far edge of the galaxy
• Its keeper was Johann Schmidt — the Red Skull — sent there by the Tesseract long ago; he could only guide seekers, never hold the stone
• The price: a soul for a soul — the life of someone truly loved, freely given
• Thanos found Vormir's location in Nebula's stored memories; Gamora had kept the secret for years
• Gamora thought she was safe — she believed Thanos didn't truly love her; she was wrong
• Thanos wept real tears on the cliff, then let Gamora go
• He descended with the orange Soul Stone, the fourth stone in the gauntlet
• Two stones remained: the Mind Stone in Vision's forehead, the Time Stone around Doctor Strange's neck
• Something cracked quietly inside Thanos on Vormir — small and irreversible
