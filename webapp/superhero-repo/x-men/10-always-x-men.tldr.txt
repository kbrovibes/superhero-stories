• Professor Xavier holds a year-end gathering in the great hall and asks each X-Man: "What surprised you most this year?"
• Logan: the quiet — more than he expected
• Rogue: that she can still be surprised (she glances at Gambit)
• Nightcrawler: that nobody ever asked him to hide his blue skin, tail, or teleport-smoke
• Jean: how much louder joy is than worry
• Hank: that the boiler keeps breaking — everyone laughs and he means them to
• Storm: that she misses the Westchester mansion when she's away, the way you miss weather
• Cyclops, last: he says the feeling has no tactical name — then names it "family"
• Xavier looks at them all and asks "Again next year?" — Logan says "Obviously"
