• Logan (Wolverine) and Storm are sent to scout a Brotherhood safehouse in tunnels beneath an old facility in upstate New York
• Logan's adamantium claws slice the first door — and trigger every alarm in the place
• Storm's weather powers don't work underground — she needs open sky to call wind or lightning
• Logan uses his superhuman hearing to map the empty tunnels; Storm uses her senses to find ventilation and the way up
• They move together in silence, Logan scouting each intersection, Storm reading the air at every junction
• At the fifth door, Storm sends a tiny pulse of static electricity through a junction box — the lock clicks open
• Logan says "Huh," which is the closest he gets to a compliment
• They surface 40 minutes later and walk out to the rest of the team — and Storm discovers that she and Logan fit together better than either expected
