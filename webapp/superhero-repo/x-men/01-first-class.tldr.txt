• Professor Charles Xavier uses Cerebro to find young mutants living in fear across the world
• He sends handwritten letters inviting each of them to his mansion in Westchester, New York
• Scott Summers (Cyclops) arrives first, wearing ruby-quartz glasses to contain his optic blasts
• Ororo Munroe (Storm) comes from Kenya, trailing storm clouds wherever she goes
• Hank McCoy (Beast) is a blue-furred genius who rebuilds the mansion's boiler on day one
• Jean Grey arrives last — she can move objects with her mind and hear thoughts she didn't ask for
• Xavier meets each student privately and tells them their power is a gift, not a curse
• He names them the X-Men at the Xavier School for Gifted Youngsters — and for the first time, none of them feel alone
