This is not a drill. This is not a practice run. This is DARKSEID.

A Boom Tube — a crackling, thundering portal through space — splits the sky above Metropolis. And through it steps the tyrant of Apokolips himself. Gray armor. Burning red eyes. A face carved from stone. Darkseid does not look angry. He looks like someone who has already decided how this ends.

He is looking for the Anti-Life Equation. A mathematical formula hidden somewhere in Earth's data networks that could erase every free choice from every mind on the planet. If he finds it, the game is over.

Not happening. Not today.

WHOOOM — the Justice League arrives in four minutes.

Cyborg goes full machine mode, flooding Metropolis's entire data network with harmless static. Every system — BUZZZZZ — drowned in noise. Darkseid's scanners can't lock on. That buys time.

Batman is coordinating from a hover-vehicle above, transmitting targeting data to everyone simultaneously. He has also prepared a frequency — weeks of work — that can scramble Darkseid's Omega Beams by a fraction of a second. A FRACTION. In this fight, that fraction is everything.

Superman drops into Centennial Park first. They collide — KRABOOM — and the impact shakes six blocks. Clark is one of the strongest beings alive. Darkseid matches him. Then exceeds him. He drives Superman into the ground with one hand. This is bad.

CRAACK! Green Lantern hits Darkseid from behind with a concentrated beam. Two seconds of recovery time, Clark!

Wonder Woman's golden Lasso of Truth SNAPS around Darkseid's left arm and she PULLS. Her heels cut through the grass. He turns and looks at her like she is interesting.

The Flash goes into hyperdrive. FOOM — two hundred laps around one point in three seconds. A vortex builds. Darkseid's targeting systems wobble.

J'onn J'onzz slides into Darkseid's mind, silent as a dream. He finds what Batman predicted — a tiny crack of uncertainty at the center of all that iron will. He doesn't break it. He just marks it and broadcasts the data to Batman.

CRASH — Aquaman arrives from the east. The Potomac River RISES behind him and water columns HAMMER into Darkseid's armor, grounding his energy systems.

And then: all at once, all seven, together — EVERY POWER, EVERY STRENGTH — combined.

Darkseid takes one step back.

ONE STEP.

The scanner connection shatters. The Anti-Life Equation stays hidden.

Darkseid looks at each of them. His voice is like a mountain speaking: "This is not finished."

Then the Boom Tube opens. And he is gone.

Superman lands. He is bleeding. Diana is beside him before his boots touch the ground.

Today: the Justice League held. That is not nothing.

That is EVERYTHING.
