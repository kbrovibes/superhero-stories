• A vast alien warship from Apokolips appears over Metropolis, releasing swarms of winged Parademons
• Superman, Batman, Aquaman, Green Lantern, Wonder Woman, the Flash, and Cyborg all arrive independently
• Each hero fights in their own way: Superman clears the sky, Batman analyzes, Aquaman commands the tide, Green Lantern nets creatures, the Flash evacuates civilians
• Cyborg's sensors reveal the Parademons are tunneling toward a buried Mother Box — a device that can open a portal to Darkseid's world
• Superman destroys the Mother Box before it can activate, cutting off the warship's signal
• The Parademons retreat and vanish through a crackling portal
• Eight heroes who never trained together stand on a rooftop, realizing they fought as one without being asked
• Batman says what everyone is thinking: "That won't be the last time" — and the Justice League is born
