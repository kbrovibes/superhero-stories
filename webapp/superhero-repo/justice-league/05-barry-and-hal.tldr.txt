• Barry Allen (careful scientist) and Hal Jordan (reckless test pilot) are sent to Coast City together
• Brainiac has planted a shrink-ray device in the power grid — three combat probes guard it, no time for backup
• Barry can't run near the device — the shrink field disrupts his speed — so he lures the probes away
• Hal builds a containment bubble around the device and begins cutting its casing with precise energy shears
• Barry leads both probes into a turbine room, jams their sensors with a tornado, then races back
• They improvise together: Barry vibrates at the device's frequency; Hal uses that signal to find the safe cutting seam
• The device goes dark without triggering the shrink field — Coast City is safe
• Afterward, takeout pancakes on a walkway and an argument about syrup: they leave as genuine friends
