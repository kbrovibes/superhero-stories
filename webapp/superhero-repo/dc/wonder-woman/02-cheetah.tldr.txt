• Barbara Ann Minerva: once a brilliant archaeologist; cursed by an incomplete African ritual
• Ritual intended to bestow the power of a cheetah goddess; selfishness twisted the result
• Became the Cheetah: incredible speed, strength, feline instincts, razor-sharp claws
• Claws capable of piercing even Diana's near-invulnerable skin
• Cheetah views Diana with jealousy and hatred; sees the Amazon's divine gifts as unearned
• Diana views Barbara with deep compassion — remembers the scholar she used to be
• Battles are savage and fast: dense jungles, ancient ruins — primal, wild settings
• Cheetah uses archaeological knowledge to uncover ancient weapons and mythical forces
• Diana's goal is not just to defeat Cheetah, but to restore Barbara to herself
