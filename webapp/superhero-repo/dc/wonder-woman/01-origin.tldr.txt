• Diana: Princess of Themyscira, born of clay and divine breath by the will of the Olympian gods
• Mother: Queen Hippolyta; raised among the Amazons on the hidden island of Themyscira (Paradise Island)
• Trained in combat, philosophy, and science — paragon of Amazonian excellence
• Steve Trevor, an American pilot, crashes into the waters off Themyscira — Diana's first encounter with a man
• Diana competes in a secret tournament; wins the title of Wonder Woman
• Bestowed with the Lasso of Truth and the Bracelets of Submission
• Arrives in the modern world as Diana Prince: military intelligence, museum curator
• Founding member of the Justice League; brings warrior discipline and divine compassion
• Lesson: true power is found in the willingness to serve others
