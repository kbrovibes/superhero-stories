Heads up — I'm about to tell you about a princess who was born on a secret island, chosen by the gods, and became the greatest warrior in the world. And her name is Diana of Themyscira. Are you ready? Because this story is EXTRAORDINARY!

Deep in the sea, hidden behind a magical veil that keeps it invisible to the whole world, there is an island called Themyscira — sometimes called Paradise Island! White marble temples gleam in the sunlight. Gardens full of flowers stretch across rolling hills. And living there are the Amazons — extraordinary warrior women who are the strongest, wisest, bravest people you will ever meet. Their queen is Hippolyta, and she is magnificent.

Here's a fun fact: Wonder Woman was created way back in 1941 — and she was created to show that women could be just as powerful and heroic as anyone! She first appeared in All Star Comics #8, and became one of the most important superheroes in history!

Queen Hippolyta wanted a daughter more than anything. And the gods of Olympus heard her wish. They gave Diana divine gifts — Demeter gave her strength, Athena gave her wisdom, Hermes gave her speed, Aphrodite gave her grace. Diana was born MAGNIFICENT. And she grew up on Themyscira running across beaches, training with the greatest Amazon warriors, learning science and medicine and how to speak a hundred languages.

Then one day — SPLASH! A plane crashed into the sea near Themyscira. Diana swam out through the waves and rescued the pilot: an American named Steve Trevor, the first man she had ever seen! He told her about the wide world beyond the island — a world with wars and trouble and people who needed help.

Diana entered a secret tournament to determine which Amazon would go help the world. Her mother, Queen Hippolyta, worried terribly. But Diana fought with everything she had and WON. She was given the golden Lasso of Truth and her bulletproof silver bracelets — and a new name: Wonder Woman! Then she left the only home she'd ever known to go help a world full of strangers. Because that's what heroes do.
