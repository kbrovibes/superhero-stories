• Themyscira (Paradise Island): hidden by a mystical veil; white marble temples, lush gardens, millennia of Amazon history
• Queen Hippolyta: Diana's mother, guide, and leader
• Antiope: Diana's warrior mentor; taught discipline and combat mastery
• Amazon society values intellect, athleticism, and compassion equally
• Amazons escaped patriarchal oppression; built a society of peace, art, science, and truth
• Diana carries Amazonian ideals — equality, wisdom, harmony — into the modern world
• Leaving Themyscira was a bittersweet sacrifice; the island remains a place of sanctuary and renewal
• Diana compares Amazon harmony with modern society's complexity; it fuels her commitment to justice
• Themyscira heritage is the heart of Wonder Woman's story: the reason she never loses hope
