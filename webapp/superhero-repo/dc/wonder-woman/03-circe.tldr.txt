• Circe: ancient sorceress, daughter of the Titans; divine longevity and mastery of dark arts
• Signature power: turns humans into animals as a mockery of those she deems inferior
• Bases herself on the mystical island of Aeaea; has plotted against the Amazons for millennia
• Animosity toward Diana rooted in hatred of Amazonian order and Diana's divine gifts
• Schemes blend modern technology with ancient mystical forces; plans span centuries
• Creates terrifying illusions, summons mythical beasts, and attempts mind-control on allies
• Uses beauty and charm to manipulate political leaders and corporate moguls
• Diana's Lasso of Truth is her primary tool for cutting through Circe's deceptions
• Diana's mental discipline and connection to truth consistently resist Circe's influence
