SWOOSH SLASH CRASH! Get ready for Wonder Woman's most personal battle — because this one isn't just about stopping a villain. It's about trying to save a friend!

Barbara Ann Minerva was one of the most brilliant archaeologists in the world. She could read ancient languages most people had never even heard of. She travelled to ruins and jungles and hidden temples, uncovering the secrets of the past. Diana admired her deeply. They were friends!

But Barbara wanted more than knowledge. She found an ancient ritual — one that was supposed to grant the power of a cheetah goddess. The ritual required a completely pure heart. But Barbara's desperate hunger for power had already cracked hers, just a little. And the ritual went terribly wrong.

WHOOOM — when the transformation was over, Barbara had become the Cheetah! Razor-sharp claws. Lightning-fast speed. A snarl where her voice used to be. She is one of the few beings whose claws can actually scratch through Wonder Woman's near-invulnerable skin — and that makes her incredibly dangerous. ZAP — she moves like a golden blur through jungles and ancient ruins. SLASH — her claws strike before you even see her coming!

The Cheetah looks at Diana and sees everything she wanted and couldn't have — divine gifts given freely, love and admiration from the world. She fights with a blazing, personal fury.

But here's what makes Diana special. When she looks at the Cheetah — even in the heat of the fiercest battle — she doesn't see an enemy. She sees Barbara. Her friend. The brilliant scholar who got lost. Diana fights not to defeat the Cheetah, but to reach Barbara. Her goal isn't just winning — it's bringing her friend home.

Can you imagine having that much hope for someone? That is the true power of Wonder Woman. Not just her strength or her lasso — but her refusal to ever, ever give up on the people she loves.
