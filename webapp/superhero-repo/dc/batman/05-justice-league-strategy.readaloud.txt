Sometimes a problem is so big that even the bravest hero cannot solve it alone. That is when the Justice League comes together — a team of the world's greatest heroes. And right there among them stands Batman: no magic powers, but the most remarkable mind on the planet. They call him the World's Greatest Detective, because he can find a clue that everyone else has walked right past.

Before any mission, while others might be flexing their muscles, Batman is bent over maps and charts, analyzing every piece of information he can find. He thinks about every possible thing that could go wrong — and then he figures out exactly what the team should do. Superman and Wonder Woman always listen carefully, because they know Batman's plans are thoughtful and precise.

And do you know what happened next? It turned out that Batman was also the one who built the team's special communication devices, so every hero could talk to every other hero no matter where they were. He even manages the Watchtower — a magnificent headquarters that floats high above the Earth in space. From up there, Batman can watch the whole world and guide his friends to wherever they are needed most.

His superpower is his calm. When the pressure is at its very highest, Batman becomes very still and very clear-headed — and that is exactly what a team needs in its darkest moment.

And that is how the World's Greatest Detective proved that the most powerful thing any person can bring to a team is not muscles or magic, but a heart full of courage and a mind full of wisdom.
