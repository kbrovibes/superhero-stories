• Batcave: hidden beneath Wayne Manor, home to advanced computers and the Batmobile
• Alfred Pennyworth is one of the few trusted people who knows about the Batcave
• Utility Belt: golden belt packed with gadgets for every situation
• Grapnel Gun: lets Batman swing between Gotham's skyscrapers like an acrobat
• Batarangs: bat-shaped throwing tools for disarming or pressing buttons from a distance
• Belt also holds a mini-flashlight, first-aid kit, and snacks for long detective nights
• Batman spends time in the Batcave testing gadgets before every mission
• No superpowers — engineering and science replace them entirely
• Lesson: preparation and smart thinking are a hero's most important tools
