Listen up, because Gotham City has a very unusual troublemaker — and trust me, you have NEVER seen anyone quite like this!

His name is the Joker, and the very first thing you notice about him is the laugh. HA HA HA! It rings out across the rooftops of Gotham, bouncing off every skyscraper and alley wall. Then you see the rest of him: a bright purple suit, an orange tie, wild bright green hair sticking out in every direction, and a huge red smile that stretches from ear to ear. Is he a circus clown? Is he a magician? He's the Joker — and he is 100% his own category!

Now here's the thing about the Joker: his schemes are BIG and SILLY and completely unpredictable. WHOOSH — he once filled the Gotham Museum with giant silly balloons! ZAP — he swapped all the statues in the park with funny rubber versions! HONK HONK — he turned the Funland amusement park into one enormous prank show! The Joker is actually incredibly clever — he builds fantastic gadgets and elaborate puzzle-machines. He could probably be a great inventor! But instead, he uses all that brain power just to cause harmless chaos.

And who shows up every single time? BATMAN. Serious, focused, methodical Batman — who is basically the exact opposite of the Joker in every possible way. The Joker laughs at everything; Batman rarely smiles. The Joker wants beautiful disorder; Batman wants careful justice. It's like peanut butter and pickles — completely different, but somehow always ending up together!

Here's something important, though. Can you guess how Batman treats the Joker when he catches him? With fairness and respect — every single time. Because Batman knows that how you treat someone, even someone who drives you absolutely bananas, says everything about who YOU are.

HA HA HA! The Joker would probably say that's the funniest thing he's ever heard. But deep down? Even the Joker knows Batman is the reason Gotham stays safe. And that is the biggest joke of all.
