• The Joker: bright purple suit, orange tie, wild green hair, huge red smile
• Signature trait: loud mischievous "HA HA!" laughter heard across Gotham
• Schemes involve harmless chaos — giant balloons, swapped statues, silly pranks
• Stages tricks at Funland amusement park and the grand Gotham Museum
• Uses intelligence to build complex puzzles and gadgets — but for chaos, not justice
• Batman and the Joker: two sides of a coin — serious vs. always laughing
• Batman ensures the Joker's mischief never causes real harm to Gotham
• Batman always treats the Joker with fairness and respect
• Lesson: everyone sees the world differently; justice keeps the balance
