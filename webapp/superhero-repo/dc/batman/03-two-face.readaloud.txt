Once upon a time, Harvey Dent was one of Gotham City's most respected leaders. He worked very hard to make sure everyone followed the law and was treated fairly. But then something surprising happened, and Harvey became someone new — someone known as Two-Face.

You can spot Two-Face right away, because his suit is split perfectly down the middle. One half is one color and pattern, the other half is completely different. Just like his suit, Harvey has two sides to him. And to decide anything at all, he pulls out a special silver coin. One side is shiny and clean — that is the happy, certain face. The other side is scratched and a little worn — that is the uncertain, grumpy face. He flips it into the air, and wherever it lands, that is what he does next.

And do you know what happened next? Batman kept trying to reach the good man still living inside Harvey Dent. He used all his detective skills to figure out which way the coin might fall and how to help Harvey choose the right path. It was like the world's most complicated game of chance and strategy, played right there on the streets of Gotham.

Batman knew that underneath the split suit and the coin-flipping, there was still a man who cared about justice. So he never gave up. He believed that the choices we make in our hearts matter far more than anything a flipped coin could decide.

And that is how Batman taught Gotham that the bravest choice is not leaving things to luck — but doing what you know in your heart is right.
