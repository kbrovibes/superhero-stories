• Batman: Gotham City's eternal protector, standing on the highest gargoyles and walking the darkest alleys
• Monitors police bands, mob families, and Arkham Asylum constantly
• Partnership with Commissioner Gordon: the system and the symbol together
• The Bat-Signal is both a call for help and a promise that Gotham is not alone
• Victories measured in small things: safe parks, businesses free from extortion, witnesses who testify
• Challenges are endless: new villains, returning schemes, the weight of the city itself
• Arkham Asylum holds Gotham's most dangerous criminals — Batman watches it closely
• Batman's long-term goal: inspire enough people to protect Gotham without him
• Hope is as important as justice — Batman offers Gotham both every single night
