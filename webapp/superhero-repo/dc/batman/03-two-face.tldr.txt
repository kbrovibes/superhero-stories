• Harvey Dent: once a respected Gotham leader devoted to the law
• A surprising event transforms Harvey into Two-Face
• Signature look: suit split down the middle in two colors and patterns
• Carries a silver coin — one side shiny and happy, one side scratched and uncertain
• Flips the coin before every decision, making him completely unpredictable
• Batman uses detective skills to predict the coin and guide Two-Face toward good
• Batman sees the good man still inside Harvey Dent
• Constant game of chance and strategy between the two in Gotham
• Lesson: use your mind and heart to decide right from wrong, not luck
