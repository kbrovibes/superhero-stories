• Edward Nygma, the Riddler, rigs broadcast towers across Gotham with high-frequency transmitters
• Goal: scramble Gotham's communication networks unless Batman solves elaborate enigmas
• First riddle answered ("a keyboard") leads Batman to an abandoned mainframe in the financial district
• Each location is a trap: pressurized gas, automated turrets, floor sensors
• Second riddle answered ("the harbor") leads to a submerged foundry beneath the water
• Final clue — a riddle about "a reflection of a shadow" — points to the Hall of Mirrors in a derelict amusement park
• Batman ignores the mirror decoys; listens for the Riddler's breathing through smoke
• Riddler found behind a false panel; disarmed before he triggers the final transmitter
• Communication networks restored; Riddler handed to GCPD and sent to Arkham Asylum
