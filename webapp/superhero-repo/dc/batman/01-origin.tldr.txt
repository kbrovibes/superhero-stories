• Bruce Wayne is a small boy walking home from the movies with his parents Thomas and Martha Wayne
• A robber on Crime Alley takes Martha's famous pearl necklace and Thomas's pocket watch, and Bruce loses his parents in one terrible night
• Alfred Pennyworth, the family butler at Wayne Manor, raises Bruce and teaches him that grief can become purpose
• Bruce travels the world to study forensics, martial arts, escape arts, and detective work — becoming the World's Greatest Detective
• A bat crashes through his study window one night and inspires his symbol: "I shall become a bat"
• He discovers the Batcave beneath Wayne Manor and builds the Batmobile, Batcomputer, batarangs, and his utility belt
• Batman never carries a gun, because he remembers what guns took from him
• By day, Bruce Wayne runs Wayne Enterprises and funds Gotham's hospitals and schools
• Commissioner Jim Gordon lights the Bat-Signal above the GCPD rooftop when Gotham needs Batman
