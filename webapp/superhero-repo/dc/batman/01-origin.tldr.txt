• Bruce Wayne grows up in Wayne Manor, Gotham City, raised by Alfred Pennyworth
• Parents' deaths motivate Bruce to dedicate his life to protecting Gotham
• Bruce spends years studying science, history, and training his body
• Chooses the bat as his symbol — misunderstood creature that helps the world
• No superpowers: relies on intellect, courage, and vast resources
• Partners with police to keep Gotham safe and lawful
• The Bat-Signal in the night sky is Gotham's call for help
• Wayne wealth funds schools, hospitals, and parks during the day
• Batman proves that any person can be a hero through dedication
