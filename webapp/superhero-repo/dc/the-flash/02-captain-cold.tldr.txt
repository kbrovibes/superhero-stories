• Leonard Snart — known as Captain Cold, one of the Flash's most persistent enemies in Central City
• Signature weapon: a cold gun that generates absolute zero temperatures, slowing the Flash's molecules
• Not driven by madness — a professional thief who plans every job with precision and discipline
• Leader of the Rogues: a criminal crew including Mirror Master, Weather Wizard, Heat Wave, and the Trickster
• Rogues' code: no senseless killing, no targeting the Flash's family — "honor among thieves"
• Cold views his rivalry with the Flash as a professional cat-and-mouse game, not personal hatred
• Has temporarily allied with the Flash when greater threats endangered Central City
• Lesson: even in cold hearts, a certain kind of honor can exist — not every villain is simply evil
