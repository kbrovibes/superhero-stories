Every river has a source. Even invisible ones! And the source of Barry Allen's incredible speed is something that no textbook has ever named, no scientist has ever measured, and no machine has ever fully understood. It's called the Speed Force. And it is MAGNIFICENT!

The Speed Force is a primordial cosmic energy — meaning it's been around longer than planets, longer than stars, longer than almost anything. It is the force that makes time itself flow forward. It runs through the whole universe like an invisible current beneath everything. And when that bolt of lightning soaked through Barry Allen in his lab that stormy night? It didn't just make him fast. It connected him — personally, directly — to this ancient, boundless cosmic river. He could feel it like a second heartbeat. Always there. Always humming. Always ready.

WHOOOOSH! But here's the thing — the Speed Force is tricky to navigate. So Barry, being a brilliant scientist, did what brilliant scientists do: he built a machine. He called it the COSMIC TREADMILL! By running on it at precisely the right speed, with his mind perfectly focused, Barry could steer himself through the Speed Force's hidden pathways — sliding through TIME, crossing between dimensions, and visiting completely different worlds and realities that most people never even dream exist!

Inside the Speed Force itself? Barry described it as a rushing river of light where past, present, and future all shimmer together at once. Beautiful. Humbling. A little bit dangerous! Because if your mind wanders, you could get lost in the infinite forever.

Do you know what kept Barry safe and found his way home every time? His love for the people of Central City. The thought of Iris. The thought of the streets he ran to protect. Love, it turns out, is an anchor even the cosmic universe respects.

The Cosmic Treadmill: the most important piece of scientific equipment ever built on a treadmill. Barry invented it. Of course he did.
