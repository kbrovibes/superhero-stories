• The Flash — the Justice League's primary rescue specialist; first to arrive, last to leave
• Can evacuate thousands of people from a disaster (skyscraper collapse, floods) in mere seconds
• Uses super-senses mid-chaos to locate people who need help
• Serves as the team's lifeline in battle: intercepting projectiles, rescuing fallen allies
• Specific rescues: catching a falling Wonder Woman, pulling a wounded Batman from danger
• Provides global logistics: disaster-zone supplies, continent-crossing transport, real-time reconnaissance
• His optimism and humor raise team morale — the heart of the Justice League
• Lesson: heroism is as much about compassion and connection as it is about power
