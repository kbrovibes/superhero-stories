• Barry Allen — forensic scientist at the Central City Police Department
• Stormy night: lightning bolt shatters lab window, drenches Barry in electrified chemicals
• Instant connection to the Speed Force, a primordial energy beyond space and time
• New abilities: running faster than sound, vibrating through solid objects, super-perception
• Adopts the identity of The Flash; builds a friction-resistant suit
• Dual life: scientist solving crimes from the inside, superhero stopping them from the outside
• Founding member of the Justice League
• Lesson: true strength is compassion and purpose, not just raw speed
• Central City becomes home turf; Barry runs toward danger rather than away from it
