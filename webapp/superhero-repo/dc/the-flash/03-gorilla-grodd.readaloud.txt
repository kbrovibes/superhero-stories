Deep in the jungles of Africa — past every road and every map and every thing that has a name — there is a city that most people don't believe exists. The buildings are tall and gleaming. The streets are orderly and clean. And every single citizen walks upright, speaks in complete sentences, and can solve complicated math problems in their head. This is Gorilla City. And its ruler is a gorilla named Grodd. And he is one of the scariest enemies the Flash has ever faced!

CRASH — Gorilla Grodd is enormous. The ground actually shakes when he walks. His muscles are like boulders stacked on top of boulders. He could pick up a car and throw it across a city block without even trying. So physically? Already a big problem!

But here's the really scary part: Grodd is a TELEPATH. That means he can reach his thoughts into other people's minds! He can make you see things that aren't there — WHOOOOA! He can pull at your memories. He can fill your head with fear and confusion. He can control the thoughts of THOUSANDS of people at once if he wants to! That's not a fight you win with fast feet. That's a fight you have to win with your BRAIN.

Grodd wants one thing above all else: the Speed Force — the cosmic energy that gives Barry his power. Grodd believes gorillas should rule the world, and the Flash is in his way!

Their battles are unlike anything Central City has seen. Grodd fights from inside Barry's head while Barry races across rooftops trying to think clearly. But here's the thing Grodd can never understand: Barry Allen has a pure, kind heart. His intentions are completely, genuinely good. And that simple goodness creates a mental shield that Grodd's telepathy has trouble piercing through.

The fastest brain isn't always the best one. Sometimes the kindest one wins. And Barry Allen proves it every time he runs.
