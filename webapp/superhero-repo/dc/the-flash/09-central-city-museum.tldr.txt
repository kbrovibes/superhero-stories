• Flash Museum — a grand monument in Central City dedicated to the legacy of Barry Allen
• Houses the Flash's original suits, forensic laboratory equipment, and a replica Cosmic Treadmill
• Hall of Rogues exhibit: captured weapons and detailed profiles of the Flash's adversaries
• Architecture celebrates speed and motion; interactive displays for all ages
• Serves as a community center: educational programs, lectures on temporal physics, city events
• Barry visits in his civilian identity, listening to how citizens have been inspired by his actions
• Museum represents the bond between hero and city — a living testament to shared identity
• Provides a sense of continuity and pride for the people of Central City
• Lesson: a hero's greatest legacy is not the battles won, but the hope left behind in others
