• The Rogues Gallery — Central City's most organized group of criminals, led by Captain Cold
• Members: Captain Cold (Leonard Snart), Mirror Master, Weather Wizard, Heat Wave, the Trickster
• Core philosophy: crime as a professional business, not chaos — planning, teamwork, discipline
• Strict code: no needless killing; never target the Flash's family or cross certain lines
• Avoid pushing the Flash to respond with maximum force — self-preservation through restraint
• The Flash and the Rogues share a strange mutual respect; each side raises the other's game
• The Rogues provide brotherhood and belonging to members who have no other community
• Territorial loyalty: the Rogues defend Central City against outside threats that disrupt their operations
• Lesson: even on the wrong side of the law, choices and limits define who a person truly is
