• Eobard Thawne — the Reverse-Flash; a scientist from the 25th century obsessed with Barry Allen
• Recreated the accident that gave Barry his powers; connected to the Negative Speed Force
• Fueled by hatred and jealousy — views himself as the architect of Barry's tragic life events
• Travels through time to orchestrate tragedies that shaped Barry's destiny
• Negative Speed Force allows time manipulation: erasing people from history, altering events
• Their battles move at incomprehensible speeds; chronal energy marks their trails through time
• Barry fights Thawne with resolve, compassion, and refusal to become what Thawne represents
• Their rivalry threatens the Multiverse — a constant war between creation and destruction
• Yellow suit vs. scarlet suit: the shadow that follows the Flash through every era
• Lesson: the greatest darkness often comes from a mirror — a reflection of greatness gone wrong
