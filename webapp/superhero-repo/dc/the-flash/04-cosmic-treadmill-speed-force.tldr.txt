• Speed Force — a primordial cosmic energy field underlying all motion in the universe
• Barry Allen's lightning accident connected him directly to the Speed Force
• Cosmic Treadmill — a device Barry designed; combines his speed with precise vibrations to navigate the Speed Force
• Allows time travel and dimensional travel when used correctly
• Inside the Speed Force: a realm of pure energy where past, present, and future coexist; described as a rushing river of light
• Additional powers via Speed Force: sharing momentum, stealing speed from objects, accelerated healing
• Cosmic Treadmill also acts as a gateway to the Multiverse — alternate realities with different versions of the Flash
• Meetings with other speedsters helped Barry refine his understanding of the Speed Force
• Lesson: the Speed Force cannot be controlled — it must be channeled through physical effort and mental clarity
