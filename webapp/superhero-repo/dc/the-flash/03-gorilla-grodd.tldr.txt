• Gorilla Grodd — ruler of the hidden, technologically advanced Gorilla City, deep in the jungles of Africa
• Powers: immense physical strength and unparalleled telepathy — can dominate the thoughts of thousands
• Views humanity as inferior; covets the Speed Force that powers the Flash
• Uses telepathy to create illusions, manipulate memories, and project waves of mental agony
• Also deploys advanced technology and ancient mystical artifacts in elaborate, multi-year schemes
• Barry Allen's mental discipline and pure intentions consistently resist Grodd's psychic assaults
• Physical threat is massive: Grodd's strength lets him match the world's most powerful heroes
• Lesson: the greatest threats combine physical might with brilliant but empathy-free intellect
