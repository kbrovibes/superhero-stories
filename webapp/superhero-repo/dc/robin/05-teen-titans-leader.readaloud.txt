Robin was growing up.

By the time Dick Grayson was fifteen years old, he had been Robin for SEVEN YEARS. He had fought the Joker. He had fought Two-Face. He had swung over Gotham on more grappling lines than he could count. And he was getting REALLY good. So good that Gotham City couldn't hold him anymore. He needed something bigger.

And way across the country, in a sunny city by the ocean called JUMP CITY, four young heroes were looking for a fifth member. Are you ready to meet them?

FIRST! CYBORG! He's half man, half machine. His arms are robot arms. His legs are robot legs. His shoulder has a giant SONIC CANNON that goes BWAAAAAA-OOOOM! And his catchphrase? "BOOYAH!"

SECOND! STARFIRE! Her real name is KORIAND'R, but everyone calls her KORI. She's a princess from a faraway planet called Tamaran. She has bright orange skin, long red hair, and she FLIES. And she can throw GREEN STARBOLTS — like little glowing balls of energy — right out of her hands! PEW PEW!

THIRD! RAVEN! Her dad is a powerful sorcerer, so Raven has MAGIC. She wears a long blue cloak. She can lift cars with her mind. She says one quiet magic word — "Azarath Metrion ZINTHOS!" — and dark shadows wrap around things and pick them up. Spooky! Cool!

FOURTH! BEAST BOY! He has BRIGHT GREEN SKIN. And listen to this — he can turn into ANY animal he has ever seen! A mouse! A T. rex! An octopus! A hummingbird! A goldfish! He's also the joke-teller of the team. His jokes are not always good, but he is always trying!

These four needed a LEADER. Somebody smart. Somebody fearless. Somebody trained by the best.

They needed Robin.

Cyborg flew their amazing T-shaped jet all the way to Gotham. He showed up at Wayne Manor. And he asked Dick to come lead the team!

Bruce surprised everyone. He said yes! He said, "Dick. You've been my partner long enough. It's time to be somebody's LEADER."

So Robin flew west.

The team set up their base in the T-TOWER — a tall building SHAPED LIKE A GIANT LETTER T! It sat on a small island right in the middle of Jump City Bay. From the top floor you could see the whole ocean.

Robin's first job was to teach them how to work TOGETHER. Because each of them was super powerful on their own — but they weren't a team yet.

Starfire wanted to FLY at every problem. Raven wanted to THINK about every problem. Cyborg wanted to BLAST every problem. Beast Boy wanted to make JOKES about every problem. And Robin had to figure out — when do you fly? When do you think? When do you blast? When do you joke?

Pretty soon, they were AMAZING. Starfire would fly up high and scout. Raven would wrap shadows around hostages to keep them safe. Cyborg would blast through walls. Beast Boy would turn into a mouse and sneak under doors. And Robin? Robin called the plays, like a quarterback. "Starfire — left! Raven — high! Cyborg — back! Beast Boy — front!" PEW! WHOOSH! BOOYAH!

Their first BIG enemy was a quiet, scary man named SLADE. He wore a mask that was HALF ORANGE, HALF BLACK. And he wanted Robin to become HIS apprentice instead. He thought Robin would be tempted by power.

Slade was very, very wrong.

Robin used everything Bruce had taught him about patience. He used everything his new friends had taught him about teamwork. And TOGETHER, the Teen Titans outsmarted Slade and trapped him in his own underground hideout!

After that, the team became FAMOUS. They handed out little round T-communicators — shaped like the letter T — to young heroes all over the world, so anybody could call for backup.

And Dick Grayson — the boy who once flew on a trapeze under a striped circus tent — was now the leader of the cheerfullest, weirdest, most powerful team of young heroes anywhere.

The little robin had grown up to lead a whole flock!

TEEN TITANS, GO!
