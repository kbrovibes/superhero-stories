• Robin has his own workbench in the Batcave next to the giant computer
• His three special inventions: a tray of Bird-arangs, a coil of jumping line, and his bright yellow R-belt
• Bird-arangs are smaller and lighter than Batman's Batarangs — Robin can throw two at once, one in each hand, like a juggler
• Robin banks his Bird-arangs off walls, ceilings, and moving objects to surprise bad guys from behind
• The R-belt has grappling-hook wrist-launcher, smoke pellets, lockpicks, climbing rope, magnifying glass, yellow flares, and Alfred's "universal tool" — tape
• Inside the belt is a small folded photo of Mary and John Grayson, hidden in a waterproof inner pouch
• Robin's greatest gadget is his own body — years of trapeze training give him perfect midair control
• Bruce says, "He doesn't fight gravity. He has a conversation with it." — Alfred pins the quote to the kitchen fridge
