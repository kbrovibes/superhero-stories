• Dick Grayson is nineteen, taller than Bruce, and has led the Teen Titans for four years
• The bright Robin costume Alfred stitched when Dick was nine is starting to feel wrong — he's not a sidekick anymore
• Dick visits Wayne Manor and tells Bruce, "I think I'm done being Robin" — Bruce says simply, "I know"
• Superman once told Dick about a Kryptonian legend: Nightwing, a hero who flew through the city of Kandor at night protecting people
• Superman said: "If you ever needed a new name, Dick, I think Nightwing is yours" — Dick has remembered it for years
• Dick and Alfred design the new suit: sleek black armored mesh, with a bold royal blue bird-shape across the chest like wings
• His weapons change too: instead of Bird-arangs, he carries two escrima sticks clipped to his back
• Dick isn't leaving Robin behind — he's carrying Robin inside him; the little robin has grown into a Nightwing
