• After moving into Wayne Manor, Dick begins training in the Batcave every evening with Bruce
• Bruce teaches Dick fighting techniques, how to throw a Batarang, lockpicking, and how to vanish into shadow
• Dick's trapeze background makes him an incredible acrobat — Bruce teaches him patience and stillness
• Bruce tells Dick he needs his own hero name — not a copy of Batman's, but his own
• Dick chooses the name "Robin," after the bright spring birds his mother Mary called him — "mio piccolo robin"
• Alfred Pennyworth sews the first Robin costume: red tunic with a yellow R, green sleeves, green leggings, yellow cape, black domino mask
• Dick climbs to the top of the Batcave and leaps into the air in a triple somersault — Bruce catches him wrist-to-wrist like the Flying Graysons
• In that moment, the Dynamic Duo is born
