When Dick Grayson moved from Jump City all the way to Blüdhaven, do you know what worried him the MOST? It wasn't the corrupt mayor. It wasn't the foggy alleys. It wasn't the buses being late.

It was leaving HIS BEST FRIEND.

His best friend on the Teen Titans was KORI. Her full name is Princess KORIAND'R, and her hero name is STARFIRE. Remember her? Bright orange skin! Long red hair that floated when she flew! Big green eyes! And she called Dick something very special — "Friend Richard" — and the way she said it sounded like a tiny SONG. "Friend Riiiiiichard!"

Dick was scared that moving away would mean losing her. But — guess what?

He did NOT lose her. Not one bit!

What happened was even better. Starfire and Nightwing built a friendship that worked across TWO cities at the SAME TIME. Because here's the thing — Starfire is the FASTEST FLIER on the Teen Titans. She can fly from Jump City to Blüdhaven in only TWENTY MINUTES! That's faster than your school bus gets to school!

So every Friday night — listen to this — every Friday night, after Dick's shift at the police department was over, and the sky was getting dark, and the fog was rolling in over Blüdhaven, KORI would come WHOOSHING down out of the sky! Glowing orange in the dark! Right onto the rooftop of Dick's apartment on Parkthorne Avenue!

WHOOOOOSH!

And she ALWAYS brought a takeout bag. Want to know what was in it? ZORKA-BERRY PIES! From a tiny little Tamaranean bakery she had found all the way over in Star City! Zorka berries are sweet and sticky and PURPLE. Imagine the most delicious blueberry pie you've ever had — and now make it PURPLE. That's a zorka-berry pie!

They sat on the rooftop together. Sometimes they went out and patrolled Blüdhaven — Starfire flying way up high looking down with her sharp eyes, Nightwing swinging between the warehouses below with his escrima sticks. SWOOSH, SPIN, SWOOSH!

Sometimes they just TALKED.

Kori told Dick about her home planet, Tamaran. She told him about the K'tarr'm festival where the sky turns NINE different shades of purple! She told him about her sister Komand'r, who was bossy. She told him about how she missed the smell of Tamaranean flowers.

Dick told Kori about Mary and John Grayson. About flying on the trapeze. About Haly's Circus. About the train that smelled like sawdust and popcorn.

They knew each other's stories. The way real best friends do.

Now — there was ONE NIGHT in Blüdhaven that everybody still talks about.

A whole big gang from OUT of town decided to attack the Blüdhaven police precinct house all at once! There were like ten of them, with all kinds of weapons! And Nightwing was OUTNUMBERED — ten to ONE!

But guess who happened to be visiting that weekend?

A long-red-haired figure with bright orange skin, standing up on the precinct roof, watching the night sky.

YEEEEAAAAH!

Starfire dropped out of the sky like a little comet! GREEN STARBOLTS BLAZING from her hands! PEW PEW PEW! The gang took ONE LOOK at her and turned around and RAN.

Sergeant Amy Rohrbach was watching all this through the police precinct window. She blinked a few times. And she said quietly to herself:

"I really need to ask Officer Grayson about his friend from out of town."

(But she never did. Amy is smart like that.)

The thing about Nightwing and Starfire — Dick and Kori — is that they didn't need to live in the same city. They were better than that. They called each other on their T-communicators at midnight just to say good night. They sent each other voice messages full of dumb jokes. They were each other's HOME BASE — no matter which rooftop either of them happened to be standing on.

Because the BEST friendships? They don't need to be next to you to make you brave.

Friend Richard. And Friend Starfire. Best friends across two cities.
