• Dick's biggest worry about moving to Blüdhaven was leaving his best friend Kori — Princess Koriand'r of Tamaran, also known as Starfire
• Starfire has bright orange skin and long red hair, and she calls Dick "Friend Richard" like a small song
• Starfire is the fastest flier on the Titans — she can cover Jump City to Blüdhaven in about twenty minutes
• Every Friday night she flies to Dick's rooftop on Parkthorne Avenue, usually with a takeout bag of zorka-berry pies
• They patrol Blüdhaven together — Starfire flies high to scout, Nightwing swings between the warehouses
• Kori tells Dick about Tamaran, her sister Komand'r, and the K'tarr'm festival; Dick tells her about Mary, John, and Haly's Circus
• One famous night a gang attacks the Blüdhaven precinct ten-to-one and Starfire drops out of the sky like a comet to save Nightwing
• Theirs is the kind of friendship that does not need to be in the same city — they are each other's home base
