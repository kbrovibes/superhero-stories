• Nightwing needs a city of his own — not Gotham (Bruce's) and not Jump City (Titans')
• He picks Blüdhaven, an old port city just down the coast from Gotham that almost every other hero has given up on
• Blüdhaven has rusting factories, corrupt cops, a crooked mayor, and the mob running the docks
• Dick rents an apartment on Parkthorne Avenue and joins the Blüdhaven Police Department as rookie officer badge 168
• His partner is Sergeant Amy Rohrbach, who quickly realizes Dick is a far better detective than a rookie should be
• Blüdhaven's bad guys are quieter than Gotham's — no clown costumes, just trucking companies and bribes
• The biggest enemy is a crime boss named Roland Desmond, who calls himself Blockbuster — huge, calm, owns half the city
• Dick takes Blockbuster apart slowly over two years; the buses start running on time, the mayor is arrested, the docks reopen
