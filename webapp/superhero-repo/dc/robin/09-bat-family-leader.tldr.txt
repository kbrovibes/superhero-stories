• During a long crisis, Bruce Wayne vanishes — lost in time — and Gotham loses its Batman for months
• Without Batman, the Joker grows bolder, the Penguin re-emerges, and gangs start carving up the city
• Commissioner Gordon keeps the Bat-Signal lit every night, but for the first time in twenty years, nothing answers
• The Bat-Family meets in the Batcave: Alfred, Tim Drake, Damian Wayne, and Barbara Gordon (Oracle)
• Everyone turns to Dick — someone has to be Batman until Bruce returns, and Gotham cannot stand without one
• Dick chooses a new Batsuit, tailored to fit, keeping the blue Nightwing bird across the chest alongside the dark cape and cowl
• It is a Batman who once was Robin — bright, acrobatic, kinder than Bruce, but still the protector of Gotham
• Damian Wayne, Bruce's ten-year-old son, becomes Dick's Robin, and the new Dynamic Duo takes the rooftops of Gotham
