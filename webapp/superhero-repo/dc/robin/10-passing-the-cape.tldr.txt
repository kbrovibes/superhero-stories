• When Dick grows out of the Robin costume, the little red and yellow tunic with the big R never stays on a shelf for long
• Over the years, Dick becomes the big brother to a whole family of Robins
• Jason Todd is first — Bruce finds him trying to steal the tires off the Batmobile in Crime Alley; Dick teaches him to turn anger into focus
• Tim Drake is second — the smartest, who figured out Batman's secret identity at age twelve from old newspaper photos by recognizing Dick's triple somersault
• Dick takes Tim onto Blüdhaven rooftops on weekends and teaches him how to read a city
• Damian Wayne is last — Bruce's son, fierce, scowling, raised by the League of Assassins, sword on his back
• Dick spars with Damian at sunrise on the Wayne Manor lawn with practice escrima sticks; Damian slowly opens up
• Asked years later what his most important accomplishment was, Dick says: "I taught my little brothers how to fly"
