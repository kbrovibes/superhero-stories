• By age fifteen, Dick has outgrown being just Batman's sidekick — he needs his own team
• Four other young heroes need a leader: Cyborg (half-man half-machine), Starfire (Tamaranean princess), Raven (sorcerer's daughter), and Beast Boy (green shapeshifter)
• Cyborg flies the team's T-jet to Gotham to ask Dick to lead them — Bruce surprises everyone by saying yes
• The team sets up base in T-Tower, a T-shaped building on a small island in Jump City Bay
• Robin's job as leader is teaching them to work together — using each member's power at exactly the right moment
• Their first major enemy is Slade, a calm masked villain who tries to recruit Robin as his apprentice
• Robin refuses Slade and the Teen Titans defeat him by combining all their powers
• The Titans become famous and hand out T-communicators to young heroes worldwide — Dick has grown from sidekick into a true leader
