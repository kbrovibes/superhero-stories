Down in the Batcave, Batman has a giant computer and a sleek Batmobile and a whole HALLWAY full of dark-colored suits in glass cases. But did you know — Robin has his OWN corner too? It's a workbench just to the left of the big computer screen. And on that workbench, Robin keeps his special gadgets.

Are you ready to see them? Let's go!

GADGET NUMBER ONE: THE BIRD-ARANGS!

Bird-arangs are like Batman's Batarangs — those flat throwing tools that fly through the air and come back to your hand — but Robin's are SMALLER. And LIGHTER. And shaped like teeny tiny birds in flight! And painted bright yellow and red, the same colors as Robin's costume.

Robin's favorite trick? He throws TWO at the SAME TIME. One in each hand. WHOOSH WHOOSH! Like a circus juggler! Left-right, left-right! He can even bank them off walls and ceilings — so they come around BEHIND the bad guys! BONK! Down they go!

One time, in a creepy warehouse down by the Gotham docks, Robin bounced a Bird-arang off a swinging meat hook. The Bird-arang spun around like a boomerang, looped behind a big henchman, and clonked him gently on the back of his helmet. The henchman went "OOF!" and slumped down. He never even saw what hit him!

GADGET NUMBER TWO: THE R-BELT!

The R-belt is Robin's yellow utility belt with a big black R for a buckle. And every pouch on it has something AWESOME inside!

POUCH ONE: A grappling-hook wrist launcher! FWOOSH! Shoots a hook up onto any rooftop!

POUCH TWO: Smoke pellets, the size of marbles, in different colors! PFFFFFT! Suddenly the whole room is full of pink smoke!

POUCH THREE: Tiny lockpicks for opening locked doors! Click. Click.

POUCH FOUR: A magnifying glass for finding clues! "Holy fingerprints, Batman!"

POUCH FIVE: Yellow flares! Strong tape — Alfred calls tape "the universal tool, sir!" — climbing rope, and so on, and so on, and so on.

And there's one MORE pouch. A secret pouch, hidden on the inside of the belt where no rain or river water can get in. Want to know what's in there?

A little folded photograph. Of Mary and John Grayson, the Flying Graysons, smiling in their bright costumes. Robin always carries them with him. Always.

GADGET NUMBER THREE — and this is the BEST one. Are you ready?

Robin's best gadget is HIMSELF.

It's true! Because of all those years on the trapeze, Robin can flip and twist and tumble through the air like nobody else. He can leap. He can catch a tiny ledge with just two fingers. He can somersault around a moving fan blade without slowing down at all!

One time, Batman watched Robin practice from way up on the catwalk. And Batman — who almost never says anything — said this:

"He doesn't fight gravity. He has a conversation WITH it."

Alfred liked that line so much, he wrote it on an index card and stuck it to the fridge in the kitchen! And it's still there!

The first time Robin used everything at once was on the Sprang Bridge in Gotham. Bad guys had taken a school bus full of hostages! Robin fired his grappling hook UP — FWOOSH! He swung across a HUNDRED-foot gap! He flipped twice in midair, threw two Bird-arangs sideways to disarm two henchmen, dropped a SMOKE PELLET for cover, and landed in front of the frightened bus driver with a big smile.

"Holy cliffhanger, Batman — we got him!" Robin shouted.

The bus driver applauded! Up on the suspension cable, even Batman gave a tiny little nod. (For Batman, that's like a STANDING OVATION.)

Because the Boy Wonder is a whole one-bird circus. And Gotham City is his big top.
