• Dick Grayson is the youngest of the Flying Graysons, a famous trapeze family that travels with Haly's Circus
• Dick's parents, John and Mary Grayson, are killed during a performance in Gotham City when a mobster named Tony Zucco sabotages their trapeze ropes
• Bruce Wayne is in the audience that night — he recognizes Dick's grief because he lost his own parents in Crime Alley
• Bruce becomes Dick's legal guardian and brings him home to Wayne Manor, where Alfred Pennyworth takes care of him
• Dick is very quiet at first — he misses his mother, his father, and the smell of sawdust
• Bruce reveals the Batcave to Dick by leading him through the secret passage in the grandfather clock
• Dick realizes that Bruce has built a whole crime-fighting life to make sure no other child loses their parents
• In that moment, Dick finds his second home and his new purpose
