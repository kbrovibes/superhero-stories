Hey — have you ever wished you could turn into ANY animal you wanted? A cheetah, a whale, a tiny little mouse? Well, one boy actually CAN — and his name is Garfield Logan, and every single inch of him is GREEN!

Picture this: a research station deep in the jungles of Africa. His mom and dad, Mark and Marie Logan, are scientists out there studying animals. And little Garfield is five years old, running around with chimpanzees and river birds like it's completely normal. Because for him — it IS!

But then — UH OH — a sickness called Sakutia hits the camp. It's really, really dangerous. Garfield gets sick. Real sick. His parents have to act FAST.

They know that green monkeys — called Chlorocebus — can survive Sakutia just fine. So they cook up a special serum using green-monkey DNA and give it to Garfield.

And it WORKS. He survives!

But then — WHOOOOA — something incredible happens. His skin turns GREEN. His hair turns GREEN. His eyes turn GREEN. Everything GREEN!

And then — FWOMP — he transforms into a tiny green gecko. SCRABBLE SCRABBLE across the wall! Then a green eagle — WHOOSH through the air! Then a green elephant — THUMP THUMP THUMP on the ground!

He can turn into ANY animal. Any animal in the whole world!

His parents are amazed. Garfield just grins his enormous green grin and becomes a gecko one more time, just to be sure.

After his parents are gone, Garfield finds a new family — the Doom Patrol, led by Niles Caulder, the Chief, who uses a motorized wheelchair and has a mind that can out-think anything. Rita Farr — Elasti-Girl — can grow as tall as a building or shrink to the size of a teacup. Robotman is a human brain in an indestructible metal body. And Garfield is the youngest of them all, always cracking jokes right in the middle of a mission — HAHA! — because laughter keeps the team going through the hard parts.

Then one day Garfield sets out for Jump City, and there he meets FOUR incredible new friends. Robin, the fearless leader who trained in Gotham. Starfire, who shoots golden starbolts from her hands. Raven, who can move through shadows and sense every emotion. Cyborg — half robot, ALL awesome, with the biggest laugh in the Tower.

Together? They are the TEEN TITANS!

And Garfield Logan — green from head to toe, grinning from ear to ear — is exactly where he was always meant to be.
