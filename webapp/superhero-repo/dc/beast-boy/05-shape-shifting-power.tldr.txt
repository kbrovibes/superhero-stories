• Beast Boy transforms completely — taking on the full biology, senses, and reflexes of any animal he becomes
• Falcon eyes see a coin from 500 feet; bloodhound nose tracks scent across rooftops; whale sonar maps the dark ocean
• His library covers living animals, extinct dinosaurs, and the mantis shrimp whose punch creates a shockwave
• Every form comes out vivid green — teammates spot him from a city block in any shape
• He tends toward dinosaurs, but has learned when small and fast beats large and obvious
• Limits: many forms in a row leaves him tired; he can't split into multiple animals at once; only creatures he's studied
• The power comes from the Sakutia serum — he didn't choose it, but chooses every day how to use it
• What he chooses: to be useful, generous, and about as subtle as a green elephant in a hallway
