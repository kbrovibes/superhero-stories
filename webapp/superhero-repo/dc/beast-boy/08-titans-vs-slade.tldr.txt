• Slade Wilson is the Titans' most dangerous enemy — patient, half-orange half-black mask, always planning around the team rather than through them
• Slade uses agents and traps to learn each Titan's weaknesses over time, never moving directly
• Beast Boy's role is surveillance — hours as a green pigeon on rooftops and a green spider mapping enemy facilities
• He tracks a supply boat across Jump City Bay as a sea lion until Raven and Starfire intercept it from the air
• At the industrial port the Titans fight as one — covering angles without being told, no gaps
• Robin takes Slade; Starfire and Raven work the air; Cyborg holds the entrance; Beast Boy drives agents as a wolf and blocks the boat as an orca
• Slade escapes but the network is shut down — the Titans call it a draw, the teamwork was a win
