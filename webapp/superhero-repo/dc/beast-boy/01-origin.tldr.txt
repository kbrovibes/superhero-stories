• Garfield Logan grows up at his parents' jungle research station in the Congo, surrounded by animals and wildlife
• At age five, he contracts Sakutia — a rare disease his parents cure using an experimental green-monkey serum
• The serum saves his life but turns his skin, hair, and eyes permanently green and gives him the power to transform into any animal
• After losing his parents, Garfield is taken in by the Doom Patrol — a team led by Niles Caulder, the Chief
• He trains alongside Elasti-Girl (Rita Farr) and Robotman, becoming the youngest, jokiest member of the squad
• Garfield leaves the Doom Patrol and travels to Jump City, where he meets Robin, Starfire, Raven, and Cyborg
• Together they form the Teen Titans, a team of teenage heroes protecting the city
• Garfield takes the name Beast Boy and finds the family — and the place — he has always been searching for
