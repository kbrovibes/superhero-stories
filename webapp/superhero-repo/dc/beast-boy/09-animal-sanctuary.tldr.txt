• Garfield volunteers at the Redwood Animal Sanctuary on the outskirts of Jump City every Thursday and Saturday
• Director Dr. Priya Nair was skeptical at first; he shows up reliably and knows biology better than most staff
• The green-monkey serum gives him a deeper attunement — animals trust him in ways they don't trust others
• He never transforms at the sanctuary — he comes as a person, and the animals accept him that way
• A California condor named Rosalind watches him for three weeks before allowing him near, then treats him with warm indifference
• When Dr. Nair asks why he keeps coming, Garfield says he likes who he is here — just a person being trusted
• It connects him to his Congo childhood, where animals trusted him long before any serum
• He ends the afternoon cleaning the hawk enclosure — some jobs need doing no matter who you are
