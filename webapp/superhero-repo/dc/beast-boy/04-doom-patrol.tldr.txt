• After losing his parents, Garfield joins the Doom Patrol at Caulder Manor, led by Niles Caulder — the Chief
• Niles Caulder uses a motorized wheelchair and believes being different is not the same as being broken
• Rita Farr — Elasti-Girl — can stretch and shrink; she treats Garfield like a little brother and insists on proper meals
• Cliff Steele — Robotman — human brain in a metal body; teaches Garfield to spar by surviving every form he tries
• The Doom Patrol faces General Immortus and the Brain — patient enemies who treat heroism as a solvable problem
• Garfield is the youngest and loudest, cracking jokes to keep things light on hard missions
• Rita keeps him company on the hard nights — a kindness he never forgets
• This is where Garfield becomes Beast Boy and learns a strange family is still a family
