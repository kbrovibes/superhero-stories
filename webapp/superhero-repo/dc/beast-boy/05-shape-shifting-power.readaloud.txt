Okay, quick question: what's the most powerful superpower you can think of?

Super strength? Flight? Laser eyes? All great. But here's one that might be even better:

The ability to turn into ANY animal. ANY animal. Ever. In the entire history of Earth.

That's Beast Boy's power, and it is absolutely incredible once you understand how it REALLY works.

When Garfield becomes a falcon — FWOMP — he doesn't just look like a falcon. His eyes become falcon eyes, which can spot a tiny COIN on the pavement from FIVE HUNDRED FEET in the air! His whole body IS the falcon.

When he becomes a bloodhound — FWOMP — he can smell things from a mile away. He once tracked Raven across three rooftops just by scent. (Raven was NOT pleased about this.)

When he becomes a whale — FWOMP — he navigates by sonar. Clicks and echoes bouncing through the water, painting a picture of everything around him. A living, swimming, green GPS.

He can become DINOSAURS. T-rex? RAAAAR! Pterodactyl? SWOOOOOP! They've been extinct for millions of years but Garfield has studied them, and studying is all he needs!

He's also figured out a secret weapon: the MANTIS SHRIMP. This is a real creature, the size of your finger, that punches so fast it creates an actual shockwave. Pop! BOOM! It can stun creatures WAY bigger than itself.

Now here's the really interesting part. Every single form he takes? GREEN. Vivid, bright, unmistakable green. A green arctic fox. A green T-rex. A green whale.

He tried to be a white arctic fox once. Got a green one instead. Which was, honestly, fine — really easy to find in a snowfield.

The color is just… him. It's Garfield. You can spot him from a whole city block away no matter what shape he's in.

Does the power have limits? Sure! Lots of transformations in a row leaves him tired and hungry. He can only be one animal at a time. And he can only become animals he's actually studied.

But you know what? Garfield has been studying animals since he was three years old. Growing up in Africa with scientist parents will do that. His library is enormous.

The power came from the green-monkey serum his parents made to save his life. He didn't pick it. But every day — every single day — he chooses what to do with it.

And what he chooses is: be helpful. Be loud. And if there's any choice at all, be a dinosaur.

RAAAAR.
