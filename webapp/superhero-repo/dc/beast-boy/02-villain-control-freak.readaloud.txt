Okay, imagine this: you're at the mall, minding your own business, eating a soft pretzel — and suddenly EVERY. SINGLE. TV. SCREEN. switches on at the same time!

That's what happened when Control Freak showed up at Jump City Mall.

Control Freak is a big guy in a jumpsuit covered in buttons and switches, and he carries the world's most dangerous TV remote. Not dangerous like a paper cut. Dangerous like PULLING MONSTERS OUT OF MOVIES. That's right — he points his remote at a screen, pushes a button, and ZZZZT — out walks a giant robot from a space movie, crashing right through a display window!

BOOM! Car alarms everywhere!

Robin shouts, "Titans, GO!" Cyborg CHARGES. Starfire fires golden starbolts — FWOOM! Raven wraps the robot in swirling black energy. And Beast Boy — FWOMP — turns into a massive green RHINOCEROS and hits it right in the knees!

CRASH! Robot down!

But Control Freak is already pointing at another screen — a fantasy movie in the food court — and out come three clanking dragon-knights with swords and armor! Beast Boy goes WHOOSH as a green hawk, circles up HIGH, and then — WHAM — drops straight down as a green gorilla right on top of the first knight. DOWN goes knight number one! DOWN goes knight number two!

The mall is total chaos. Control Freak runs into the electronics department and turns on a WHOLE WALL of screens! A pixelated army pours out! The Titans are surrounded!

But Beast Boy grins.

He knows something. Every remote needs batteries.

SHRINK. He becomes a teeny tiny green mouse and darts across the floor — SKITTER SKITTER SKITTER — past everyone's feet, unseen. He scrambles UP Control Freak's leg, UP his jumpsuit, and — CHOMP — bites the battery compartment right off the remote!

ZAP! SPARK! FLICKER!

The whole screen-army freezes. The monsters go still. One very confused dragon-knight stops mid-swing and just… stands there.

"HEY!" yells Control Freak. He looks at his useless remote. He looks at the standing army of frozen movie characters. He looks at Beast Boy, who has turned back into himself and is standing on a display counter, swinging his legs.

BOOM — Cyborg's sonic cannon finishes the job. Control Freak hits the floor.

The police arrive to find him sitting cross-legged in a pile of batteries, surrounded by confused movie monsters slowly fading back into their screens.

Beast Boy finds a pretzel on a food court table, dusts it off, and takes a very satisfied bite.

"Not bad," he says, "for a kid who turns into animals."
