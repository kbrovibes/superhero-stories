• Titans Tower is a T-shaped building on a rocky island in Jump City Bay — headquarters, training ground, and home
• Beast Boy's room is on the fourth floor: posters, snack wrappers, and his mother's field guides organized by continent on a high shelf
• The common room is where the team eats, watches films, and argues about breakfast (Cyborg: meat; Garfield: cereal)
• The training room is where Robin runs drills on reaction time and leverage — not just brute force
• Beast Boy and Cyborg have a long-running obstacle course competition; the score is tied and neither admits it
• Cyborg maintains the computer system; Garfield checks threats every morning, borrowed habit from Robin
• The kitchen has enough cereal to survive a siege — unofficial but essential
• The Tower's real value is that five people come home to it — and for Garfield, that makes it the best place in the world
