• J'onn J'onzz — Martian Manhunter — a Manhunter (peacekeeper) on Mars, devoted husband to M'yri'ah and father to K'hym
• Martian civilization: telepathic harmony, architecture sung from living rock; the "Great Song" connected all minds
• H'ronmeer's Curse — a telepathic plague — destroys the Martian people; J'onn is the last survivor
• Dr. Saul Erdel's transmatter beam pulls J'onn across the void to Earth; the shock is fatal to Erdel
• J'onn arrives alone on a strange, loud, chaotic world — a stranger in a strange land
• Adopts human alias: John Jones, detective in Middletown
• Powers: shape-shifting, super-strength, vast telepathic abilities, intangibility — weakness: fire
• Discovers humanity's unique resilience and "messy, beautiful capacity for hope"
• Founding pillar of the Justice League — the team's "heart"; watches from the Watchtower
• Small joy: Chocos (chocolate-sandwich cookies) — a grounding force bridging alien heritage and new home
