Inside the mind of J'onn J'onzz there is a library unlike any other in the entire universe. He calls it the Martian Chronicles.

It holds every story, every poem, every piece of art, every scientific discovery, and every memory of a whole world that no longer exists on any map — the world of Mars, Ma'aleca'andra, where billions of remarkable beings once lived and laughed and built cities by singing them into existence!

When J'onn needs to visit, he closes his eyes. And there it is — the shining city of Z'onn Z'orr, as vivid and real as the day he last walked its streets. He can hear the voices of friends who are gone. He can feel, for a moment, the warmth of the Great Song wrapping around him like an embrace. He can read the philosophical writings of Martian sages who lived thousands of years ago!

But this is not just J'onn's private refuge. He has made it into a GIFT for Earth.

He translates what he knows — the wisdom of a civilization that thrived for millions of years — into books, into lectures, into quiet telepathic glimpses that he shares with those he trusts. The lessons of Mars: that empathy matters. That honesty makes communities stronger. That every living thing is connected to every other!

And do you know what happens every time a human chooses kindness over cruelty? Or wisdom over pride? Or friendship over fear?

A small, bright piece of Mars lives on.

J'onn J'onzz is the last son of Mars and the keeper of its flame. He tends those memories the way he tends his little garden in the Watchtower — carefully, lovingly, understanding exactly how rare and precious they are.

A civilization never truly ends as long as one person remembers it with love — and carries it forward into the world.
