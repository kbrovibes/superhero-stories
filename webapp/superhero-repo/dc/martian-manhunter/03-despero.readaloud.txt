There are villains who are simply powerful, and then there is Despero. He rules the planet Kalanor with iron force and a mind that can reach out and take hold of your thoughts before you even realize what is happening. At the center of his forehead burns a third eye — a glowing orb of psionic energy that can shatter a mind or reshape reality into a prison of pure fear.

Despero is not content with one world. He wants them all. He arrives at a new planet not with armies, but with his mind — reaching out to enslave the population before a single soldier sets foot on the ground. And when the Justice League stands in his way, he considers them the most worthy opponents he has ever found.

For J'onn J'onzz, fighting Despero is both a physical battle and a mental one at the same time. Despero uses his Mayavic energy to trap heroes inside their worst fears, making victory feel impossible. J'onn fights back as a psychic anchor — a steady presence in his teammates' minds, holding them to reality while the tyrant tries to sweep them away.

Despero has even harnessed the Flame of Py'tar, an ancient power source from deep within Kalanor, making him stronger than ever. He retreats. He grows. He returns.

And do you know what J'onn understands about Despero that others miss? That Despero's greatest fear is being defeated by the very thing he most despises: compassion and friendship working together.

From that day on, the Martian Manhunter proved that the strongest shield against a tyrant's mind is a heart that refuses to stand alone.
