There are villains who are strong, and then there is Despero. Right there in the middle of his forehead burns a THIRD EYE — a glowing psionic orb of crackling mental energy that can reach into your mind and take control of your thoughts before you even realize what is happening! ZAP!

Despero rules the planet Kalanor with absolute force. He does not conquer worlds with armies — he conquers them with his MIND. He reaches out with his third eye and simply takes over an entire population's thoughts before a single battle is fought! And when he has finished with one world, he looks for more!

He also has something called the Flame of Py'tar — an ancient power source from deep within Kalanor — that makes him even stronger. He grows. He returns. He always comes back more powerful than before!

So when Despero attacks, the Justice League faces both a physical battle AND a mental war at the exact same time! CRASH! Despero throws someone across the room physically. ZAP! He traps someone else inside a nightmare made of their own worst fears!

That is where J'onn J'onzz becomes absolutely essential. He creates a telepathic shield around all his teammates — a steady, calm, protective mental anchor that holds them connected to reality while Despero tries to sweep them away!

J'onn and Despero clash on two levels at once: fist against fist in the physical world, and mind against mind in the invisible battle of thoughts and will!

And here is what J'onn understands about Despero that nobody else does: Despero's deepest fear is being defeated by the very thing he most despises — compassion and friendship working together!

A tyrant's greatest enemy is a team of heroes who refuse to abandon each other. Always.
