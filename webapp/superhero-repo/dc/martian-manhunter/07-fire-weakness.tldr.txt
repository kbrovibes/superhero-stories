• Fire is J'onn J'onzz's greatest vulnerability — psychological as much as physical
• Roots in H'ronmeer's Curse: a psychic fire that destroyed the Martian civilization; J'onn watched his race consumed
• Fire became the ultimate symbol of destruction — wired into J'onn's subconscious as primal terror
• In battle: even a small flame causes J'onn's concentration to waver; shape-shifting and psychic links destabilize
• Intense heat makes his molecular structure unstable — he can collapse in pain
• Enemies exploit this weakness; J'onn has spent years training his willpower to push through the fear
• Works with Justice League teammates to develop strategies and equipment to protect against flame
• Every time he faces fire and does not back down, it is a victory over his deepest trauma
• The "Burning Martian" known as Fernus: a suppressed, ancient fire-breathing aspect of J'onn's biology that can emerge
• Weakness is the crack in the armor that makes him human — the most relatable aspect of his immense power
