• Mars — Ma'aleca'andra to its people — a world where thought and reality blurred beautifully
• Martian architecture was not built but sung into existence through psionic resonance
• The "Great Song": collective telepathic consciousness binding every Martian soul — no secrets, total transparency
• Education in the Hall of Memories: young Martians telepathically experienced the lives of ancestors
• Martian science: a blend of physics and metaphysics; mastery of fundamental cosmic energies
• H'ronmeer's Curse exploited the Great Song — telepathic openness became a bridge for the plague
• J'onn is the last survivor — a living archive of lost art, forgotten philosophy, billions of psychic signatures
• J'onn views humanity with mixture of hope and sorrow — sees the same potential for greatness and tragedy as his own people
• Retreats to a mental construct of Mars — walks the shimmering streets of his home to maintain his identity
• Duty: ensure Earth never suffers the same fate as Mars; honor the dead by protecting the living
