• Despero — tyrant of the planet Kalanor — immense physical stature and terrifying psychic power
• Third eye: a glowing psionic orb allowing mind domination, illusions, and devastating mental bolts
• Rose to power on Kalanor through ruthless ambition and awakened latent psychic abilities
• Conquers worlds through mental enslavement — populations broken before a fight begins
• "Mayavic" energy: reshapes reality to trap enemies inside their greatest fears
• Flame of Py'tar: ancient Kalanor energy source that granted him near-god-level abilities
• J'onn acts as psychic anchor for the Justice League, shielding his teammates from Despero's mind attacks
• Battles fought on multiple planes simultaneously — physical combat and a mental war at the same time
• Despero always returns, each time stronger — feeds on the psychological torment of his enemies
• Represents the dark side of telepathic power: brilliance without morality, strength without mercy
