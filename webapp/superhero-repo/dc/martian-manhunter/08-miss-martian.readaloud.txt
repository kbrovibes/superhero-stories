J'onn J'onzz had believed for a long time that he was the only Martian left. And then, in the quiet aftermath of a cosmic battle, he met M'gann M'orzz.

She was young, bright-eyed, and filled with the kind of hope that can only come from someone who truly wants to do good. She had taught herself about Earth by watching old human television shows, which meant she arrived believing life was mostly cheerful and solved by the end of every episode. She called herself Miss Martian. And she was a White Martian — a member of the sub-species that had long been at odds with J'onn's own Green Martian people.

M'gann was afraid of what that meant. She hid her true appearance, disguising herself as a Green Martian, because she feared that if anyone knew the truth, they would turn her away. That secret weighed on her every day.

J'onn understood that kind of loneliness. He became her mentor — teaching her to control her telepathic powers, to navigate the complicated feelings of the humans around her, and to be honest about who she really was. He told her, gently and firmly, that what matters is not where you come from. It is the choices you make and the love you give.

And do you know what M'gann gave back to J'onn? She reminded him what it felt like to have another Martian in the world. Someone to share memories with. Someone who understood the Great Song.

From that day on, J'onn and M'gann proved together that even the most distant past does not have to write the future.
