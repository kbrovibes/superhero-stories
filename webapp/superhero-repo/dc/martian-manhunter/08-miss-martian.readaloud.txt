For a long time, J'onn J'onzz believed he was the last Martian in the universe. The very last one. Completely alone.

And then, in the quiet aftermath of a cosmic battle, he met M'gann M'orzz!

She was young and bright-eyed and filled with the kind of big-hearted hope that only someone who truly wants to do good can carry. She had taught herself about Earth entirely by watching old human television shows — which meant she arrived believing that all problems get solved happily by the end of every episode! She called herself Miss Martian. And she was a White Martian — a member of a Martian sub-species that had historically been in conflict with J'onn's own Green Martian people.

M'gann was frightened of what that meant. So she hid her true appearance, disguising herself as a Green Martian, because she feared that if anyone knew who she really was, they would turn her away. That secret weighed on her like a stone every single day.

J'onn understood that kind of loneliness better than anyone in the universe could.

He became her mentor — teaching her to control her telepathic powers, helping her navigate the complicated feelings of the humans around her, and gently but firmly encouraging her to be honest about who she really was. "What matters," he told her, "is not where you come from. It is the choices you make and the love you give."

And do you know what M'gann gave back to J'onn? She reminded him what it felt like to have another Martian in the world! Someone who understood the Great Song. Someone to share memories with. Someone who could hear in his silences everything he could not put into words.

Together, J'onn and Miss Martian proved: even the most distant past does not have to write the future.
