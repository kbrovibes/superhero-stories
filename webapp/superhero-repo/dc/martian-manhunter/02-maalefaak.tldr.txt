• Lived in agonizing silence while all other Martians shared thoughts like music
• Delved into forbidden Martian science to create revenge: H'ronmeer's Curse
• The plague exploited the very psychic links binding Martians together — turned their greatest strength into a lethal weapon
• Ma'alefa'ak's goal: make everyone as silent as he was; achieved a twisted "equality"
• Mars burned; J'onn watched his civilization die in psychic fire
• Ma'alefa'ak eventually follows J'onn to Earth — sees it as a new canvas for his hatred
• Uses J'onn's own form and memories to deceive and sow discord
• J'onn fights to protect his new home from the shadows of his own past
• Ma'alefa'ak: tragic mirror of J'onn — the vision of what grief becomes when it turns to hatred
