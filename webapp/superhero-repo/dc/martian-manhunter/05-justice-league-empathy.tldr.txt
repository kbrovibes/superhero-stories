• J'onn J'onzz is the "heart" of the Justice League — founding member through its greatest triumphs and darkest hours
• Maintains a telepathic web in battle: team communicates at the speed of thought, shares strategies instantly
• Senses when teammates are in distress; anticipates their moves; keeps everyone working toward the same goal
• Provides emotional support that powerful heroes are too proud to ask for — understands every burden they carry
• Acts as mediator in team disagreements; a voice of caution about unintended consequences
• Spends off-duty time among humans to keep the League's actions grounded in human dignity
• Ritual of sharing chocolate-sandwich cookies (Chocos) — a gesture of friendship bridging his alien nature
• Quiet sentinel of the Watchtower: does not seek the spotlight, works from the shadows
• Role reflects his personal journey — from lonely survivor to the vital center of a global community
