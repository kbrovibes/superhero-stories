Quick — name the most powerful member of the Justice League! Superman? Wonder Woman? Batman? What if the answer was someone you might not have guessed?

What if it was the quiet green figure standing in the background — the one who makes sure every single hero on the team never feels alone?

J'onn J'onzz is the heart of the Justice League. And when the team goes into battle, he does something nobody else can do: he weaves a telepathic web between all his teammates at once! SHIMMER! Suddenly Superman and Wonder Woman and the Flash and Batman and everyone else can share thoughts at the speed of light — no radios needed, no delays, no confusion! J'onn feels when anyone is in danger. He anticipates the next move before it is even spoken. He makes sure nobody is ever left alone in the dark!

But here is the part that matters even more: J'onn knows what each of his teammates carries in their hearts. The grief they never mention. The fears they never say out loud. The incredible weight of being what they are. And he offers — quietly, without making a fuss — a steady presence, a listening ear, and a willingness to sit with a friend who needs company.

And then there is the most famous ritual of all: Chocos! Those chocolate-sandwich cookies that J'onn discovered the week he arrived on Earth and has loved every single day since! He shares them with his teammates as a way of saying: I am glad to be here. I am glad you are my family.

Here is a truly wonderful fact: J'onn became so devoted to Chocos that when someone once hid his supply, his shape-shifting went completely out of control from the distress! That is some SERIOUS cookie love!

The most powerful force in the Justice League? The invisible thread of empathy and trust that J'onn weaves between them all.
