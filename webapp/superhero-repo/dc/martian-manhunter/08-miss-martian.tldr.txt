• M'gann M'orzz — Miss Martian — a White Martian; White Martians were historically at odds with J'onn's Green Martians
• Arrived on Earth with hope and a mind shaped by old Earth TV sitcoms — naive but warm-hearted
• J'onn and M'gann: mentor-student relationship; also like surrogate father and daughter
• J'onn teaches her to control her powers, navigate human emotion, and confront the darkness of her heritage
• M'gann hides her White Martian identity — disguises herself as a Green Martian out of fear of rejection
• Learns from J'onn's support and her teammates' friendship to reveal her true form
• Lesson: a person is defined by their choices and the love they show, not their species
• Miss Martian serves as psychic coordinator for her young team — empathy is her greatest strength
• M'gann's presence forces J'onn to see the universe with fresh eyes; restores a sense of connection lost with Mars
• Together they are the legacy of a lost world — proof that the future is not written by the past
