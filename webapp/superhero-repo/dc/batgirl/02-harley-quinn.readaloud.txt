HONK! SQUIRT! POP!

Something very silly was happening at the Gotham City Carnival — and it was not silly in a fun way. Whoopee cushions had appeared on every single seat. The cotton candy machines were blowing soap bubbles instead of making sugar floss. And somewhere in the crowd, someone was cackling at the top of their lungs.

Batgirl spotted her immediately. Harley Quinn — in her red and black outfit, doing a backflip over a popcorn cart like it was the easiest thing in the world!

FLIP! SPIN! CARTWHEEL! Harley is one of the most acrobatic people you will ever see. She launched herself from a trapeze, slid headfirst down a helter-skelter, and bounced off a giant inflatable clown — BOING! — all while laughing that huge echoing laugh of hers.

But Batgirl is no ordinary chaser! She glided on her purple cape to close the gap, leaping from rooftop to rooftop above the carnival stalls. Left! Right! Over! Under! The crowd gasped as two incredible athletes turned the entire carnival into their own obstacle course.

Then the pranks got bigger. Harley's oversized props started wobbling — and someone could get hurt! That was the moment Batgirl reached into her utility belt. She had been waiting. She had been watching.

At exactly the right moment — right in the middle of Harley's biggest somersault — Batgirl let fly with her bolas! SNAP! The lasso wrapped around Harley's ankles, and down she tumbled, giggling even as she landed.

"Jokes are wonderful," Batgirl told her firmly, "but never when people can get hurt."

Harley thought about that. And then the two of them cleaned up the bubble-filled cotton candy machines together and put the Gotham City Carnival back to rights.
