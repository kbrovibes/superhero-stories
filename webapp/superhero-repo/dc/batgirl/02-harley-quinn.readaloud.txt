One afternoon at the Gotham City Carnival, something very strange started happening. Whoopee cushions appeared under seats. The cotton candy machines blew bubbles instead of sugar. Batgirl spotted the signs right away — Harley Quinn was in town.

Harley Quinn is one of the best gymnasts you will ever see. She flipped through the air like a circus performer, swinging from trapezes and sliding down the tallest slides, laughing the whole time. Batgirl chased her across rooftops and over benches, gliding on her cape to close the gap whenever Harley tried to cartwheel away.

And do you know what happened next? The pranks started getting bigger. Harley's oversized props were wobbling dangerously, and someone could have gotten hurt. Batgirl reached into her utility belt and pulled out her bolas. She waited. She watched. And at exactly the right moment — right in the middle of Harley's biggest flip — she threw.

The bolas wrapped safely around Harley's ankles, and the chase was over. Harley giggled even as she landed. Batgirl told her that jokes are wonderful things, but never when they put people in danger. Then the two of them worked together to clean up the bubble-filled cotton candy machines and put the Gotham City Carnival back to rights.

And that is how Batgirl showed that a calm mind and steady training will always win the day, even against the most acrobatic prankster in Gotham.
