Big news! Barbara Gordon is BACK on her feet — thanks to a super-smart neural implant — and she has decided it is time for a completely fresh start!

She packed her gadgets, grabbed her books, and moved to Burnside — the trendiest, most tech-savvy neighborhood in all of Gotham City. And she designed a brand new Batgirl suit: purple and yellow, bright and practical and absolutely cool-looking!

Can you imagine? A fresh neighborhood, a fresh suit, a fresh adventure!

But Burnside had a different kind of problem than the rest of Gotham. The villains here were not wearing capes and throwing things. They were hackers! Sneaky keyboard criminals draining money from local shops! Social-media gangs causing trouble online! Rogue coders crashing computer systems — BZZT! CRASH! — and making a real mess!

One evening, cyber-attacks hit Burnside businesses all at once. BAM BAM BAM! Money was being stolen from everywhere! Barbara fired up her custom-built smartphone — the one with seventeen layers of protective software — and tracked the signal all the way to a rooftop lounge where a gang of bored, wealthy hackers were sitting around bragging about their crimes!

WHOOSH! Batgirl crashed the party. She flipped their own technology against them — ZAP! — stopped every attack in seconds, logged the evidence, and made sure every single stolen dollar went back to its rightful owner. The GCPD showed up shortly after to handle the rest!

But do you know what Batgirl did next? Something even better than fighting. She started teaching local kids in Burnside how to code and how to stay safe online. Because the best kind of hero does not just stop trouble — she helps her whole community grow stronger!
