Something happened to Barbara Gordon that was very hard. The Joker came to her door one night, and he hurt her so badly she could not walk anymore. It was a sad, sad day for Gotham City.

For a while, Barbara sat in her wheelchair and felt like the world had gone quiet. The acrobatics, the rooftop chases, the Batarangs — it all felt very far away.

But then — can you guess what happened? Barbara had a BRILLIANT idea.

She realized that information is the most powerful weapon of all! She could not be the hands and feet of a mission anymore. But she could be the MIND. The eye in the sky. The invisible genius behind every victory!

She gave herself a brand new name: Oracle. And she got to work. TAP TAP TAP TAP TAP! She hacked into Gotham's police databases. She broke into satellite networks. She cracked encrypted criminal radio frequencies that bad guys thought were completely secret forever.

From a hidden clocktower in Gotham, Oracle became the voice in every hero's ear during their most dangerous moments. BATMAN heard her. NIGHTWING heard her. Even the entire JUSTICE LEAGUE heard her steady, calm voice guiding them through impossible situations!

One night, she talked Nightwing through a dangerous docks heist in total darkness — spotting exits, bypassing security grids, alerting police — all in seconds!

And here is the most wonderful part: her own father, Commissioner James Gordon, started finding perfectly detailed reports appearing on his desk every morning. Faster and smarter than anything his officers could produce. He had no idea his own daughter was sending them!

Barbara Gordon proved something incredible: a hero's strength is not about walking or running. It is about never, ever giving up.
