One day, every phone on Earth went silent. Every radio crackled into static. Every satellite blinked off like a light switch had been flipped. CLICK. Darkness. Silence. From one end of the world to the other!

The Justice League scrambled into action — but this was not a job for punching or flying. This was a job for the world's greatest detective-hacker. This was a job for Batgirl!

Superman and Wonder Woman zoomed out to protect confused cities. Batgirl stayed aboard the Justice League's Watchtower space station and got to work. TAP TAP TAP TAP TAP. She studied energy readings. She cross-referenced satellite data. She ran the numbers backwards and forwards until — PING! — she found it. The signal was coming from a hidden base on the ocean floor!

Then she did something incredible. She hacked straight through the base's security from outer space, downloaded the whole floor plan, and projected a holographic map for her teammates — all before they even got there! CLICK CLICK WHOOSH! The whole mission plan appeared in glowing detail.

The Justice League followed Batgirl's map through every corridor. The global communications blackout was stopped before a single TV show got interrupted!

On missions like this, Batgirl teams up with Cyborg and the Flash to form a tech-squad that can crack any digital puzzle. And after every mission — every single one — Batgirl holds a debriefing meeting. She talks about what was learned, what could be done better, and how to be more prepared next time.

Can you imagine that? Even the world's mightiest heroes have homework! And Batgirl is the best teacher any superhero team could ask for.
