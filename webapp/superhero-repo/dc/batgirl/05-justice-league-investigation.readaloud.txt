Sometimes the Justice League faces a mystery that all the strength in the world cannot solve. That is when they call on Batgirl.

One day, communications systems all over the world went dark — phones, radios, satellites, everything. Superman and Wonder Woman rushed out to protect cities from the confusion. Batgirl stayed at the Watchtower and went to work. She studied the strange energy readings, running the numbers over and over until she found something hidden: the signal was coming from a secret base on the ocean floor.

And do you know what happened next? Batgirl broke through the base's security without ever leaving her chair. She downloaded the floor plans, mapped every corridor in holographic detail, and guided her teammates straight to the source. The global blackout was stopped before it ever began.

On missions like these, Batgirl works closely with Cyborg and the Flash — a tech-squad that can crack any digital puzzle the world throws at them. She reminds the whole team that even the most powerful heroes need a good plan and accurate information to win.

After every mission, Batgirl holds a debriefing. She explains what they learned, what they could do better, and how to be more prepared next time.

From that day on, every member of the Justice League knew: Batgirl's sharp mind was just as powerful as any super-strength in the universe.
