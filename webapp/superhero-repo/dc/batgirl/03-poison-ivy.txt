The Gotham City Botanical Gardens suddenly transformed into a giant, tangled maze of vines and oversized flowers. Poison Ivy had decided to turn the public park into her own private jungle, blocking the pathways with thorny bushes and sneezing powder lilies. Batgirl was called in to solve the garden puzzles and ensure that the plants didn't take over the entire city block.

Poison Ivy believes that plants are more important than anything else, often leading her to create plant-based mischief. She isn't trying to hurt people, but her "experiments" can cause quite a mess and make it impossible for people to enjoy the gardens. Batgirl had to use her knowledge of biology to understand how to safely calm the plants without causing any damage to the rare species Ivy had cultivated.

The maze was a complex series of living walls that shifted whenever Batgirl turned a corner. She used her mini-drone to get an aerial view of the garden, mapping out the patterns of the vines. Batgirl realized that the movement of the plants was controlled by a series of musical chimes hidden in the trees. By solving the musical puzzle and playing the right notes on her gauntlet, she could guide the vines to retract safely.

Along the way, Batgirl encountered various "traps" like sticky sap pits and clouds of tickle-dust spores. She used her specialized filters and solvent sprays to navigate these obstacles with ease. Batgirl finally reached the center of the gardens, where she found Poison Ivy carefully tending to a giant, glowing orchid. Batgirl spoke to her calmly, explaining that the people of Gotham loved the gardens and wanted to help protect them too.

Ivy eventually agreed to return the gardens to their original state, provided the city promised to plant more trees in the downtown area. Batgirl worked with the park rangers to restore the paths and ensure the flowers were healthy. This mission showed that sometimes, the best way to solve a puzzle is with patience and a deep understanding of the world around you. Batgirl's intellect once again saved the day, proving she is a master of both technology and nature.
