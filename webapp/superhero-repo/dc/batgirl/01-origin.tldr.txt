• Barbara Gordon — daughter of Gotham City Police Commissioner James Gordon
• Trained in judo, karate, forensic science, criminology, and advanced computer hacking
• Self-imposed detective training shadowing her father's investigations from a distance
• Studied in the Gotham City Library to build expertise beyond physical skill
• Designed her own Batgirl suit and gadgets entirely herself
• Joined Batman and Robin as Batgirl, bringing both athletic prowess and sharp intellect
• Patrols rooftops and works behind the Bat-Computer to protect Gotham
• Proves that courage, dedication, and a sharp mind outweigh superpowers
