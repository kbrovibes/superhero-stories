Quick question: what do you get when you bring together a brilliant detective-hacker, the world's greatest vigilante, a fearless acrobat, and a whole crew of brave, loyal heroes who have all faced hard times and chosen to keep going anyway?

You get the Bat-family! And right at the very center of it all — holding everyone together like the strongest glue in Gotham — is Barbara Gordon!

Her closest friend in the whole family is Dick Grayson — the very first Robin, who grew up to become the hero called Nightwing. They trained together in the Batcave when they were young, and they have been each other's anchors ever since. On the darkest nights, they remind each other exactly why they keep going.

Barbara is also a big sister to the younger heroes. She mentored Tim Drake. She steadied Cassandra Cain. She cheered on Stephanie Brown. As Oracle, her calm voice was in ALL of their ears during every dangerous mission — steady and clear even when everything around them was going sideways.

And sometimes — when Bruce Wayne spent too long brooding alone in the shadows of the Batcave — Barbara was the one who knocked on his door and said: "You do not have to do this alone."

Because that is what a family does! They show up for each other.

The Bat-family has been through sadness together and through tremendous acts of courage together, and every hard moment made their bond stronger. On nights when their silhouettes appeared together against the Gotham moon, the whole city could feel it: comfort for the innocent, and a very clear message for anyone thinking of causing trouble.

Barbara Gordon is Batgirl. She is Oracle. But most of all, she is family. And that is her greatest power of all.
