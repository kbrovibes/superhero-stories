Shhh. Do you hear that? That low, creaking, rustling sound? That is the sound of the Gotham City Botanical Gardens… being taken over by VINES!

Poison Ivy had been busy. Overnight, she had turned the beautiful gardens into a twisting, turning maze of enormous green plants. Thick vines curled over the paths. Giant leaves blocked every doorway. Nobody could get in or out — and the public gardens were completely shut!

But Batgirl was on the case. WHOOSH! She arrived at the gate, took one look at the vine maze, and launched her mini-drone straight up into the sky. The tiny camera buzzed above the treetops — BZZZZ! — and sent a map of the whole maze right to Batgirl's cowl display. Clever!

Then she spotted something strange. Tucked among the tree branches were small musical chimes. Every time the wind blew — TING! TONG! TANG! — a vine moved. The whole maze was being controlled by music!

Batgirl listened carefully. She worked out the pattern. Then she played the right notes on her gauntlet — ding, ding, doooo — and the vines slowly, gracefully, pulled themselves back. Like magic!

She still had to dodge sticky sap pits — SPLURT! — and clouds of tickle-dust spores — ACHOO! — but with her filters and solvent sprays, she made it all the way to the center of the gardens.

There was Poison Ivy, tending a giant glowing orchid with one gentle hand. Ivy did not want to hurt anyone. She just wanted her rare plants to be safe!

Batgirl listened. Then she made a deal: restore the gardens, and the city would plant more trees in Gotham's neighborhoods. Ivy agreed. And the gardens bloomed more beautifully than ever before.
