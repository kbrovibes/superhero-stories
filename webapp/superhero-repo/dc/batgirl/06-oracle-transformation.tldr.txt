• Barbara realizes information is a more powerful weapon than any Batarang

• Oracle becomes the primary intelligence officer for the Bat-family and the Justice League
• Operates from a clocktower sanctuary monitoring every corner of Gotham
• Guides Nightwing through a docks heist — identifies exit route, bypasses security grid, alerts authorities in seconds
• Commissioner Gordon unknowingly benefits from Oracle's work every day
• Barbara's injury becomes the catalyst for a greater, more far-reaching heroism
