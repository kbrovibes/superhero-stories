• Poison Ivy transforms the Gotham City Botanical Gardens into a shifting vine maze
• Ivy's goal: protect her rare plants, not hurt people — but the chaos blocks the public from the gardens
• Batgirl deploys a mini-drone to map the vine maze from above
• Musical chimes hidden in the trees control the vines' movement
• Batgirl solves the musical puzzle and plays the right notes on her gauntlet to retract the vines
• Traps along the way: sticky sap pits and tickle-dust spore clouds — Batgirl uses filters and solvent sprays
• Batgirl finds Ivy tending a giant glowing orchid at the center of the gardens
• Ivy agrees to restore the gardens; Batgirl negotiates a city promise to plant more trees downtown
• Batgirl and park rangers restore pathways and keep Ivy's rare species healthy
