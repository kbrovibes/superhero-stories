Deep inside the Rock of Eternity, locked behind walls of ancient magical stone, live seven very dangerous prisoners. They are not people exactly — they are the Seven Deadly Sins of Man: Pride, Envy, Greed, Lust, Anger, Gluttony, and Sloth.

Each one is a living force that feeds on the worst parts of the human heart. Pride whispers to you that you are the most important person in the room. Envy tells you to hate everyone who has something you do not. Greed makes you want more and more and more until you cannot see anything else at all. They are ancient, powerful, and always, always looking for a way out.

The Wizard Shazam spent three thousand years making sure they stayed locked up. Now that job belongs to Billy.

Doctor Sivana and Black Adam have both tried to release the Sins to use their power for themselves. Every time they try, Billy must stop them. But here is the interesting part: you cannot defeat the Seven Deadly Sins with a punch. You fight Pride with humility. You fight Envy with gratitude. You fight Greed with generosity. Every battle is as much about what is inside you as what is outside.

And do you know what? Billy Batson has never lost that battle. Because a boy with Solomon's wisdom and a truly good heart is exactly the right guardian.

From that day on, Shazam kept his watch over the Rock of Eternity, proving that the brightest light will always hold back the deepest shadow.
