Deep inside the Rock of Eternity, locked away behind walls of ancient magical stone that have held firm for three thousand years, live seven very dangerous prisoners. Are you ready to meet them? Because they are very unpleasant!

PRIDE: whispers in your ear that you are the most important person in every room, and everyone should bow to you. ENVY: a twisted creature that makes you hate everyone who has something you want. GREED: an insatiable monster that makes you want more and more and MORE until you cannot think about anything else at all. ANGER: makes you rage at everything even when there is no reason to. GLUTTONY: makes you take and take and take without thinking of others. SLOTH: makes you give up on everything worth doing. And LUST: makes you want things in all the wrong ways!

Each one is a living force that feeds on the worst parts of the human heart — and they are always, always, always looking for a way OUT of those chains!

The Wizard Shazam spent three thousand years keeping them locked away. Now that job belongs to Billy Batson!

Doctor Sivana and Black Adam have both tried to release the Sins to use them as weapons. Every time they try, Billy has to stop them. But here is the truly interesting part: you CANNOT defeat the Seven Deadly Sins with a punch!

You fight Pride with humility. You fight Envy with gratitude. You fight Greed with generosity. You fight Anger with patience. You fight Sloth with effort. Each battle is as much about what is inside YOU as what is outside!

And Billy Batson — with the wisdom of Solomon living in his heart — has never lost that battle.

Because a boy with genuine goodness is exactly the right person to guard against the worst of human nature!
