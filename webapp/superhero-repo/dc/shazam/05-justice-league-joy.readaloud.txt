Picture the most powerful superhero team on Earth: Superman, who can move mountains! Wonder Woman, who can stop a train with her bare hands! The Flash, who runs faster than the speed of sound! Batman, the world's greatest detective! Aquaman, king of the seas!

Now picture them all sitting in a very serious meeting about a very serious situation — and one hand shoots up in the back.

"Are we going to celebrate when we win? Because we should definitely celebrate when we win!"

That is Shazam! The youngest member of the Justice League. The one with the biggest grin. The one who gives the highest high-fives. And — do not let anyone tell you otherwise — one of the most important heroes on the whole team!

Billy Batson brings something the others sometimes forget: pure, contagious, unstoppable JOY. He is first to cheer a victory and absolutely last to give up when things get difficult. He looks up at Superman and Wonder Woman with real admiration and never feels embarrassed to ask questions or admit when he does not know something yet. That kind of honesty makes every single teammate feel safer doing the same!

During tactical meetings, Billy comes up with ideas that catch even the most experienced heroes off guard — creative, sideways solutions that only someone who still sees the world with wonderfully fresh eyes could imagine!

And here is a very important fact: when the Justice League first began, Shazam was right there as a founding member! A boy with a pure heart, sitting at the same table as the greatest heroes the world had ever seen. Belonging absolutely.

Billy also stays last at every celebration. Not because he has to — because he genuinely loves cheering for his teammates! Because joy, freely given, is actually one of the most powerful forces in the universe.

The everyday happiness of ordinary people is exactly what all those powerful heroes are protecting. Shazam never lets them forget it!
