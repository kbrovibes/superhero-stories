• The Rock of Eternity — located at the center of time and space, hidden from the mortal world
• Source of all magic in the universe; home of the council of wizards for millennia
• Walls composed of ancient stone humming with the energy of the Seven Deadly Sins
• Seven Deadly Sins imprisoned inside: Pride, Envy, Greed, Lust, Anger, Gluttony, and Sloth
• Houses the Library of Eternity — all knowledge and secrets of the magical realms
• The Wizard Shazam's throne at its heart — now Billy's place of guidance and sanctuary
• Gateway to magical dimensions and homes of mystical beings — hub of cosmic activity
• Doctor Sivana and Black Adam constantly attempt to penetrate the Rock and claim its secrets
• Billy's role: guardian of the Rock, keeping the Seven Deadly Sins secure to protect the entire world
