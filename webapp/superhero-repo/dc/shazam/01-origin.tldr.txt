• Billy Batson — orphan living in Fawcett City — lonely but possessed of rare, unwavering goodness
• A mysterious figure leads him through an abandoned subway station into another realm
• Arrives at the Rock of Eternity and meets the ancient Wizard Shazam — last of a council of seven
• Wizard guarded the world from the Seven Deadly Sins of Man for three thousand years
• Billy shouts "SHAZAM!" — divine lightning strikes and transforms him into an adult superhero
• Powers: wisdom of Solomon, strength of Hercules, stamina of Atlas, power of Zeus, courage of Achilles, speed of Mercury
• Learns heroism requires purity of intent and compassion, not just strength
• Shares his powers with foster siblings — Freddy, Mary, Darla, Eugene, and Pedro — forming the Shazam Family
• Battles Doctor Sivana and Black Adam, who both seek to claim the Wizard's power
• Founding member of the Justice League — brings youthful optimism to the world's most powerful team
