Somewhere that is not quite anywhere — sitting at the very center of time and space, where past and present and future all exist together — there is a place that hums with thousands of years of ancient stored magic. The walls are made of living stone. Torches burn with light that has been burning since before recorded history. This is the Rock of Eternity!

And it is one of the most important — and most dangerous — places in the entire universe!

Deep in the Rock's ancient walls, seven very powerful beings are held prisoner. They have been there for thousands of years, locked away because the world becomes a much worse place whenever they escape. Their names are: Pride, Envy, Greed, Lust, Anger, Gluttony, and Sloth — the Seven Deadly Sins!

They are not ordinary prisoners. They are living forces that feed on the worst parts of the human heart! RATTLE RATTLE — that is the sound of their chains. The Wizard Shazam spent three thousand years sitting in that great cavern, keeping watch, making absolutely sure they never got out!

Now that job belongs to Billy Batson!

The Rock also holds the Library of Eternity — the most extraordinary library imaginable, containing every secret of every magical realm that ever existed! Billy visits often, learning from the Wizard's spirit and consulting the ancient texts. He has discovered that the Rock is also a gateway to magical dimensions and the homes of ancient deities. The universe goes much, much further than most people realize!

And here is the problem: Doctor Sivana wants inside. Black Adam wants inside. They would love nothing more than to crack open the Rock and unleash those seven dangerous prisoners on the world!

Billy Batson stands guard every single day. Being a champion is not just about the battles. It is about protecting the things that hold the whole world together!
