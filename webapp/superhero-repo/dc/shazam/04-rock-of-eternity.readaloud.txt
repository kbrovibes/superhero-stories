Imagine a place that sits at the very center of time and space — a place where the past, the present, and the future all exist together, and the walls themselves hum with ancient power. That place is called the Rock of Eternity, and for Billy Batson, it is the most important place in all the universe.

The Rock is enormous and cavernous and magical. Deep in its walls, seven very dangerous beings are held prisoner: Pride, Envy, Greed, Lust, Anger, Gluttony, and Sloth — the Seven Deadly Sins of Man. For thousands of years the Wizard Shazam sat on his great throne here, keeping watch and making sure those seven troublemakers never escaped.

Now that responsibility belongs to Billy.

The Rock also holds the Library of Eternity — a storehouse of all the knowledge and secrets of every magical realm that ever existed. Billy visits often, consulting the Wizard's spirit, learning from the wisdom kept inside those ancient walls. He has discovered that the Rock is also a gateway to other magical dimensions and the homes of ancient deities. The universe is far bigger than most people imagine.

And do you know who else wants to get inside? Doctor Sivana and Black Adam. They scheme and plot, searching for a way in. Billy must remain vigilant every single day.

From that day on, Billy Batson understood that being a champion is not just about fighting villains. It is about standing guard over the things that hold the whole world together.
