Doctor Thaddeus Sivana was once a brilliant scientist who spent his life searching for the same magic that Billy Batson had found. However, because his heart was not pure, the Wizard rejected him. This rejection turned Sivana's curiosity into a deep-seated rivalry. Instead of using his intelligence for good, he dedicated his vast resources to building advanced technological gadgets that could mimic or counter magical powers. Sivana believed that science should be the ultimate power in the world, and he viewed Shazam's magic as an obstacle to his own greatness.

Sivana created incredible inventions, such as high-tech exoskeletons and energy-blasting drones, to challenge the World's Mightiest Mortal. He often used his gadgets to create chaos in the city, forcing Shazam to intervene. These battles were more than just physical fights; they were a clash between two different ways of looking at the world. While Shazam relied on the ancient gifts of the Wizard, Sivana relied on his own complicated machines. Shazam had to be very clever to stay one step ahead of the doctor's unpredictable technology.

One of Sivana's most tricky inventions was a device that could temporarily block magical energy. He hoped that by neutralizing Shazam's powers, he could prove that his science was superior. During their encounters, Shazam had to use his wisdom and quick thinking to find weaknesses in Sivana's gadgets. He realized that while technology could be powerful, it lacked the heart and spirit that made a true hero. Shazam often found ways to turn Sivana's own machines against him, showing that magic and science can sometimes work in surprising ways.

Despite their rivalry, Shazam always tried to show Sivana that there was a better path. He believed that if the doctor used his brilliant mind to help people, he could be a great asset to the world. However, Sivana's pride often got in the way, leading him to create even more elaborate plans to defeat his magical foe. Shazam remained vigilant, knowing that as long as Sivana continued to choose science for selfish reasons, he would be there to protect the city from the doctor's mechanical mischief.
