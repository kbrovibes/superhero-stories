In the cold streets of Fawcett City, there lived a boy named Billy Batson. He was an orphan without a family, but he had something very rare inside him — a heart full of pure, genuine goodness. And one remarkable night, that goodness changed his life forever.

A mysterious figure led Billy deep into an abandoned subway station. A train came roaring out of nowhere and carried him not to another stop, but to another world entirely. He arrived at the Rock of Eternity, a timeless cavern crackling with ancient magic. There, an old, old wizard named Shazam had been waiting — waiting for three thousand years — for someone exactly like Billy.

"Say the word," the Wizard told him.

Billy took a breath and shouted: "SHAZAM!"

A bolt of divine lightning split the air. And do you know what happened next? Billy was transformed into a towering adult hero, filled with the wisdom of Solomon, the strength of Hercules, the stamina of Atlas, the power of Zeus, the courage of Achilles, and the speed of Mercury. In one bright flash, a boy became Earth's Mightiest Mortal.

Billy used his new gifts carefully and kindly. He even discovered that the power of Shazam was meant to be shared — so he gave some of it to his foster siblings: Freddy, Mary, Darla, Eugene, and Pedro. Together they became the Shazam Family, and for the first time, Billy had a home.

From that day on, Billy Batson proved that the most powerful weapon in the world is not lightning. It is a pure heart.
