SHAZAM!

That is the most exciting word in this whole story — and by the time you have heard it all, you will know exactly why! Are you ready? Because this is the story of how one ordinary boy became the most powerful hero on Earth — just by having the purest heart!

His name is Billy Batson, and he is an orphan living in Fawcett City. No parents, no permanent home — but something very rare beating in his chest: a heart made of solid goodness. Real, unwavering, always-choosing-to-be-kind goodness. The Wizard Shazam had been watching and waiting for someone like Billy for THREE THOUSAND YEARS!

One remarkable night, a mysterious figure led Billy through an abandoned subway station. A magical train arrived — WHOOOOSH! — and carried him not to another stop, but to another world entirely! He arrived at the Rock of Eternity, a vast ancient cavern crackling with thousands of years of stored magic!

The ancient Wizard Shazam sat on his throne and pointed at Billy. "Say the word," he said.

Billy took a deep breath and SHOUTED!

"SHAZAM!"

KA-CRACK! — a bolt of divine lightning split the air in two! Billy was transformed in a flash into a towering adult hero, shining with the power of six legendary figures: the wisdom of Solomon, the strength of Hercules, the stamina of Atlas, the power of Zeus, the courage of Achilles, and the speed of Mercury!

Fun fact: when Shazam was first created back in 1940, he was actually MORE popular than Superman — outselling him for years! Kids loved Billy because he was one of them — a regular child who could become something extraordinary!

And the best part? Billy shared his power with his foster family — Freddy, Mary, Darla, Eugene, and Pedro. And for the first time in his life, Billy Batson had a home.
