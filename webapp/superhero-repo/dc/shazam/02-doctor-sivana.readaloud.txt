Doctor Thaddeus Sivana is one of the most brilliantly clever villains in all of Fawcett City. He can build machines that would make any scientist's jaw drop. He has unlocked secrets that baffled scholars for centuries. He works from a high-tech laboratory packed with gadgets and ancient mystical artifacts side by side, because — here is his big idea — he believes science and magic are really just two sides of the same coin!

That is actually quite clever! But here is where Sivana went wrong: somewhere along the way, all that brilliant thinking curdled into bitter resentment.

Sivana found out that the ancient Wizard had chosen a child — an ordinary orphan boy named Billy Batson — to receive the greatest magical power in the universe. And Sivana was FURIOUS! He believed, with his whole cold calculating heart, that a genius scientist like himself was infinitely more worthy than any random child off the street!

So he plotted. And schemed. And built. And sent drones — BZZZZ CLICK ZAP! And hatched plan after plan from his laboratory to strip Billy of his powers and claim the Rock of Eternity for himself!

But Sivana could never understand one crucial thing: the Wizard had not chosen Billy for his muscles or his science scores. He chose him for his HEART. His pure, unwavering, always-kind heart.

Sivana's heart? Cold. Closed. He saw kindness as weakness and compassion as something foolish. He thought empathy was a flaw!

And that was always — every single time — exactly why he lost.

Lightning, laughter, and a genuinely good heart will always beat a cold brilliant mind that has forgotten how to care about anyone but itself!
