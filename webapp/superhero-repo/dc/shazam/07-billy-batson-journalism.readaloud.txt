Here is something wonderful you might not know about Billy Batson: when he is not flying through the sky shooting lightning and stopping villains, he is sitting in front of a microphone at WHIZ Radio in Fawcett City — telling the TRUTH to anyone who will listen!

Billy became a reporter because he truly believes that a good story is just as powerful as a good lightning bolt. Maybe more! He covers local news, human-interest stories, breaking emergencies — always trying to present the facts with honesty and care. He has a warm, genuine way of talking to people that comes from truly, deeply listening to them. His colleagues at WHIZ Radio liked him immediately!

But keeping his secret identity? That is always a little bit of an adventure!

When a crisis breaks out and Billy needs to transform — well, he cannot exactly shout "SHAZAM!" in the middle of the radio studio! So he uses the chaos of a breaking news moment as his cover. One second: mild-mannered, slightly bumbling Billy Batson, shuffling his notes and looking apologetically nervous. The next second: an extremely loud clap of thunder — KA-CRACK! — and Shazam is already on the scene before anyone noticed Billy left!

And here is what journalism has taught Billy about being a hero: asking the right questions, following the evidence carefully, and telling the clear truth can stop a villain just as effectively as Zeus's power! Because Billy's enemies — Doctor Sivana especially — use lies and deception. A hero who knows how to find the real truth underneath the false one has an incredibly powerful weapon!

A reporter and a champion have the same job: protect the truth, and use it to help the people who need it most.

WHIZ Radio: where a boy hero tells the truth — and saves the city during commercial breaks!
