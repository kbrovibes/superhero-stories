Most superheroes keep their powers to themselves. Not Billy Batson! Billy did something that had never been done before in the history of superheroes — he SHARED! Can you imagine?

Billy realized that the power of Shazam was never meant for just one person. So he gave parts of it to his foster siblings — Freddy, Mary, Darla, Eugene, and Pedro — and together they became the Shazam Family!

Here is something amazing: this was THE VERY FIRST superhero family ever created in comics history. Nobody had ever thought of it before! Shazam invented the superfamily!

Now let us meet these incredible people. Freddy Freeman has the sharpest mind and knows absolutely everything there is to know about superheroes — he is the team's tactical genius! Mary Bromfield is the oldest and wisest, the moral anchor who keeps everyone on the right path when things get chaotic. Darla Dudley is the youngest and most energetic, and her joy is so bright and real that it lights up every single room she runs into — and she runs VERY fast! Eugene Choi is a technological wizard who builds and invents things that leave everyone speechless. And Pedro Peña has strength that matches his quiet, rock-steady heart!

Together, when they all transform at once — SHAZAM! SHAZAM! SHAZAM! SHAZAM! SHAZAM! — something extraordinary happens. The divine power creates a bond between them. They can sense each other's feelings. They move in perfect harmony without a single word spoken. They fight not just for justice — they fight for each other!

And back at their foster home in Fawcett City, between the cosmic battles and the magical adventures, they are just a family. Eating together. Talking. Laughing. Looking out for one another.

The most powerful thing the Wizard ever gave Billy was not the lightning. It was the chance to belong.
