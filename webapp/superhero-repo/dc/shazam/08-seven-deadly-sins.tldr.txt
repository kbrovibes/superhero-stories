• The Seven Deadly Sins — Pride, Envy, Greed, Lust, Anger, Gluttony, Sloth — imprisoned in the Rock of Eternity
• Sentient, powerful beings — each manifests a different corrupting force on the human spirit
• Pride: manipulates ego and ambition; Envy: twisted green-skinned creature feeding jealousy; Greed: insatiable monster
• Shazam's primary duty: keep the Sins locked in the Rock and stop enemies from releasing them
• Doctor Sivana and Black Adam repeatedly attempt to unleash the Sins as weapons
• Battle against the Sins is mental and spiritual — not just physical
• Antidotes: humility, gratitude, generosity, love, patience, temperance, and diligence
• Multiple escapes and partial releases over the years — each time Billy restores the balance
• The Sins remind the world of what happens when human impulses go unchecked by wisdom
