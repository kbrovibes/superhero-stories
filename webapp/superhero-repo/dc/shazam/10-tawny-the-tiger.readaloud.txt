Not all of Shazam's allies wear capes. One of the most remarkable is Tawky Tawny — a tiger who walks on two legs, wears a beautifully tailored suit, carries a walking stick, and speaks in the most polished, thoughtful sentences you have ever heard.

Tawny began his life as an ordinary tiger in the jungles of India. A mysterious magical encounter changed everything, giving him human intelligence and a gentle, civilized heart. He moved to Fawcett City and fit in wonderfully — reading books in the library, listening to music at the concert hall, enjoying afternoon tea at the finest shops in town. The people of Fawcett City adored him.

And do you know what else Tawny brought to his friendship with Billy Batson? His senses. A tiger's nose and ears can detect things no human ever could. Tawny had noticed threats early and warned Billy in time to make the difference on more than one occasion. His loyalty to Billy was absolute — a bond built through many shared adventures.

When real danger arrived, Tawny was ready. He preferred words and calm reasoning over fighting. But when the moment came, he was every bit as formidable as the apex predator he had always been at heart. Ancient magical threats and modern villains alike had learned not to underestimate the tiger in the suit.

From that day on, Tawky Tawny proved what Billy had always believed: the magical universe is full of wonderful, unexpected wonders, and the most remarkable friends can come from the most surprising places.
