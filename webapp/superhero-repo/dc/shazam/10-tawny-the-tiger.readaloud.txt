Not every ally wears a cape. And not every friend looks quite like you expect! One of the most remarkable figures in all of Billy Batson's life is a tiger — a walking, talking, suit-wearing, walking-stick-carrying, poetry-loving, afternoon-tea-drinking tiger named Tawky Tawny!

Can you imagine?! A tiger. In a beautifully tailored suit. Ordering tea. In Fawcett City!

Tawky Tawny started life as an ordinary tiger in the jungles of India. Then a mysterious magical encounter changed everything — giving him the gift of human intelligence and a wonderfully gentle, civilized heart. He made his way to Fawcett City, and he fit in absolutely beautifully. He reads books at the library. He attends concerts. He enjoys the finest tea shops in town. The people of Fawcett City ADORE him!

He is thoughtful, polite, and has the most impeccable manners of anyone Billy knows!

But Tawky Tawny is also one of the most important members of Billy's circle — because a tiger's nose and ears are extraordinary. He can detect things no human hero could ever notice: a trace of a distant scent, the faintest footstep, the whisper of a threat approaching long before it arrives! His early warnings have saved Billy's life more than once!

His loyalty to Billy Batson is absolute. Their friendship grew from many shared adventures, and when real danger comes, Tawky's preference is always calm reasoning and good words first.

But when the moment demands it — when there is no other choice — the civilized scholar in the fine suit reveals exactly what he has always been at heart: one of the most formidable predators in the natural world.

Ancient magical threats have learned not to underestimate the tiger in the suit. EVER.
