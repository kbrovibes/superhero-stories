• Doctor Thaddeus Sivana — brilliant scientist consumed by resentment and obsession with power
• Believes science and magic are two sides of the same coin — works to bridge them from his high-tech laboratories
• Views the Wizard's choice of a child as a personal insult; convinced the power of Shazam should belong to him
• Orchestrates schemes from the shadows to strip Billy of his powers and claim the Rock of Eternity's secrets
• Master of both advanced technology and the occult — uses drones and mystical artifacts as weapons
• Fundamental flaw: views empathy and compassion as weaknesses rather than sources of strength
• Shazam fights Sivana's cynicism and cruelty with values, not just fists
• Conflict centered on Fawcett City — Sivana wants dominion; Billy wants freedom and joy for all
• Sivana is a reminder: brilliant intellect without humility becomes a prison of its own making
