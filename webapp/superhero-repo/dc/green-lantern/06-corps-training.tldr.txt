• Oa — the training ground and headquarters of the Green Lantern Corps; a planet-sized city
• Hal's title as a new recruit: "poozer" — Kilowog's term for green trainees
• Training environment: floating platforms, emerald constructs, high-gravity arenas, simulated supernovas
• Kilowog of Bolovax Vik — Hal's drill instructor; legendary for intensity and tactical brilliance
• Key lesson from Kilowog: discipline, courage, and a Green Lantern never backs down
• Hal studies the Great Library of Oa: ancient wars against the Manhunters, laws of the Guardians, the emotional spectrum
• Mental training: facing internal fears and doubts through advanced psychic exercises by the Guardians
• The oath becomes a mantra: "In brightest day, in blackest night" — grounding him in darkest moments
• Graduation ceremony at the Central Power Battery; Hal returns to Sector 2814 as a true Green Lantern
• Lesson: training strips away arrogance and replaces it with deep purpose — preparation earns the stars
