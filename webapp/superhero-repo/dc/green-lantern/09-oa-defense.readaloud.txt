There are places in the universe that must never fall. And for the Green Lantern Corps, that place is Oa — the shining city at the center of everything, where the Central Power Battery burns with the energy of willpower and the light of every Lantern who ever lived.

Sinestro knew that. And one day, his entire Sinestro Corps descended on Oa with the full force of fear.

The sky above the planet turned into a battlefield. Green light and yellow light cracked against each other. Buildings shook. Streets ran with the glow of a thousand dueling rings. Hal led the Green Lantern counter-attack, building a squadron of emerald fighter jets that swept through the enemy ranks. Kilowog was in the thick of it, his enormous constructs shielding the wounded. Guy Gardner and John Stewart held the orbital lines. The Corps fought together, side by side, Lanterns from a thousand different worlds united by one oath.

But Sinestro was heading for the Central Power Battery itself. He intended to corrupt it with Parallax — the ancient entity of fear — and extinguish the green light forever. Hal caught up with him in the great plaza, just outside the Battery's doors. Their rings met with a crash. Sinestro reached into Hal's mind and pulled out his deepest fears, trying to make him freeze. "Fear is the only way to maintain order!" Sinestro hissed.

Hal didn't flinch. He reached out with his ring — drawing on the willpower of every Green Lantern on Oa — and built the largest construct of his life: an enormous hand that seized Sinestro and hurled him back into the void of space.

And that is how the Green Lantern Corps showed the universe that when brave hearts stand together, no fear in the galaxy is strong enough to dim their light.
