CRACK! FLASH! BOOM!

The sky above Oa exploded into light — green versus yellow, willpower versus fear, the Green Lantern Corps versus the entire Sinestro Corps — and it was the biggest battle the center of the universe had ever seen!

Sinestro had brought his whole army. Thousands of yellow rings blazing with fear energy, all aimed at the most important target in the galaxy: the Central Power Battery — the great glowing heart of the entire Green Lantern Corps!

If Sinestro could corrupt that Battery with Parallax — the ancient entity of fear — he could extinguish the green light forever!

Hal Jordan led the counter-attack! WHOOOOSH! He conjured a whole squadron of emerald fighter jets — ZOOM ZOOM ZOOM — sweeping through the enemy ranks! Kilowog held the ground with enormous constructs shielding the wounded. Guy Gardner and John Stewart defended the orbital positions up above. Lanterns from a thousand different worlds fought side by side, united by one oath, one ring, one blazing green light!

But Sinestro pushed through to the great plaza right outside the Central Power Battery. Hal caught him there.

Their rings crashed together — CRACK! — and the impact shook the whole plaza. Then Sinestro did something terrible: he reached into Hal's mind and tried to pull out his deepest fears!

Hal felt it. He felt all of it. But he did not flinch.

He recited his oath in his head — "In brightest day, in blackest night" — and reached out with his ring. He drew on the willpower of EVERY Green Lantern on Oa, all at once. An enormous green hand rose up, seized Sinestro — and flung him away into the void of space! WHOOOOOM!

Oa was defended. The green light shone on. And the Corps showed the universe: together, brave hearts can overcome any fear!
