Did you know that across the whole universe, there are different colored rings for different kinds of feelings? Green is willpower. Yellow is fear. Red is rage. Blue is hope. And violet? Violet is LOVE — and it might just be the most powerful of all!

The Star Sapphires guard the power of love. They wield beautiful violet gemstones that glow with a light the color of a twilight sky. Their constructs shimmer and shine in deep purple, and their power flows from the very deepest feelings of the heart!

And one of the Star Sapphires is someone Hal Jordan knows very well here on Earth — his closest friend, Carol Ferris!

Carol works at Ferris Aircraft in Coast City, right alongside Hal. She is determined, brilliant, and one of the bravest people Hal knows. When she becomes Star Sapphire, a stunning violet suit blazes around her, and she can build shields of purple energy, fire beams of violet light with perfect precision, and create graceful bridges that arch across impossible distances! WHOOSH! ZAP! SHIMMER!

But the gemstone is full of the force of love — and love does not always follow instructions. Sometimes it acts on its own, protecting the people Carol cares about in ways nobody planned! And do you know what that can lead to? A rather colorful, rather unexpected argument between a green-suited test pilot and a violet-suited champion!

In those moments, Hal has to be patient. He helps Carol find the balance between her own will and the gemstone's enormous energy.

And when they manage it together — green light and violet light weaving side by side — the result is truly beautiful and genuinely very difficult to stop!
