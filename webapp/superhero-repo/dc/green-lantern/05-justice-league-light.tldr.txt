• Green Lantern's Justice League role: the team's builder and structural support specialist
• Creates massive constructs to assist during large-scale missions: towing spaceships, holding up buildings
• Works alongside Batman (the planner) and Superman to figure out optimal construct strategies
• Can generate a "Justice League Light" — a bright green glow that illuminates underwater or space environments
• Builds constructs for teammates: shields, platforms, and support structures customized per hero
• Creates green energy domes to protect cities; series of bridges across dangerous terrain
• His versatility — builder, transport, defense, light — makes him essential in any crisis
• Lesson: dedication and imagination are the foundations of a great team — and someone always needs to hold things up
