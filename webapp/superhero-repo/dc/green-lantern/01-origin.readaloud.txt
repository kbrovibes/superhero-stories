High above the desert, where the sky meets the endless blue, a test pilot named Hal Jordan pushed his jet to the very edge of what was possible. Other pilots pulled back when things got scary. Hal Jordan pushed FORWARD! He worked at Ferris Aircraft in Coast City, and everybody there knew he was the bravest person in any cockpit, anywhere!

Here is something amazing about Hal: as a little boy, he watched his father — who was also a pilot — and promised himself he would be just as brave. He kept that promise every single day.

Then one afternoon, something fell from the sky.

Not a bird. Not a cloud. A spaceship — and it crashed in the desert near Hal's flight path! He rushed over and found an alien creature named Abin Sur lying inside. Abin Sur was a member of the Green Lantern Corps — an interstellar police force that protected the entire universe! And Abin Sur was running out of time.

But before he was gone, he passed on his most important possession: his Power Ring. The ring did not wait to be told who to pick. It flew on its own, searching the area for someone brave and honest — and it flew STRAIGHT to Hal Jordan!

FWOOOOSH! Green light blazed around him! A bright emerald suit appeared! And a Power Ring hummed on his finger, connecting him to the most powerful force in the universe: his own willpower!

Hal recited the Green Lantern Oath for the first time. Then he flew — not in a jet, but on pure green light — all the way to the planet Oa, where thousands of alien heroes from every corner of the galaxy were waiting to train him.

A test pilot from planet Earth had just become one of the universe's greatest protectors!
