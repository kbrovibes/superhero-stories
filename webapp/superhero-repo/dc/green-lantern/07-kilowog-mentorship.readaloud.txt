Every good student needs a great teacher. And for Hal Jordan, that teacher was the biggest, gruffest, most wonderfully demanding alien in the entire Green Lantern Corps: KILOWOG of Bolovax Vik!

Kilowog was famous on Oa. Not just for his size — which was very, very considerable — but for his wisdom. He had trained more Green Lanterns than anyone could count, and he had a genius for finding the spark inside a person even when they were covered in the dust of their latest spectacular failure!

With Hal, it took patience. And volume. And a very, very direct approach.

"You're trying to OUTMUSCLE it, Jordan!" Kilowog bellowed one day, watching Hal build construct after giant construct trying to hold back a swarm of simulated enemies. "Don't build a WALL when you can build a FUNNEL. Use your head!"

Hal stopped. He thought about it. He tried again — smarter this time, not harder. And it worked!

From that day on, Hal stopped trying to overpower every problem and started trying to outwit it instead!

Their bond was tested for real on a mission at the far edge of the known universe. A sun was about to explode — KABOOM! — and hundreds of thousands of lives were in danger. Hal and Kilowog built an enormous emerald shield together, holding back solar flares while the evacuation rushed forward. When the biggest flare came, Kilowog threw himself into the line of fire. Hal did not hesitate — he poured his ring's energy straight into Kilowog's — WHOOOOOM — and together they held! The planet was saved!

Afterwards, Kilowog sat quietly and told Hal about Bolovax Vik — his home world — and the people he had lost. Then he said only seven words: "You've got the spark, Jordan. Well done."

For Hal, that was everything.
