Repeat after me! Are you ready?

"IN BRIGHTEST DAY, IN BLACKEST NIGHT — NO EVIL SHALL ESCAPE MY SIGHT! LET THOSE WHO WORSHIP EVIL'S MIGHT BEWARE MY POWER — GREEN LANTERN'S LIGHT!"

FWOOOOSH! That is the Green Lantern Oath, and every single day, Hal Jordan recites those words while holding his Power Ring to his Power Battery — a small glowing device shaped like an old-fashioned green lantern. The words fill the ring with green energy, the color of willpower, the color of hope and determination!

Now here is where it gets really exciting: with a fully charged ring, Hal can build ANYTHING he can picture in his mind!

SNAP! A giant green boxing glove to send a villain spinning! WHOOSH! A sleek emerald fighter jet to race across the sky! BOING! A giant trampoline to catch someone falling! SPLASH! An enormous dam of solid green energy to hold back a flood! BOOM! Two enormous hands to catch a crumbling building before it falls!

He calls them constructs — solid objects made of pure green energy, as strong as steel, for as long as he truly believes in them. Because here is the secret: if a doubt creeps into Hal's mind — if his concentration slips even a little — the construct flickers and fades away!

That is why Green Lanterns train their MINDS just as hard as their bodies. Staying calm. Staying focused. Staying sure — even in the middle of the most terrifying situation imaginable!

And being a test pilot, Hal's very favorite constructs? Anything that FLIES! Wings, jets, rockets, hang gliders made of light — you name it!

The most powerful tool in the universe is a ring powered by an imagination that never gives up!
