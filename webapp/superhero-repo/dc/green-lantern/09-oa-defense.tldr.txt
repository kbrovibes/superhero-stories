• Oa — the center of the universe and the heart of the Green Lantern Corps; target of constant attack
• The Sinestro Corps launches a massive multi-pronged assault fueled by yellow fear energy
• Sky above Oa: clashing green and yellow energy, like a cosmic fireworks display
• Hal leads the counter-attack with a squadron of emerald fighter jets
• Battle moves into Oa's streets; Kilowog holds the line; Guy Gardner and John Stewart defend orbital positions
• The Central Power Battery is Sinestro's true target — he intends to corrupt it with Parallax
• Hal intercepts Sinestro in the great plaza before the Battery; their rings clash like grinding stone
• Sinestro manifests Hal's deepest insecurities; Hal counters with his oath and collective willpower
• Hal channels every Lantern's energy into a massive construct — a giant hand that hurls Sinestro into space
• Outcome: Oa defended; city scarred but spirit unbroken; Guardians publicly thank the Lanterns
• Lesson: unity and willpower together can overcome any fear — even fear weaponized
