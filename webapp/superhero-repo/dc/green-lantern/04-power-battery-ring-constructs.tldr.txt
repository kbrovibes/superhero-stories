• Power Battery — a device shaped like a green lantern; the charging source for every Green Lantern's ring
• Recharging ritual: once a day, the Lantern places their ring to the battery and recites the Green Lantern Oath
• The Oath fills the ring with the green energy of willpower
• Ring constructs — solid objects made entirely of green energy, as strong as steel
• A Lantern can construct anything they can imagine: weapons, shields, tools, machines
• Hal, being a pilot, often builds green energy airplanes, parachutes, and giant boxing gloves
• Stronger belief = stronger construct; doubt or distraction makes them fade
• Green Lanterns train their minds constantly to stay focused under pressure
• Hal's creative applications: energy dams to stop floods; giant hands to catch falling objects
• Lesson: imagination and willpower together are a boundless tool — the bigger the heart, the bigger the construct
