Hal Jordan was a brave test pilot who loved flying planes high above the clouds. He was known for having "no fear" and being able to stay calm during the most difficult flights. One day, while Hal was near a desert, a mysterious spaceship crashed nearby. Inside was an alien named Abin Sur, who was a member of the Green Lantern Corps, an interstellar police force that protects the entire universe.

Abin Sur was looking for someone who was brave and honest to take his place as a Green Lantern. He told his special Power Ring to find a worthy successor, and the ring chose Hal Jordan. When Hal put on the ring and spoke the Green Lantern oath, he was transformed into a hero with a bright green suit and amazing powers. He learned that the ring is powered by his own willpower and can create anything he can imagine.

Hal was taken to a distant planet called Oa, where he met many other Green Lanterns from all over the galaxy. Some looked like humans, while others looked like colorful aliens or even talking plants! They taught Hal how to use his ring to fly through space and create "constructs"—solid objects made of green energy. Hal realized that he was now part of a team dedicated to keeping peace and order throughout the stars.

Even though he has incredible powers, Hal remains a pilot at heart. He often uses his ring to create green energy planes or shields to protect people. He knows that being a Green Lantern is a big responsibility, but he is always ready for the challenge. With his Power Ring and his strong willpower, Hal Jordan protects Earth and the rest of the Sector 2814, proving that courage can light up even the darkest corners of the universe.
