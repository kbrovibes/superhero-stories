Think you are brave enough to be a Green Lantern? Well, first you have to survive training on OA!

Oa is the planet at the center of the universe — an entire world that is one giant city, humming with the energy of thousands of heroes from every corner of the galaxy! When Hal Jordan arrived there for the first time, the other Lanterns had a special name for new recruits. They called them POOZERS!

And a poozer's job was to learn how much they still did not know!

The training arenas used real gravity — not pretend gravity, not gentle gravity, but heavy, crushing, relentless gravity that made every step feel like wading through wet concrete. UUUGH! Simulations could conjure a supernova — an exploding star! — and you had to deal with it! And over all of it, like a thunderclap that never stopped, boomed the voice of Hal's drill instructor: KILOWOG of Bolovax Vik!

Kilowog was enormous. Kilowog was relentless. And Kilowog was the finest teacher in the entire Green Lantern Corps.

"You call THAT a construct, Jordan?!" Kilowog would roar, looming over Hal's trembling efforts. But every insult was a lesson! And every lesson made Hal tougher, sharper, better!

Between training sessions, Hal studied in the Great Library of Oa — reading about ancient wars, learning the laws of the Guardians, understanding the full emotional spectrum of the universe.

Then came the hardest part: the Guardians sent recruits into psychic simulations designed to bring out their deepest fears. Hal had to face everything that frightened him. And every time he came through it, reciting his oath — "In brightest day, in blackest night" — he grew stronger.

On graduation day, standing before the Central Power Battery, the poozer became a true Green Lantern!
