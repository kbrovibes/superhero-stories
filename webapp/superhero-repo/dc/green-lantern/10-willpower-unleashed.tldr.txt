• Unleashed willpower — the state where the boundary between Hal and the Power Ring dissolves entirely
• Triggered by extreme circumstances when standard constructs are not enough
• Mission: a sentient nebula consuming entire solar systems; the pressure crushes normal constructs
• Hal's ring fades — then the thought of billions of lives and those he loves on Earth reignites it explosively
• He manifests a massive emerald sun that counteracts the nebula's gravity and restores balance to the star system
• Power comes from Hal himself, not just the Central Power Battery — he is the source
• In this state: raw energy waves respond to instinct; he dismantles Volthoom's (the First Lantern's) reality-warping abilities
• Physical and emotional cost: drains his life force; days of weakness follow; risk of losing his humanity in the light
• The Guardians fear this state; consider it unstable — but acknowledge it as their ultimate weapon
• Connection to Earth keeps him grounded: his humanity is the anchor, not a weakness
• Lesson: willpower is not meant to be caged — it is the final line against the dark, and its source is love
