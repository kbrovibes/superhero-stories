Superman can punch through a mountain. Wonder Woman can stop a tank with her bracelets. The Flash can run around the world three times before breakfast! So when a massive building starts to crumble, or a spaceship needs to be towed to safety, or the team needs a bridge across a bottomless pit — who does the building?

HAL JORDAN! Green Lantern! The Justice League's master builder!

Batman figures out the plan. Superman provides the raw strength. But Green Lantern provides the TOOLS — built in seconds from pure imagination and willpower, shaped perfectly to match whatever the situation needs right now!

Need a dome to protect a whole city from falling debris? WHOOOOOM — green dome, done! Need a bridge across a lava field? FWOOOOSH — glowing green bridge, ready to walk on! Need a platform for your teammates to stand on while fighting in deep space? SNAP — floating platform, right there, perfectly sized!

And in the places that are dark and frightening — deep underwater, or far out in the cold silence of space — Hal can light the way! His ring creates a warm green glow that reaches as far as the team needs to see. Like carrying the sun in your hand!

Here is what Hal's teammates love most about him: no matter how strange or impossible the problem looks, Hal never says "I can't build that." He tilts his head, squints a little, thinks for just a moment — and then the green light begins to flow.

Sometimes it becomes a staircase. Sometimes a dome. Once it was an entire bridge wide enough for a whole crowd to run across.

The brightest light on the team is not always the one who hits the hardest. Sometimes it belongs to the one who builds what everyone else needs to shine!
