Somewhere deep in the universe, a nebula was ALIVE — and it was hungry. It was swallowing entire solar systems, one by one, pulling them apart with crushing gravity! And Hal Jordan flew straight toward it to stop it!

The pressure was like nothing he had ever felt. CREAK! The constructs he built flickered and faded — the gravity was too strong! SPUTTER! His ring grew dim! The green light shrank down to almost nothing.

For one terrifying moment, the darkness pressed in close.

And then Hal thought about the billions of people in the solar systems around him. He thought about Coast City on Earth — the city he loved. He thought about Carol. He thought about his father. He thought about every face he had ever promised to protect.

And something inside him caught FIRE!

BLAZING BRILLIANT BLAZING GREEN — not the steady glow of a charged battery, but a wild, raw, tremendous light that came from INSIDE Hal himself — every atom of who he was, everything he refused to give up, pouring out through the ring like a burst of sunlight through a cracked door!

He built something he had never built before: an EMERALD SUN! A blazing green star rose between him and the nebula, burning with warmth and willpower, pushing back the crushing darkness until the star system steadied and the threat retreated!

The Guardians on Oa saw it and felt uneasy. This kind of power — coming entirely from one person's heart — was unpredictable, uncontrollable. But they could not deny what had saved those billions of lives.

And do you know what kept Hal from losing himself in all that light? His memories. His ordinary, wonderful, human life back on Earth.

The most powerful force in the universe was never stored in a battery. It was always living inside him.
