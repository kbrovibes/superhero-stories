• Star Sapphires — a group of warriors who wield violet gemstones powered by the energy of love
• Carol Ferris — Hal Jordan's close friend on Earth; sometimes becomes Star Sapphire
• Violet Star Sapphire suit; constructs made of purple energy: shields, energy beams, violet bridges
• The gemstone can overwhelm its wearer, causing protective, unpredictable behavior
• Love is one of the strongest emotions in the universe — the Star Sapphire power reflects this
• Carol's gemstone sometimes brings her into playful, unexpected conflict with Green Lantern
• Hal often helps Carol find balance with her gemstone's power
• When working together: green light of willpower + violet light of love = formidable team
• Lesson: even friendship gets complicated — but teamwork and care can solve any problem
