• The Guardians of the Universe — ancient blue-skinned beings on Oa; founders of the Green Lantern Corps
• Billions of years old; value logic, tradition, and emotional detachment above all else
• Hal's conflict: driven by emotion and human morality vs. the Guardians' cold "greater good" calculus
• Major disagreement: Guardians order a star system evacuation but refuse resources to save indigenous wildlife — statistically insignificant to them
• Hal defies orders and builds a ring-powered energy ark, saving the species himself
• Guardians are furious at his insubordination but cannot deny the results
• Hal also recognizes the Guardians' wisdom: eons of perspective and peace brought to thousands of worlds
• He becomes a bridge: helps the Guardians understand Lanterns' emotional needs; helps Lanterns see strategic necessity
• Hal uncovers the Guardians' hidden secrets: Manhunter history, the existence of the other colored corps
• Lesson: authority without accountability loses its way; truth-telling serves a mission better than blind obedience
