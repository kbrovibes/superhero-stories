• Sinestro — once the greatest and most skilled Green Lantern; trained Hal Jordan personally
• Turned to fear: believed true order required controlling people through terror, not inspiring willpower
• The Guardians of the Universe expelled him from the Green Lantern Corps for his authoritarian methods
• Founded the Sinestro Corps; wields a yellow ring powered by fear — the opposite of green willpower
• Green Lanterns' historical weakness: yellow (the color of fear)
• In battle, Sinestro constructs fear-based creations designed to terrify opponents
• Hal must overcome fear to keep his green ring strong — willpower always defeats fear when held firm
• Complicated history: both Hal and Sinestro want to protect the universe, but disagree fundamentally on how
• Lesson: true strength is overcoming fear, not using it — courage beats control every time
