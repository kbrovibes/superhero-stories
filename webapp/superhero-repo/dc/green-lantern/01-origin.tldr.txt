• Hal Jordan — brave test pilot at Ferris Aircraft; known for fearlessness and calm under pressure
• Abin Sur — a dying alien Green Lantern whose spaceship crashes in the desert near Hal
• Abin Sur's Power Ring chooses Hal as its new bearer — selecting for bravery and honesty
• Hal speaks the Green Lantern Oath and is transformed: bright green suit, ring-powered abilities
• Travels to Oa — the Green Lantern Corps homeworld; meets thousands of Lanterns from across the galaxy
• Learns to create "constructs" — solid objects made of green energy, limited only by willpower and imagination
• His sector: Sector 2814, which includes Earth
• As a pilot, Hal often constructs green energy planes, shields, and tools
• Lesson: courage can light up even the darkest corners of the universe
