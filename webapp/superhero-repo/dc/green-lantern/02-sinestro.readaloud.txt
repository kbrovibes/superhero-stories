Not every story about a great teacher has a happy ending. And the most gifted Green Lantern who ever lived — before Hal Jordan — was a man named Sinestro.

Sinestro was brilliant. He was precise. He was so skilful with his Power Ring that other Lanterns would stop their own training just to watch him work! He was also the one who first showed Hal how to fight and survive in the vastness of space. In those early days, Hal looked up to Sinestro more than almost anyone.

But then — slowly, like a cloud covering the sun — Sinestro showed who he truly was.

He believed that the only way to keep order in the universe was through FEAR. Make people frightened enough, he thought, and they would never cause trouble! The Guardians of the Universe on Oa told him he was wrong. He refused to listen. So they removed him from the Green Lantern Corps entirely!

But Sinestro was not finished. Not even close. He found a yellow ring powered by FEAR — the exact opposite of green willpower — and built his own army called the Sinestro Corps! CRACK! FLASH! When Hal and Sinestro fought, the sky blazed with green and yellow light — courage clashing against terror, willpower against control!

And here is what gave Hal his edge every single time: when Sinestro tried to flood Hal's mind with fear, Hal looked it square in the face and REFUSED to blink. The green ring grew stronger the more firmly he stood. His courage was not the absence of fear. It was the choice to keep going anyway — even when he was afraid!

Green beats yellow. Willpower beats fear. Courage beats control. Every. Single. Time.
