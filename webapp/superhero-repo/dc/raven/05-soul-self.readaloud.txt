Okay — get comfortable — because I am about to tell you about the most extraordinary ability in the entire Teen Titans lineup. And it belongs to Raven.

You know how some heroes can punch really hard? Or run really fast? Raven can do something so amazing it doesn't even have a regular word for it. She can send out her SOUL-SELF.

Are you ready? Here is what that looks like.

Raven goes still. Her eyes close. And then — WHOOOOOM — a shape of deep black and violet energy RISES from her, enormous and silent, stretching outward with wings that are wider than a bus. It is shaped like a raven. Her raven. Her name, her shape, the truest picture of who she is inside. And it is REAL. It is actually, genuinely a piece of her inner self, sent out into the world to act while she stays standing in one spot.

The soul-self can FLY ahead of the team through walls and locked doors like they are made of nothing! WHOOSH, right through. It can carry people — gently — out of collapsed buildings or sealed rooms. It can SHIELD her teammates from harm by wrapping itself around them like a living umbrella of shadow. It can scout. It can protect. It can reach into places that nobody else can get to.

And here is the part that will really get you. The soul-self knows everything Raven is feeling. When she is worried, it moves differently — a little cautious, a little searching. When she is focused and sure, it FLIES straight and fast like an arrow. Once — ONCE — Cyborg looked up during a long and hard mission, and he noticed the soul-self was just… hovering over the whole team. Spread out wide. Like a canopy. Like a shadow umbrella.

Raven had been worrying about all of them. And she had done that without saying a single word about it.

That is the kind of person she is.

Now. The soul-self takes WORK. In Azarath, learning to send it out properly is an ancient, serious skill that takes years of meditation. Raven has been practicing since she was small. And she has to keep her feelings steady while using it, because when emotions go wobbly, the soul-self gets harder to control — like trying to pour water from a cup you are also shaking.

She has learned the balance. It took time. But she learned it.

Starfire once asked Raven what it feels like to BE the soul-self — to be that great bird of shadow, flying free.

Raven was quiet for a while. Then she said:

"It feels like being entirely yourself. With nowhere to hide and no reason to."

Starfire said that sounded beautiful.

Raven said she thought so too.
