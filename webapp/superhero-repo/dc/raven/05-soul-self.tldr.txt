• Raven's soul-self is a raven-shaped form of black-violet energy that rises from her body
• It is genuinely part of her inner self — not a trick or illusion — and acts while she stands still
• It can fly ahead, pass through barriers, carry things, shield teammates, and rescue people
• In Azarath, projecting a soul-self is an ancient discipline requiring years of meditation
• The soul-self reflects her emotions: cautious when troubled, swift and direct when focused
• Cyborg once noticed it spread over the whole team like a canopy — Raven had been protecting them silently
• She keeps it inside most of the time; releasing it fully requires deep inner steadiness
• When Starfire asks what it feels like, Raven says it feels like being entirely yourself with no reason to hide
