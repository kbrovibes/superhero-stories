• Trigon is Raven's father — a demon of vast power who rules his own dark dimension
• For years he waits patiently, sending whispers and shadows to try to reach Raven
• His red energy begins to shimmer in Jump City, distorting walls and trying to tear open a passage
• Trigon speaks in a voice like underground thunder, trying to convince Raven she is like him
• Raven tells the Teen Titans; Robin, Starfire, Cyborg, and Beast Boy all stand ready beside her
• Raven closes her eyes and pushes back with her soul-self — not with anger, but with calm certainty
• She uses the steady practice the monks of Azarath taught her to close the passage
• The red shimmer cracks, Trigon's voice fades, and Jump City's sky clears
• Raven nods to Robin; the team knows she has chosen her own path once again
