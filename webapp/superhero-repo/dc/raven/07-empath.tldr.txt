• For Raven, empathy is not a skill but a constant sense — she feels others' emotions involuntarily, like hearing sound
• Walking through Jump City she absorbs everyone nearby: anxious commuters, happy children, contented elders
• She learned witnessing from Azarath's monks: being fully present to a feeling without losing her own footing
• She holds the whole team's emotions at once: Robin's focus, Cyborg's warmth, Beast Boy's chaos, Starfire's joy
• Her empathy makes her a perceptive teammate — she senses when a mission is going wrong before data shows it
• She quietly appears with the right thing at the right moment: a word, a cup of tea, a steady presence
• Deeply kind, but quietly — not a hug but a calm nearness that lets teammates know they are not alone
