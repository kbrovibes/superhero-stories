• Doctor Light — real name Arthur Light — attacks Jump City harbor with blinding beams and light constructs
• He reflects Starfire's starbolts back at her and neutralizes Cyborg, Robin, and Beast Boy one by one

• She wraps her shadow around the power coupling in his suit and switches it off
• Cyborg contains him before he can recover
• Starfire tells him the Tamaranian word for someone who underestimates an empath; Raven says nothing — she never needed to
