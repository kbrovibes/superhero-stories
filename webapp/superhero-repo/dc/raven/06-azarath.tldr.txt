• Azarath is an ancient dimension parallel to Earth, shaped by centuries of collective meditation
• Its towers are white stone; the water is deep silver; the sky is a gentle blue-grey that never rushes
• Raven's mother Arella raised her there with the monks' guidance
• The central meditation hall has no walls — just columns open to the sky — where monks practiced stillness each morning
• Raven sat in that circle from a very young age; it was hard at first, then settled into her bones
• When she left, she took only two things: her Azarathian cloak and a thin book of meditations
• The book shifts language when others read it — Starfire found it appearing in Tamaranian
• Raven carries Azarath in her breathing, her steadiness, and her calm in hard moments
