• At 2:47am, the Dissonance materializes in Jump City's power grid, growing by absorbing fear
• It sends waves of emotional overwhelm; Cyborg's instruments fail, Starfire's starbolts dissolve, Robin's tactics don't land
• Raven studies it with empathy: vast and loud but with no real center — noise without a source
• She recalls an Azarath scroll: entities of pure noise are defeated by genuine feeling, not force
• She sends her team a wordless empathic signal: gather and feel something real and specific
• Robin thinks of Gotham; Starfire of Tamaran; Cyborg of his father's workbench; Beast Boy of a quiet sunset
• Raven weaves their real feelings together with her own memory of Azarath's silver water and bells
• She sends it outward into the Dissonance — real emotion dissolves noise that never had anything genuine inside it
• The entity collapses in four minutes; the Titans sit together in the quiet dark
