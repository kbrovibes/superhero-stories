• Raven grows up in Azarath, a peaceful dimension of white towers and meditation, raised by gentle monks
• Her mother is Arella; her father is Trigon, a demon — but Raven is trained to choose goodness
• She wears a dark blue cloak and has violet eyes; she keeps her emotions calm to control her power
• Her abilities include telekinesis, projecting a shadowy soul-self shaped like a raven, and empathy
• Because she feels others' emotions as her own, she keeps her feelings steady — not cold, but careful
• Sensing a great threat gathering, Raven travels to Earth and to Jump City
• She finds Robin, Starfire, Cyborg, and Beast Boy and they form the Teen Titans together
• They make their home in Titans Tower and defend the city from extraordinary dangers
• Raven chooses goodness every day, knowing that what matters is not where you come from but what you decide to do
