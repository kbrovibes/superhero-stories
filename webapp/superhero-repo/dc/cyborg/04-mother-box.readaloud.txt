What if you had a living computer fused right into your body — a partner who thinks alongside you, helps you understand alien technology, and can open a magical doorway to anywhere in the universe? That is what Victor Stone has! It is called a Mother Box, and it comes from a faraway world called New Genesis!

A Mother Box is not like a phone or a tablet. It is sentient — which means it is ALIVE, in its own technological way! It hums with the thoughts of the universe. It knows things no Earth computer could ever know. And it works with Victor like a true partner, reading his intentions and responding to what he needs before he even finishes thinking it!

But wait — Victor has ANOTHER extraordinary tool too! His Sonic Cannon! WHOOOOOOM! With just a thought, Victor's arm transforms into a powerful device that fires high-frequency sound blasts. BOOM! It can stop a rampaging robot cold. WHAM! It can clear rubble from a disaster site in seconds. It can even generate a shield of sound that bounces energy blasts right back!

Victor always uses exactly the right amount of force — no more, no less. He practices constantly to be sure, because being powerful means being responsible.

Together, the Mother Box and the Sonic Cannon make Cyborg one of the most versatile heroes on Earth: he can travel to any point in the universe AND handle almost any threat once he gets there!

But here is the part Victor loves most: the Mother Box also lets him communicate with other digital beings. It makes him an ambassador — a bridge between the human world and the vast, ancient, extraordinary world of technology beyond our planet.

Part human. Part machine. One hundred percent hero!
