• Boom Tube: a portal through the fabric of space-time created by aligning Victor's systems with the Mother Box
• Named for its deafening "boom" sound — loud enough to vibrate Victor's organic heart
• Opening a tube requires precise calculation and immense energy — Victor's soul feels stretched across the cosmos
• Inside the tube: chaotic swirl of colors and fragmented dimensions; Victor senses distant galaxies and the New Gods
• A gravity well nearly collapsed a Boom Tube with the Justice League inside — Victor held the portal open with his own systems
• Every Boom Tube is a reminder of its origin: Apokolips, the world of Darkseid
• Victor must constantly assert his will over the Mother Box's own agenda — using a tool of oppression for justice
• Boom Tubes allow the League to respond to galactic threats in seconds
• Victor: the "master of the Boom" — the only one whose unique physiology makes the portals possible
