• Mother Box — a sentient alien supercomputer from the planet New Genesis, fused into Cyborg's body
• Allows Victor to teleport across vast distances and interface with advanced alien technology
• Works in perfect harmony with Victor's own thoughts — a living technological partner
• Sonic Cannon: Victor's most famous tool — transforms his arm to fire high-frequency sound blasts
• Sonic Cannon uses: disabling robots, clearing obstacles, creating protective shields, sound-pattern communication
• Victor controls the Cannon's intensity carefully — uses only as much power as the situation requires
• Mother Box + Sonic Cannon together make Cyborg a uniquely versatile hero: travel and combat in one
• Victor spends significant time practicing with his technology to use it safely
• Mother Box helps him communicate with other digital beings — making him an ambassador for technology
