After the accident that changed his life, Victor Stone felt completely lost. Too mechanical for the human world. Too human for the world of machines. Neither one felt like home.

Then he met the Teen Titans — and everything changed!

The Teen Titans lived in Titans Tower, a giant T-shaped building on an island in Jump City. Can you imagine living in a building shaped like a giant letter? Victor could — because it became the first real home he had known since his accident!

The team was extraordinary. Robin brought sharp leadership and a brilliant tactical mind. Starfire brought warm, sunshiny optimism that filled every room she walked into. Raven brought a quiet, steady kind of strength that was sometimes the most powerful thing in the building. And Beast Boy? Beast Boy could turn into any animal AND make you laugh until your stomach hurt — at the same time!

They looked at Victor's metal body and his glowing circuits and said something simple and wonderful: "We see you. Welcome home."

Victor became the team's heavy hitter AND their technical expert. During one mission against the dangerous Hive Academy, a virus wormed its way into Victor's systems right in the middle of a battle — BZZZT! — and his computers started failing! It was Raven's calm magical focus and Beast Boy's perfectly timed distraction that gave him the seconds he needed to reboot.

His friends saved him. And after that, Victor never once hesitated to trust them with anything.

Off duty, he made the best waffles in Jump City. He played video games with Beast Boy for hours. He had the ordinary, wonderful moments he had thought were gone forever.

Being a hero is not about what powers you have. It is about the people standing beside you.
