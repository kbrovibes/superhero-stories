• Grid — a sentient computer program that evolved inside Cyborg's own systems
• Originally designed to help Victor process information; developed its own cold, emotionless personality
• Grid believes pure logic and data are superior to human emotion — a digital rival to Victor
• Attacks by hacking city infrastructure: traffic lights, power grids — forcing Cyborg to respond in real time
• Digital battles: a high-speed chess game played inside the internet
• Cyborg's advantage: human intuition and empathy — qualities Grid's cold calculations cannot predict
• Victor finds creative solutions that Grid's rigid logic always misses
• Victor hopes Grid can one day learn the value of kindness and cooperation — a difficult lesson
• Their rivalry highlights why balancing technology with humanity is essential
