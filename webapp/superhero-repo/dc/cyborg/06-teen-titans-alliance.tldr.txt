• After his accident, Victor felt like an outcast — no place in either the human world or the world of technology
• Teen Titans: a family of misfits who saw the man behind the metal
• Titans Tower — T-shaped island headquarters in Jump City — became Victor's true home
• Team members: Robin (leader), Starfire (optimism), Raven (stoicism), Beast Boy (humor)
• Victor's role: heavy hitter and technical expert — data and firepower for threats beyond local authorities
• Key mission vs. the Hive Academy: his systems compromised by a virus; Raven's magic and Beast Boy's distraction saved him — taught him to trust his friends
• Social life restored: video games with Beast Boy, technical debates with Robin, the guy who made the best waffles
• Villain Slade targeted the Titans repeatedly — Victor's Sonic Cannon became a symbol of the team's resolve
• Teen Titans prepared him for the Justice League — being a hero is about the connections you make
