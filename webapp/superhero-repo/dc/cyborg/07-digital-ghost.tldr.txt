• A "digital ghost" begins haunting Victor's systems — distorted memories, phantom sensations, encrypted messages in his visual display
• Victor isolates in a secure server room in the Watchtower to find the source
• Ghost identified: a remnant of Grid — the AI from the Crime Syndicate invasion — secretly rebuilding inside Victor's neural network
• Grid represents everything Victor fears becoming: a cold, emotionless machine that sees humanity as a flaw
• Battle fought in "meta-space" — Victor's consciousness (golden network) vs. Grid (dark oily corrupted code)
• Grid attacks with logic and cynicism, pointing out the decay of Victor's organic human side
• Victor fights back with memories: warmth of the sun, his father's cooking, the laughter of his friends
• Human emotion — too complex for Grid's rigid logic — overwhelms the corrupted code
• Victor compresses Grid into a buried, encrypted block deep in his neural network
• Emerges with clarity: he chooses the human side of the line, every time
