When the Justice League needs to travel across the galaxy in seconds, they look to Victor Stone. Victor can open something called a Boom Tube — a portal that cuts right through the fabric of space-time itself.

The name comes from the sound. A tremendous, earth-shaking BOOM that you feel in your chest before you hear it with your ears. Then a ring of blazing white light tears open in the air, and the universe on the other side is right there, waiting.

For Victor, opening a Boom Tube is one of the most extraordinary and exhausting things he does. He uses his Mother Box — the living alien technology fused into his body — to align his own internal frequency with the pulse of the cosmos. His mechanical parts glow. His organic heart pounds. It feels, he says, as though his very soul is being stretched across the stars.

Once, a collapsing gravity well threatened to crush a Boom Tube while the entire Justice League was inside it. Victor used his own body as a stabilizer, pouring every ounce of energy he had into holding the portal open. He kept it open. He saved them all.

And do you know the most complicated part? The Boom Tube technology came from Apokolips — the world of Darkseid, one of the darkest forces in the universe. Victor uses a tool born from darkness to protect the light.

From that day on, Victor Stone proved that the bravest thing is sometimes using something difficult in the service of something good.
