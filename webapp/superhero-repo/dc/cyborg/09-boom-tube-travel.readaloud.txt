Are you ready for the loudest, most spectacular thing in Victor Stone's entire superhero toolkit? Then cover your ears — because here comes a BOOM TUBE!

BOOOOOOM!

A ring of blazing white light tears open in the middle of the air. The whole room shakes. Victor's chest vibrates. And there, right through that ring of light, is somewhere completely different — maybe on the other side of the world, maybe on the other side of the galaxy! The Justice League steps through in seconds. WHOOSH WHOOSH WHOOSH!

That is a Boom Tube, and only Victor Stone can open one!

He uses his Mother Box — the living alien technology fused into his body — to align his internal frequency with the pulse of space-time itself. His mechanical parts glow bright white. His organic human heart pounds like a drum. He says it feels like his entire soul is being stretched across the stars!

One time, a collapsing gravity well threatened to crush a Boom Tube while the ENTIRE Justice League was traveling through it! Victor threw all his energy into the portal — WHOOOOOM — and held it open with nothing but willpower and cybernetics, keeping every single teammate safe until they were through!

But here is the most interesting part: Boom Tube technology originally came from Apokolips — the world of the terrible villain Darkseid. It was a tool built for dark purposes.

Victor uses it to protect the light.

Every time he opens a Boom Tube, he is taking something born from darkness and turning it into something good. And that — every single time — is one of the bravest things a hero can do.
