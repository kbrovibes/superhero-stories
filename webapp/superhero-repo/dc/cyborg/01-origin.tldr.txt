• Victor Stone — brilliant student and talented athlete from Detroit
• Father: Silas Stone, world-renowned scientist working with advanced alien technology at S.T.A.R. Labs
• Accident during an experiment at Silas's lab leaves Victor critically injured
• Silas uses experimental technology to rebuild Victor's body, fusing cybernetic parts to save his life
• New body: supercomputer in his chest for lightning-fast information processing; built-in boosters for flight
• Arms can transform into various tools and gadgets on demand
• Victor can connect to any computer system in the world — a digital genius
• Learns that being a hero is about using whatever you have to help others
• Victor Stone becomes Cyborg — symbol of hope for anyone who has ever felt different
