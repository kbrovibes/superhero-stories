BOOYAH! That is the word you need to know before we start this story — because it is the best word in the whole Cyborg dictionary, and by the end you will understand exactly why!

Victor Stone grew up in Detroit — a star athlete AND a brilliant student. His father, Silas Stone, was one of the world's greatest scientists working at a place called S.T.A.R. Labs. S.T.A.R. Labs stood for Science and Technology Advanced Research — and it was absolutely packed with extraordinary technology from other worlds!

One day Victor visited his dad's lab. And something went terribly, frighteningly wrong. An experiment exploded. Victor was hurt very badly — so badly that Silas faced the most terrifying moment of his entire life.

He had to make a choice. And he chose his son.

Silas used everything he knew — every piece of alien technology, every scientific discovery he had ever made — to rebuild Victor's body. When Victor woke up, he was different. Very different! He had a supercomputer built right into his chest! His arms could transform into tools. He could fly on built-in boosters! WHOOSH! He could connect to any computer anywhere in the world. CLICK CLICK BEEP!

At first Victor worried. He looked different. He felt different. Would his friends see him the same way?

But here is a fun fact worth knowing: Victor's famous word "Booyah!" was originally a special phrase shared between him and his mother — a little piece of joy he carried with him right into his life as a hero!

And the moment everything clicked into place, when Victor understood his new powers could save people and change the world? You already know what he shouted.

BOOYAH!
