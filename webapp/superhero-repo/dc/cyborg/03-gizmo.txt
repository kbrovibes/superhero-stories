Gizmo is a brilliant but mischievous inventor who loves to use his high-tech gadgets to pull elaborate pranks and cause trouble. He is much smaller than Cyborg, but his intelligence and creativity make him a formidable opponent. Gizmo often builds mechanical spiders, flying drones, and even giant robots to challenge Victor and his friends. He views the world as a giant playground for his inventions and often forgets that his "games" can sometimes be disruptive to the city.

The encounters between Cyborg and Gizmo are often filled with surprising technology and clever traps. Gizmo's gadgets are unpredictable and often feature funny or unexpected functions, like bubble-blowing lasers or tickle-bots. Cyborg has to use his own advanced systems to analyze Gizmo's machines and find ways to disable them without causing any damage. It's a battle of wits where Victor must be just as creative as the prankster inventor he is trying to stop.

Despite his mischief, Cyborg sees that Gizmo has an incredible talent for building things. He often tries to convince Gizmo to use his skills for good, perhaps by building helpful robots or solving community problems. However, Gizmo's love for excitement and pranks often leads him back to his troublesome ways. Victor remains patient, knowing that underneath the gadgets and the jokes, Gizmo is just a kid who loves technology as much as he does.

Cyborg's goal is to protect the city from Gizmo's more chaotic inventions while also hoping to inspire the young inventor to do better. He often uses his own gadgets to counter Gizmo's pranks in a way that is both effective and harmless. Their relationship is a mix of rivalry and mutual respect for each other's technical abilities. Victor knows that as long as Gizmo continues to build his high-tech toys, he will be there to make sure the fun doesn't get out of hand.
