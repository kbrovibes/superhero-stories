Picture Metropolis — gleaming towers, busy streets, bright blue sky. Now look at the TOP of the tallest, shiniest building of all, and there he stands: Lex Luthor. The richest man in the city. The cleverest man in the room. And the most frustrated man on Earth — all because of Superman!

Lex Luthor runs a company called LexCorp, and he is brilliant. Genuinely, truly brilliant. He can invent things, build things, plan things that most people can't even imagine. But here's his problem: he CANNOT STAND Superman. Why? Because Lex believes that humans — just regular humans — should reach greatness on their own. No help from some alien with a cape! Having Superman around feels to Lex like... cheating. And that drives him absolutely bananas.

So Lex pours that enormous brain into scheme after scheme. He builds advanced weapons. He buys newspapers and tries to write stories that make people distrust Superman. He whispers to politicians. He invents high-tech battle suits. CLANG — one scheme. CRASH — another scheme. ZZZZT — one more elaborate plan. Each one defeated. Each one making Lex furrier than before!

But here's the truly mysterious thing. Can you guess it? Lex Luthor can't understand why Superman helps people without wanting ANYTHING in return. His brain is so busy looking for the hidden trick, the secret plan, the selfish ambition — that he can't see the obvious truth: Superman just... cares. That's it. That's the whole thing.

And Superman? He doesn't look at Lex with anger. He looks at him with sadness. Because he can see what an incredible force for good that brilliant mind could be — if only it weren't so busy being jealous. Two powerful men, two very different choices. One of them is right. And one of them is too proud to see it. Which one do YOU think made the better choice?
