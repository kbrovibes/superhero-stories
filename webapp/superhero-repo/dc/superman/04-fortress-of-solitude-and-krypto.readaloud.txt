Far, far to the north, past the last road and the last town and the last everything — past where any map has ever gone — something magnificent rises from the ice. Giant crystal towers that hum and shimmer in the pale Arctic light. A door that only one person in the entire universe can open. This is the Fortress of Solitude — Superman's secret home — and it is the most incredible private hideout that has ever existed!

WHOOOOA. Are you ready for what's inside? The Fortress holds holograms of Jor-El and Lara, Superman's Kryptonian parents, who can speak to him with love and wisdom whenever he needs guidance. There's the Bottled City of Kandor glowing softly on its shelf. There are records of all of Krypton — its history, its science, its music — preserved forever so Superman's home planet will never truly disappear. And the door is made of a Kryptonian key so heavy that only someone with Superman's strength could lift it. Talk about a lock no one can pick!

But here's the BEST thing about the Fortress of Solitude. Are you ready? It's a dog.

Krypto! A big, fluffy, white, absolutely wonderful dog from Krypton who was launched into space on a test rocket — yes, before Krypton was destroyed — and who eventually found his way to Earth! Under Earth's yellow sun, Krypto has ALL the same powers as Superman. He can fly! He can shoot heat vision! He has super-strength and X-ray vision and freezing breath!

He is also still, unquestionably, a dog. He chases Arctic foxes. He plays fetch with titanium-reinforced balls (regular balls shatter immediately). He zooms through the Arctic sky doing loop-de-loops for no reason at all. And when Superman comes home to the Fortress — tired, maybe sad, carrying the weight of the world — Krypto's tail wags so hard the ice shelf shakes.

The most powerful being on Earth? He comes home to a dog. And that might be the most perfect thing in the whole universe.
