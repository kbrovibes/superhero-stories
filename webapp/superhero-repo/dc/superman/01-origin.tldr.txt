• Kal-El born on Krypton, a world of crystalline cities at its scientific peak
• Krypton faces destruction; parents Jor-El and Lara Lor-Van launch infant Kal-El to Earth
• Spacecraft lands in wheat fields of Smallville, Kansas; found by Jonathan and Martha Kent
• Raised as Clark Kent with values of honesty, hard work, and compassion
• Earth's yellow sun unlocks extraordinary powers: strength, speed, X-ray vision
• Jonathan Kent teaches Clark that powers are a responsibility, not a personal gift
• Clark moves to Metropolis; becomes reporter at the Daily Planet to stay connected to people
• Dons red and blue suit; becomes Superman — champion of truth and justice
• Lesson: our choices, not our origins, define who we are
