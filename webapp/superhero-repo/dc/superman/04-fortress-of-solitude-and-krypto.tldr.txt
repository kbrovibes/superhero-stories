• Fortress of Solitude: crystalline sanctuary hidden in the Arctic behind massive glaciers
• Only Superman can open the door; built from self-assembling Kryptonian crystals
• Houses the Bottled City of Kandor and holographic projections of Jor-El and Lara
• Serves as a museum of Kryptonian history, science laboratory, and place of reflection
• Krypto the Superdog: Kryptonian survivor sent into space on a test flight before Krypton's destruction
• Under Earth's yellow sun, Krypto has the same powers as Superman
• Krypto patrols the frozen Arctic landscape; plays fetch with titanium-reinforced balls
• Fortress also shelters rescued alien species; Superman researches restoring Kandor's people
• Place where Kal-El can fully embrace his dual identity without disguises
