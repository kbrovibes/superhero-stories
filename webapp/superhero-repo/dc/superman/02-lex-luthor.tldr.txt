• Lex Luthor: immensely wealthy, unparalleled intellect, CEO of LexCorp in Metropolis
• Views Superman as an existential threat to human progress and a personal insult
• Believes Superman's alien altruism is a façade hiding hunger for power
• Builds advanced weaponry and funds clandestine projects to destroy Superman
• Uses political influence and public manipulation to turn opinion against the Man of Steel
• Core flaw: cannot believe anyone would use god-like power purely to help others
• Superman views Luthor as a tragic figure with potential wasted on obsession
• Conflict is a chess match — corporate sabotage to high-tech power suits — fought globally
• Lesson: the greatest threats come from hearts that have forgotten humility
