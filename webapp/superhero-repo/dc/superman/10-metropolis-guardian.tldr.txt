• Metropolis, the City of Tomorrow: Superman's adopted home and primary responsibility
• Daily duties: intercepting falling elevators, extinguishing skyscraper fires, stopping high-speed chases
• Uses freezing breath and other powers in precise, targeted interventions
• Partners closely with the Metropolis Police Department and city officials
• Respects laws; his presence inspires order, not chaos
• Clark Kent's reporting keeps him grounded in the stories of individual Metropolitans
• Neighborhoods range from the Financial District to Suicide Slum — Superman serves them all
• During crises, a red and blue streak in the sky restores hope and calm to the city
• Superman is the soul of Metropolis: its ideals embodied, its dreams protected
