• Superman: founding member and moral compass of the Justice League
• Leads not through authority but through the quiet strength of his character
• Serves as mediator between Batman's tactics and Wonder Woman's divine power
• League's shared values are stronger than their combined physical power
• Superman's red and blue suit provides hope to teammates and public alike
• Shares the "World's Finest" partnership with Batman: light and darkness in balance
• Encourages heroes to serve humanity, not rule over it
• First to arrive at disaster, last to leave — always offers reassuring words
• Teaches the League: power is meaningless without purpose rooted in compassion
