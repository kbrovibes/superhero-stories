• Clark Kent works as a reporter at the Daily Planet in Metropolis
• Editor-in-Chief Perry White demands excellence and journalistic integrity
• Lois Lane: fearless investigative reporter, Clark's closest partner and inspiration
• Jimmy Olsen: enthusiastic young photographer; Clark serves as his mentor
• Clark maintains his mild-mannered disguise through slouch, glasses, self-deprecating humor
• Uses chaos of breaking news stories to slip away and change into Superman
• Super-hearing keeps him one step ahead of the competition
• The Daily Planet grounds Clark in the stories of ordinary people he protects
• Lesson: heroism sometimes requires only a pen and the courage to ask hard questions
