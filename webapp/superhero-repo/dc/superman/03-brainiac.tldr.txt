• Brainiac: cybernetic entity called the Collector of Worlds; shrinks cities before destroying planets
• Operates from a massive skull-shaped ship — mobile laboratory and tomb for collected civilizations
• Responsible for shrinking Kandor, the capital city of Krypton, into a bottle
• Targets Metropolis as the next jewel in his macabre collection
• Discovery of the Bottled City of Kandor brings Superman both grief and hope
• Brainiac's technology uses advanced robotics, hyper-intelligent AI, and bio-organic enhancements
• Battles are mental and physical: mazes of traps, drones, and psychological manipulation using Kandor
• Superman's empathy and refusal to see people as data consistently defeats Brainiac's cold logic
• Superman keeps the Bottled City of Kandor safe within the Fortress of Solitude
