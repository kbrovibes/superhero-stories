• Kara Zor-El grows up in Argo City on Krypton with her parents Zor-El and Alura — a scientist's daughter who loves languages and stargazing
• When Krypton's core begins to fail, her father builds a small rocket and launches Kara toward Earth
• Kara's last memory of home is her mother's voice saying "Be brave. Be kind. Be who you are."
• She arrives in Midvale years after her cousin Kal-El, who has already become Superman
• Superman meets her ship in a field and welcomes her to Earth with a warm coat and kind words
• Earth's yellow sun gives Kara incredible powers: flight, super-strength, heat vision, and freeze breath
• She discovers she is just as powerful as Superman — but wisdom takes longer to learn than flying
• Kara sews the Kryptonian S-shield onto a blue suit with a red cape and takes the name Supergirl
• Her first full-speed flight rattles every window in Midvale — and she laughs with surprise at her own strength
