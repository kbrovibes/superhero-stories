• Kara's heat vision fires accidentally for the first time and melts the kitchen toaster — she decides she needs practice
• Superman explains: heat vision runs on concentration and emotion; freeze breath is its precise cold opposite
• He admits he once accidentally froze a Manitoba lake at age twelve, which makes Kara feel better
• She practices at the Midvale salvage yard — tin cans for heat vision, water barrels for freeze breath
• She masters beam width from a broad warm wash to a needle-thin cutting line, and writes her name in Kryptonian on a steel panel
• For freeze breath she learns to coat surfaces with ice without shattering them or affecting adjacent objects
• A chemical fire at Midvale Harbor freight terminal puts her skills to a real test
• She uses freeze breath on the chemical sections and precise heat vision on the electrical wiring — the whole fire is contained in four minutes
• The fire chief calls it the most precise fire suppression he's ever seen; Kara mentions the toaster and decides not to explain
