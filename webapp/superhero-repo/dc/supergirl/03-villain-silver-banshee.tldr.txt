• Siobhan McDougal, a Midvale High student, touches an ancient Irish artifact called the Caoránach Crest and is transformed into Silver Banshee
• Her hair turns white, her skin goes pale, and she gains a scream so powerful it can shatter steel
• Silver Banshee attacks downtown Midvale, her sonic scream knocking police officers off their feet
• Kara flies in despite the scream being painfully overwhelming, using her Kryptonian durability to push through
• She positions herself between Banshee and the crowd, acting as a living sound barrier to protect bystanders
• Using x-ray vision, Kara spots the Caoránach Crest lying in a nearby doorway where Siobhan dropped it
• Instead of fighting, Kara talks — telling Siobhan the truth and pointing out where the Crest fell
• Siobhan retrieves the Crest; the spirit quiets and the white energy dissolves
• S.T.A.R. Labs secures the artifact; Kara keeps checking in on Siobhan because listening worked
