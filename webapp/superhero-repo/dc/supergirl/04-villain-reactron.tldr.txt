• Ben Krull is a former soldier rebuilt with a nuclear-powered armor suit called Reactron, specifically designed to threaten Kryptonians
• With Superman busy in Metropolis, Reactron targets Supergirl and attacks Midvale at dawn
• Kara engages him at the old rail bridge, feeling the nuclear radiation drain her cells
• His attacks are powerful but predictable — she backs off and studies his pattern
• Kara changes tactics: wide unpredictable arcs, using steel girders as cover, drawing him away from populated Midvale
• She lures him to the quarry outside town, where she can use the rock walls and geometry in her favor
• Looping behind him at superspeed, she strikes the nuclear core housing and disables the kryptonite generator
• Kara pins Reactron against the quarry wall and calls the DEO, who takes him into custody
• She spends the next afternoon at S.T.A.R. Labs studying gold kryptonite and nuclear-suit tech to be ready next time
