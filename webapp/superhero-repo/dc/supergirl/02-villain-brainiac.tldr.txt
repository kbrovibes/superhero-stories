• A cold deep-space signal reaches Kara — she recognizes the frequency as Kryptonian and traces it to Brainiac's skull-shaped spacecraft
• Brainiac is a machine intelligence who travels the galaxy shrinking cities and sealing them in glass bottles — he already stole Kandor from Krypton
• He targets Midvale for his collection and begins deploying skull-drones over the city
• Kara flies straight at the ship and battles hundreds of drones with heat vision and raw speed
• Brainiac fires a kryptonite-radiation beam that drains Kara's strength and she begins to fall
• Remembering Kandor and what Brainiac stole from Krypton, she pushes through the pain
• Kara reaches the ship's emitter array and reverses the polarity, collapsing the shrink beam and restoring Midvale
• The kryptonite radiation vents into space; Brainiac's ship retreats, unable to account for her cleverness
• Kara watches the skull-ship vanish into the stars, hands shaking — but the city is safe
