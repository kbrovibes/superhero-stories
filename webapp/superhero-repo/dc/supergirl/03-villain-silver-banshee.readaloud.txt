Okay, here is a story about a hero who wins not by punching the hardest — but by LISTENING. Are you ready?

There was a girl at Midvale High named Siobhan McDougal. She seemed normal enough — until she found an old artifact in her family's attic. It was called the Caoránach Crest, and it had been sitting there for a very, very long time. When Siobhan picked it up — WHOOOMP — an ancient spirit poured right into her!

Her hair went white as snow. Her skin went pale as chalk. And she discovered she could produce a SCREAM. Not a regular scream. A scream so powerful it could shatter windows, crack pavement, and send grown adults flying backward off their feet.

SKREEEEE!

She called herself Silver Banshee, and she was NOT happy.

When she attacked downtown Midvale, that scream hit Kara like a wall of sound — SKREEEEE — so loud and sharp it was almost impossible to move through. Even with Kryptonian super-hearing, this was a LOT. But Kara pushed forward anyway. She planted herself between Silver Banshee and the crowd below like a human — well, Kryptonian — wall.

SKREEEEE! The scream hit Kara again. She winced. She stayed put.

While Kara held her ground, she used her x-ray vision to scan the streets below. There — two blocks away in a shop doorway — was the Caoránach Crest, lying on the ground where Siobhan had dropped it.

And Kara had an idea.

She stopped fighting. She started talking.

"Siobhan," she said calmly. "I know what happened to you. I know you didn't ask for this. And I know where the Crest is."

Silver Banshee froze. Nobody had ever done that before. Nobody had listened.

Kara used her freeze breath — FWOOOOSH — to cool down the spiraling white energy around Banshee, containing the worst of it. Then she pointed to the doorway.

Siobhan picked up the Crest. The spirit quieted like a TV switching off. The white energy faded.

They sat on the curb afterward. Two girls who had both ended up somewhere very different from where they started. Siobhan said being a monster was mostly just exhausting. Kara said she understood more than Siobhan might think.

S.T.A.R. Labs came to secure the Crest later. And every few weeks after that, Kara stopped by to check in on Siobhan.

Because sometimes the bravest thing you can do is stop punching and start listening.

Kara knew that now.
