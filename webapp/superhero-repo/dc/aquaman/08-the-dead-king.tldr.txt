• Atlan, the Dead King — the first ruler of Atlantis; his rage once sank the continent
• His tomb: hidden beneath Arctic glaciers; entrance is a massive obsidian archway coated in frost that never melts
• Arthur travels alone to the icy Arctic to confront the awakened Dead King
• Atlan rises from a frozen kelp throne, wielding a dark scepter; communicates through crushing psychic visions
• Visions show Atlantis falling again and a king who failed because of his dual heritage
• The Dead King commands ice and the essence of cold — each strike numbs Arthur's limbs
• Arthur uses the Trident of Neptune to channel warmth and the vitality of the living sea
• Victory: Arthur does not destroy Atlan — he grounds the Dead King's resentment back into the earth
• Arthur claims Atlan's dark scepter — a relic of a gone era — and escapes the collapsing tomb
• Lesson: true kingship means confronting the darkest parts of your heritage and choosing a different path
