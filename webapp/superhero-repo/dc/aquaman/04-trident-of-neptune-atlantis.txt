As the King of Atlantis, Aquaman carries a legendary weapon called the Trident of Neptune. This is no ordinary trident; it is a magical artifact made of a special gold-colored metal that never rusts. The Trident allows Aquaman to control the water itself, creating waves, calming storms, and even forming paths through the sea. It is a symbol of his authority and his commitment to protecting the ocean kingdom.

The City of Atlantis is a breathtaking place located deep beneath the Atlantic Ocean. It is filled with majestic towers made of coral and shimmering glass that glows with a soft, blue light. The Atlanteans use advanced technology that is powered by the currents of the sea. There are beautiful gardens of seaweed, playgrounds where children swim with sea turtles, and grand palaces where Aquaman and Queen Mera look after their people.

Life in Atlantis is very peaceful and full of wonder. The streets are filled with people swimming from place to place, and there are even underwater vehicles that look like colorful fish. Aquaman works hard to ensure that the city stays hidden and safe from any trouble. He also makes sure that the environment around the city is healthy, with plenty of clean water and happy sea creatures for everyone to enjoy.

The Trident of Neptune and the City of Atlantis are the heart of Aquaman's world. The Trident gives him the power he needs to be a hero, and the city provides him with a home and a people to protect. Arthur is very proud of his kingdom and loves sharing its beauty with his friends in the Justice League. He reminds everyone that the ocean is a magical place that we all need to take care of for the future.
