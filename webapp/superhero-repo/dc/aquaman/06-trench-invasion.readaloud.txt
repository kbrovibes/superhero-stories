DEEP in the ocean — past where sunlight reaches, past where most submarines dare to go — there is a place called the midnight zone. The water there is so cold and so dark and so heavy that it would crush almost anything flat. Arthur Curry loves it down here. But tonight? Tonight something terrifying is surging UP from the deepest trenches of all!

They are called the Trench — creatures of pure hunger from the darkest crevices of the Atlantic. Their eyes glow bioluminescent in the blackness, blinking like cold blue-green stars. Their teeth are as sharp as needles. Their claws can shred reinforced steel. And there are HUNDREDS of them, rising from the deep like a living tide — SPLASH CRASH SNAP!

Arthur grips the golden Trident of Neptune and calls out across the ocean. WHOOOOOM — the signal goes out through the water! Great white sharks come racing in from three directions. Orcas — the wolves of the sea, the most powerful hunters in the ocean — form up in a charging formation. The battle explodes in a swirling storm of silt and shadow and silver! CRASH CRASH CRACK — Arthur moves through the chaos like a current, fast and fluid, his trident carving arcs of golden light through the dark.

The matriarch of the Trench swarm — enormous, ancient, furious — lunges directly at Arthur! He spins, drives the trident deep — KABOOOM — and a shockwave of energy blasts outward, sending the horde tumbling back in every direction!

But there are too many! Too many even for Arthur and his sea allies! So Arthur does something drastic. He drives the butt of the Trident into the seafloor with everything he has — and geothermal vents ERUPT! Volcanic heat and blazing light explode upward through the deep! The Trench creatures, whose eyes can only handle total darkness, screech and scatter — retreating back into the deepest crevices where the light can't follow.

Arthur floats in the suddenly quiet dark, catching his breath. A true king watches over every corner of his kingdom. Even the scary ones.
