• Black Manta — Aquaman's deep-sea rival; no superpowers, but a brilliant inventor
• Signature gear: large silver helmet with glowing red eyes that fire powerful energy beams
• Suit features: jet thrusters for Atlantean-matching speed; mechanical arms for heavy lifting
• Spends time searching for hidden ocean-floor treasures and ancient technology
• Aquaman defends himself with super-strength, the Trident of Neptune, and sea allies (dolphins, giant octopus)
• Their rivalry: skill and technology vs. natural power and the authority of the sea
• Aquaman hopes Black Manta will one day use his inventiveness to protect the ocean rather than exploit it
• Lesson: gadgets are no match for purpose and a hero who knows their home
