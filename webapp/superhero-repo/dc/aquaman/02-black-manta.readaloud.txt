The ocean floor is full of mysteries — ancient shipwrecks, glowing minerals, technology so old it has no name. Most people can't get anywhere near it. But there is one person who has built a suit specifically designed to go anywhere in the deep — and he wants to take everything he finds! His name is Black Manta!

Black Manta has no superpowers. Zero. Not even one! What he has instead is a brilliant, restless inventor's mind. He designed and built his entire high-tech deep-sea suit by himself! And what a suit it is: jet thrusters that let him swim as fast as any Atlantean — WHOOOOSH! Mechanical arms strong enough to lift massive objects from the seafloor. And the most striking feature of all: a large silver helmet with two glowing red eyes that can fire powerful energy beams straight through the water. ZAP ZAP! It looks like something from a science fiction movie — but it's completely real!

When Black Manta and Aquaman meet beneath the waves, the whole ocean lights up! Manta fires his energy beams while Arthur uses his tremendous strength and the golden Trident of Neptune to fight back. CLANG CLANG! But here's Arthur's secret advantage: the ocean is his home, and EVERYONE who lives there is on his side. A school of dolphins darts around Manta's helmet, blocking his targeting. A giant octopus wraps massive arms around Manta's mechanical limbs — SQUEEEEEZE! A pod of orcas moves in formation like the best Navy anyone has ever seen!

Black Manta is fighting one hero. Arthur is fighting with an entire ocean behind him.

Arthur hopes that someday, Manta will realize all that brilliant inventing talent could do SO much good if it were used to protect the ocean instead of plunder it. The sea needs smart defenders, not raiders. But until that day comes, the King of Atlantis will always be there — watching, protecting, defending every reef and every creature who calls the deep their home!
