• Mera — Queen of Atlantis; possesses Xebelian hydrokinesis (the power to shape and weaponize water)
• Arthur and Mera investigate localized whirlpools disrupting trade routes between the seven kingdoms
• Whirlpools are artificial — created by rogue mercenaries from a Xebel military splinter cell
• Mercenaries wield high-pressure sonic cannons; target Mera first as the primary water-control threat
• Mera shapes hard-water shields and crystalline spears to neutralize enemy weapons
• Arthur fights on the front line with raw physical power and the Trident of Neptune
• Their coordination is seamless: two halves of one unit, forged by years of shared combat and trust
• Outcome: mercenaries flee, whirlpools subside, trade routes stabilized
• Atlantis needs both Arthur and Mera: the protector and the strategist, the trident and the wave
• Lesson: the strongest partnerships are built on trust, complementary strengths, and years of standing together
