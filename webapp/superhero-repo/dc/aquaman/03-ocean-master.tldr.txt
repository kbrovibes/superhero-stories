• Ocean Master — real name Orm; Arthur Curry's half-brother, also a prince of Atlantis
• Orm believes Atlantis should be the most powerful and most isolated kingdom in the world
• Takes the name Ocean Master; wears a purple and silver suit with a flowing cape
• Carries his own trident capable of controlling ocean currents and creating massive whirlpools
• His rivalry with Arthur is ideological: isolation vs. cooperation with the surface world
• Their disagreements are settled through tests of strength and wisdom, not pure destruction
• Example challenges: racing through glowing jellyfish fields; helping a stranded whale first
• Arthur continues to care for Orm and believes they could one day rule together in peace
• Lesson: family members can have opposite views — kindness and cooperation are stronger than any trident
