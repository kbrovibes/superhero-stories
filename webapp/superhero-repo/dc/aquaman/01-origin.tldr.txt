• Arthur Curry — born to a surface-world lighthouse keeper father and Atlantis's queen mother
• Half-human, half-Atlantean: can swim at Olympic-surpassing speed, breathe underwater, withstand deep-sea pressure
• Telepathic ability: communicates with all sea life, from the smallest fish to the largest blue whale
• Realizes he is the rightful King of Atlantis and accepts his destiny as a hero
• Costume: shimmering orange scales and green leggings for swift underwater movement
• Patrols the oceans: protects coral reefs, ensures safety of all sea creatures
• Guardian of seventy percent of Earth's surface
• Bridge between two worlds: loves the lighthouse on the shore and the palaces of Atlantis equally
• Lesson: we are all connected, no matter where we live — land or sea
