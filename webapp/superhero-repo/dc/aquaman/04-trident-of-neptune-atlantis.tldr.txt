• Trident of Neptune — Aquaman's legendary weapon; a magical artifact made of gold-colored metal that never rusts
• Powers: controls water itself — creates waves, calms storms, forges paths through the sea
• Symbol of Arthur's authority as King of Atlantis
• City of Atlantis — hidden deep beneath the Atlantic Ocean
• Atlantean architecture: majestic coral-and-glass towers that glow with soft blue light
• Technology powered by ocean currents; underwater vehicles shaped like colorful fish
• Life in Atlantis: people swim the streets, children play alongside sea turtles, grand palaces house the royal court
• Queen Mera rules alongside Arthur
• Arthur protects the city's secrecy and the health of its surrounding ocean environment
• Lesson: a true king's power lies in what he protects, not what he controls
