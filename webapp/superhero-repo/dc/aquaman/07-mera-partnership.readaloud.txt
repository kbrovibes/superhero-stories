Arthur Curry is a powerful king. But beside him, the ocean has an even more formidable force: Mera, Queen of Atlantis. And when these two work together? Watch out, because NOTHING in the seven seas can stand against them!

Mera comes from a distant underwater realm called Xebel, and she has a gift called hydrokinesis — the ability to reach out her hands and SHAPE water itself as if it were clay. She can harden it into shields as strong as steel — CLANG! She can sharpen it into crystalline spears that fly with perfect aim — WHOOOOSH! She can build walls of water, create underwater cyclones, weaponize the very ocean itself. She is an extraordinary warrior AND a sharper tactician than most generals either of them has ever met.

One day, strange artificial whirlpools began appearing in the trade routes between the seven underwater kingdoms — precise, controlled, spinning spirals that were disrupting ships and merchants and everything flowing between the realms. Arthur and Mera dove down together to investigate. WHORL WHORL WHORL! At the center of the disturbance: a group of rogue mercenaries from a forgotten Xebel military unit — armed with high-pressure sonic cannons! BOOM! They targeted Mera FIRST. Because they were smart enough to know she was the biggest threat.

That was their mistake. While Arthur launched himself at the front line like a living torpedo — CRASH CRASH — Mera turned the entire ocean into her arsenal. Crystalline water-spears shot straight at the mercenaries' cannons — SMASH! When an attack slipped past Arthur, Mera's hard-water shield was already there — CLANG! When Mera needed cover, Arthur had already cleared the path! They didn't even need to talk. After years of fighting side by side, they move like one single force!

The mercenaries scattered. The whirlpools dissolved. Trade routes: safe. Arthur and Mera: undefeated. The King and Queen of Atlantis: two hearts that move as one!
