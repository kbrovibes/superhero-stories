• Setting: the midnight zone — the dark, freezing, crushing deep of the Atlantic
• The Trench — savage creatures of the deep; bioluminescent eyes, needle teeth, steel-rending claws
• Arthur grips the Trident of Neptune as hundreds of Trench creatures surge upward
• Calls apex predators to his aid: great white sharks and orcas
• A large matriarch of the swarm lunges — Arthur drives the trident into her; shockwave scatters the horde
• Overwhelmed by sheer numbers; Arthur slams his trident into the seabed, triggering geothermal vents
• Volcanic heat and light disorient the light-sensitive Trench; they retreat into the deep crevices
• Arthur returns to Atlantis, sobered by the reminder that ruling means protecting even the monsters from themselves
• Lesson: a true king defends all of his kingdom — even the darkest and most frightening parts
