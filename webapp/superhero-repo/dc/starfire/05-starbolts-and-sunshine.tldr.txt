• Koriand'r's powers come from absorbing solar energy — any star's light powers her, not just Tamaran's twin suns
• The Citadel's experiments amplified her abilities far beyond typical Tamaranean levels
• She generates emerald starbolts: focused bursts of solar energy fired from her hands
• She flies under her own power, fast enough to cross from Jump City to New York in under a minute
• In space she needs no oxygen — vacuum is as natural to her as air
• Her powers are linked to her emotions: joy and love double her starbolt output
• Robin notices this early; Cyborg measures it in tests that confirm the connection
• This means attacking her friends accidentally makes her stronger
• She aims at engines, weapons, and foundations rather than at people — she always thinks past the fight to what comes after
