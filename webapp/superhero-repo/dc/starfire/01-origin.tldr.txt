• Koriand'r is born a princess on the sun-powered planet Tamaran, beloved by her people
• Her older sister Blackfire, jealous of Koriand'r's flying and starbolt powers, secretly betrays her to the Citadel empire
• Koriand'r spends years as a prisoner aboard Citadel warships, far from home
• Citadel scientists try to strip her powers — instead the experiment amplifies them enormously
• She breaks free with blinding emerald light, blasts through the ship hull, and flies under her own power for the first time
• She crash-lands in Jump City, California, and meets the young hero Robin (Dick Grayson)
• One touch lets her absorb any language — she learns English from Robin instantly
• She chooses to stay on Earth, taking the name Starfire
• Fighting from love, not just duty, she becomes one of Earth's most joyful and powerful defenders
