• Years later, new young heroes come to Titans Tower to train; each Titan teaches what they know best

• She takes trainees to the Tower roof to name the stars; she points out Tamaran's star in the southwest
• She tells her story: falling toward Earth, one hand extended in a parking lot, a language entering in one touch
• Trainee Ari, who can make force fields but fears open spaces, says she doesn't feel brave enough
• Starfire says: bravery means doing what matters before you feel ready
• Ari asks how Starfire left her whole planet; Starfire says she left some family and found the rest
• Belonging somewhere you chose is the deepest belonging there is
• The lesson passes on: every Titan was once new, and the Tower is home for anyone brave enough to stay
