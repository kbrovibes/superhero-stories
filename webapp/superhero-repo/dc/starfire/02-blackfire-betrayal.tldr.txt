• Blackfire arrives at Titans Tower claiming she searched the galaxy to visit her sister Starfire
• She charms the whole team — winning races, playing games, using Koriand'r's old nickname
• Behind the warmth she is spying on the Tower and gathering information about Starfire's powers
• Robin, trained by Batman, notices the suspicious behavior and warns Koriand'r
• Blackfire reveals a new deal: she will hand Starfire over to a Gordanian warlord in exchange for a Centauri Moon Diamond
• Starfire and Blackfire battle in the sky above Jump City — emerald and violet starbolts lighting the night
• Starfire wins, fighting from love rather than ambition, and drives Blackfire away
• She sends her sister off without anger, leaving the door open for change someday
• Robin sits with her on the Tower roof, and friendship proves stronger than any old wound
