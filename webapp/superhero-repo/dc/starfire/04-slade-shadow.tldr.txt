• Slade, wearing his orange-and-black mask, is the Titans' most patient and calculating enemy — he plans for months, not hours
• He studies Starfire closely, learning that her powers are fueled by emotion and joy
• Slade engineers a fake crisis: civilians trapped in a Jump City warehouse to split the team up
• EMP bursts disable Cyborg; Starfire is isolated to face a Slade combat robot alone
• The robot has light-dampening shields that disperse her starbolts, and it tracks her flight precisely
• Rather than freezing up, Starfire switches strategy — using her Tamaranean physical strength instead of starbolts
• She uses the robot's own momentum against it and destroys it in four minutes
• The Titans rescue the civilians but cannot catch Slade, who is always ahead of a direct chase
• Cyborg reverse-engineers a robot fragment, uncovering Slade's methods; Starfire learns her heart under pressure is her greatest power
