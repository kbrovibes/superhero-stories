Are you ready to travel across the whole entire galaxy? Because that is exactly where this story begins!

Way, way out in space — past stars and asteroid belts and swirling clouds of gas — there is a planet called Tamaran. It has TWO suns. Can you imagine? Twice the sunshine, twice the warmth. And the people who live there, the Tamaraneans, can soak up all that sunlight and shoot it out of their hands as blazing energy!

Our hero was born there. Her name is Koriand'r, and she was a princess with hair like a comet tail and a smile that could warm up any room in the galaxy.

But here is the problem. Her older sister, Blackfire, was jealous. Blackfire could not fly. Blackfire could not shoot starbolts. And she was so jealous that she made a terrible deal — she handed Koriand'r over to a scary space empire called the Citadel. Her own sister!

For years, Koriand'r was a prisoner on cold, dark starships. But she did NOT give up.

The Citadel scientists ran experiments on her — trying to take her powers away. BIG MISTAKE. Instead, her powers EXPLODED. BOOM! Emerald green light burst from her whole body! She punched right through the starship wall — CRASH — and flew out into open space under her own power for the very first time!

WHOOOOSH! She streaked across the galaxy like a green shooting star.

She aimed for a small blue-green planet called Earth — and she came in fast. KABLAM! She smashed into a parking lot in Jump City, California, and left a smoking crater in the asphalt.

She stood up. Dusted off her hair. Looked around. Humans were staring. She had no idea what they were saying!

But Koriand'r had an incredible gift. One touch — just one — and she could absorb any language. A young hero named Dick Grayson, who went by Robin, stepped forward and offered his hand.

She touched it. And WHOOSH — English filled her mind like sunlight filling a dark room.

She looked at Robin and said: "My name is Koriand'r. I am free."

From that day on, she chose to call herself Starfire. She chose Earth as her home. She chose to protect it — not because she had to, but because she WANTED to, with her whole bright heart. Robin introduced her to other young heroes — Cyborg, Raven, and Beast Boy — and together they formed the Teen Titans. A team. A family. Friends who understood what it meant to be different and still be wonderful.

Because you cannot keep a Tamaranean princess locked up. You cannot take away a heart full of sunlight.

And when Starfire fights for you, she fights with pure joy.

STARBOLT! Watch out, bad guys. Here she comes!
