• Jump City's power grid fails in a geometric pattern — Robin recognizes it as a trap with Titans Tower at the center
• Slade has planted three robotic sentinels beneath the city, each bearing his orange-and-black insignia
• As the Titans split to investigate, the sentinels activate and knock out their communicators one by one
• Cyborg fights a sentinel in the East Side tunnels and defeats it by attacking its motor joints directly
• Robin faces the second sentinel at the waterfront station — Slade watches silently from the shadows above
• Robin realizes the sentinel wasn't trying to hurt him; it was studying his fighting style for Slade
• Starfire and Beast Boy destroy the third sentinel together — Beast Boy as a ram, Starfire with starbolts
• Slade vanishes but leaves an orange coin on Robin's windowsill; Raven tells Robin they must know themselves better than Slade does
