• Scott Lang: trapped for five years in the Quantum Realm — felt like only five hours
• His unique experience gave the Avengers the key insight: time behaves differently at subatomic levels
• Plan called the Time Heist: travel back through history to retrieve the six Infinity Stones
• Scott collaborated with Tony Stark and Bruce Banner to build the Time Space GPS and Quantum Tunnel
• Scott served as primary navigator through micro-singularities and quantum vortexes
• In New York: Scott used his tiny size to sabotage the arc reactor of a past version of Tony Stark
• Secured the Space Stone through that infiltration — the heist's most critical moment
• Provided humor and grounded humanity alongside Hulk (Bruce Banner) and Thor
• The Time Heist was only possible because of Scott's knowledge of the Quantum Realm
• Scott emerged as a key architect of the universe's restoration — the smallest Avenger was essential
