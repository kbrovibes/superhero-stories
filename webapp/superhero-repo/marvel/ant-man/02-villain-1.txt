One sunny afternoon in the city, a cackle echoed through the streets as the Green Goblin zoomed by on his emerald glider. This version of the Goblin wasn't looking for a big fight; instead, he was being a giant mischief-maker! He was using his "Pumpkin Pranks"—hollowed-out gadgets that sprayed colorful glitter and sticky slime on all the statues in the park.

Ant-Man saw the glittery mess from a distance and knew he had to act. He didn't want the park to be ruined for the kids playing there. Scott tapped his helmet and called out to his friend, Antony the flying carpenter ant. Together, they dove into the swarm of glitter. Scott shrunk down so small that the Green Goblin couldn't even see him coming.

As the Goblin prepared to toss another "Slime Sparkler" at the fountain, Ant-Man landed right on the glider's control panel. He used his engineering skills to gently unplug a few wires. Suddenly, the glider started blowing giant soap bubbles instead of flying fast! The Green Goblin was so surprised by the bubbles that he started laughing along with the crowd.

Seeing the fun, Ant-Man grew back to his normal size and helped the Goblin realize that making people smile was much better than making a mess. The Goblin agreed to use his glider to help clean up the glitter, turning the whole event into a surprise party for the neighborhood. Ant-Man waved goodbye, happy that a potential problem turned into a day of laughter.
