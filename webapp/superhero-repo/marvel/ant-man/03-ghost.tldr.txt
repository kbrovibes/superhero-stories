• Ava Starr, known as Ghost: caught in an explosion caused by her father Elihas Starr's failed experiment
• Elihas Starr was a former partner of Hank Pym, dismissed for unorthodox methods
• Explosion left Ava in "molecular disequilibrium" — constantly flickering in and out of phase with reality
• Ghost's powers: phase through solid objects, turn invisible, deliver strikes that bypass defenses
• But every moment of existence was agonizing pain; her cells perpetually torn apart and rebuilt
• Sought energy from the Quantum Realm as the only potential cure — clashed with Scott and the Pym family
• Scott couldn't track her using suit sensors alone; ant allies sensed her vibrations when invisible
• Scott saw past the villain label — Ava was a victim, not a conqueror; empathy complicated the mission
• Resolution: Janet van Dyne, survivor of decades in the Quantum Realm, provided quantum energy to stabilize Ava
• Lesson: heroism is as much about healing as fighting; even the most broken can find redemption
