• Pym Particles: discovered by Dr. Hank Pym; alter distance between atoms while preserving mass and density
• Shrunk Scott Lang delivers a punch with full-sized momentum — concentrated into a pinhead-sized point
• Versatility: applies to Scott, his equipment, or entire vehicles; belt holds "miniature" gear grown to full size
• Cybernetic helmet enables communication with ants via pheromones and electromagnetic signals
• Different ant species offer different tactical roles: carpenter ants (flight), fire ants (offense), crazy ants (electronics sabotage)
• Ant-thony, Ant-tonio, and Ulysses: named ant companions Scott honors and mourns
• Key synergy: thousands of coordinated ants build bridges, carry objects, swarm security systems
• Mastery required Scott to move past skepticism and develop genuine respect for the insect world
• Risk factor: imprecise calibration can lose the user in the subatomic void
• Scott's role: steward of the Pym legacy — protecting the formula from those who would weaponize it
