• Giant-Man protocol: reverses the Pym Particles, growing Scott to sixty feet tall
• First activation: high-stakes confrontation at Leipzig-Halle Airport — stunned the world
• At giant size: buildings become toy blocks, vehicles become pebbles, Quinjets become flies to swat
• Physical cost: enormous energy drain, cardiovascular strain, risk of losing consciousness
• Mental challenge: sensory overload, spatial disorientation, radical change in perspective
• Tactical value: living shield, heavy artillery support, massive distraction for teammates
• Leipzig battle: Scott's transformation gave allies the crucial window to escape
• Later: Giant-Man fought alongside Avengers in final battle against Thanos — crushed alien leviathans
• Giant-Man is a last resort — "break glass in case of emergency" option
• Lesson: potential is not limited by size; courage to grow when needed and humility to shrink when not
