• Quantum Realm: dimension beyond the subatomic where distance and time lose conventional meaning
• Scott was once lost there for five years (felt like five hours) — transformed from fear to frontier
• Navigation requires Pym Particles and mental fortitude; environments shift without warning
• Time flows in unpredictable loops — can age or revert a person without warning
• Ecosystem of life forms: microscopic tardigrades the size of skyscrapers, intelligent civilizations in quantum folds
• The Quantum Void: region of absolute nothingness between layers; where Scott first sensed Janet van Dyne's presence
• Janet van Dyne survived decades in the Quantum Realm by adapting to its unique energies
• Janet's knowledge was essential for the Time Heist navigation
• Ancient threats lurk: creatures of pure energy; every action causes ripples in the macroscopic world
• Lesson: time is the most precious resource; connections and the people you protect give life meaning
