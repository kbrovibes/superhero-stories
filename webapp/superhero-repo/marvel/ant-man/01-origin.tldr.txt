• Scott Lang: skilled electronics engineer, ex-convict, desperate father to daughter Cassie
• Broke into the home of retired scientist Dr. Hank Pym; found a strange high-tech suit
• Accidentally triggered the shrinking mechanism — first contact with Pym Particles
• Pym had been watching; saw in Scott a clever, family-driven man fit for the mission
• Offered Scott a choice: face consequences or become the new Ant-Man and stop a threat
• Grueling training: shrink/grow on command, master the cybernetic helmet to communicate with ants
• Bonded closely with a carpenter ant named Ant-thony, his primary aerial steed
• Mentored by Hope van Dyne, Pym's daughter — a combat expert and master of the technology
• Key insight: being small shifts perspective; heroism doesn't require towering stature
• Lesson: everyone deserves a second chance; the smallest hero can make the biggest impact
