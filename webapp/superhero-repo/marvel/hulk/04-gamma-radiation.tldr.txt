• Gamma rays: most energetic form of light; typically lethal to humans due to massive cellular damage
• Bruce Banner's dormant Gamma-Gene allowed him to survive and adapt rather than perish
• Radiation unlocked a primal force in Banner's DNA — the source of the Hulk's near-limitless strength
• Each transformation floods Banner's cells with gamma energy, causing exponential growth in bone density and muscle
• Green skin: direct result of radiation interacting with his pigments — a permanent mark of gamma energy
• Gamma radiation amplifies the subconscious: Banner's repressed anger became the Hulk; others' ambition became villainy
• Banner spent years trying to "cure" himself, viewing radiation as a poison; eventually accepted it as part of his identity
• Legacy extends beyond Banner: gamma radiation created She-Hulk, Red Hulk, Abomination, the Leader
• A whole sub-species of "Gamma-Mutates" now exists in the world
• Gamma energy: a wild card — infinite potential and terrifying danger; fuels the entire legend of the Hulk
