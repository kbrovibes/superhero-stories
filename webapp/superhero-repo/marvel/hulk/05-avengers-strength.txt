The Avengers are a team of the world’s mightiest heroes, and each member brings a special skill to the group. When the challenge requires pure, unstoppable force, they look to the Hulk. He is the team’s "anchor," the hero who can stand his ground against the largest threats in the universe. Whether it’s an invading army of robots or a giant asteroid heading for Earth, the Hulk is the one who steps forward to take the hit.

One of the Hulk's most important jobs on the team is rescue. When a bridge is about to collapse or a skyscraper is damaged, the Hulk can literally hold the structure together with his bare hands while the other Avengers get people to safety. His teammates, like Captain America and Iron Man, trust him completely. They know that as long as the Hulk is there, they have a shield that can't be broken and a hammer that can't be stopped.

Hulk also shows his teamwork by handling the "heavy lifting" during battles. If a villain has a massive shield generator, Hulk is the one who smashes it. If a giant monster is attacking the city, Hulk is the one who wrestles it to the ground. His presence on the battlefield changes everything, as enemies often focus all their attention on him, allowing the other Avengers to execute their clever plans.

Being part of a team has been good for the Hulk too. It has given him friends who see him as a hero rather than a monster. He enjoys helping his "friends from work," as he sometimes calls them. By using his incredible strength as part of a team, the Hulk shows that even the most powerful person is even stronger when they work together with others. He is a true guardian of Earth and its greatest protector.
