• Maestro: a future version of the Hulk who survived a nuclear holocaust by absorbing centuries of extra radiation
• Ruler of a ruined city called Dystopia — the Hulk who lived long enough to become a villain
• Physically larger and more muscular than the present Hulk; paired with Banner's cold, ruthless intellect
• Wears trophies of fallen heroes: Captain America's shield, Iron Man's helmet, Thor's hammer
• Master manipulator — maintains power through sadistic efficiency, crushing any rebellion
• Present-day Hulk pulled into the future; confronted with his own potential for darkness
• Maestro mocks heroism as childish and futile; their battle is a clash between a man fighting for his soul and one who sold his
• Hulk defeats the Maestro but leaves the encounter deeply shaken
• Legacy of the Maestro haunts Banner: a constant reminder that power without morality leads only to ruin
• Dystopia is the mirror of what the world becomes if the Hulk ever truly stops caring
