• Emil Blonsky: KGB agent, cold and calculating, obsessed with the power Bruce Banner accidentally gained
• Infiltrates the gamma testing facility; deliberately exposes himself to a far higher radiation dose than Banner received
• Transformation permanent: turns Blonsky into the Abomination — scaly, reptilian horror with ridges on his skull
• Unlike the Hulk, Abomination retains Blonsky's sharp, malicious intellect and never reverts to human form
• First clash levels an entire town — ground-shaking punches with the force of earthquakes
• Abomination starts stronger; designed for combat, he taunts the Hulk for lacking control
• Hulk's unique biology is the turning point: the angrier Hulk gets, the stronger he gets
• Abomination's mocking fuels the Hulk's fury; tide turns as the Hulk's power surpasses all tactical moves
• Final surge of rage: Hulk cracks the Abomination's hide and sends him crashing through a mountain range
• Abomination becomes the Hulk's most persistent foe — a dark mirror of what Banner could have been
