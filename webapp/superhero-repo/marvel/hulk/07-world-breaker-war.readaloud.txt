Have you ever heard thunder so loud it rattled every window? Multiply that by a million, and you'll have a tiny idea of what it was like when the World Breaker came home.

The Hulk who landed back on Earth after his time on Sakaar was not the Hulk anyone remembered. He was bigger. He was angrier. He was so powerful that each footstep left a crater in the ground. Scientists had a name for this new level of power: the World Breaker. And every superhero on Earth knew they were in serious, serious trouble.

His first stop? The Moon, where the powerful Inhuman king Black Bolt was waiting. Black Bolt's voice alone can shatter mountains. He screamed with everything he had. The Hulk walked through the blast like it was a light breeze. ONE punch. Done.

Then the Hulk turned toward New York City — and Earth's mightiest heroes lined up to stop him.

Iron Man climbed into his biggest, most powerful armor — the Hulkbuster suit, built specifically to fight one opponent. CRUNCH! Torn apart like paper. Doctor Strange called upon his most ancient and powerful mystic spells. Not nearly enough. The Hulk captured them all and turned Madison Square Garden — the famous arena in the heart of New York — into a gladiator ring, making the heroes who had sent him away fight to understand how it felt.

And then came the Sentry. The Sentry was said to have the strength of a million exploding suns! Their battle was so enormous it sent shockwaves crashing across the entire Eastern Seaboard.

But right at the peak of it all — right at the moment when the World Breaker's power could have cracked the continent — the truth came out.

The bomb that had destroyed Sakaar? That had taken Caiera? It hadn't been planted by the Earth heroes. It had been planted by Miek — one of the Hulk's own Warbound — who had wanted to keep the Hulk furious and fighting forever.

Are you ready for this? The World Breaker stopped.

Not because he was defeated. Because his heart broke. He had almost destroyed an entire city over a lie. He begged the heroes to stop him before he caused more harm.

The war that had started as vengeance ended in grief. And the heroes of Earth understood something forever after: the Hulk truly was the strongest one there is. But strength alone had never been what made him matter.
