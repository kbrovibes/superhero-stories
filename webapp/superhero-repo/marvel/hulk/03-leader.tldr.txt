• Samuel Sterns: ordinary laborer at a chemical plant until radioactive waste transformed him
• Unlike Banner, the radiation targeted Sterns' mind — skull expanded to hold a super-efficient brain
• Skin turned green; became the Leader — a mastermind who views humanity as pawns in a global game
• Leader's intelligence functions as near-precognition: simulates millions of scenarios per second
• Goal: capture the Hulk, dissect him, unlock the secrets of near-limitless strength
• Fights with technology, manipulation, and psychological warfare — never fists
• Blind spot: pure logic cannot account for the power of human emotion and the Hulk's rage
• Builds an empire of shadows: funds terrorist organisations, develops weapons that defy physics
• Creates gamma-powered "Humanoids" — his own army without the Hulk's weaknesses
• Two sides of a gamma coin: the Hulk's pure rage vs. the Leader's cold intellect — neither can rest
