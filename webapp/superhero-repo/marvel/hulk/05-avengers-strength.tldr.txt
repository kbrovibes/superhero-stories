• First Avengers threat: Loki, god of mischief — no single hero could face him alone
• Hulk viewed with skepticism by teammates at first; known for rampages, not teamwork
• Chitauri army pours through the sky above New York; Captain America's command: "Hulk, smash"
• Hulk's role: the "heavy hitter" — takes on threats too large for anyone else; living shield for teammates
• Brings down massive Leviathans with a single punch; withstands punishment that would vaporize a normal human
• "The Lullaby": a psychological technique used by Black Widow to help the Hulk transition back into Banner
• Avengers treat the Hulk with respect no one else in the world ever has — they become his family
• First time in Hulk's life he has a purpose beyond mere survival: he is a protector and a champion
• Even when forced to leave the team, his impact remains — proved the most misunderstood can belong among heroes
• Hulk is the Avengers' soul, their rage, and their most powerful asset
