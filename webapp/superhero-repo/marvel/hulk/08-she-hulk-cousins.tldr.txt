• Jennifer Walters: Bruce Banner's cousin, a talented lawyer living a relatively normal life
• Life-threatening injury forces Bruce to give her an emergency blood transfusion of his gamma-irradiated blood
• Jennifer becomes She-Hulk — transformation more controlled than Bruce's; retains personality and intellect
• She-Hulk views her green form as an opportunity, not a curse — more confident, powerful version of herself
• Bruce and Jennifer: only two people in the world who can discuss the experience of cellular rewriting by radiation
• Jennifer acts as a grounding force for Bruce during his darkest moments; he looks to her as a beacon of hope
• Tension: Jennifer believes in the law and the system; Bruce is often forced to live outside it
• They have faced each other on opposite sides of a battlefield — heartbreaking conflict born of love and difference
• When Jennifer is severely injured, Bruce's grief is profound and triggers one of his most tragic transformations
• Together: two sides of the gamma experience — the struggle between rage and reason, the enduring power of family
