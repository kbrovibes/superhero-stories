• Hulk exiled from Earth by a group called the Illuminati who deemed him too dangerous to stay
• Lands on the harsh planet Sakaar, fitted with a disc that dampens his strength; forced into gladiator combat
• Earns a new name in the Great Arena: the Green Scar — a warrior feared and revered across the planet
• Forms the Warbound: fellow gladiators Miek, Korg, and Hiroim — a brotherhood forged in blood and sand
• Leads a rebellion against the tyrannical Red King; topples the empire and takes the throne himself
• As king, brings peace to Sakaar; marries the warrior Caiera and finds happiness for the first time
• Tragedy: an explosion from the shuttle that brought him to Sakaar destroys the planet, killing millions including Caiera
• The Hulk believes the heroes of Earth planted the bomb — swears a vow of vengeance
• Power reaches cosmic intensity; every step threatens to crack planetary tectonic plates
• Story of Planet Hulk: the search for belonging — the one place Hulk stopped running and fought for something larger
