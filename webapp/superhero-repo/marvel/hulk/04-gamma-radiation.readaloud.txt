Have you ever wondered what actually MADE the Hulk? Not just the bomb — but the invisible, incredible, almost magical energy inside it? Let's talk about gamma radiation, and why it is the most fascinating and wild force in the entire Marvel Universe!

Gamma rays are the most energetic form of light that exists. They're so powerful they can travel right through walls, right through metal, right through almost anything. In the real world, too much gamma radiation would be very dangerous. But in the Marvel world — oh! — something extraordinary happens instead.

You see, Bruce Banner was different from everyone else. Hidden in his DNA was something called a Gamma-Gene — a tiny switch that had been sitting quietly inside him his entire life, just waiting. When the Gamma Bomb exploded out in the New Mexico desert and that wall of brilliant green energy crashed over him, instead of hurting him, it FLIPPED that switch.

And the Hulk was born.

Are you ready for this? Every single time Bruce transforms, gamma energy floods through his body at enormous speed. His bones grow denser than steel. His muscles expand to the size of small cars. His skin turns that famous vivid green — a permanent, glowing badge of the radiation that lives inside every one of his cells. And the angrier the Hulk gets, the more gamma energy surges through him, and the STRONGER he becomes!

But here is the most astonishing part of all. Gamma radiation doesn't just change your body. It seems to reach inside a person and pull out what's already deepest within them — and then it makes that thing ENORMOUS.

For Bruce Banner, what was deepest was buried fear and righteous anger. Hello, Hulk!

For Emil Blonsky, the cold and scheming spy, what was deepest was cruelty and the hunger for power. Hello, Abomination!

For Samuel Sterns, the man obsessed with being cleverer than everyone else, what was deepest was pure blazing intellect. Hello, the Leader!

And for Jennifer Walters — Bruce's cousin, a passionate lawyer who loved justice with her whole heart — gamma energy brought out her confidence and her fierce drive to do what's right. Hello, She-Hulk!

A whole world of gamma-powered heroes and villains exists in Marvel, and every single one of them carries a little piece of that original green explosion. Bruce Banner spent years trying to find a cure, believing the radiation was a poison. But eventually, he understood the truth: it wasn't a curse. It was part of who he was. Part of who he had always been.

And that is how one invisible wave of energy created a whole universe of legends.
