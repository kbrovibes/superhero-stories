• Dr. Bruce Banner: quiet scientist at a New Mexico testing site, inventor of the Gamma Bomb
• Rick Jones: teenage civilian who drives onto the restricted range moments before detonation
• Banner sprints from the bunker, throws Rick into a protective trench, saves his life
• The Gamma Bomb detonates and bathes Banner in an emerald wall of gamma radiation
• Radiation rewrites Banner's DNA; he survives but is permanently transformed
• First transformation occurs that same night — gray-skinned behemoth of pure rage emerges
• General "Thunderbolt" Ross leads the military in hunting the Hulk relentlessly
• Hulk is the manifestation of Banner's suppressed fear, loneliness, and righteous fury
• Tragedy: Banner's greatest act of heroism — saving a stranger — cursed him to live as a monster
• Within the monster lies a heart still seeking to protect the innocent, even as the world sees only destruction
