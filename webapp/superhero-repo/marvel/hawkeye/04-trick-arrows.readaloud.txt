When you fight alongside people who can lift a city bus with one hand or fire energy beams from their eyes, a simple arrow might seem like a small thing. Clint Barton knows this. Which is exactly why his arrows are never simple.

Look into Clint's quiver and you will find an entire workshop on his back. There are arrows that explode. Arrows that fire grappling hooks so he can zip across a rooftop. Arrows that release a net to tangle up enemies in mid-sprint. Arrows that release a puff of gas to make an opponent drowsy without hurting them. There are arrows built around Tony Stark's brilliant engineering, and arrows designed for missions deep in the strange territory of the Quantum Realm using Pym particles.

Every single arrow is perfectly balanced. Clint tested each design again and again until he was certain the tip would trigger at exactly the right moment. In the middle of a battle — running, dodging, arrows flying past him — he has to reach into the quiver and pick the right one instantly, without looking. The wrong choice could mean disaster. The right one saves the day.

What makes Clint proudest is the arrows designed to help without hurting. He can knock someone unconscious, pin them to a wall with a net, or create a distraction — all without causing lasting harm. Because Clint believes that every life has value, even the ones he is fighting against.

And do you know what? It works. Again and again, the man with the bow has saved the day right alongside the gods and the super-soldiers.

From that day on, Hawkeye's quiver became proof that the best weapon is not the biggest one — it is the most clever one.
