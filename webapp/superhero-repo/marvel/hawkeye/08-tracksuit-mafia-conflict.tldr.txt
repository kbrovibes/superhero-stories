• Tracksuit Mafia: persistent street-level criminal organization with connections to the Kingpin
• Conflict triggered when the gang targeted an auction for the Ronin suit — dragging Clint back into his dark past
• Led by Maya Lopez, the Tracksuits proved a determined force operating in Clint's own neighborhood
• Clint had to protect his apartment building, mentor Kate Bishop, and neutralize the gang simultaneously
• Battles fought in cramped hallways and city streets; final showdown at Rockefeller Center
• The gang's relentless use of "Bro" was almost comedic, but their loyalty and willingness to use force were real threats
• Clint frequently outnumbered; relied on trick arrows and his partnership with Kate to stay ahead
• The conflict escalated, drawing in deadly assassin Yelena Belova and the Kingpin himself
• Forced Clint to confront the full consequences of his Ronin actions and find a path to peace rather than violence
• Lesson: no threat is too small to be ignored — street-level heroism protects communities and reflects the same values as saving the world
