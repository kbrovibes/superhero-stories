What happens when two incredible heroes who are also very good at very different things find each other on a mission? Well, sometimes they save the world. And sometimes — if they are very lucky — they also fall in love.

That is the story of Clint Barton and Bobbi Morse — also known as Mockingbird.

Bobbi was a brilliant scientist AND a master spy. She had a Ph.D. in biology! She had trained with S.H.I.E.L.D. since she was young, and she had the kind of sharp mind that could solve a problem six ways before anyone else had even understood the problem existed. And in hand-to-hand combat? She was extraordinary — able to take on opponents twice her size with graceful, precise moves that left them completely bewildered.

Clint met Bobbi when he was working as a chief of security for a company called Cross Technologies — and he was investigating a mystery that turned dangerous very quickly. Their first meeting was action-packed, fast, and full of the kind of banter where two people are definitely showing off for each other without quite admitting it.

They were perfect partners. Where Clint's arrows flew from a distance — TWANG! — Bobbi worked up close and personal, her movements quick as thought. CRACK! Each one covered exactly the gap the other left open. Together they were more powerful than either could be alone.

They fell in love and got married — two superheroes trying to build something real and warm in the middle of a life full of danger and spy missions. And together, they did something extraordinary: they co-founded the West Coast Avengers! A whole new team of heroes, based in California, bringing protection to parts of the world that needed it. Hawkeye at the helm, Mockingbird right beside him, with heroes like Wonder Man and Tigra joining the mission.

Bobbi pushed Clint to think bigger — more strategy, more tactics, more big-picture thinking. Clint gave Bobbi something she'd never had in her spy career: a reason to come home every night.

Life is better, and battles are won more often, when the right person is standing beside you.
