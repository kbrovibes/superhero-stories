• Kate Bishop: determined young woman who idolized Clint Barton; thrust into a criminal underworld conspiracy
• Clint recognized in Kate the same raw talent and fierce sense of justice that defined him
• Mentorship built on mutual respect and shared understanding of being a human hero
• Clint taught Kate: the burden of responsibility, physical toll of the lifestyle, tactical knowledge, and expertise in trick arrows
• Kate's energy pushed Clint to confront his own past — including the darker chapter of his Ronin identity
• Their partnership was tested by the Tracksuit Mafia, requiring perfect synchronization
• Clint saw his best qualities reflected in Kate, along with potential that might one day surpass his own
• Clint was transparent about his mistakes — wanted Kate to be a better person, not just a great archer
• The Hawkeye identity became a shared symbol: skill, heart, and standing up for the little guy
• Lesson: the greatest act of heroism is ensuring the values you fought for are carried forward by someone worthy
