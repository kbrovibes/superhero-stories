• Bucky's notebook: a long list of names — people the Winter Soldier hurt while HYDRA was controlling him
• Court-appointed therapist: Dr. Raynor; Bucky promised to close out every line in the notebook
• Some people on the list were not ready to forgive; some were; some did not know what had happened
• Yori Nakajima: older man, ran a sushi restaurant in Brooklyn; Winter Soldier had ended his son RJ's life
• Bucky ate dinner at Yori's restaurant regularly; listened; kept him company on holidays — did not reveal who he was
• Dr. Raynor said this did not count as making amends; Bucky finally told Yori the truth on a park bench
• Yori cried and told Bucky to leave; Bucky did — and understood: amends is not about being forgiven, but about being honest enough to risk it
• When Sam Wilson asked Bucky to be his partner, Bucky surprised himself by wanting to say yes
• Dynamic: Sam reminded Bucky he was a person, not a weapon; Bucky reminded Sam not every fight is won with a smile
• Best part of being Sam's partner: a routine without revenge — Brooklyn mornings, riverfront runs, dinners at Yori's when allowed, helping Sarah Wilson fix her boat in Louisiana
