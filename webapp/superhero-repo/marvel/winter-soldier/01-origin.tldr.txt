• James Buchanan "Bucky" Barnes: kid from Brooklyn, best friend and protector of sickly Steve Rogers
• Shipped out with the 107th Infantry Regiment; fought through France and Italy while Steve was rejected from every recruitment office
• Captured by HYDRA agents near Azzano, Italy; taken to scientist Arnim Zola's hidden laboratory
• Zola injected Bucky with an early Super Soldier Serum — he should have died; instead he survived, changed
• Steve Rogers — now Captain America, transformed by the same serum in Brooklyn — walked in and rescued him
• The two friends fought together until a mission on an Alpine train: HYDRA agent Heinrich Zemo knocked Bucky off the open side car
• Steve lunged for his hand and missed; Bucky fell into the snowy mountains below and was presumed dead
• HYDRA recovered Bucky from a crevasse, replaced his missing left arm with a metal prosthetic, erased his memory
• Ten Russian trigger words implanted by HYDRA: wishing, furnace, daybreak, seventeen, benign, nine, homecoming, one, freight car
• Frozen and unfrozen for seventy years as HYDRA's secret weapon; Bucky Barnes waiting underneath, unknown to himself
