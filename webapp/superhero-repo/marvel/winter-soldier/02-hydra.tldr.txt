• HYDRA: the secret organization that created the Winter Soldier; buried inside S.H.I.E.L.D. since Operation Paperclip imported Arnim Zola into the U.S. after the war
• By the time Steve Rogers came out of the ice, HYDRA had infiltrated Congress, the World Security Council, and the Triskelion
• Project Insight: three new Helicarriers to eliminate every person HYDRA deemed a future threat — in a single afternoon
• Steve, Sam Wilson, and Natasha Romanoff uncovered the plot; HYDRA sent the Winter Soldier to stop them
• Highway fight in Washington D.C.: Bucky caught a knife between Steve's hands at blinding speed; mask fell off; Steve recognized him
• "Bucky?" "Who the hell is Bucky?" — HYDRA pulled Bucky back and put him in the chair
• Final showdown on the third Helicarrier: Steve refused to swing his shield at his friend; kept whispering Bucky's name
• Helicarrier crashed into the Triskelion; Steve, broken-jawed, looked at Bucky: "Then finish it. Because I'm with you to the end of the line."
• Those words — ones Bucky had said to a sick Steve in Brooklyn — cracked the HYDRA conditioning
• Bucky pulled Steve from the Potomac and disappeared; began the slow, terrified work of remembering himself in Bucharest
