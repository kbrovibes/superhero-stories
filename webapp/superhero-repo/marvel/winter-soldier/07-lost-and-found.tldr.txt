• Bucky fell from a HYDRA cargo train high in the Alps; Steve lunged, their fingertips brushed, and Bucky disappeared into the snow
• The serum in his blood kept him alive against impossible odds — his left arm did not survive the fall
• HYDRA search teams in gray coats found him; Arnim Zola recognized him from old files and ordered him moved to a hidden Soviet facility
• Doctors fitted him with a heavy slab of dark gray metal, riveted to his shoulder
• HYDRA's plan was simple: control him by keeping him from remembering — new name, new language, false stories about himself
• They used a special chair with electrical currents to gently push his old memories down into the dark where he couldn't reach them
• He had no idea Steve Rogers existed; no idea about Brooklyn; no idea his mother Winifred was still hoping the telegram had been a mistake
• He was not gone — just lost, like a coat dropped behind a couch, waiting to be found
• It would take seventy years, but someone would come for him
