• Sam Wilson and Bucky did not become partners overnight — they argued their way into it, a quarter-turn at a time
• Steve Rogers had left them to each other; Sam was the new Captain America, Bucky was a 106-year-old recovering Winter Soldier
• Their first real mission together: stopping the Flag Smashers and their leader Karli Morgenthau
• Drove from Munich to Riga in a tiny rental car arguing about strategy, food, jazz, and the Brooklyn Dodgers
• Partnership got real on Sarah Wilson's family fishing boat in Delacroix, Louisiana — Bucky patched the hull and stayed for dinner
• Sarah's sons Cass and AJ asked detailed questions about the vibranium arm; Bucky answered every one
• Sam built a new winged Captain America suit using Wakandan vibranium from T'Challa — wings and shield in one
• The rhythm: Sam in the air, Bucky on the ground; Sam talking, Bucky standing just close enough
• They never argued about who had each other's back — and on quiet evenings in Delacroix, Bucky finally understood he was home
