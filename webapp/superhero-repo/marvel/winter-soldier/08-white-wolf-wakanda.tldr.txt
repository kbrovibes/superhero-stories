• After the Leipzig airport fight and the Siberian bunker, Bucky had a metal arm, ten Russian trigger words in his head, and nowhere safe to go
• T'Challa, the Black Panther, offered him a home in Wakanda — the hidden African nation behind the world's most advanced cloaking technology
• Wakanda: silver cities, grassland villages, long-horned cattle wandering between them
• Bucky lived in a small thatched-roof hut at the edge of a quiet village, near a stream and acacia trees
• Princess Shuri, 16 and a genius, studied his brain scans for months — building a system to gently turn off each trigger word
• While Shuri worked, Bucky tended a small herd of goats and learned a little Xhosa from the farmers
• The village children called him Ingcuka Emhlophe — White Wolf — for his pale skin and his calm, watchful way
• On the day Shuri was finished, she read all ten trigger words aloud in order — and nothing happened
• Bucky smiled a real Brooklyn smile for the first time in seventy years; the kid who had been lost was, at last, found
