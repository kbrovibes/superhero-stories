• Dora Milaje: all-female elite order serving as T'Challa's personal bodyguard and vanguard of Wakanda's defense
• Clad in vibrant red armor; primary weapon is a custom Vibranium spear capable of close combat and kinetic shockwaves
• Membership requires years of grueling training: multiple martial arts, advanced tactics, and mastery of their signature weapons
• General Okoye leads the order — formidable warrior, brilliant tactician, absolutely uncompromising in principle
• Okoye's loyalty extends beyond the king to the institution of the monarchy and the safety of the realm
• Dora Milaje fight with terrifying synchronized coordination, as if sharing a single mind
• Serve as cultural ambassadors: shaved heads and intricate tattoos symbolize total devotion to their order
• Battle cry "Wakanda Forever" signals readiness to sacrifice everything for their nation
• Execute covert global operations as well as defending the Golden City directly
• Lesson: discipline, collective purpose, and honor are the foundations of true strength
