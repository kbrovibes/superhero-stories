• Ulysses Klaue: international arms criminal and black-market dealer obsessed with stealing Wakandan Vibranium
• Lost his left arm during a previous raid on Wakanda; replaced it with a stolen sonic cannon from Wakandan mining technology
• Sonic cannon fired high-frequency sound waves able to shatter steel and bypass conventional armor
• T'Challa hunted Klaue across multiple continents — including through the streets of Busan — as a matter of national honor
• Okoye and the Dora Milaje provided elite ground support, working in synchronized combat against Klaue's mercenaries
• Key confrontation in an underground casino: T'Challa used his Vibranium suit to absorb and redirect Klaue's sonic blasts
• Shuri remotely piloted a vehicle to assist in Klaue's capture during the Busan chase
• Klaue was ultimately killed by Erik Killmonger, ending a long chapter of Wakandan history
• T'Challa learned that global security requires international cooperation, not just superior technology
• Lesson: protecting Vibranium is a sacred trust; vigilance must be paired with wisdom and transparency
