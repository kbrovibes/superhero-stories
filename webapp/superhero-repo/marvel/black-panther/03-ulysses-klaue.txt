Ulysses Klaue is a dangerous international criminal who has spent years trying to steal Wakanda's most precious resource: Vibranium. Klaue is a master of sound-based technology and has a powerful sonic cannon replaced where his left arm used to be. He is greedy and will do anything to get his hands on the rare metal, which he wants to sell to the highest bidder. This makes him a constant threat to the safety and security of Wakanda.

T'Challa has chased Klaue all over the globe to prevent him from smuggling Vibranium out of Africa. Because Klaue uses sound waves as a weapon, the Black Panther’s Vibranium suit is the perfect defense, as it can absorb the sonic energy. However, Klaue is clever and often sets traps to distract the Panther while he makes his escape. T'Challa must use his tracking skills and his advanced technology to stay one step ahead of the villain.

The battle against Klaue is often a high-speed chase through city streets or deep inside hidden mines. T'Challa relies on his loyal guards, the Dora Milaje, and his sister Shuri’s inventions to help catch the thief. They work together as a team to protect their home's secrets. Klaue’s greed is a reminder of why Wakanda must remain careful about who they trust and how they share their incredible technology with the world.

Protecting Vibranium is a sacred duty for the Black Panther. T'Challa knows that if this powerful metal fell into the wrong hands, it could be used to create dangerous weapons. By stopping Klaue, he ensures that Wakanda's resources are used for healing and progress instead of harm. T'Challa’s dedication to justice and his love for his country make him the ultimate guardian against those who would exploit its treasures for their own selfish gain.
