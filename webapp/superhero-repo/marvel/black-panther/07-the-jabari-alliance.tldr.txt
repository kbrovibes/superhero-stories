• Jabari tribe: live in the snow-capped Gorilla Mountains above Wakanda's jungles; reject Vibranium and advanced technology
• Led by the formidable M'Baku, who worships the gorilla deity Hanuman; values physical strength and harmony with nature
• Historical tension and mutual suspicion between the Jabari and Wakanda's Golden City
• During Killmonger's coup, M'Baku and the Jabari gave sanctuary to the royal family — a turning point for the nation
• Jabari warriors joined the fight to reclaim the throne, forging a powerful new alliance
• M'Baku now holds a permanent seat on Wakanda's tribal council, providing grounded, pragmatic counsel
• Jabari warriors complement the Dora Milaje: brute-force hardwood weaponry vs. high-tech Vibranium spears
• T'Challa trains with M'Baku to keep his combat skills versatile and unconventional
• Jabari villages carved into mountain rock; echoing with traditional drumming and warrior chants
• Lesson: Wakanda is stronger when all its peoples — mountain and city alike — stand united
