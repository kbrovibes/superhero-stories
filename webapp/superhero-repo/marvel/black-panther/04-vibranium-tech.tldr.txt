• Vibranium: rare extraterrestrial metal that crashed into Wakanda thousands of years ago; absorbs kinetic energy, manipulates sound and light
• Capital city Birnin Zana: Vibranium-reinforced skyscrapers with tribal patterns, magnetic levitation trains, Talon Fighters and Dragon Flyers in the sky
• The Great Mound: massive Vibranium deposit powering all of Wakanda with clean, infinite energy
• Kimoyo Beads: Vibranium-based communication and medical tools capable of repairing catastrophic injuries in hours
• The Black Panther suit: flexible Vibranium mesh that absorbs impacts and releases stored energy as a powerful outward blast; anti-metal Vibranium claws
• Shuri leads innovation from her lab inside Mount Bashenga; her work includes holographic arrays, advanced prosthetics, and remote-piloting systems
• Wakandan International Centers share Vibranium-based medicine and science with the world
• Strict ethical oversight governs all Vibranium use — every piece tracked to prevent misuse
• Wakanda is a model of technology that preserves the natural world while empowering people
• Lesson: the greatest achievement of science is its impact on human lives, guided by a noble spirit
