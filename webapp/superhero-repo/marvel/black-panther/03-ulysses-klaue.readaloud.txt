Have you ever heard of someone so obsessed with ONE thing that they could not stop thinking about it for their entire life? Well, meet Ulysses Klaue — and the thing he could not stop thinking about was Vibranium.

Klaue was a sneaky, slippery arms dealer who sold dangerous weapons to dangerous people. He laughed too loud, joked too much, and made people think he was just a harmless eccentric — so they would not notice how truly dangerous he was. But T'Challa noticed. Every Wakandan noticed. Because years ago, Klaue had snuck into Wakanda, stolen precious Vibranium, and barely escaped. In doing so, he lost his left arm. And do you know what he did? He found a stolen Wakandan mining tool and REBUILT himself a new arm — a sonic cannon that fired crushing sound waves so powerful they could shatter STEEL and flip whole cars into the air. BOOM! KRAK! One shot and a wall crumbles to rubble.

For T'Challa, hunting Klaue was a matter of deep national honor. Klaue had stolen from Wakanda. Klaue had sold Wakanda's secrets. And Klaue had never once answered for it.

The chase swept all the way to Busan, a city in South Korea. Klaue hid inside an underground casino, thinking he was safe. He was not. T'Challa, General Okoye of the Dora Milaje, and their team closed in. When Klaue fired his sonic cannon — POOM! — T'Challa's Vibranium suit SWALLOWED the blast and stored every last bit of it. Then — WHAM — he released it right back!

But Klaue bolted into the streets. A wild chase blazed through Busan, with Shuri back in Wakanda remotely piloting a vehicle through her holographic controls to help cut him off. Can you imagine? Shuri sitting in her lab, hands dancing over screens, steering a car through streets halfway around the world! She was only a teenager, and she was already one of the smartest people alive.

They captured Klaue. He was later taken away by Killmonger — a part of the story for another day. But the chase taught T'Challa something important: Vibranium attracts every greedy eye on Earth. The best way to protect it is not walls and silence, but wisdom, partnership, and the courage to open Wakanda's hands to the world.

From that day on, T'Challa worked harder than ever to make sure Vibranium's gifts helped everyone — and ended up in the wrong hands never again.
