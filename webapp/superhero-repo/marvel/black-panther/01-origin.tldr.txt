• T'Challa, eldest son of King T'Chaka, born to rule the hidden kingdom of Wakanda in East Africa
• Wakanda protected by a holographic veil; most technologically advanced nation on Earth; home of Vibranium
• Years of martial arts training and academic study prepared T'Challa for the throne
• After T'Chaka's death, T'Challa consumed the Heart-Shaped Herb — a Vibranium-mutated plant granting superhuman strength, speed, and senses
• The ritual connected T'Challa to the Ancestral Plane and the spirits of previous Black Panthers
• Brilliant sister Shuri designed the Black Panther suit: Vibranium mesh that absorbs kinetic energy and releases it as shockwaves; retractable Vibranium claws
• T'Challa leads the Dora Milaje, his elite royal guard, in defending Wakanda
• He communes with ancestors on the Ancestral Plane when facing impossible decisions
• T'Challa chose to end Wakanda's isolation and share its knowledge and resources with the wider world
• Lesson: power guided by duty and a noble heart is the greatest force for good
