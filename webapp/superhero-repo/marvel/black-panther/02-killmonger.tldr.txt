• Erik Stevens, born N'Jadaka, was the son of Prince N'Jobu — the brother of King T'Chaka — and T'Challa's cousin
• N'Jobu was killed by T'Chaka for attempting to share Wakandan technology with the oppressed; Erik was left behind in America
• Killmonger trained as a Navy SEAL and black-ops soldier, spending his life preparing to claim Wakanda's throne
• Erik challenged T'Challa to ritual combat at Warrior Falls; his battle-hardened ferocity defeated T'Challa
• Killmonger consumed the Heart-Shaped Herb, claimed the crown, and began militarizing Wakanda for global revolution
• T'Challa recovered, confronted his father's sins on the Ancestral Plane, and returned to reclaim the throne
• Final battle took place in the Great Mound, where Vibranium's magnetic fields disrupted both Panthers' suits
• T'Challa won but granted Killmonger's dying wish: to see Wakanda's sunset — a moment of profound empathy
• In honor of Erik's grief, T'Challa opened Wakanda's borders and established international outreach centers in Oakland
• Lesson: true power lies in listening, empathy, and justice for all people, not just one's own nation
