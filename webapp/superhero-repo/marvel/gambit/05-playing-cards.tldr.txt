• Gambit's mutant power is charging objects with kinetic energy — stored-up explosive force — until they detonate
• The release creates a distinctive pink-purple flash and a precise, directional explosion
• He can charge almost any object: coins, pebbles, rope, hinges — but playing cards are his specialty
• Cards are lightweight, aerodynamic, and easy to throw with the wrist-flick he learned in the Thieves Guild
• He keeps a worn practice deck in his left pocket and a fresh emergency deck in his right
• His favorite card to throw is the ace of diamonds, which he says flies truest
• In the Danger Room at Xavier's mansion he practices flash charges, delay sequences, and defusing charges
• He can drain a charge back OUT of an object — a defensive skill that has saved lives
• He tells Bobby Drake: the cards aren't the weapon — he is; the cards are just how he introduces himself
