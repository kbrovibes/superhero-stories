• Remy LeBeau grows up in New Orleans, raised by the Thieves Guild with no memory of his parents
• The Guild trains him in lock-picking, three languages, and the spinning bo staff
• Remy discovers his mutant power: charging objects with kinetic energy until they glow pink-purple and explode
• His glowing red eyes and black sclera make him stand out, but he never stops being kind
• He begins quietly returning stolen items, no longer comfortable keeping what isn't his
• Charles Xavier arrives in the French Quarter and explains that Remy's power is a mutant gift
• Xavier invites Remy to join the X-Men at his school in Westchester, New York
• Remy packs his playing cards and bo staff and boards a train north — choosing family over the Guild
• At Xavier's school he takes the name Gambit, a chess term for a bold opening move
