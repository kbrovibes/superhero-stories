• Mister Sinister — real name Nathaniel Essex — is an ancient, armored villain obsessed with studying mutant DNA
• Long ago, Sinister made a dark bargain with the young Remy LeBeau that Gambit still carries as a secret
• X-Men trace attacks on mutants to a hidden research facility in the Louisiana bayou
• Gambit recognizes the place immediately and tells Cyclops and Storm everything
• Sinister has captured seven mutants, including a girl named Louisa who can generate heat
• Cyclops destroys the containment fields; Storm's lightning takes out robotic sentinels
• Gambit moves silently through the corridors and confronts Sinister in the central lab
• A charged playing card causes a massive explosion, and Gambit frees all the captives
• Sinister escapes through a hidden tunnel, but the prisoners are safe and the facility is empty
