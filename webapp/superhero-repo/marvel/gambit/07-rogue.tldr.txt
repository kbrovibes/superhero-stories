• Rogue — real name Anna Marie — absorbs memories and abilities through skin contact, which she cannot control
• She has worn gloves since she was a teenager, keeping careful distance from everyone
• When Gambit arrives at the mansion, Rogue is skeptical of his charm — she's seen charm before
• Remy doesn't push; he sits with her, learns what songs she likes, and is simply present
• He plays her favorite songs badly on the mansion piano until she comes to correct him — that was the plan
• Their friendship grows slowly through real conversations, arguments, and card peace offerings
• What Rogue needs is not pity or someone trying to fix her — she needs someone who takes her seriously
• Gambit gives her that; she gives him back a person who sees through his charm to the real him
• They hold hands sometimes, wearing gloves, at the mansion window — warm, real, and theirs
