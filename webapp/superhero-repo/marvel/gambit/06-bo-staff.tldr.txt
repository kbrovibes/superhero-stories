• The Thieves Guild trained young Remy with a bo staff before age ten; Marcel said it makes you think first
• A bo staff is a smooth six-foot hardwood pole — no edge, no trigger, no shortcuts
• Remy trained for years until the staff became an extension of his own body
• Cards are ranged; the staff handles everything up close — together they make Gambit extremely versatile
• His staff was crafted in New Orleans by Guild artisan Celestine Arceneaux from white ash, on Magazine Street
• It has a small fleur-de-lis burned near one end — the symbol of New Orleans
• In battle, the staff's long reach keeps stronger opponents at a distance and creates a wide defensive arc
• Gambit's signature move: a low ankle sweep timed precisely when the opponent's weight commits forward
• The same sweep worked on Sabretooth, on Weapon X soldiers, and (he admits sheepishly) on Wolverine once
