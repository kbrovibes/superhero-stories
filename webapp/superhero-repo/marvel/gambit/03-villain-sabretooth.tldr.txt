• Sabretooth — real name Victor Creed — is a brutal, clawed villain who hunts without conscience
• The Thieves Guild hired Sabretooth to retrieve an envelope from a young courier named Beau Mireaux
• Gambit finds Sabretooth cornering seventeen-year-old Beau in Audubon Park, New Orleans
• Gambit draws his bo staff and orders Sabretooth to stand down, calling it a fair fight
• Sabretooth lunges; the battle rips through the park — claws vs. staff and charged playing cards
• Gambit uses his knowledge of the New Orleans streets to dodge, distract, and outmaneuver Creed
• A sweeping bo staff trip sends the heavy mutant crashing to the ground
• Gambit holds a charged ace of spades against Creed's collar — and Sabretooth backs down
• Beau keeps the envelope; Gambit proves a thief's skills can be used to protect the innocent
