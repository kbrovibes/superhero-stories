- Hope, Hank, and Janet all vanished in the Snap when Thanos used the Infinity Stones
- Scott Lang was trapped in the Quantum Realm during the Snap and did not even know it happened
- For Scott it felt like five hours; on Earth, five years passed without Hope
- A rat in an abandoned warehouse accidentally activated the Quantum Tunnel and released Scott
- Scott brought the Avengers the idea: use the Quantum Realm as a time tunnel for the Time Heist
- Tony Stark and Bruce Banner built a full-size Quantum Tunnel platform to send Avengers back for the six Infinity Stones
- When Bruce undid the Snap with a borrowed gauntlet, Hope and her parents returned
- Hope suited up and joined the final battle at the ruined Avengers compound
- In the sky, Hope and Janet — mother and daughter, two Wasps — fought side by side while Scott smashed enemies as Giant-Man below
