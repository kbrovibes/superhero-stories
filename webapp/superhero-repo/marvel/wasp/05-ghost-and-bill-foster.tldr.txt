- Ghost is Ava Starr — a young woman who phases through walls and turns invisible, but who is in constant pain
- Ava's powers came from a quantum lab accident that killed her father, Elihas Starr, when she was little
- Her molecules flicker in and out of phase with reality, hurting her every second of every day
- Ava was raised in secret by Dr. Bill Foster — Hank Pym's old research partner, the original Goliath
- Bill had built a chamber that eased Ava's pain, but it kept coming back
- Hope realized Ava was not a villain but a victim — she chose compassion over revenge
- Janet van Dyne offered to transfer healing quantum energy from her body to Ava's
- The treatment stabilized Ava's molecules and quieted her pain
- Bill Foster and Hank Pym reconciled after years of estrangement, and Ava stopped being an enemy
