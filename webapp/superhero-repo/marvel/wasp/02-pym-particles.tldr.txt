- Pym Particles: discovered by Hope's father Hank Pym; alter distance between atoms while keeping mass and density the same
- Red particles shrink, blue particles grow — Hope knew the color code as a small child
- A person who shrinks past the subatomic barrier falls into the Quantum Realm, the way Janet did
- Hope refined the Wasp gauntlets, calibrated Scott's Ant-Man helmet, and built a rolling laboratory that grows on a street corner
- She manufactures improved Pym discs so Scott never runs out of particles in the field
- After Darren Cross weaponized the formula as the Yellowjacket suit, the Pym family swore to guard the science
- A single vial of red particles could shrink a tank, a missile, or a skyscraper — so the formula must stay safe
- Hope's rule, from her father: "Pym Particles are not a toy. They are physics — and physics keeps its promises."
- Hope knows the science better than anyone alive except Hank — she was raised speaking it
