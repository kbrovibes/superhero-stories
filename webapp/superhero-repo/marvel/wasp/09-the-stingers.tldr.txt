- Stingers: bio-electric blasters built into the wrists of the Wasp suit, designed by Hope herself
- Yellow arcs of focused electricity, fired from emitter rings on the gauntlets
- Janet's original suit had stingers too, but Hope's are smaller, faster, and twice as accurate
- Short burst stuns a guard; sustained burst melts a lock or punches through a steel door
- Rapid-fire mode sends three or four pulses per second, weaving through moving crowds
- Stingers and wings are both powered by a microbattery driven by Pym Particle reactions
- Two pairs of clear panel wings fold out of housings on Hope's back — Scott Lang has none, he rides Ant-thony
- Hope can fire a stinger from ant size with the same precision as full size
- Other Avengers focus on her wings OR her stingers OR her size and miss the combination — by which time she has won
