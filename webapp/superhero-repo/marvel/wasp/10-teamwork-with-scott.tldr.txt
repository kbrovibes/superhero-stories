- Hope did not like Scott at first — he was a goofy ex-thief who got the Ant-Man suit instead of her
- Scott fumbled his shrinking and once shrank himself in a bathtub by accident
- Over time Hope learned Scott was the partner she needed, not the one she expected
- Hope is the technical lead (calibrations, gear design, mission planning); Scott is the heart (humor, scrappy creativity)
- In combat Hope owns the air with wings and stingers; Scott owns the ground with his ant army
- Hope is precision; Scott is chaos — bad guys cannot defend against both at once
- Hope still does most of the technical work — Scott jokes that he forgets "red to shrink, blue to grow"
- Scott's daughter Cassie sees Hope as a mentor; Hope teaches her Pym Particle math and how to build her first suit
- Together Hope, Scott, and Cassie form a small, tight, extended family that defends the world together
