- Hope van Dyne is the daughter of Janet van Dyne (the original Wasp) and Dr. Hank Pym
- When Hope was seven, Janet shrank past the subatomic barrier to disable a Soviet missile and was lost in the Quantum Realm for decades
- Hope grew up in a quiet San Francisco house with a heartbroken, work-obsessed father
- She studied physics and business, mastering Pym Particle research better than anyone except Hank himself
- She knew every detail of the original Wasp suit: wings, bio-electric stingers, shrinking calibration
- Hank locked the suit away — he had already lost Janet and could not bear losing Hope too
- He gave the smaller Ant-Man mantle to ex-thief Scott Lang instead, which infuriated Hope
- Hope trained Scott herself, drilling him in combat skills she had mastered as a teenager
- After Scott proved himself with the Avengers, Hank finally walked Hope to a workshop and gave her the new red-and-gold Wasp suit
