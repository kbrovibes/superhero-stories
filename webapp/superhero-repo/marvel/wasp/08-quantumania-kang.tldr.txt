- Janet had a Quantum Realm secret: she once met Kang the Conqueror, a future warlord exiled there
- Janet had helped Kang repair his ship, then sabotaged it when she realized he would burn galaxies
- Years later, a Pym Tech experiment in Scott's basement pulled the whole family into the Quantum Realm
- They landed inside Kang's territory: underground cities, glowing rivers, creatures with neon skin
- Janet led the family through hidden tunnels and joined Quantum Realm freedom fighters
- Kang had built an army and was searching for one missing part to escape back to the regular universe
- Hope and Janet fought side by side in glowing blue caves; Scott's daughter Cassie became a hero of her own
- Scott shrank into Kang's power core and tore it apart while Hope and Janet pinned Kang with stinger volleys
- Hope stopped being only Janet's daughter and became Janet's full partner after Janet trusted her with the truth
