• Tony Stark is the architect of the Avengers' infrastructure: designing Avengers Mansion, Avengers Tower, and the upstate compound
• He upgrades Captain America's communications, builds the Quinjets, and equips each teammate with specialized gear
• Tony's greatest engineering mistake: Ultron — a rogue AI of his own creation that nearly destroys the team
• To fix his mistake, he builds the Vision — a synthetic being combining the Mind Stone and J.A.R.V.I.S.'s intellect — a masterpiece of biological and mechanical engineering
• Tony's dream: a "suit of armor around the planet" — the Iron Legion and later the global defense satellite Veronica
• Steve Rogers consistently challenges Tony's reliance on technology; the tension between past and future is a constant Avengers theme
• Tony works through the nights, driven by coffee and the knowledge of threats approaching from space — always building the next defense
• Even after Tony is gone, his technology protects the world through Spider-Man's Stark tech and the planet's AI defense systems
