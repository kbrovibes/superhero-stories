• Aldrich Killian develops Extremis: a bio-technological treatment that rewrites human DNA — allowing regeneration and the ability to generate intense heat
• Tony injects a modified version of Extremis into his own nervous system after being badly outmatched by an Extremis-enhanced soldier
• With Extremis, Tony can summon his armor with a thought — pieces flying across the room and locking onto him like a second skin
• He can see through satellites and feel the state of his global networks — the line between his consciousness and his AI blurs
• The dark side: Extremis amplifies Tony's anxiety and obsession with security, leading to increasingly paranoid and overreaching behavior
• In others, Extremis proves dangerously unstable — causing explosive deaths and civilian casualties
• Tony eventually purges Extremis from his system, recognizing that the cost of "godhood" was his own humanity
• The lesson: Tony's greatest strength is not merging with machines — it is choosing to remain human in spite of them
