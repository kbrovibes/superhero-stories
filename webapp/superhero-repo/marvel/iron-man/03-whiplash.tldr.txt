• Ivan Vanko — brilliant physicist and son of Anton Vanko, who co-invented Arc Reactor technology with Howard Stark — blames the Stark family for his father's poverty and death
• Ivan builds his own Arc Reactor and two electrified plasma-charged whips capable of slicing through reinforced steel; he becomes Whiplash
• First clash: the Monaco Grand Prix — Ivan walks onto the racetrack; Tony, defenseless, is saved when Happy Hogan and Pepper Potts deliver the Mark V "Suitcase Suit"
• The portable Mark V assembles around Tony in seconds; Whiplash's coils leave deep gouges in the armor but Tony prevails
• Whiplash is hired by Justin Hammer — a rival industrialist — who gives Vanko resources to build a fleet of remote-controlled military drones
• Vanko betrays Hammer and turns the drones against the crowd at the Stark Expo; Iron Man and James Rhodes in the War Machine armor battle the fleet together
• Tony and Rhodey combine their repulsor blasts — a "double-tap" — to finally defeat Vanko
• Tony realizes his technology is a responsibility requiring constant vigilance; the battle cements his partnership with Rhodey
