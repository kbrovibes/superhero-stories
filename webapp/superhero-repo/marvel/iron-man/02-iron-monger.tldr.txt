• Obadiah Stane — Tony's long-trusted mentor and business partner — secretly recovers the Mark I armor from the Afghan desert
• Stane has engineers reverse-engineer the Mark I to build the Iron Monger: a massive steel behemoth that dwarfs Tony's Mark III
• Stane steals the Arc Reactor directly from Tony's chest, leaving Tony for dead in his Malibu home
• Tony uses his original, less-efficient Arc Reactor to survive and dons the Mark III to stop Stane from leveling the city
• The two armors clash on the streets and rooftops above Stark Industries in a battle of firepower and philosophy
• Stane roars: "We're iron mongers, Tony! We make tools of war!" — Tony fights back with agility over brute force
• Tony lures Stane onto the roof of the large Arc Reactor facility; Pepper Potts overloads the reactor in a blue energy surge that destroys the Iron Monger
• At the press conference, Tony announces: "I am Iron Man" — the armor becomes a public symbol, and his life as a protector begins
