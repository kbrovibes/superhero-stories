• Pepper Potts begins as Tony Stark's executive assistant — the one who keeps Stark Industries running and his ego in check
• Their relationship grows from professional respect to deep, enduring love that survives alien invasions and Tony's self-destructive impulses
• Tony builds the Mark 49 — the "Rescue" armor — specifically to protect Pepper; unlike his suits, it features advanced shielding and non-lethal weaponry
• The Rescue armor sits in Tony's workshop for years, a gift he hopes she will never need to use
• During the final battle against Thanos, Pepper finally puts on the Rescue armor — fighting side-by-side with Tony for the fate of the universe
• The sight of red-and-gold Iron Man and purple-and-silver Rescue together is a symbol of their united front
• After Tony is gone, Pepper continues as Rescue — a symbol of resilience for survivors of the Blip; she raises their daughter Morgan
• Their story: the strongest bonds are not made of iron or steel, but of trust and mutual sacrifice
