Have you ever wished you could be a little bit FASTER? A little bit STRONGER? What if someone offered you a secret formula that could make you almost unstoppable — but it came with a very dangerous catch? That's exactly what Tony Stark had to think about. And what happened next will surprise you!

A clever and very sneaky man named Aldrich Killian had developed something he called Extremis. It was a bio-technological formula — that means it changed the instructions inside a person's very own body. People who were injected with Extremis could heal from almost any injury in seconds. They could grow so hot on the inside that they actually breathed fire — like a dragon! — and melted metal with their bare hands. Killian could even regrow a whole limb in the time it takes you to tie your shoes. Incredible, right?

But dangerous. VERY dangerous. Because if someone's body couldn't handle Extremis, they would explode — sending out a heat blast measuring three thousand degrees. That is hotter than lava. That is hotter than a lightning bolt. That is very, very, VERY bad.

Tony faced Killian's Extremis soldiers and found his armor getting ripped apart by their bare hands. So Tony — being Tony — did the most Tony thing possible. He modified the formula himself and injected it into his own body.

And oh my goodness, what happened next was extraordinary! Tony could call his armor with just a THOUGHT — the pieces would fly across the room and snap onto him before he'd even finished thinking. He could see through satellites high above the Earth. He could feel the whole planet's digital networks humming around him like music. He felt connected to everything at once. WHIRR! ZAP! WHOOOOSH!

But here is what worried Tony. The more connected he became to his machines, the more afraid he became of losing control. The more afraid, the more he obsessed over security and safety and every possible danger. He wasn't sleeping. He wasn't laughing. He wasn't really himself anymore.

Tony made a hard and brave decision. He removed Extremis from his body. It hurt. It was difficult. But it was the right thing to do.

Because here is what Tony Stark figured out — and this is very important, so listen carefully — his greatest power was never a formula or a gadget or a super-ability. His greatest power was choosing to stay human. To stay connected to the people he loved, with all his feelings and fears and wonderful stubborn persistence. That's the part of Tony Stark that no technology could ever replace.
