• The Sokovia Accords — international legislation requiring the Avengers to operate under United Nations oversight — splits the superhero community
• Tony, haunted by civilian casualties and his role creating Ultron, becomes the Accords' strongest supporter; Steve Rogers opposes them
• Tony's team: Black Widow, War Machine, and a young Peter Parker; Steve's team: Bucky Barnes (Winter Soldier), Falcon, and others
• A massive clash between the two teams erupts at an airport in Leipzig — former friends trading blows in divided loyalty
• The manipulations of Baron Zemo drive the conflict to a brutal final confrontation in an abandoned Siberian bunker
• In the bunker, Tony learns the truth: his parents Howard and Maria Stark were murdered by Bucky Barnes while under Hydra's control
• Tony fights Steve and Bucky in raw grief — the battle ends with the Avengers effectively dissolved; Steve leaves his shield behind
• Tony is left alone in the ruins of his dream — the conflict proves that even the most advanced technology cannot solve a problem of the heart
