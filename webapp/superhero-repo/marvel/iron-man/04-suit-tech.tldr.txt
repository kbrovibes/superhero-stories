• The Iron Man suit evolves from the crude scrap-metal Mark I to the nanotech-powered Mark 85 — each iteration solves a real problem Tony faced in the field
• Primary weapons: repulsor rays from the palms, and the chest-mounted Unibeam — both provide propulsion and offensive power
• Armor material: gold-titanium alloy — the ideal balance of strength and light weight
• AI integration: J.A.R.V.I.S. (later Friday) acts as co-pilot, managing systems, analyzing enemy weaknesses, and providing live tactical data
• Specialized suits: Hulkbuster (massive modular armor for containing a rampaging teammate), Silver Centurion (energy absorption), deep-space and underwater variants
• The nanotech revolution: the suit is stored inside Tony's Arc Reactor and manifests as billions of tiny machines — creating any weapon or tool on demand
• Cost of the technology: obsession over upgrades and fear of suits falling into the wrong hands strains Tony's relationships and mental health
• The suit makes Tony a hero, but it is his heart — the real one — that defines him
