• Tony Stark — billionaire arms dealer for Stark Industries — is ambushed by the terrorist group the Ten Rings in Afghanistan
• Shrapnel from a Stark Industries missile lodges near his heart; he wakes in a cave with a car battery and electromagnet keeping him alive
• In captivity, Tony meets Ho Yinsen — a brilliant physicist — who becomes his partner and gives him a reason to live
• Together they secretly build the Arc Reactor: a palm-sized power source replacing the car battery and powering Tony's survival
• They build the Mark I — a crude suit of scrap-metal armor — to escape; Yinsen sacrifices his life to buy Tony the time to power up
• Yinsen's last words: "Don't waste it. Don't waste your life." — Tony's defining mandate
• Back in Malibu, Tony shuts down Stark Industries' weapons division and begins refining the armor into the iconic Mark III with its red-and-gold design
• Tony Stark walks out of the cave a changed man — no longer a merchant of war, but the beginning of Iron Man
