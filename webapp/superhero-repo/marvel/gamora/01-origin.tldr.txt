• Gamora grows up on Zen-Whoberi until Thanos arrives and takes her as his adopted daughter
• Aboard the Sanctuary, she trains daily against her sister Nebula and becomes the deadliest woman in the galaxy
• She masters blades, staffs, and combat — earning a fearsome reputation across the stars
• Over time she realizes Thanos's goals are about power and darkness, not protection
• She makes the brave choice to leave Thanos, taking her sword Godslayer and following her conscience
• The separation from Nebula leaves a painful wound between the sisters
• In the prison called the Kyln she meets Star-Lord, Rocket, Groot, and Drax
• Together they form the Guardians of the Galaxy — not by plan but by choice
• Gamora finds a new family and becomes a hero who fights for others, not for Thanos
