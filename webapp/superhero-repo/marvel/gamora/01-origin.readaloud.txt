Are you ready to hear about the most dangerous — and the most BRAVE — woman in the whole entire galaxy? Then buckle up, because we are blasting off right now!

Her name is Gamora. She has bright green skin, sharp dark eyes, and moves so fast she looks like a blur even when she's just walking. She started life on the most peaceful little planet you can imagine: Zen-Whoberi, a quiet world where three moons rose in a row at nightfall, like lanterns on a string.

But then — BOOM — everything changed. A very powerful and very dangerous man named Thanos arrived, and the only person he took with him was a small green girl. He called her his daughter. Her new home was a spaceship called the Sanctuary, drifting through the cold dark of outer space — very, very far from those three gentle moons.

Every single morning on the Sanctuary, CLANG CLANG CLANG — Gamora was training. Swords, staffs, fists, feet. She trained against her sister Nebula. She trained against robots. She trained until she was BETTER than anyone, until warriors all across the galaxy said her name the way you say something a little bit scary: carefully, quietly, with respect.

They called her — the DEADLIEST woman in the GALAXY.

Pretty impressive, right?

But here's the thing about Gamora. The more she learned, the more she understood that Thanos wasn't teaching her to protect people. He was training her for his own dark plans. And Gamora — brave, brilliant Gamora — decided she wanted NO part of that.

So she left. WHOOSH — just like that. She took her twin blades and her famous sword Godslayer, and she walked away from everything she had ever known. That takes REAL courage, you know? Even more than winning a fight.

And then — the universe had a surprise in store. In a space prison called the Kyln, she bumped right into the most ridiculous group of people you have ever heard of. Star-Lord, who thought he was way cooler than he was. Rocket, a talking raccoon-like creature with a very big attitude and a bigger toolbox. Groot, a gentle giant who only ever said three words. And Drax, a fierce warrior with a very serious face.

They were a mess. But they were HER mess.

TOGETHER — they became the Guardians of the Galaxy!

Gamora wasn't a weapon anymore. She was a Guardian, a protector, a hero.

And the galaxy? The galaxy was very, very glad she made that choice.
