Okay, you need to hear about the most dangerous villain the Guardians of the Galaxy ever faced. His name? RONAN THE ACCUSER. And just his NAME sounds scary, right?

Ronan was a giant Kree warrior — blue-black armor from boots to shoulders, a face painted white and black like a war mask, and a voice like a thunderstorm rolling across the sky. He carried a massive war-hammer called the Universal Weapon, and let me tell you, it was NOT for hammering nails. It was for crushing entire armies. WHAM! WHAM! WHAM!

But Ronan wanted MORE power. He had a deal with Thanos to retrieve a small silver orb hiding something inside — the Power Stone, one of the six legendary Infinity Stones. And what did Ronan do when he got it? He kept it. He pressed the glowing purple Stone RIGHT into his war-hammer, and suddenly that weapon could destroy whole planets with a single strike.

His target: Xandar. A beautiful, peaceful planet full of people, protected by a brave space police force called the Nova Corps. Ronan was sailing toward it on his enormous warship, the Dark Aster. If that hammer came down on Xandar — it was all over.

So the Guardians loaded up the Milano, their dented but trusty little ship, and flew straight at the Dark Aster. Were they scared? Maybe just a little. Did they turn around? ABSOLUTELY NOT.

BOOM — Rocket launched every weapon he had at the enemy ship! CRASH — Drax leapt aboard the Dark Aster and charged through Ronan's soldiers! WHOOSH — Groot stretched his great arms wide to wrap around his friends and keep them safe when the ships smashed together!

And then Star-Lord did the most jaw-dropping thing you have ever heard of. He grabbed the Power Stone with his BARE HANDS.

The energy blazed through him — a whole Infinity Stone that destroys planets, right there in his fingers! He was shaking. He was sparking. He could not hold on alone!

But Gamora did not even blink. She GRABBED his hand.

Then Drax grabbed on. Then Rocket grabbed on.

FOUR GUARDIANS, sharing the weight of the impossible — together.

They turned that blazing energy around and aimed it straight at Ronan.

KRACOOOOOOM!

The Accuser fell. The Dark Aster came down in flames. Xandar was safe.

The Guardians landed on Xandar's green grass, wind-burned and laughing and completely amazed they were still alive. Gamora looked at Star-Lord, at Rocket, at Drax. At Groot, smiling his slow, wonderful Groot smile.

She laughed. Actually laughed.

They had done the impossible. TOGETHER.
