• Gamora and Nebula grew up as adopted sisters on the Sanctuary, training together every day
• Thanos pitted them against each other — each time Nebula lost, he replaced a part of her with machinery
• Nebula blamed Gamora for every painful upgrade and hunts her across the galaxy
• In a fierce battle over Knowhere, Nebula almost wins — she knows every one of Gamora's moves
• Drax and Rocket arrive just in time to help Gamora recover
• Gamora wins not by brute force but by refusing to give up
• Nebula retreats, but her fury hides an old grief: she wanted to matter to someone
• Gamora sees the pain beneath the anger and carries quiet hope for her sister
• Their rivalry is one of the galaxy's most complicated relationships — part enmity, part longing for family
