Look very closely at Doctor Strange's chest. Do you see it? That small, glowing, golden amulet — round as a full moon, carved with ancient swirling symbols? That is the Eye of Agamotto, and it is one of the most extraordinary objects in the entire universe!

The Eye was created by a man named Agamotto — the very first Sorcerer Supreme who ever lived! And here is a wild and wonderful fact: Agamotto was so wise and so powerful that when he finally passed away, he didn't just disappear. He became one of the Vishanti — a being of pure higher power, like a star that transforms into a whole new kind of light. He became a guardian of Earth from beyond the earthly plane itself! ZAP!

But he left behind his greatest creation. The Eye of Agamotto is actually only one of THREE magical eyes Agamotto made — the others are the Eye of Power and the Eye of Prescience. And inside this one sleeps something extraordinary: the Time Stone, one of the six primordial Infinity Stones that govern the deepest forces of existence.

Are you ready for this? With the Time Stone, Strange can reach out and TOUCH time itself. He can look forward to see the different paths the future might take — like flipping through the pages of a book that hasn't been written yet. WHOOSH! He can reach back and gently undo a terrible thing that already happened. And when an enemy is too powerful to fight directly, he can trap them in a time loop — spinning the same moment over and over — until they agree to stop.

But FWOOM — the Eye does even more than bend time! It shines a blazing golden light that nothing can hide from. Hidden monsters, sorcerers in disguise, invisible shadow creatures drifting through the air — the Eye of Agamotto sees them ALL. When Strange opens the Eye fully, a warm golden radiance pours out, and evil things shrink back from it like shadows fleeing a sunrise.

Strange wears the Eye every single day. He can feel it pulse gently against his chest — a steady heartbeat connecting him to every Sorcerer Supreme who came before, all the way back to Agamotto himself.

One small glowing amulet. One extraordinary link to the whole long history of Earth's magical guardians. What a treasure!
