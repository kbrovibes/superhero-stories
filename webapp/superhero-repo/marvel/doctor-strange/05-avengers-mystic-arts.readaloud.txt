Ready for some serious teamwork? Because when the Avengers need someone to solve a problem that no amount of muscles, shields, or iron suits can fix — they call the man in the red Cloak of Levitation. They call Doctor Strange!

Now, here is what makes Strange so special on the team. WHOOSH! He can open shimmering golden portals that let the whole group leap from one side of the planet to the other in a single step. One second you're in New York City. Next second — FWOOM — you're in Wakanda! Tony Stark might have a cool rocket suit, but even he can't beat that for getting around.

Strange also weaves glowing shields of light that snap up between his teammates and danger — ZAP! — just in time. He can even slow down or rewind a tiny pocket of time during a battle, sneaking a precious extra second into the fight when someone desperately needs it. Can you imagine having a sorcerer on your team who can pause time? How amazing is that?!

Here is an incredible real fact: in the Avengers' greatest battle against the terrible Thanos, Doctor Strange opened inter-dimensional portals and summoned THOUSANDS of heroes from across the world — armies flooding through golden doorways all at once — to fight side by side. CRACK! BOOM! The whole sky was full of heroes, and Strange made it happen!

He and Tony Stark understood each other in a special way. They were both brilliant, stubborn people who carried enormous responsibility on their shoulders. Strange also had deep respect for Steve Rogers, whose moral compass never wagged or wobbled. And whenever the Avengers encountered something magical and strange — artifacts from other dimensions, enchantments they couldn't understand — it was Doctor Strange who explained it, keeping everyone safe.

Strange traded healing one patient at a time in surgery for protecting an entire world of patients. The same healer's instinct — but pointing at the whole of humanity.

And that is how one sorcerer made a team of Earth's mightiest heroes into something even mightier: defenders of every dimension, ready for any threat from any corner of the universe.
