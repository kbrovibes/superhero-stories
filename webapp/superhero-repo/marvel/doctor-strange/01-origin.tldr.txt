• Stephen Strange: world-renowned neurosurgeon whose skill and arrogance defined his identity
• A devastating car accident severely damaged the nerves in his hands, ending his surgical career
• Exhausted his fortune seeking a cure; Western medicine failed him
• Followed rumors to Kamar-Taj in the mountains of Nepal, arriving as a broken man demanding a physical fix
• Met the Ancient One, who showed him that reality is one layer of a vast multiverse
• Grueling training in the Mystic Arts required unlearning science and setting aside his ego
• His analytical surgeon's mind proved a unique asset: precise memory for spell geometry and incantations
• Learned to tap dimensional energy, create shields of light, and manipulate time
• Appointed Sorcerer Supreme — primary guardian of Earth against magical and extra-dimensional threats
• Lesson: our greatest challenges can be the catalysts for our most profound transformations
