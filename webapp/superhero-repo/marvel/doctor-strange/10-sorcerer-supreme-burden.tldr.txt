• Constant vigilance and study required — hours of meditation and research to anticipate threats beyond human comprehension
• Magic carries a cost: physical and emotional toll accumulates over time; Strange must guard against corruption
• His own arrogance remains his greatest vulnerability — he must fight for humility every day
• Finds strength in knowing the work is essential; protects a world that will never know the dangers he faces
• Chose the Sanctum Sanctorum over the operating room — the same healer's precision, now applied to reality itself
