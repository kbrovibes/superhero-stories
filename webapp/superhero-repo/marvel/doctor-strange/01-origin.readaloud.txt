Picture this: the most dazzling surgeon in all of New York City — a man with hands so steady he could fix the tiniest nerve in a human brain. That man was Doctor Stephen Strange. And yes, he absolutely knew how brilliant he was. Maybe a little TOO well!

Stephen's life was all rush and shine and applause. He worked at a gleaming hospital in New York City, and his name was known all the way around the world. But one rainy night, SCREECH — CRASH — BOOM! His car skidded off a mountain road, and when he woke up, his hands were a trembling, shaking mess. The very hands that made him special... were broken.

Can you imagine? Stephen tried every doctor, every clinic, every cure he could find. He spent every last coin he had searching for a fix. Nothing worked. He was just about ready to give up when he heard a rumor — a whisper, really — about a place called Kamar-Taj, hidden high up in the snowy Himalayan mountains of Nepal, where impossible things happened every single day.

So Stephen packed a bag and went. And do you know what he found? The Ancient One — a teacher so wise she had been alive for over FIVE HUNDRED years! Five hundred whole years! The Ancient One didn't fix his hands with medicine. She did something much more surprising. She pushed him through a doorway... into the entire multiverse. WHOOSH! Suddenly Stephen wasn't looking at one world anymore — he was looking at millions of worlds, all stacked on top of each other like the most enormous cosmic layer cake you've ever imagined.

Stephen's clever surgeon's brain lit up like a lamp. You see, in the comics, Kamar-Taj first appeared all the way back in 1963 — a hidden land in the Himalayas that most people never find. Stephen studied day and night, learning to make shields of sparkling light, to open shimmering golden portals with his hands, and even to bend time itself. His precise memory — the same one that mapped every nerve during surgery — helped him remember every swirl and syllable of every spell. FWOOM!

Are you ready for this? Stephen Strange, the surgeon who thought he knew everything, became Doctor Strange, the Sorcerer Supreme — Earth's first line of defense against every magical monster and extra-dimensional menace in the whole wide multiverse. He discovered something amazing: sometimes the biggest disaster of your life is secretly the doorway to your greatest adventure!

CRACK! And just like that, the world's most proud doctor became its most extraordinary hero.
