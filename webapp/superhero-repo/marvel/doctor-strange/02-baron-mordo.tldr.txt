• Baron Karl Mordo: once the Ancient One's most promising student, wielder of immense disciplined magic
• Believed himself the rightful successor to the title of Sorcerer Supreme
• Resentment grew when Strange arrived and quickly ascended the ranks of Kamar-Taj
• Mordo's rigid philosophy: magic must be strictly regulated; those who misuse it must be stripped of power
• Wields the Staff of the Tribunal with lethal precision; favors ancient traditional techniques over creative improvisation
• His worldview fractured when he discovered the Ancient One had been drawing power from the Dark Dimension
• Betrayal of Kamar-Taj was a profound blow to Strange, who had looked up to Mordo as a mentor and friend
• Mordo became a villain by his own twisted logic: hunting sorcerers he deems unworthy, believing this protects the multiverse
• Represents the shadow of the Sorcerer Supreme — all the skill, none of the humility
• Lesson: discipline without humility and a dogmatic mind can corrupt even the most gifted hero
