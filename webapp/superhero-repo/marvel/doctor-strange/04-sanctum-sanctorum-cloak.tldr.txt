• Sanctum Sanctorum: Doctor Strange's mystical headquarters in Greenwich Village, New York City
• Appears as an eccentric townhouse to passersby; inside, rooms shift and defy the laws of physics
• The Seal of Vishanti: massive circular window projecting a protective aura; masks the building from magical detection
• Houses a vast library of ancient texts and artifacts gathered from across the multiverse
• The Cloak of Levitation: sentient mystical artifact that chose Strange as its master; has its own personality
• Cloak abilities: flight, physical shielding, independent action, entangling enemies
• Wong serves as Strange's assistant and fellow guardian of the Sanctum
• The Sanctum is also a sanctuary for heroes entangled in supernatural crises — time flows differently inside
• Ongoing threat: the Sanctum is a target for those who want its artifacts or a gateway to Earth's defenses
• Lesson: even the most powerful guardian needs a home and a partner; the Sanctum is both fortress and foundation
