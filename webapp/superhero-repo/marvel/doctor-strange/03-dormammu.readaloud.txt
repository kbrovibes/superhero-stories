Lean in close, because I'm about to tell you about the most enormous, most hungry, most extraordinary villain Doctor Strange ever faced — a being called Dormammu, Dread Lord of the Dark Dimension!

Dormammu isn't a person exactly. He isn't even a creature exactly. He is pure swirling mystical energy — a cosmic fire that never stops burning, never stops wanting MORE. His Dark Dimension is a wild, twisting place where the sky is always the color of a bruise and nothing ever stands still. And Dormammu wants to swallow our entire world — every city, every park, every sleeping baby — and drag it all into his Dark Dimension forever. BOOM!

Now, you might think: well, Doctor Strange just ZAPs him with a spell! But here is the tricky part. Are you listening? Dormammu is SO powerful that no magic, no shield, no clever trick could ever beat him in a straight fight. He is bigger than any weapon. Fighting Dormammu head-on would be like trying to win a rainstorm with a paper umbrella.

So Strange did something very clever instead. He flew into the Dark Dimension — which is a place beyond time itself, where time doesn't even EXIST — and he brought with him the Eye of Agamotto, which holds the Time Stone. And in a place with no time, time is the most powerful thing in the whole universe!

WHOOSH! Strange created a time loop. "Dormammu — I've come to bargain." Dormammu destroyed him. FLASH! Strange came back. "I've come to bargain." Again! FLASH! Again! The filmmakers revealed that Strange likely went through this loop a THOUSAND times — maybe more. Can you even count to a thousand? That is how patient and brave he was.

And eventually, even Dormammu — the endless, all-devouring lord of darkness — got so tired of the loop that he agreed: leave Earth alone, take his followers with him, and never return.

Strange did not beat Dormammu by being stronger. He beat him by being patient, and brave, and willing to keep trying no matter how many times it took.

Sometimes the greatest power isn't a spell at all. It's the courage to never, ever give up.
