• Clea: daughter of Prince Orini and niece of Dormammu; born and raised in the Dark Dimension
• Noble spirit despite her lineage — she desired to protect her home from her uncle's tyranny
• First assisted Strange when he ventured into the Dark Dimension, risking everything to help him fight Dormammu
• Under Strange's mentorship at Kamar-Taj, Clea mastered the Mystic Arts and became a formidable sorceress
• Their partnership deepened into profound love — a bond that transcended dimensional barriers
• Clea eventually became the Sorcerer Supreme and ruler of the Dark Dimension, unseating her uncle's influence
• Recurring conflict: choosing between duty to the Dark Dimension and supporting Strange in his battles on Earth
• Clea gave Strange a deeper understanding of the Dark Dimension's complexity and its people who seek the light
• Strange provided Clea with a moral compass — using power for protection, not for personal gain
• Lesson: even the most fundamental differences between two worlds can be overcome through shared purpose and mutual respect
