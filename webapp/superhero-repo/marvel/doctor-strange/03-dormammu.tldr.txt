• Dormammu: dread lord of the Dark Dimension; pure mystical energy of infinite hunger and no fixed form
• Goal: absorb all dimensions into his own, erasing individual existence across the multiverse
• Cannot be defeated through traditional combat — his power is too vast
• Strange used the Eye of Agamotto to create a time loop, trapping himself and Dormammu in an infinite cycle of death and rebirth
• Strange endured the loop countless times — dying over and over — to force a stalemate and protect Earth
• The bargain: Dormammu agreed to leave Earth alone and take his Zealot followers with him
• Dormammu typically works through intermediaries — granting fragments of his power to willing servants to open gateways to Earth
• The Dark Dimension itself is a chaotic landscape hostile to human life and sanity
• Strange guards Earth's dimensional barriers knowing Dormammu is always watching for a single moment of weakness
• Lesson: sometimes the strongest weapon is the willingness to endure — not power, but patience and sacrifice
