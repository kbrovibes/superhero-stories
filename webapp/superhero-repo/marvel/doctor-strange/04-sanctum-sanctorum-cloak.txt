Doctor Strange has two very special things that help him in his daily life: his house, the Sanctum Sanctorum, and his faithful friend, the Cloak of Levitation. The Sanctum Sanctorum is a mysterious building located in New York City. On the outside, it looks like a normal house, but on the inside, it is filled with magical rooms, ancient artifacts, and doors that lead to other dimensions. It is a place of study and protection.

The Cloak of Levitation is not just a piece of clothing; it has a personality of its own! The Cloak chose Doctor Strange because of his brave heart and magical potential. It can fly, help Strange hover in the air, and even protect him from attacks. Sometimes, the Cloak even acts on its own to help out, like a loyal pet that is also a very cool cape. It is one of the most recognizable parts of Doctor Strange's look.

Inside the Sanctum Sanctorum, you can find the Window of the Worlds, a large circular window with a unique symbol that helps protect the building from magical intruders. The house is full of strange noises and magical surprises, like stairs that never end or library books that can talk. It is a very busy place, as Doctor Strange is always researching new spells and keeping an eye on magical threats around the globe.

The combination of the Sanctum and the Cloak gives Doctor Strange a solid foundation for his work. The Sanctum provides a safe place to learn and store powerful items, while the Cloak gives him mobility and extra protection during battles. Both are essential to his role as the Sorcerer Supreme. They show that being a hero isn't just about what you can do, but also about the special tools and places that support you.

The Sanctum Sanctorum and the Cloak of Levitation are beloved parts of Doctor Strange's world. They add a touch of wonder and magic to everything he does. Whether he's hosting a meeting of the Avengers or studying an ancient scroll, these two magical elements are always by his side, helping him keep our dimension safe and sound.
