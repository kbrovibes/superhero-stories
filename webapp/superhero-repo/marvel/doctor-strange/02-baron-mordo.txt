Baron Mordo is a powerful sorcerer who was once a student alongside Stephen Strange. However, unlike Doctor Strange, Mordo became bitter and jealous over time. He believes that he should be the one with the most power and that magic should be used in a much stricter and more controlling way. This creates a deep rivalry between the two, as Mordo often tries to prove that his way is better.

Mordo is a very serious and disciplined individual. He has studied the Mystic Arts for many years and is a master of many dark and complex spells. He often thinks that Doctor Strange is too reckless with his magic and that the world would be safer if Mordo were in charge. This rivalry isn't just about who is stronger, but about how magic should be treated and used for the good of the universe.

One of Mordo's most dangerous traits is his persistence. He never gives up on his quest to reclaim what he believes is his rightful place. He often seeks out ancient and forbidden knowledge to gain an advantage over Strange. However, his anger and jealousy often cloud his judgment, leading him to make mistakes that Doctor Strange can use to stop him. Mordo’s serious nature makes him a formidable but predictable opponent.

The battles between Strange and Mordo are filled with spectacular displays of magic. They clash with glowing shields, energy blasts, and complex illusions. While Mordo uses his magic to impose his will, Strange uses his to protect and preserve. This difference in purpose is why Doctor Strange is the Sorcerer Supreme, and why Mordo continues to be a rival who struggles to find his way.

The conflict with Baron Mordo serves as a reminder that power can be dangerous if it isn't balanced with kindness and wisdom. Doctor Strange’s ability to stay calm and use his magic for the right reasons is what truly sets him apart. Even though Mordo is a skilled sorcerer, his rivalry with Strange shows that talent alone isn't enough to be a true hero.
