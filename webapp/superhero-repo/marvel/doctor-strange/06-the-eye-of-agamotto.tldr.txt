• Eye of Agamotto: mystical talisman created by Agamotto, the very first Sorcerer Supreme
• Sentient relic that chooses its own master, requiring spiritual purity and mental discipline
• Contains the Time Stone — one of the six primordial Infinity Stones governing fundamental aspects of existence
• Time Stone powers: reverse catastrophic events, study potential futures, trap enemies in inescapable time loops
• Can perceive the true nature of beings — reveals extra-dimensional parasites, hidden motives, and mystical disguises
• Projects an "all-revealing light" from the Vishanti that banishes shadows and weakens malevolent entities
• Light dispels even the most potent dark magic, providing sanctuary in chaotic mystical battles
• Strange wears the Eye as a constant companion — its steady pulse a source of power and guidance
• Symbol of the Sorcerer Supreme's office; a link to the long lineage of Earth's magical guardians
• Lesson: in a multiverse of infinite possibilities, clarity, truth, and restraint in wielding power are the greatest gifts
