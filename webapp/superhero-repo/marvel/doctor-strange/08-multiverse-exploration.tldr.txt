• The multiverse: a sprawling reality Strange must navigate and protect as Sorcerer Supreme
• Strange uses the Sling Ring to open gateways between alternate realities
• Multiversal travel requires anchoring one's own identity against realities that overturn every assumption
• Strange has encountered alternate versions of himself — some who never had the accident, some who became dark sorcerers
• Alternate encounters show that every decision ripples across the multiverse; choice and character matter profoundly
• Inter-dimensional incursions: two universes colliding, threatening erasure of both — Strange works with heroes of other realities to prevent them
• Strange has seen worlds where he sacrificed everything for a reality that was ultimately doomed
• The Sanctum Sanctorum serves as a hub for monitoring multiversal activity and unauthorized intrusions
• Strange stands at the intersection of infinite possibilities, ensuring their world remains protected
• Lesson: exploring infinite realities deepens appreciation for the unique and fragile beauty of one's own world
