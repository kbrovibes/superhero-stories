Deep inside the swirling, churning darkness of the Dark Dimension — a terrifying place of impossible shapes and endless hunger ruled by the dreadful Dormammu — there lived someone who didn't belong to that darkness at all. Her name was Clea, and she was extraordinary.

Clea's family was very complicated, as families sometimes are. Her uncle was the terrible Dormammu himself. Her mother, Umar, was a powerful being of pure magical energy called a Faltine. And her father was Prince Orini, a noble ruler of the Dark Dimension. Clea grew up surrounded by enormous power and chaos — but she had a generous heart that none of it could harden. Can you imagine being Dormammu's niece? And still choosing to be KIND?

When Doctor Strange first ventured into the Dark Dimension to face her uncle, Clea did something amazingly brave. She HELPED Strange. She risked everything — her safety, her home, the wrath of the most terrifying being in any dimension — because she believed in what Strange was trying to do. That kind of courage is incredibly rare.

WHOOSH! Strange never forgot that. He invited Clea to study at Kamar-Taj, and she became a powerful sorceress in her own right. Her knowledge of the Dark Dimension was something Strange could never have learned from any book — she understood its secrets, its people, its hidden places. And Strange gave her something in return: a clear and steady moral compass, showing her how to use enormous power to protect others rather than control them. ZAP!

Their partnership deepened into something profound. In the Marvel comics, they even wore magical rings that were actually ONE ring split in two — mystically connected for all eternity! Clea eventually took her mother on in direct battle and WON, becoming the new ruler of the Dark Dimension, bringing order and kindness where there had only been cruelty. CRACK!

And in tribute to the person who helped shape her into the hero she became, Clea now goes by a new name: Clea Strange.

Even the darkest dimension in the universe couldn't stop one person with a good heart from shining.
