• Strange's Avengers role: mystical strategist and primary defense against threats that cannot be defeated by physical force
• Contributions in battle: opening portals for team transport, creating shields of light, manipulating time for tactical advantage, reshaping the environment
• Versatile magic neutralizes robotic drones, alien threats, and collapsing dimensional events
• He is the "wild card" — provides solutions teammates simply cannot
• The isolation tension: Strange sometimes makes decisions his teammates find incomprehensible because he sees the "long game"
• Shares mutual respect with Tony Stark and Steve Rogers based on the shared weight of leadership
• Educates Avengers on supernatural dangers; established protocols for handling magical artifacts
• Made the Avengers a more comprehensive force capable of facing threats from every dimension
• Strange traded one patient at a time in surgery for protecting an entire planet — the same healer instinct, wider scope
• Lesson: wisdom is knowing when to act and when to sacrifice — even when others cannot understand why
