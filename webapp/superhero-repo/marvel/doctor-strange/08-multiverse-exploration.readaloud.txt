What if I told you that our world is just ONE room in a house that has no walls, no ceiling, and goes on absolutely FOREVER? A house with billions and billions of rooms — each one a different universe — where history turned different corners, where heroes made different choices, and where familiar faces live completely different lives? WHOOSH! That is the multiverse, and Doctor Strange has walked through more of its doors than almost anyone alive!

Strange travels between these worlds using a Sling Ring — two small metal bands worn on his fingers. He spins his hands in the air and FWOOM — golden sparks spiral outward and a shimmering portal appears. But here is the tricky thing about the multiverse: every reality has its own rules, its own gravity, its own version of what is true. Strange has to hold onto who he is very tightly, or the shifting worlds might pull him apart.

And do you know what Strange found behind some of those doors? Other Stephens. There was one who never had his car accident — still a surgeon, still proud, with no idea magic even existed. There was one who had fallen into darkness, using his power for himself until his whole world fell apart. CRACK! Each one was like looking into a mirror — and Strange had to be very honest about what he saw looking back.

Here is an amazing fact: an incursion is when two universes actually START CRASHING INTO EACH OTHER — like two enormous ships colliding in slow motion, threatening to erase BOTH of them completely! Strange has raced to stop these terrifying events, sometimes working alongside heroes from completely other realities — people with familiar faces and very different stories — to protect worlds most people don't even know exist. BOOM!

Back at the Sanctum Sanctorum in New York City, Strange keeps careful watch over the multiversal borders, staying alert to any tremor in the fabric between worlds. The Sanctum is his watchtower, his library, and his home — all at once.

The multiverse is vast and wild and full of wonder and danger. But here is what Doctor Strange learned from exploring it: every single choice you make matters. Not just for your own life — but for all the lives that branch outward from that one moment, rippling across the infinite universe like rings on a pond.

Choose well, friend. The whole multiverse is watching!
