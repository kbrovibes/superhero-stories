• Kamar-Taj: secluded sanctuary hidden in the Himalayas; the spiritual center of Earth's sorcerer community
• Training begins not with spells but with unlearning — students must abandon preconceived notions of reality
• Students study ancient texts including the Book of Cagliostro and the Darkhold
• Physical precision required: every gesture and every syllable of incantation must be perfect
• Strange's surgical discipline and brilliant memory gave him an unprecedented rate of advancement
• Training covers mystical combat, portal-making with the Sling Ring, artifact use (including the Cloak of Levitation), and dimensional defense
• Students learn to work as a cohesive force, combining individual abilities against extra-dimensional threats
• The Ancient One and other master sorcerers guide students; Kamar-Taj's environment is infused with mystical energy
• Strange found purpose and peace at Kamar-Taj that he never knew in his surgical career
• Lesson: true mastery requires the balance of intellect and spirit, and the courage to unlearn what you think you know
