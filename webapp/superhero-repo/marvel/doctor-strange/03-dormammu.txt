Dormammu, the dread lord of the Dark Dimension, is the most formidable entity that Doctor Strange has ever faced. A being of pure mystical energy and infinite hunger, Dormammu exists outside the conventional laws of time and space, presiding over a realm where reality is a nightmarish landscape. His goal is the absorption of all dimensions into his own, a cosmic expansion that would result in the erasure of individual existence. For the Sorcerer Supreme, Dormammu is the ultimate test.

The threat is existential, as Dormammu cannot be defeated through traditional combat. When he first attempted to consume Earth, Strange realized he could not win through force. Instead, he utilized the Eye of Agamotto to create a time loop, trapping himself and Dormammu in an infinite cycle of death and rebirth. Strange endured countless agonies to force a stalemate and protect his world. This victory established the unique relationship between the Sorcerer Supreme and the ruler of the Dark Dimension.

Dormammu views Strange not as a rival, but as an annoying obstacle that must be bypassed. He often works through intermediaries, granting them a fraction of his power in exchange for their service in opening a gateway to Earth. This subtle approach makes Dormammu a constant, looming shadow over the mystical community, requiring Strange to remain eternally vigilant. The Dark Dimension itself is a manifestation of Dormammu’s nature—a place of chaotic beauty and terrifying power hostile to human life.

Strange has had to venture into this nightmare landscape on numerous occasions to prevent Dormammu’s influence from spreading. These journeys are a grueling test of Strange’s mental fortitude, as he must maintain his sanity in a place designed to strip it away. Dormammu remains a constant threat, his hunger for conquest never truly sated. He is a reminder of the scale of the dangers the Sorcerer Supreme must face—threats that are beyond the comprehension of ordinary mortals.

The conflict between the two is a perpetual struggle between the light of our reality and the encroaching darkness. Strange knows he cannot truly destroy Dormammu, only hold him at bay, a responsibility that defines his life. Every moment of peace is a hard-won victory in a war that has no end. The legacy of Dormammu is one of profound sacrifice and understanding of true power. By facing the dread lord, Strange proved the strongest weapon is the willingness to endure.

Strange continues to guard the barriers between dimensions, knowing Dormammu is always watching for a single moment of weakness. The Sorcerer Supreme stands as a silent sentinel at the edge of the abyss, ensuring the Dark Dimension remains a distant threat rather than our final destiny. His dedication to this cause is absolute, protecting a world blissfully unaware of the cosmic stakes. As long as he stands watch, the hunger of Dormammu will be held back from our world.
