Okay. This is the BIG one. Strap in!

Five years after Thanos's terrible snap, Tony Stark had an idea. A wild, breathtaking, quantum physics idea. "What if," he said, "we went back in TIME — and collected the Infinity Stones from the PAST before Thanos got them? And then used them to fix everything?"

The remaining Avengers called it the Time Heist. And Nebula was on the team!

Her mission: travel to 2014 — five years in the past! — alongside War Machine, James Rhodes, to the barren planet Morag, where the Power Stone was sitting in a silver orb inside an ancient temple. Get in, get the Stone, get out. SIMPLE, right?

Except for one huge problem.

Nebula's cybernetics were still partly linked to Thanos's systems. And when the mission started, Thanos — the 2014 version, younger and even MORE dangerous — somehow detected the quantum signal and reached right into Nebula's neural memory.

He saw everything. The Time Heist. The Avengers. The plan. The future.

Uh oh. UH. OH.

Past-Nebula — the version of Nebula who had not yet made her choices, who was still 100% working for Thanos — was sent to take present-Nebula's place. And she helped bring Thanos's entire fleet forward through time to the present.

Meanwhile, Hulk was using a brand new Gauntlet — built by Tony, holding all six new Infinity Stones — to restore everyone who had vanished five years ago. SNAP! And it WORKED!

But before the celebrating could even start — KRAKOOM! The sky broke open and Thanos arrived with his whole army!

The Battle of the Avengers Compound! The biggest fight in the history of ever!

And Nebula was already there. Fighting on the right side. Not because anyone told her to. Not because Thanos commanded it. Because she CHOSE it.

The hardest moment? Looking at her past self across the battlefield. That other Nebula — still closed off, still angry, still believing in Thanos — looked back at her.

Present-Nebula did not waver. She knew who she was now. She made her choice. CLEAR AND FAST, like a blade cutting true.

And then — the sky opened again! Portals! Purple and gold and blazing — WHOOOOSH! Every restored hero, every Avenger, every Guardian, every warrior, stepping through from across time and space!

"Avengers! ASSEMBLE!" called a very certain voice.

And Nebula was already there, already standing, already fighting — not behind anyone, not following anyone, but exactly where she had chosen to be.

That was the moment. The moment everything was won.

And Nebula was part of it. She always had been.
