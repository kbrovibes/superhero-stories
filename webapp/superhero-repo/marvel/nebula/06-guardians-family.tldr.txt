• The Guardians are Star-Lord, Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot — the most unlikely family in space
• They fly the battered, beloved Milano from their base at Knowhere, a station inside an ancient Celestial's skull
• Peter Quill plays 1980s Earth music tapes on every flight; Rocket never stops tinkering with the engines
• Nebula first came to Knowhere as an enemy; she returned, slowly, as something different
• Trust among the Guardians builds one honest moment at a time — no ceremony, no vote
• Groot leaves tiny flowers where people will find them; Drax takes everything literally; Rocket solves problems nobody asked him to
• Being a Guardian means showing up — answering a call, sitting with someone in a hard moment, flying through danger together
• The Guardians never wanted Nebula's obedience; they wanted her, exactly as she is
• She learns that chosen family can be stranger, warmer, and more real than anything Thanos promised
