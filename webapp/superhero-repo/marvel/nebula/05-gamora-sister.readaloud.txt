Have you ever had a friend — or maybe a brother or sister — who you argued with all the time, but deep down you loved more than almost anyone?

That is the story of Nebula and Gamora.

Gamora had green skin and incredible fighting skills and golden-brown eyes that never missed anything. She had been taken from her home world of Zen-Whoberi by Thanos, just like Nebula had been taken from Luphom. They were both girls who had lost everything and ended up in the same cold warship, the Sanctuary, trying to survive.

And they sparred. Every single day. CLANG — swords clashing! THUD — kicks landing! CRASH — down goes Nebula again!

Here is the terrible part: every time Nebula lost to Gamora, Thanos replaced a piece of her with metal. So losing to her sister literally changed her body, piece by piece. You can understand why Nebula was a LITTLE bit upset!

For years and years, Nebula told herself she hated Gamora. She chased her through the galaxy — through the Kyln prison where the lights buzzed and the cells were locked with force fields! Across the shining skies of Xandar! Even above Ego's Living Planet, a world that was also a giant thinking brain! WHOOSH — punches and kicks and blades flashing in the alien sunlight!

But you know what was really happening?

Nebula didn't hate Gamora at all.

She missed her. She wanted a sister who would say, "You are good enough. You have always been good enough."

The anger was just the way the missing-ness felt when it did not know how to come out.

And then — after all the battles and the years and the hardest times — they finally sat together and talked.

REALLY talked.

Gamora said: "You were always the braver one. It is much harder to keep going after losing than it is to win."

Nebula's circuits flickered. Her cybernetic eye blinked twice — that meant she was surprised!

"I never wanted to beat you," Nebula said. "I just wanted to be your equal."

"You always were," Gamora said.

Oh wow.

Sometimes the words we needed most were right there the whole time, just waiting for the right moment.

Gamora: fierce and green and warm and brave.
Nebula: fierce and blue and sharp and brave.

Two sisters who took the longest, hardest road to exactly the same place.

And now? Now they stand shoulder to shoulder, blue and green together, and the whole galaxy had better watch out!

Because when two sisters who took the hardest road decide to face the same direction? There is absolutely nothing in the universe that can stop them.
