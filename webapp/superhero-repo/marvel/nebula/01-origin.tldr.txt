• Thanos takes Nebula as an adopted daughter and raises her alongside Gamora on his warship, Sanctuary
• Thanos trains them as warriors, pitting sister against sister in sparring matches
• Each time Nebula loses to Gamora, Thanos replaces part of her body with a mechanical upgrade — arm, eye, shoulder
• Nebula grows fierce and determined, covered in cybernetic parts, but never loses her will to be free
• She watches Gamora escape to join the Guardians of the Galaxy and feels furious and alone
• After years of fighting the wrong battles, Nebula begins to see Thanos for what he truly is
• She breaks away from Thanos's control and searches the galaxy for a better purpose
• She makes peace with Gamora, choosing sisterhood over rivalry at last
• Her mechanical parts are not marks of failure — they are proof she survived everything Thanos threw at her
