• Ego the Living Planet is a Celestial who shaped himself into a beautiful world of golden hills and blue flowers
• He is Peter Quill's biological father — and he invites the Guardians to his surface warmly
• His true plan, the Expansion, is to spread his consciousness across every world in the universe
• Nebula escapes captivity on the Ravager ship Eclector just as crisis erupts on Ego's surface
• The golden hills ripple, tendrils push through the soil, and the blue flowers reveal themselves as nodes of Ego's growing mind
• Nebula maps the terrain with her cybernetic eye and works alongside Mantis to evacuate the wounded
• Rocket builds a bomb from the Milano's power source; the Guardians carry it to Ego's living core
• Nebula coordinates the evacuation, making sure everyone reaches a ship before the countdown ends
• Ego's planet breaks apart in rings of golden light; Nebula watches and reflects on what it means to choose
