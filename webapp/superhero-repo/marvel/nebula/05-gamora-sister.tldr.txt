• Nebula and Gamora grow up on Thanos's warship Sanctuary — Gamora taken from Zen-Whoberi, Nebula from Luphom
• Gamora usually wins their training matches; each of Nebula's losses costs her a piece of herself, replaced by metal
• Nebula tells herself she hates Gamora, but beneath the anger she wants a sister who believes in her
• She chases Gamora through the Kyln prison, across Xandar, and above Ego's Living Planet
• Their battles are fierce — but the real fight is inside Nebula's heart
• After years of grief and hard work side by side, they finally sit and talk honestly
• Gamora says Nebula was always the braver one — it takes more courage to keep going after a loss than to keep winning
• Nebula admits she never wanted to beat Gamora; she only ever wanted to be seen as equal
• They were always equal — and that is what makes them true sisters
