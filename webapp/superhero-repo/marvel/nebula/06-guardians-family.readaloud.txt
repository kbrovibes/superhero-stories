Okay — I need to introduce you to the greatest, weirdest, most wonderful team in the entire galaxy!

These are the Guardians of the Galaxy!

First there is Peter Quill — Star-Lord! He is half-human, born in a small town in Missouri on planet Earth, and he carries a portable music player everywhere he goes. He loves songs from the 1980s. He once challenged a very powerful villain to a dance-off. He is the absolute best kind of ridiculous.

Then there is Gamora — green-skinned, fierce, and quite possibly the best fighter you have ever seen!

Then Drax! He is enormous and covered in tattoos and he takes EVERYTHING absolutely literally. You say "this place blew my mind" and Drax will look very, very worried about your brain.

Then Rocket! A talking raccoon who is a mechanical genius and also has Strong Opinions about everything at all times. VERY LOUD STRONG OPINIONS.

And Groot! A tree. The gentlest, warmest, most magnificent tree in the universe. He only ever says three words: "I am Groot." But somehow those three words cover every single thing he needs to say.

Their ship is the Milano — scratched and patched and absolutely full of character. Peter plays music on the cockpit speakers during every single flight. Rocket is always tuning the engines. Groot sometimes grows tiny flowers and leaves them somewhere you will find them later and feel SO happy about.

Their home base? Knowhere! A space station built inside the enormous ancient head of a Celestial being, drifting at the very edge of the galaxy. It has a mining colony and a market and a bar and approximately ten thousand interesting smells.

Nebula first visited Knowhere as an enemy, chasing the Guardians to stop them. CLANG — CRASH — definitely not a great first impression!

But the Guardians are the kind of people who believe anyone can do better.

Slowly — in battles and quiet moments and long flights across the stars — Nebula found her place. She learned to answer the comms when Rocket needed backup. She learned to sit quietly next to Drax when words were not working. She learned to fly wingtip-to-wingtip with Gamora and not say anything at all, because sometimes that is enough.

The Guardians never wanted Nebula to follow orders.

They just wanted Nebula.

Stubborn. Precise. Loyal as a titanium bolt once she decides you are worth it.

And occasionally — very occasionally — extremely funny in the most deadpan way possible.

Welcome to the family, Nebula. THWIP! WHIRR! GROOOOT!
