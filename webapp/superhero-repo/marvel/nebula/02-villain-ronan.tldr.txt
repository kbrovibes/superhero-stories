• Ronan the Accuser is a Kree warrior in black armor who carries the massive war hammer called the Universal Weapon
• Thanos sends Nebula to serve alongside Ronan aboard his warship, the Dark Aster, as an enforcer
• Ronan plans to seize the Power Stone from Morag and destroy the planet Xandar — Nebula watches his cold cruelty and feels deeply uneasy
• The Guardians — Star-Lord, Gamora, Rocket, Drax, and Groot — fly to Xandar to stop Ronan's fleet
• Nebula and Gamora clash mid-air above Xandar's golden spires — fierce, evenly matched
• Star-Lord seizes the Power Stone; the five Guardians share its energy and blast Ronan together
• The Dark Aster crashes; Nebula escapes alone into the stars
• She leaves knowing she fought on the wrong side — and that knowledge becomes the first step toward choosing right
