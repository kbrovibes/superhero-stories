• Thanos wears the Infinity Gauntlet — a golden glove holding six Infinity Stones — and plans to erase half of all life
• Nebula is captured aboard his warship the Sanctuary II; her own cybernetic memories are read and used against the people she cares about
• On the ruined moon Titan, Iron Man, Doctor Strange, Spider-Man, Star-Lord, Drax, Mantis, and Nebula fight Thanos together
• They nearly remove the Gauntlet — one finger free — but the plan breaks and Thanos escapes their grip
• Thanos snaps his fingers; half of all living things vanish in an instant
• Nebula survives and makes a promise: she will find a way to make this right
• She works alongside the remaining Avengers through years of hard effort
• When vanished lives are restored, Nebula faces Thanos on Earth in the final battle
• She does not flinch — every piece of her, metal and heart alike, holds steady
