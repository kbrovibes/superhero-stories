• Nebula's cybernetic parts were installed by Thanos as punishments — each replaced a piece of her after a training loss to Gamora
• Her left arm is fully mechanical: grips with great force, extends cables, and fires energy blasts
• Her right eye is a sensor array that tracks movement at great distances and sees clearly in total darkness
• Her spine is a titanium lattice; her neural processors run faster than most organic brains
• She can rewire her own arm mid-fight and hack electronic locks by plugging directly into hardware
• She carries retractable blades in her cybernetic forearm, ready in an instant
• Her greatest power is resilience — she treats pain as information, not a reason to quit
• Gamora calls Nebula the most determined person she has ever known — a quality no upgrade can install
• Her metal parts are not a punishment anymore; they are hers, built into who she became
