If you ever hear Natasha Romanoff and Clint Barton talking about Budapest and both burst out laughing at the same exact moment — well, that laugh is hiding one of the most incredible, most frightening, most friendship-making adventures in Marvel history. Are you ready to hear what REALLY happened?

Budapest is a beautiful old city, full of winding rivers and ancient stone bridges, in a country called Hungary. And for Natasha Romanoff, it was the place where her whole life changed. Here is why!

Clint Barton — code name Hawkeye, best archer in the world, never misses a shot — had been sent on a mission by S.H.I.E.L.D. to find the Black Widow and stop her. It was a serious mission. An important mission. But when Clint finally stood in front of Natasha, he did not do what his orders said. He looked at her — really looked — and he saw a person who was exhausted from being used, who wanted to do something good. And instead of stopping her, he offered her something no one ever had: a second chance.

She took it. And then things got extremely LOUD.

You see, in Budapest, Natasha and Clint had secretly planted explosives in a building where a very bad man named General Dreykov was hiding — the very same villain who ran the Red Room. BOOM! The plan worked. But then the Hungarian security forces came pouring after them, and suddenly our two new almost-friends found themselves running for their lives through the most twisty, turny, complicated streets you have ever seen! They hid in an apartment building. They scrambled through underground tunnels. For TEN WHOLE DAYS they were on the run — and two of those days were spent hiding inside the ceiling of a subway station! Can you imagine crouching in a ceiling for two days? Your knees would never forgive you!

But in those quiet moments between the running and the hiding, they talked. Clint with his bow, Natasha with her quick hands — they found out they were not so different after all. Both had hard pasts. Both wanted to do better.

By the time they finally escaped the city, Natasha had made her decision. S.H.I.E.L.D. was her new home. Hawkeye was her new best friend. And Budapest? Budapest became their forever inside joke — the adventure that started everything.
