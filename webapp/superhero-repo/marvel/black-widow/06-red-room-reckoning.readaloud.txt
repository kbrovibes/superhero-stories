Have you ever thought something was finished, all taken care of, gone for good — and then discovered it was still lurking in the shadows? That is exactly what happened to Natasha Romanoff when she found out the most terrifying truth: the Red Room was still out there. And this time, it had gotten even worse.

The man behind it all was called General Dreykov — a sneaky, cowardly mastermind who ran the whole operation from a floating fortress hidden in the clouds! Can you imagine? A whole secret base in the SKY, drifting above the earth where nobody could find it. And inside, he had a terrible new plan. The new Black Widows he controlled were not trained the old way — they had been given a chemical that took away their free will entirely. They could not choose to stop. They could not say no. They were prisoners even inside their own minds.

When Natasha found out, she felt a cold jolt of anger and sadness together. She knew exactly what it felt like to have your choices stolen away. She had lived it. And she was NOT going to let it happen to anyone else.

So she did something she had needed to do for a very long time. She reached out to her family. Her surrogate little sister, Yelena Belova. Her former pretend-mother, Melina Vostokoff — a brilliant scientist who had herself been a Red Room spy. And the big, booming, bear-sized Red Guardian, Alexei Shostakov. They had all been apart for years. There were old hurts between them. But shared history and shared love pulled them back together — SNAP — just like that.

The four of them infiltrated Dreykov's sky fortress together. It was a tricky, dangerous, careful operation. When Natasha came face to face with the mind-controlled Widows, she did not fight them. She looked at their blank expressions and saw herself, years ago, and her heart broke a little. Gently, carefully, she used a chemical antidote to break Dreykov's hold over them one by one. She freed them!

Then she turned to Dreykov's operation and dismantled every last piece of it. THUNK — his network, gone! CRACK — his control, destroyed! And afterwards, Natasha worked to find every single freed Widow a safe, warm place to begin a brand new life.

Because nobody — nobody — deserves to have their choices taken away from them. Especially not by someone hiding in the clouds.
