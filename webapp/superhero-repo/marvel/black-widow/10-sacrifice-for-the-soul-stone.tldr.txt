• Setting: the desolate, wind-swept plains of Vormir — the location of the Soul Stone
• The universe's requirement: the Soul Stone demands the sacrifice of that which one truly loves
• Natasha arrives at Vormir with Clint Barton (Hawkeye), her closest friend and Budapest partner
• Journey to Vormir represents the culmination of her lifelong quest to clear the "red in her ledger"
• Both Natasha and Clint fight for the right to sacrifice themselves so the other can live
• Natasha overpowers Clint — her determination is absolute and unbreakable
• She leaps from the cliff so the Avengers can obtain the Soul Stone and reverse Thanos's victory
• Her choice: the ultimate expression of reclaimed agency — a life no longer controlled but freely given
• Impact: Clint carries the grief; the remaining Avengers are galvanised to finish the fight
• Legacy: remembered not for the darkness of her origins but for the brilliant light of her final heroic choice
