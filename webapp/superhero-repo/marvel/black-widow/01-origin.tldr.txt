• Natasha Romanoff: born in Russia, recruited as a child into the covert Red Room program
• Red Room: secret facility that trained young girls to be the world's most lethal spies and assassins
• Training included espionage, hand-to-hand combat, and psychological manipulation — identity stripped away
• Natasha excelled; became the Red Room's top operative under the codename Black Widow
• Growing guilt: realised she was a weapon for a dark regime, not a protector
• Turning point: crossed paths with S.H.I.E.L.D. agent Clint Barton (Hawkeye), who was tasked with eliminating her
• Clint recognised her inner conflict and offered her a lifeline — a chance to defect
• Joined S.H.I.E.L.D.; used Red Room training to dismantle terrorist networks and protect global security
• Goal: clear the "red in her ledger" by saving innocent lives
• Founding member of the Avengers — proves true power comes from bravery, precision, and unbreakable will
