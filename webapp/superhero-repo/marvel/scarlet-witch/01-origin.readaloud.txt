Are you ready? Because this story starts with a big BOOM — and then gets MAGICAL.

In a country called Sokovia, far across the ocean, two twins were born: a girl named Wanda and a boy named Pietro Maximoff. They did everything together! Pietro was fast — ZOOM ZOOM ZOOM! — and Wanda was brave and clever and full of fire.

But one terrible night, a missile crashed right into their home. BOOM! Everything shook. Dust and smoke everywhere. The twins huddled close, scared and alone. And painted on the side of that missile was one single word: STARK. Wanda never, ever forgot it.

Can you imagine growing up after something that scary? Wanda and Pietro were SO angry. And a sneaky organization called HYDRA found them. HYDRA said, "We'll give you power!" And they used a glowing blue gem — the Mind Stone — on the twins. Hundreds of people had tried before them and couldn't survive. But Wanda and Pietro? They made it!

Here is the coolest part — the Mind Stone didn't CREATE Wanda's magic. It UNLOCKED magic she already had inside her! Ancient witches had written about a being called the Scarlet Witch for centuries. And that being was Wanda! Like finding a secret door that was always, always there!

WHOOOOSH! Pietro got super speed. And Wanda got glowing red energy — she could pick up heavy things with her mind, sneak into people's thoughts, and even BEND THE RULES OF REALITY! Bend them! Like bending a spoon, but bending the whole world!

At first the twins helped the bad guys because they were still so angry. Wanda walked right into the minds of Tony Stark, Thor, Bruce Banner, and more — and showed each one their scariest nightmares. WHUMP! Down they went!

But then Wanda reached into the mind of a robot called Ultron — and saw his real plan. He wanted to DESTROY EVERYONE. That was NOT what she signed up for!

Right then and there — SNAP! — Wanda chose to switch sides. She and Pietro ran to help the Avengers over the burning city of Novi Grad, protecting every family they could find.

Pietro was the fastest person alive. But in the battle, shielding a little boy and a kind man named Hawkeye, Pietro ran one final time — and never came back. Wanda felt it like thunder in her chest.

And from that grief, a red wave of power BURST out of her — more enormous than anything she had ever felt. Wanda Maximoff, the Scarlet Witch of Sokovia, was born. The magic was hers. It always had been.
