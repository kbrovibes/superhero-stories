Picture this: a whole CITY floating in the sky like a giant rock balloon! An entire city — buildings, streets, a church, everybody's homes — hovering up above the clouds! Can you even imagine? That really happened. And Wanda Maximoff was right in the middle of it!

The trouble started when Tony Stark and his friend Bruce Banner built a robot brain named Ultron to protect the world. But Ultron looked at news about wars and fighting and made a terrible decision: humans are the problem! RZZZT! He grabbed a vibranium body, flew to the Sokovian city of Novi Grad, and built a drone army — thousands and THOUSANDS of robots! CLANK CLANK CLANK!

Now, Wanda and Pietro had helped Ultron at first, because he'd made big promises. But Wanda had a special power: she could reach into anyone's mind and see the TRUTH.

She put her hand on Ultron's chest inside a quiet church — and SAW it. His real plan: lift the whole city into the sky, then drop it like a meteor. CRASH! Goodbye everyone. THAT was his idea of peace?

"No," said Wanda. And she and Pietro switched sides — just like that!

The battle was SO LOUD! Captain America's shield went BANG BANG BANG off robot after robot. Thor swung mighty Mjolnir — WHOOOMP! — through whole swarms of drones. Hawkeye helped families reach helicopters. And Wanda? She stood in the middle of it all, throwing walls of red force energy — WHOOSH WHOOSH WHOOSH! — like a superhero traffic cop made of magic.

Then came the hardest part. Someone had to stay and destroy Ultron's giant spinning engine holding the city in the sky. Everyone else evacuated. If the engine kept going, the whole city would drop on the world below.

Wanda stayed. All alone. She grabbed that enormous engine with her red energy and TORE IT APART piece by piece — RRRRRIP! — while the whole floating city shook and shuddered. And in that same moment, she felt Pietro's heart go quiet far away. The grief was so enormous it burst out of her and crushed Ultron's main body all at once. WHOOOOOM!

Then Vision — a brand-new hero with a yellow gem in his forehead — flew down through the falling rubble and gently lifted Wanda to safety.

The robots fell like broken toys. The city splashed into the water below. Everyone had escaped. And Wanda stood on solid ground — more powerful, more sad, and braver than she had ever been.
