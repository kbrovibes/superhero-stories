• Wanda and Pietro Maximoff are twins — Pietro is ten minutes older and never lets her forget it
• Their parents are killed in Sokovia by a Stark missile; a second missile falls but does not go off, and the twins hold each other under the rubble for two days
• HYDRA's Mind Stone experiments give Pietro impossible speed — to him, the whole world stands still
• He uses his speed to make Wanda laugh — pulling chairs out and back again before she falls
• On the day Ultron tries to drop Novi Grad out of the sky, Pietro fights as an Avenger and saves a Sokovian boy named Costel
• When Ultron fires at Hawkeye and a child in the open, Pietro is the only one fast enough to reach them — and he catches every bullet
• His last words, with a small smile: "You didn't see that coming"
• Years later, Hawkeye names his fourth child Nathaniel Pietro Barton, after the brother Wanda Maximoff would ever have
