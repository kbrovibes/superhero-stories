• After Westview, Wanda goes to a quiet cabin in the mountains with one book: the Darkhold, a glowing green book of dark magic
• The Darkhold whispers a promise — somewhere in the multiverse there is another Wanda with Billy and Tommy, and she can reach them
• Doctor Strange shows up with a brave young girl named America Chavez, who can kick open star-shaped portals between universes
• The Darkhold tells Wanda that if she takes America's gift, she can travel to her children
• Strange and America run across strange worlds — a green-glass universe, a black-and-white paint universe — trying to stay ahead of the Scarlet Witch
• Nothing Doctor Strange tries is strong enough — not Kamar-Taj spells, not the Book of the Vishanti
• Wanda finally reaches another universe's Billy and Tommy — but they cry, because she is a stranger, and they want their real mother
• Hearing her own boys frightened of her wakes Wanda up; she crushes every copy of the Darkhold in every universe so no one will ever be tempted by it again
