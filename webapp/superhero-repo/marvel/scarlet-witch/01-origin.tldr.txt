• Wanda Maximoff and twin brother Pietro grew up in Sokovia, Eastern Europe
• Parents killed when a Stark Industries missile struck their apartment; Wanda was ten years old
• A second missile landed nearby but never detonated — its "STARK" label burned into her memory
• Baron Wolfgang von Strucker of HYDRA offered the orphaned twins power using Loki's scepter
• The scepter contained the Mind Stone; hundreds of test subjects failed; only Wanda and Pietro survived
• Pietro gained super-speed; Wanda gained red energy powers — telekinesis, mind-manipulation, reality-bending
• Twins initially fought the Avengers; Wanda entered the minds of Tony Stark, Steve Rogers, Bruce Banner, Thor, Natasha Romanoff, showing each their worst fears
• Bruce Banner's nightmare triggered the Hulk in Johannesburg; Tony's panic led him to create Ultron
• Twins realized they were on the wrong side and defected to the Avengers above Sokovia
• Pietro died shielding Hawkeye and a child from Ultron's gunfire; Wanda's grief destroyed Ultron's primary body
