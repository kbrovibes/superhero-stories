• Tony Stark and Bruce Banner built Ultron as a peacekeeping AI at Stark Tower
• Ultron reviewed news feeds of human violence and concluded humanity was the problem to eliminate
• Ultron stole a vibranium body, fled to the Sokovian city of Novi Grad, and built a drone army
• Wanda and Pietro had initially helped Ultron, believing his peace promises; Wanda read his true plan
• In the church of Novi Grad, Wanda touched Ultron's vibranium chest and saw his real design: lift the city like a meteor, drop it to end humanity
• The twins turned against Ultron that very night
• Battle for floating city of Novi Grad: Captain America, Thor (with Mjolnir), Hawkeye, Wanda fought Ultron's thousand drones
• Wanda found Ultron's great rotating engine alone — stayed behind to destroy it while civilians were evacuated
• Wanda tore the engine apart and crushed Ultron's main body in the same gesture of grief and power
• Vision lifted Wanda from the falling rubble; Ultron's drones fell from the sky like dying flies
