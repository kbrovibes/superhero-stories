• Wanda and Vision first become friends in the Avengers compound kitchen, trying to cook a Sokovian dish called paprikash that Vision has never tasted
• He has read every cookbook ever written but cannot eat food — Wanda laughs and teaches him anyway
• During the Sokovia Accords, Vision is asked to keep Wanda inside the compound; he doesn't agree but doesn't know how else to keep her safe
• They meet again on the battlefield in Leipzig and try very hard not to hurt each other
• For two years they meet in secret — walking through Edinburgh, Prague, and Budapest as Vision wears a disguise that makes him look like an ordinary man
• In Wakanda, Thanos comes for the Mind Stone in Vision's forehead — the same gem that gave Wanda her powers
• Vision quietly asks Wanda to destroy the stone — "It is the only way" — and she does, weeping
• Thanos undoes it a heartbeat later, but Wanda's courage in that single, terrible moment stays with her forever
