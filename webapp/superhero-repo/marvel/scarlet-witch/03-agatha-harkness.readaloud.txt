Okay, listen up! This story has a SECRET hiding inside it. Are you ready? Even Wanda didn't know the secret until it was almost too late!

After losing Vision, Wanda's heart was so broken that her magic did something nobody had ever seen before. A huge glowing red bubble — called a HEX — spread out from where she stood and swallowed an entire town in New Jersey called Westview! WHOOOM!

And inside the bubble? Everything turned into a cozy old-fashioned television show! Black and white pictures, cheerful music, a laughing audience. Wanda had a white picket fence and two little boys named Billy and Tommy. She had Vision back! It felt like home.

But the people of Westview were trapped inside — acting out parts in a show that Wanda was writing without even knowing it! Outside, a S.W.O.R.D. agent named Monica Rambeau and scientists pressed against the shimmery wall, trying to understand what was happening!

And inside the bubble lived a neighbor named Agnes. Always smiling. Always wearing pearls. Always bringing over snickerdoodle cookies. Seemed SO nice, right?

Wrong! Agnes was actually Agatha Harkness — a witch who was HUNDREDS of years old! She had survived something called the Salem Witch Trials way back in history. How? By stealing other witches' magic! ZAP! She was very, very good at that.

Agatha had sensed Wanda's chaos magic from far away and moved into Westview ON PURPOSE. Because the ancient witches had written about a being called the Scarlet Witch — and the Darkhold, the most dangerous book of magic ever written, had an entire chapter dedicated just to her!

One day the cheerful street rewrote itself into something dark. Agnes dropped her pearls and laughed a witch's laugh. A basement full of candles and glowing runes appeared.

"Hello, Wanda. Let's talk about what you really are."

ZAPPP! Purple lightning! WHOOOOSH! Red walls of chaos magic! They fought through the streets of Westview — purple lightning versus red force — while the hex bubble shook around them!

But Wanda was SMARTER than Agatha thought. She had secretly been painting Agatha's own magical runes — protective symbols — around the inside of the hex. So when Agatha tried to steal her power... SNAP! The runes locked around Agatha like a cage. Not one spark of magic left!

And Wanda stood in her floating red crown of energy and understood everything. She was the Scarlet Witch. The most powerful witch alive. The one the prophecy was about.

On her cabin table, the Darkhold appeared. She opened it. A whole chapter inside was written just for her.
