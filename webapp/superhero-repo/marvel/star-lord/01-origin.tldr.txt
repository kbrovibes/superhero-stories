• Peter Quill: eight years old in 1988 in Missouri; mother Meredith Quill dying in hospital; too scared to take her hand
• Ran outside; abducted by a beam of blue light from a Ravager ship; captain Yondu Udonta had been hired to deliver Peter to his alien father Ego
• Yondu looked at the sobbing boy and kept him instead
• Grew up among the Ravagers: multispecies pirate crew; learned to fly every kind of ship, negotiate, and fight
• Yondu trained him with a yaka arrow guided by whistling
• Flew the Milano — a beat-up M-ship he named after his second-grade crush Alyssa Milano — calling himself Star-Lord
• Listened to his mother's mixtape every single day — the only thing he had left of Earth
• Mission: retrieve the Orb from the ruins of the planet Morag — contents unknown
• Orb contained the Power Stone (one of the six Infinity Stones); Kyln prison cell brought together Gamora, Groot, Rocket, and Drax the Destroyer
• Five strangers escaped the Kyln, accidentally became a family, held the Power Stone together to stop Ronan on Xandar — the Guardians of the Galaxy were born
