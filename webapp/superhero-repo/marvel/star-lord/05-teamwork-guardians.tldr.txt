• The Guardians: five strangers in the Kyln prison — Peter Quill (half-human pirate), Gamora (assassin trained by Thanos), Drax the Destroyer, Rocket (genetically modified raccoon), Groot (Flora colossus who says only three words)
• No single leader: Peter pretended; Gamora actually led in strategy; Rocket led with engines and weapons; Drax led when the only choice was to charge; Groot held everyone together with his branches
• First group act: linking hands on Xandar to share the Power Stone — unknowingly promising to do the same for life
• When the Avengers entered their world: Thor crashed onto the Benatar's windshield; Tony Stark and Spider-Man arrived on Titan
• Peter and Tony nearly came to blows over leadership of the Titan mission
• Peter punched Thanos in rage over Gamora — accidentally kept the gauntlet from being removed
• Hard lesson: being a Guardian is not about being right; it is about staying steady and forgiving later
• After the snap was reversed: Gamora returned in a strange, half-remembered form from the past
• The Benatar flew on: Peter kept the mixtape playing; Rocket built better guns; Drax laughed; Mantis (who joined after Ego) translated Baby Groot's three words
• Peter finally understood what his mother meant calling him her Star-Lord: he had been hers first — now he was theirs
