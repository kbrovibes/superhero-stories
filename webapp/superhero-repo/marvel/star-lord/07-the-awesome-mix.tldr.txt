• Peter's mother Meredith Quill made him a cassette tape labeled "Awesome Mix Vol. 1" — songs from her own youth, recorded one at a time off the radio
• The night she died in 1988, the tape was waiting in a wrapped package on her bedside; Peter took it when the Ravagers pulled him into the sky
• The tape goes everywhere with him for thirty years — cockpit of the Milano, prison breakouts, dancing through asteroid fields and battle zones
• Famous Awesome Mix songs include "Come and Get Your Love" by Redbone, "Hooked on a Feeling" by Blue Swede, and "I Want You Back" by The Jackson 5
• Rocket complains the songs are ancient, Drax learns the words wrong, baby Groot bobs perfectly on the beat
• When his original Walkman breaks, the Nova Corps gives him a Zune that holds five hundred songs — Peter keeps the original cassette taped to the back for safekeeping
• Years later he opens a second package — Awesome Mix Vol. 2 — that Meredith had made for "when you're a little older"
• Some mothers send love forward in time. Meredith Quill sent it forward on tape — and even after all those years, she had not finished raising him
