• Ronan the Accuser: most feared warrior of the Kree Empire; wielded the Cosmi-Rod; believed all who disagreed with the Kree should be removed
• Thanos offered Ronan a deal: deliver the Orb from Morag, receive the planet Xandar
• Ronan opened the Orb, saw the glowing purple Power Stone, and decided he no longer needed Thanos
• Pressed the Power Stone into the head of his Cosmi-Rod — the hammer sang with unimaginable energy
• Dark Aster, Ronan's ship, descended on Xandar Prime; the Nova Corps scrambled hundreds of ships to form an energy net below it
• Guardians flew the Milano straight into Ronan's fleet; crashed into the Dark Aster's bridge
• Groot sacrificed himself — wrapped all four Guardians in his branches to absorb the crash impact; only a twig survived
• Ronan rose from the rubble with the Power Stone; Peter started dancing and challenged him to a dance-off
• In Ronan's moment of stunned distraction, Rocket fired at the hammer — the Power Stone broke loose
• Peter, Gamora, Drax, and Rocket linked hands and shared the Power Stone's energy together, then turned it on Ronan; he dissolved in purple light
