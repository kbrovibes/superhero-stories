• Mantis: quiet empath who grew up at Ego's side; revealed the truth to Gamora
• Ego had ended all his other children when they couldn't help him; their skeletons were stored in a cave beneath the planet
• Ego had given Meredith Quill the tumor that killed her — because he feared he was falling in love with her
• Peter aimed his Quad Blasters at his own father
• Battle: Rocket and Groot dropped crystals into the planet's core; Peter fought Ego in Celestial light; chose to be human again; Yondu died giving Peter his own space suit to escape
