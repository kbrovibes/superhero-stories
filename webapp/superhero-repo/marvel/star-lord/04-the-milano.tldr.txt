• The Milano: wedge-shaped M-class starship, worn red paint, two stubby wings; words "Knowhere Cantina" half-stenciled on her belly
• Peter won her in a game of Sakaaran dice at a moon-base bar when he was nineteen
• Named after Alyssa Milano, his second-grade crush from Earth television — never told the crew
• Two pulse cannons, small cargo hold, a single sleeping bunk — not built for combat, but Peter flew her like a miracle
• Flew through asteroid fields without scratching the paint; drifted sideways between cargo cruisers; once landed on a flying Knowhere whale to "Come and Get Your Love" by Redbone
• Rocket modified the engines; Drax's knives stuck in a panel above his bunk; Gamora kept the medical bay neat; Groot lived in a flower pot in the cockpit
• After Groot's sacrifice on Xandar, Baby Groot danced on the dashboard in the same pot every time the ship lifted off
• The Milano was torn apart in the battle against Ego's planet; Peter cried for two days
• Crew built a new ship from Sovereign Quadrant and Ravager hauler parts; called her the Benatar; painted her red again
• Lesson: homes can be rebuilt; what matters is who is inside the ship, not the ship itself
