Oh boy oh boy oh BOY — are you ready for a dance-off in outer space?! Because that is exactly how this story ends!

Ronan the Accuser was the most feared warrior in the entire Kree Empire. He carried a massive hammer called the Cosmi-Rod and stomped around believing that anyone who disagreed with him should be erased from the universe. Thanos — a really scary villain — made Ronan a deal: bring me one small glowing artifact called the Orb, and I'll give you a whole planet named Xandar.

Ronan agreed. He got the Orb. He opened it up... and saw what was inside.

The Power Stone. One of the six Infinity Stones! A purple gem of incredible, staggering, terrifying power. Can you imagine holding something like that?

Ronan pressed it right into his Cosmi-Rod. BOOOOOM! The hammer hummed with impossible energy. Suddenly Ronan was the most powerful being alive — and he decided he didn't need Thanos anymore. He would just destroy Xandar himself! All by himself!

His great ship, the Dark Aster, descended on Xandar Prime like a giant fist dropping from the sky. The Nova Corps scrambled hundreds of fighters to form a glowing energy net in the air! And the Guardians flew the Milano STRAIGHT into Ronan's fleet — weaving and blasting — PEW PEW CRASH — until they crashed right into the Dark Aster's bridge!

WHAM! The ship went down. And brave, amazing Groot — the gentle tree — wrapped all four Guardians in his branches to absorb the terrible crash. When the dust cleared? Only one small twig of Groot was left. He had given everything to keep his friends safe.

And Ronan? He rose from the rubble with the Power Stone blazing purple fire. Ready to destroy a whole planet full of people!

This is where Peter Quill did the bravest, silliest, most perfect thing imaginable.

He started DANCING.

BOOM BOOM BOOM! Peter pointed right at Ronan and challenged him — the most feared warrior in the universe — to a DANCE-OFF.

Ronan STARED. He couldn't understand what he was seeing. And in that one bewildered, baffled, totally confused heartbeat — BOOM! Rocket fired a beam at the hammer! The Power Stone knocked loose!

Peter grabbed it with his bare hands. Purple energy nearly tore him apart! His whole body lit up like a firework! But then Gamora grabbed his hand. Then Drax. Then Rocket. They held on together — SNAP SNAP SNAP — and turned that enormous power straight at Ronan.

KABLOOOOM! Ronan dissolved in purple light.

The Guardians of the Galaxy had saved an entire world. And Peter had done it by dancing. Which is honestly the best superhero move ever.
