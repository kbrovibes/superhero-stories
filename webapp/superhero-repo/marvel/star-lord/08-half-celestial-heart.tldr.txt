• Peter learns he is half-Celestial when Ego shows him how to gather a glowing ball of blue-gold light between his palms
• The light pulses in time with his heartbeat — it is a piece of a Celestial, one of the oldest beings in the universe, living inside him
• Peter is excited at first — imagines saving planets, lighting up galaxies, being a god
• When he learns Ego wants to use the light to destroy worlds, Peter makes the hardest choice — he sets it down on the dying planet's hillside
• He chooses Yondu, Meredith, Rocket, Groot, Gamora, Drax, and Mantis — he chooses to be the human boy his mother loved
• A small ember of the light stays in his chest — he can sense danger, feel gravity, and read strangers' intent, but he never wields it as a weapon
• "The hardest power to wield is the power not to"
• Years later he tells Mantis the Celestial part was never really his — it was Ego's — and the human Missouri-boy part is what he wants to keep
