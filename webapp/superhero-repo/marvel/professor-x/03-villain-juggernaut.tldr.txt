• Cain Marko is Professor X's stepbrother, who always resented Charles's brilliance and calm
• During a mission in Korea, Cain discovers the Crimson Gem of Cyttorak, transforming him into the Juggernaut — unstoppable and armored
• Juggernaut charges the Xavier mansion; all students evacuate to sublevels under Cyclops and Colossus
• Professor X's telepathy cannot penetrate Juggernaut's helmet, which is sealed against psychic attack
• Jean Grey slows Juggernaut with telekinesis; Beast and Cyclops try to knock the helmet free — none fully succeed
• Professor X observes Cain is charging blindly — his anger makes him careless
• The X-Men lure Juggernaut into underground tunnels; Beast drops the ceiling and buries him in concrete
• When the helmet cracks, Professor X uses a gentle telepathic nudge to end the fight and feels quiet sadness for the boy Cain once was
