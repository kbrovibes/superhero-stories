• Bolivar Trask builds the Sentinels — fifty-foot purple robots programmed to detect and capture mutants — and launches the first fleet from Washington, D.C.
• Cerebro alerts Professor X; he mobilizes the X-Men immediately
• Storm, Cyclops, and Wolverine intercept three Sentinels over Manhattan but the full fleet of twelve is too large for the team alone
• Professor X makes a bold choice: he contacts his rival Magneto for help
• The two meet above Central Park; Magneto listens to the situation and agrees without hesitation
• Magneto crushes the remaining nine Sentinels with his magnetic power, crumpling them into useless steel spheres
• Professor X drafts a formal message to the U.S. government protesting weapons that target people by genetics alone
• He sends Magneto a private telepathic "thank you" — and feels a brief, warm response come back
