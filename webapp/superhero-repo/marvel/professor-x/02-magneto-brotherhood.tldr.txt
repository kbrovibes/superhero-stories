• Erik Lehnsherr takes the name Magneto and forms the Brotherhood of Mutants — including Mystique, Sabretooth, and Toad — declaring that mutants should rule rather than hide
• Magneto targets a United Nations assembly with a radiation device meant to forcibly turn world leaders into mutants
• The X-Men respond: Cyclops, Jean Grey, and Beast defend the building while Professor X uses Cerebro to pinpoint Magneto's location
• The two old friends come face to face; Magneto hesitates when Charles holds up his hand in a gesture of friendship
• Jean Grey shields Professor X; Cyclops destroys the device; the Brotherhood retreats
• Charles reflects that Magneto is not entirely wrong about the world's cruelty — but believes fear only produces more fear
• That evening Professor X sends a silent, private telepathic message to Magneto — not anger, but hope
• Their friendship, old and complicated, survives even their deepest disagreement
