• Professor X's telepathy is always active — he spends decades learning to filter it to a manageable background hum
• His most precise skill: the telepathic bridge — a private channel letting two people share ideas and memories with perfect clarity
• He treats entering a mind without permission as a serious violation and refuses except in life-or-death emergencies
• In battle he uses telepathy carefully: psychic blind spots, projecting calm into panicked crowds, precise adjustments to stop catastrophes
• Once, to prevent an attack in Midtown Manhattan, he replaced an assassin's dangerous certainty with gentle doubt
• He tells the X-Men immediately — he won't do it again unless truly necessary
• Decades of reading thousands of minds has made him nearly incapable of genuine hatred — he has felt fear and loneliness from the inside
• Understanding others does not mean agreeing with them, but it makes cruelty very hard to sustain
