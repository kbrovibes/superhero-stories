• The island of Genosha had forced mutants to labor under harsh rule — facing survivors' stories tests Professor X's optimism harder than any battle
• A crisis brings the X-Men to help mutants rebuild while opposing a faction that wants to restore old policies
• Professor X comes himself — some moments require presence more than strategy
• He builds an honest relationship with Polaris (Lorna Dane), a Genosha survivor deeply mistrustful of institutions
• He does not promise everything will be fine — he tells her: it will take work and time, and the X-Men will be there
• Cyclops and Storm contain the opposing faction tactically
• Professor X asks faction leader Phineas Whitmore what he had been afraid of — the question disarms him more than any force could
• Whitmore surrenders; Professor X writes: "Progress is not a moment. It is a direction."
