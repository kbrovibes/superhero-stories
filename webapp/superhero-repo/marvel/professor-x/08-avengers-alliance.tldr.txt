• A psychic entity called the Shadow Mind lands in the Canadian Rockies, broadcasting a signal that collapses every telepathic mutant within a thousand miles
• Professor X can barely think through the static; he calls Tony Stark at Avengers Tower for help
• Tony designs a sonic counter-frequency device to jam the signal; Beast's calculations shorten the work by hours
• Storm and Thor fly the device to the Rockies; Cyclops leads the ground team; Captain America coordinates both groups
• With the signal jammed, Professor X reaches the Shadow Mind through Cerebro
• He discovers it is not malicious — it is in pain, broadcasting distress the way an injured creature cries out
• Through patient telepathic contact, Charles establishes communication and the signal fades to something workable
