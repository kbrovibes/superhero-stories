• Cerebro is a massive helmet-machine beneath the Xavier School, built by Professor X and inventor Forge over 18 months
• Without Cerebro, Professor X's telepathy reaches about a mile; with it, his range covers the entire planet
• Through Cerebro, he senses every mutant mind on Earth — finding isolated young mutants before they face the world alone
• Sessions are limited to 40 minutes before the mental strain becomes dangerous
• The Hellfire Club attacks with a telepathic disruptor to scramble Cerebro's signal during a crisis
• Professor X pushes through the static, narrowing focus from the whole world down to a single building in Nevada
• He locates the trapped mutants and relays their position to Cyclops, who leads a successful rescue
• When Forge offers extra shielding, Charles declines — he won't reduce range if it means missing one child
