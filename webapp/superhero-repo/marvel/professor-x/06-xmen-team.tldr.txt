• Professor X finds his first students through Cerebro: Cyclops (Scott Summers), Jean Grey, Beast (Hank McCoy), Iceman, and Angel
• He trains them in the Danger Room — a programmable combat simulator — and teaches them to understand their own powers
• Cyclops learns to lead under pressure; Jean Grey holds two power channels at once; Beast discovers intellect and athleticism are one gift
• Professor X gives them vocabulary: mutation is natural evolution, not a defect
• His bond with Jean Grey runs deepest — she is his most gifted and most careful student
• He works with Jean privately as her abilities grow beyond expectations
• He trusts her completely — she feels that trust the way you feel sunlight through a window
• Every X-Men mission makes the argument: mutants and humans can coexist and thrive
