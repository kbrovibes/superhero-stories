• Charles Xavier grows up in a Hudson River mansion in Westchester, a child prodigy who secretly hears other people's thoughts
• He discovers he is a mutant with telepathy — the power to read and communicate with any mind on Earth
• At Oxford University he meets Erik Lehnsherr, a metal-bending mutant; they become best friends but disagree whether humans and mutants can ever live in peace
• An accident leaves Charles unable to walk; he returns to the Westchester mansion and builds the Xavier School for Gifted Youngsters
• The school is a safe home where mutant children learn to understand and control their powers
• Charles designs Cerebro, a powerful helmet-machine that amplifies his telepathy to reach every mutant mind on Earth
• He trains his students as the X-Men, a team of heroes who protect both mutants and humans
• Professor X's dream: a world where mutants and humans live side by side without fear
