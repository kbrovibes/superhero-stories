• Professor Xavier steps back from active field leadership and names Storm — not the expected choice Cyclops — as leader
• Cyclops shows friction through careful tactical arguments; Jean Grey watches supportively; Wolverine stays silent
• Storm leads with presence: clear orders, explained reasoning, honest decisions made at the speed of the moment
• Her first big test: a mission to the Savage Land in Antarctica where a flash flood splits the team
• Storm and Jean Grey are cut off with the mission objective; Cyclops and Wolverine are stranded on the opposite bank
• Storm creates a wind-bridge of compressed air; Jean Grey anchors it with telekinesis — the team crosses in ninety seconds
• After that, Cyclops brings his arguments to Storm before missions rather than during them
• Wolverine calls her "Not bad, Ro" — the highest praise he gives anyone
• Storm leads the X-Men for years as herself: clear-eyed, fierce, and always fully present
