• Three giant Sentinel robots — built to capture mutants — descend on Westchester at dusk
• Storm flies up to meet them alone while Cyclops and Wolverine get into position below
• She hits the lead Sentinel with lightning but the machine barely staggers; a containment blast spins her sideways
• She steadies herself, breathes, and reaches back to the reliable air beneath her
• She creates a thick fog bank that blinds the Sentinels' optical sensors and calls for her teammates
• Cyclops blasts the third Sentinel's knee joints through the fog; Wolverine climbs the second and shreds its servos
• Storm rises above the clouds, builds a massive charge, and fires one precise thunderbolt into the last Sentinel's core
• All three Sentinels fall in eight minutes; Storm lands with a small, satisfied smile
• The battle shows how she combines raw weather power with calm tactical teamwork
