• Ororo and T'Challa first meet as teenagers in East Africa — both finding themselves — before their paths divide for years
• Years later Storm is a cornerstone of the X-Men; T'Challa has become Black Panther and king of Wakanda, the most advanced nation on Earth
• T'Challa proposes at the Great Mound — Wakanda's sacred Vibranium deposit — and Ororo says yes
• Their wedding in Birnin Zana draws dignitaries across Africa; Professor Xavier and the full X-Men attend; the sky that day is famously beautiful
• As Queen Consort, Storm uses her broad experience of the world to encourage Wakanda to engage more openly with other nations
• She remains an active X-Man alongside her royal duties; T'Challa, who carries the same weight of promise, understands completely
• The Cairo orphan and the warrior-king stand on Wakanda's escarpment looking out over the continent they both call home
