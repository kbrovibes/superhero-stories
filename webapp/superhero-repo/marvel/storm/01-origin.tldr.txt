• Ororo Munroe is born in Cairo to N'Dare, a Kenyan princess, and David Munroe, an American photographer
• Both parents are killed when she is six; she survives as a pickpocket under the tutelage of thief Achmed el-Gibar
• Drawn south by instinct, she crosses the Sahara alone and reaches the Serengeti plain of East Africa
• Her weather powers awaken fully — she brings rain, rides the wind; the village people call her Goddess
• Professor Charles Xavier finds her and explains she is a mutant with a real and extraordinary gift
• Xavier introduces her to the X-Men: Cyclops, Jean Grey, and Wolverine at the Xavier Institute in Westchester, New York
• She takes the name Storm and learns to control her powers with precision under Xavier's guidance
• The orphan from Cairo becomes one of the most powerful heroes on Earth
