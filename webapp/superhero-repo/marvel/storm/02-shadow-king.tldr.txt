• The Shadow King — once Cairo crimelord Amahl Farouk — lurks in the astral plane and slips into Storm's dreams
• He uses her claustrophobia against her, showing her trapped memories from the Cairo rubble
• Storm goes three nights without sleep trying to keep him out; Wolverine notices something is badly wrong
• Jean Grey uses her telepathy to find the Shadow King coiled around Storm's mind
• Professor Xavier enters the astral plane to battle Farouk directly while the X-Men support Storm
• The Shadow King presses down with illusions of closed spaces and darkness — Storm begins to falter
• Storm fights back by filling her mind with open sky and the Serengeti wind — the feeling Farouk cannot replicate
• Xavier strikes in that moment of weakness and scatters the Shadow King's consciousness
• Storm wakes safe; Wolverine hands her tea without a word, and she laughs
