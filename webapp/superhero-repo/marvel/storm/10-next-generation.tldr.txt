• Three frightened young mutants arrive at the Xavier Institute: Suri (temperature), Emile (wind), Petra (electrical sensing)
• Professor Xavier asks Storm to help; she starts by taking them outside and telling them her story — Cairo, the Serengeti, the claustrophobia
• She teaches them that being frightened, powerful, and still learning can all be true in the same person at once
• She teaches Suri that temperature is a spectrum, not a switch — they practice until Suri can shake hands safely
• She shows Emile on the roof that slowing wind means equalizing pressure, not forcing it — the gust finally quiets
• She sits silently with Petra beside the river for an hour; Petra learns sensing something is not being overwhelmed by it
• In December all three give a demonstration; Storm watches quietly from the back
• When Petra asks how Storm knew they could do it, Storm says: "Because I was you"
