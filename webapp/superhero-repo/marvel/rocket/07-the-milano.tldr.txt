• The Milano is the Guardians' ship — originally Peter Quill's, but heavily modified by Rocket until it became something much better
• Rocket and Star-Lord have a running argument about who really owns it; nothing is ever resolved
• It is an M-class starship Rocket has rebuilt twice, pushing the engines well past their original limits
• The Milano can reach sublight speed in eight seconds and pull maneuvers the original designers never intended
• Rocket calls the emergency speed burst "oh no mode"
• Inside: cockpit for two, a navigation system that argues back, Rocket's rear workshop, and a warm spot near the engine bay that Groot likes — which Rocket engineered quietly on purpose
• Rocket has rebuilt the Milano from near-total destruction twice — once on Contraxia, once after Ego's planet
• He would say a ship is just a machine, but he spends more care on the Milano than anything else he has ever touched
