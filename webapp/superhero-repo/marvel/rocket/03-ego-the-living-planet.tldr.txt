• The Guardians answer a distress call and find Ego — a being called a Celestial who has made himself a beautiful living planet
• Ego reveals he is Peter Quill's father, which makes everything much more complicated
• Ego's real plan is the Expansion: tendrils of living matter secretly planted on thousands of worlds, which he wants to activate using Star-Lord's Celestial energy
• Rocket stays behind with Yondu and Baby Groot while the others are distracted inside Ego's world
• Rocket builds a bomb from Anulax batteries — the most explosive material in the galaxy
• He and Yondu fly into Ego's hollow core using Yondu's glowing blue whistling arrow to cut a path
• Baby Groot bravely plants the bomb at Ego's brain
• The bomb goes off, destroying Ego from the inside out and ending the Expansion forever
• Peter loses his Celestial powers when Ego falls, but he escapes alive
