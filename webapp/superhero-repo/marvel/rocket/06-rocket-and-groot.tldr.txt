• Rocket and Groot are total opposites — enormous and small, wood and flesh, three words and thousands — and the best of friends
• They meet on a bounty job that falls apart, get arrested together, and end up in the Kyln prison, where the Guardians form
• Groot sees Rocket completely — the sharpness, the prickliness, all of it — and is unbothered
• Groot offers his company without conditions; Rocket, lonely for a long time, accepts it while grumbling
• In the Battle of Xandar, when the Dark Aster crashes, Groot wraps himself around all four Guardians to absorb the impact, saying "We are Groot"
• Rocket saves a tiny cutting from Groot's remains and carries it everywhere, protecting it more carefully than any weapon
• He talks quietly to Baby Groot in the dark, watching every new leaf grow back
• Their friendship is built on being fully seen and fully wanted, exactly as you are
