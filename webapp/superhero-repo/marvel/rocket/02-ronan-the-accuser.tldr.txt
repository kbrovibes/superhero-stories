• Ronan the Accuser is a fearsome Kree warlord with a giant stone hammer called the Universal Weapon
• He gets hold of the Power Stone — one of the six Infinity Stones — and plans to destroy the peaceful planet Xandar
• Xandar is home to the Nova Corps, the galaxy's great peacekeeping force
• Rocket spends forty minutes in the Milano's cargo bay building a weapon from spare parts to punch through Ronan's warship, the Dark Aster
• The Guardians fly their fighters into battle above Xandar while Rocket fires the crucial shot
• The Dark Aster crashes, but Ronan climbs out — still holding the Power Stone
• Star-Lord distracts Ronan with a dancing challenge; in that moment Rocket, Gamora, Drax, and Star-Lord seize the Stone together
• All four Guardians hold the Stone at once, sharing its tremendous power, until Ronan falls
• The Nova Corps takes the Stone for safekeeping; Xandar is saved
