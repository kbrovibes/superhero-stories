• Rocket is created through experiments on Halfworld, a distant planet where scientists gave animals human-level intelligence — leaving him feeling different and alone
• He has the mind of a master engineer, lightning-fast reflexes, and an extraordinary sense of smell
• He leaves Halfworld and survives as a bounty hunter across the galaxy, preferring to work alone
• He meets Groot, a gentle giant tree-being from Planet X, and the two become inseparable best friends
• Rocket handles the plans and weapons; Groot provides quiet strength and steady loyalty
• Together they drift into the orbit of the Guardians of the Galaxy: Star-Lord, Gamora, and Drax
• The Guardians are all misfits with big hearts — and Rocket finally has a family who understands him
• He goes from a lonely creature shaped by someone else's experiment to the most brilliant, brave Guardian in the cosmos
