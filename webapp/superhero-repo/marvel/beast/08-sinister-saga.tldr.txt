• Mister Sinister — Nathaniel Essex — builds a Genetic Sequencer beneath New York City that can permanently rewrite mutant DNA
• His plan: use it to create an army of modified mutants under his total control
• Hank spends three days reconstructing stolen schematics to map Sinister's underground laboratory
• The X-Men enter through the tunnels — Cyclops, Jean Grey, Storm, Gambit, and Beast
• Hank faces Sabretooth in a narrow corridor, using walls and ceiling to stay out of reach while coordinating the team by radio
• Jean Grey reaches the Sequencer but Sinister hits her with a psychic counter-blast
• Hank shoulder-blocks Sinister into a wall, giving Jean six seconds to rip the machine's power core free
• The Sequencer is destroyed and Sinister escapes; Hank spends two days erasing all stolen data at the Institute
