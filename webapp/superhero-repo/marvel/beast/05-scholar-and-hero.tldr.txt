• He can discuss genetics with Nobel laureates and quote Percy Bysshe Shelley or Walt Whitman in the same breath
• His intelligence and heroism are inseparable — he builds antidotes, designs traps, and finds enemy weak spots mid-battle
• He can call out a Sentinel's blind spot while landing a flying kick on a different Sentinel across the field
• He is also a gifted cook — his Moroccan lamb dinner once silenced Bobby Drake for nearly ten minutes
• When new students arrive at the Xavier Institute, Hank sits with them and asks what they love
• He knows from experience that unusual gifts are worth celebrating, and he passes that belief on to everyone he meets
