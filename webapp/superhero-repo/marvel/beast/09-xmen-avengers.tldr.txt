• Apocalypse — the ancient mutant warlord En Sabah Nur — releases a synthetic virus that spreads to six cities
• The virus amplifies any ability past its safe limit, turning people into uncontrollable forces
• Stopping it requires both the X-Men and the Avengers working together
• Hank and Tony Stark share a laboratory at Avengers Tower for seventy-two hours without sleeping
• They compromise on moderate classical music and produce an aerosol cure of extraordinary precision
• Thor uses Mjolnir to generate a controlled electromagnetic storm that carries the cure through the air of every infected city
• Jean Grey coordinates the psychic broadcast while Captain America, Cyclops, and Storm manage the ground
• Within eight hours the virus is completely gone; Tony says "Good work, McCoy" and Hank immediately calls the Xavier Institute to check on his students
