• After leaving the X-Men, Hank works as a biochemist at the Brand Corporation in New York
• He discovers the company is secretly developing a mutation-amplifying serum — and someone is about to steal it
• Hank synthesizes a temporary dose of the serum to disguise his own mutant signature and catch the thief
• The plan works, but Hank waits too long to reverse the effect and the serum becomes permanent
• His body is now covered in blue-black fur, with pointed ears, bright yellow eyes, and fangs
• Hank spends three days re-checking his calculations — the answer doesn't change
• He calls Professor Xavier, who tells him the door to the Xavier Institute has always been open
• The X-Men welcome him home warmly; Hank pins a Shakespeare quote above his lab bench and returns to work
