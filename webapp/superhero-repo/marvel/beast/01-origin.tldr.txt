• Hank McCoy is born in Dunfermline, Illinois, with unusually large hands and feet — a mutation caused by his father's exposure to nuclear radiation
• Growing up, Hank discovers his oversized limbs give him incredible agility and acrobatic strength
• He excels at both sports and science, breaking every athletic record and winning the state science fair at Waukegan High School
• When the criminal group the Conquistador kidnaps his parents, Hank fights them off and realizes he is a mutant
• Professor Charles Xavier — founder of the Xavier Institute in Westchester — invites Hank to join the X-Men
• Hank joins Scott Summers, Jean Grey, Bobby Drake, and Warren Worthington III as one of the original five X-Men
• As Beast, Hank combines raw strength and acrobatics with a brilliant scientific mind
• He becomes the heart of the X-Men: brave, kind, and always ready with a book recommendation
