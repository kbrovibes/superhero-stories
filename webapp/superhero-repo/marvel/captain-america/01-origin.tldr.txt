• Steve Rogers: scrawny young man from Brooklyn, rejected by military multiple times due to poor health
• Dr. Abraham Erskine seeks a "good man" with strong character, not strong muscles, for Project Rebirth
• Super Soldier Serum + Vita-Ray radiation in the transformation chamber turns Steve into a peak-human specimen
• Saboteur assassinates Dr. Erskine immediately after the experiment — Steve is the one and only super-soldier
• Steve adopts the identity Captain America, dons a patriotic uniform and prototype shield
• Leads soldiers on the front lines of World War II, infiltrating enemy strongholds and rescuing captives
• Greatest asset is his moral compass, not enhanced muscles; earns respect through humility and honor
• Never forgets his roots in Brooklyn; advocates for justice and equality as his legend grows
• Proves true heroism is defined by values and actions, not physical power
• Story of the First Avenger: greatness can come from the most unexpected places
