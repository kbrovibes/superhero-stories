• Alliance between Captain America and Wakanda — founded on mutual respect for justice, not a formal treaty
• King T'Challa of Wakanda: key partner and cornerstone of global defense
• Wakanda: world's only source of Vibranium, granting unmatched technological superiority
• Cap's Vibranium shield is a constant symbol of the deep bond between hero and kingdom
• Cap provides tactical expertise and global perspective; Wakanda provides advanced technology and medicine
• Collaboration: Cap works side-by-side with the Dora Milaje during global crises
• Both Cap and T'Challa share the weight of leadership and ethical debates about intervention
• Cap serves as a vocal advocate for Wakandan interests on the world stage
• Wakanda opened its borders as a safe haven and staging ground for the Avengers during global invasions
• For Steve Rogers, Wakanda offers community and a sense of belonging — mirrors his own dedication to duty
