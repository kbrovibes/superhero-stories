• Baron Helmut Zemo: aristocratic tactical genius, descended from a line of warriors
• Blames Captain America for his family's downfall; carries a deep personal grudge
• Prefers chess-like long-game strategies over brute force — spends months setting up elaborate traps
• Leads the Masters of Evil, uniting disparate villains under a single, coordinated plan
• Psychological warfare is his primary weapon: targets heroes' personal lives and histories
• Creates impossible moral dilemmas designed to break a hero's spirit rather than their body
• Arrogance is his fatal flaw: underestimates the power of human connection and selfless teamwork
• Cap's battles with Zemo are as much intellectual as physical
• Zemo sees the world as a game to be won; Cap sees it as a community to protect
• Every defeat leaves Zemo already plotting his next masterpiece, certain he will eventually checkmate Cap
