• Captain America: primary leader and strategic commander of the Avengers
• Respects every member's unique talents — Hulk's raw power, Black Widow's precision, Thor's lightning, Iron Man's tech
• Tactical mind processes rapidly changing battlefield information and formulates efficient team plans
• Leads by example: first to step into the line of fire, never asks teammates to do what he won't
• Builds a culture of transparency and mutual respect — no secrets, no ego
• Mentors younger heroes, teaching that character and values matter more than winning fights
• Described as the Avengers' moral compass — guides them when the path forward is unclear
• Communication cornerstone: every voice is heard, every concern addressed
• Commands respect from world leaders and ordinary citizens alike
• Legacy of his command: a safer, more unified world protected by a team that fights together
