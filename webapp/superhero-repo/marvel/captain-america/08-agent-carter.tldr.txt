• Peggy Carter: top-level intelligence officer during World War II, foundational partner who shaped Captain America's soul
• Recognised Steve Rogers as a "good man" before the Super Soldier Serum ever touched him
• Fought alongside Steve in the field, providing strategic intelligence to the Howling Commandos
• Challenged Steve to think broadly and never lose sight of the people he was fighting for
• Steve disappeared into the Arctic ice; Peggy went on to become a founding member of S.H.I.E.L.D.
• Steve's promise: a dance with Peggy that circumstances prevented him from keeping for decades
• Reunion in the 21st century — Steve found Peggy still alive, now much older, a moment of joy and sorrow
• Even as her memory faded, Peggy remained a source of wisdom and moral clarity for Steve
• Peggy's influence visible in every decision Cap makes — she is the mirror reflecting his best self
• Their story: a great partnership and romance, and an enduring commitment to a better world
