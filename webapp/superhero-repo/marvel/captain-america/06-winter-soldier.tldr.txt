• Bucky Barnes: Steve Rogers' closest friend since childhood; fought together in the Howling Commandos
• Bucky fell from a high-speed train during a World War II mission — Steve believed him dead for decades
• Bucky survived: recovered by a secret organization, brainwashed, physically enhanced, fitted with a cybernetic arm
• Transformed into the Winter Soldier — a cold, highly efficient assassin used for decades of covert missions
• Steve discovers his old friend is the assassin he has been hunting — a moment of profound emotional conflict
• Despite layers of conditioning, Steve refuses to give up on Bucky; his unwavering faith drives the quest to save him
• Long, painful process of breaking free from conditioning — physical battles and deep psychological recovery
• Cap stands by Bucky every step of the way, providing friendship and reminders of his Brooklyn past
• Bucky eventually begins reclaiming his identity, finds new purpose as a defender of justice
• Bond between Steve and Bucky: testament to friendship's power to overcome almost any darkness
