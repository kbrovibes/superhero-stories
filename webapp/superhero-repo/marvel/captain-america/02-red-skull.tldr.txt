• Red Skull: real name Johann Schmidt, high-ranking officer who believes only the ruthless deserve to rule
• Obsessed with ancient myths and powerful artifacts; one of the first to recognize the Tesseract's potential
• Tesseract: cosmic power source he views as a weapon for absolute authority, not a tool for humanity's good
• Rivalry with Captain America is a clash of ideologies — fear and manipulation vs. justice and unity
• Schmidt's crimson skeletal face is designed to strike terror into anyone who defies him
• Complete lack of empathy: uses people as resources to be discarded in his quest for dominance
• Master of psychological warfare; tries to break enemies' spirits before any physical battle begins
• Always returns from defeat with grander, more dangerous schemes — from hidden mountain bases to other dimensions
• Cap's victories prove fear wins battles but cannot win the war against courage and truth
• Red Skull's failures are the inevitable result of a philosophy that ignores human resilience and unity
