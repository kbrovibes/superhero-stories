• Loki never stopped being a trickster — he still turned sandwiches into snakes and shape-shifted to startle friends
• What changed was what he used the tricks FOR — for protection instead of pride
• When Mobius was nearly pruned, Loki used a shimmering double of himself to draw the Hunters away
• When Sylvie was being tracked, Loki planted a clever rumor in the right ear to throw the TVA off her trail
• When Casey needed help with a stuck door, Loki shape-shifted into a wrench — Casey almost dropped him
- Even his old habit of faking death stopped being an escape — by the end, he did not need to escape anymore
• He sat at the root of the great tree of stories and finally got to be himself, with no costume
• Being clever, used for the right reasons, turned out to be a small kind of love
• Frigga would have been proud — and somewhere in the wild multiverse, Loki was still her son
