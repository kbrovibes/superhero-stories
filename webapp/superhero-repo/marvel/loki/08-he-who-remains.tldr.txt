• Even captured, Sylvie has a plan — find whoever is running the TVA and ask why
• Loki and Sylvie fight their way out with help from Hunter B-15, who has become an ally
• They aim a stolen TemPad at the strangest coordinate ever — the Citadel at the End of Time
• First they must cross the Void, a junkyard outside the multiverse with the cloud-monster Alioth overhead
• At the top of the Citadel stairs they find no army, no throne — just a man eating an apple
• His name is He Who Remains — he has been holding the whole multiverse together alone for ages
• He gives them a choice: kill him and the multiverse splits, or take his chair and continue his system
• Sylvie chooses — she kisses Loki gently, pushes him through a portal, and strikes He Who Remains
• The multiverse blossoms; Loki returns to the TVA to find statues and officers all changed — a new war beginning
