• Mobius offers Loki a deal: help catch a dangerous Variant on the loose, which is also a Loki
• Hunter B-15 and Casey, the TVA officers, do not love this plan, but Mobius does it anyway
• For the first time, somebody is curious about Loki as a person — asking "what do you actually want?"
