• Loki has secretly faked his death and ruled Asgard disguised as Odin for years — eating grapes, watching plays about himself
• Thor returns, sees through the trick, and drags Loki to Earth to find the real Odin
• The brothers crash-land on a junkyard planet called Sakaar, ruled by the strange Grandmaster
• Loki charms the Grandmaster while Thor is forced to fight in the arena
• They learn their sister Hela, the Goddess of Death, has escaped and is destroying Asgard
• To escape Sakaar's hangar, they use the "get help" gag — Thor yells while heaving Loki like a bowling ball
• Loki hates the trick but it works every time — they steal the Commodore and fly to Asgard
• Loki bravely convinces Thor to let Surtur destroy Asgard itself to defeat Hela and save the people
• On the refugee ship Statesman, Thor smiles and says, "It's good to have you back" — and this time Loki means it
