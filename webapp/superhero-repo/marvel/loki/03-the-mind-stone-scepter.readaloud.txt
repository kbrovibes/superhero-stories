Have you ever been so upset and so lonely that you made a really, really bad choice? A choice you would later look back on and think, "Oh no. What was I thinking?" Well, today we're going to talk about one of those moments in Loki's life. A big one. THE big one.

Loki had been hurting for a long time. The secret about being a Frost Giant. The feeling of never quite measuring up to Thor. The long fall through the spaces between worlds when he let go of the Bifrost. And in those cold dark spaces between stars, somebody scary FOUND him.

A purple giant named Thanos.

Thanos sat on a stone throne in the dark. He had a deep voice that made the air rumble. He was the kind of villain who made other villains nervous. And he saw Loki — hurt, alone, full of anger — and Thanos thought, "Hmm. I can USE this one."

So Thanos handed Loki a long golden scepter. SHIIIING! It had a sharp blade like a spear, and right in the middle of the blade was a glowing BLUE STONE. That stone was the Mind Stone, one of the six Infinity Stones, and it was VERY VERY powerful.

"Take this scepter to Earth," Thanos said. "Find a glowing blue cube called the Tesseract. Conquer the planet. And I will give you an army of Chitauri to help you."

What Loki didn't know — what NOBODY knew yet — was that the scepter was whispering to him. The Mind Stone was very good at telling a sad heart what it wanted to hear. "You are above them. You were born to rule. They are tiny little ants compared to you." And Loki, hurting and lonely, listened.

POP! A glowing portal opened over New York City. Loki stepped out, smiling his sharpest, smuggest smile. The Tesseract crackled overhead — KRACKLE! ZAP! ZAP! — and the Chitauri army poured through the hole in the sky on flying scooters and giant space whales.

Loki touched Hawkeye with the scepter — WHOOSH! — and made him a temporary helper. He stood in a crowd in Stuttgart and told everybody to kneel. He thought, "This is it. This is what I was BORN for."

But then the Avengers showed up.

Iron Man — ZOOM! Captain America — THWANG! Black Widow, Hawkeye, Thor, and Hulk. Six heroes who did NOT think Loki was the boss of them. They argued with each other on the Helicarrier. They yelled. They almost fell apart. But when the Chitauri came pouring out of the sky, they STOOD.

The portal closed. The Chitauri ran. And then came the part everyone remembers.

Loki was standing in Stark Tower, giving Hulk a long, dramatic speech. "ENOUGH! I am a GOD, and I will not be bullied by —"

THUD! Hulk grabbed Loki by the ankles. THUD THUD THUD THUD THUD! He smacked Loki up and down, side to side, over and over, until Loki was lying in a Loki-shaped CRATER in the floor.

Hulk walked off muttering, "Puny god."

The scepter was taken away. Loki was taken away. And later — MUCH later — Loki figured out that Thanos and the Mind Stone had been whispering in his ear the whole time. Twisting him. Steering him.

He had done wrong. That was true. But he had also been used. And somewhere inside him, the boy who used to turn apples into snakes in his mother's library was still in there.

Waiting to find his own story again.
