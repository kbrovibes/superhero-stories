• After falling through the spaces between worlds, Loki is found by the purple titan Thanos
• Thanos hands Loki a golden scepter with a glowing blue Mind Stone and orders him to conquer Earth
• The scepter is whispering to Loki — it tells a hurting heart everything it wants to hear
• Loki arrives in New York, opens a portal with the Tesseract, and unleashes the Chitauri army
• He meets the Avengers for the first time — Iron Man, Cap, Black Widow, Thor, Hulk, Hawkeye
• The Battle of New York goes badly for Loki — the portal closes, the Chitauri retreat
• Hulk grabs Loki and smacks him repeatedly into the floor — "Puny god"
• Later Loki realizes Thanos was twisting his mind the whole time with the Mind Stone
• He feels complicated shame — he did wrong, but he was also used; the real Loki is still inside
