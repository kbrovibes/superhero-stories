• Groot and Rocket are total opposites — one enormous and patient, one tiny and restless — but their friendship is the strongest in the galaxy
• Rocket came from somewhere painful he never discusses; Groot was one of the first beings he ever truly trusted
• Rocket alone understands every version of "I am Groot" — the warning, the hunger, the curious, the gentle check-in after a fight
• In battle their teamwork is wordless: Groot builds the barrier, Rocket climbs his shoulder and fires; no plan needed
• When Groot was reduced to twigs after the battle over Xandar, Rocket collected every piece and kept them in a small pot of soil
• Rocket talked to the pot, argued with it, and once apologized to it — and called everyone in to see the first new shoot
• Their friendship is total loyalty, freely given: the kind that expects nothing and returns everything
• Having someone who always understands you, in a large and often unkind galaxy, is everything
