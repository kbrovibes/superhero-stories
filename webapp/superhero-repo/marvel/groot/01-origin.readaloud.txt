Are you ready? Because this hero is unlike anyone you have ever met — and the only thing he can say is THREE WORDS!

His name is Groot. Say it with me — GROOT! He is a giant walking tree from a planet called Planet X, far, far away in a galaxy of rustling forests and amber skies. He is tall as a building. He has bark for skin and branches for arms. And his eyes? They glow with soft VIOLET light, like two little lanterns in the dark.

Now here is the extraordinary part. Groot can only say three words: "I am Groot." That's it! Three words, every single time. But those three words can mean ANYTHING — "hello," "I'm scared," "I love you," "watch out!" — because Groot puts his whole giant tree-heart into every single syllable. His best friend Rocket understands every single version. Every one.

On Planet X, Groot was different from all the other Flora Colossi — that's what his species is called. FLORA COLOSSI. Magnificent name, right? While the others were stiff and proud, Groot was gentle. He let birds nest in his branches. He sheltered tiny creatures in the rain. He was the kind of person who always noticed the smallest, most forgotten thing in the room and made sure it was okay.

And one day, he sheltered a small human child who had wandered into the sacred groves of Planet X — and the elders said ENOUGH. Too soft! Too kind! Out you go, Groot!

And so Groot went out — into the stars.

He drifted through the galaxy doing cargo work at space stations. CLANG! Lift that crate! WHOOSH! Load that ship! Past red giant stars and ice rings and trading posts with a hundred alien languages at once. Until one day on the planet Xandar, he sat down in a cantina and a small, very loud, very clever bounty hunter named Rocket walked in.

Rocket was like a raccoon with mechanical upgrades and a plan for every situation — including this one. He and Groot were COMPLETELY different. Rocket never stopped talking. Groot said three words. And yet — CLICK — they fit together perfectly, the way the right key fits the right lock.

Together they joined a team called the Guardians of the Galaxy — Star-Lord, Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot! In battle, WHOOOMP, Groot grows walls of bark that stop blasterfire cold. CREAK CREAK, his arms stretch across huge gaps into instant bridges. THUD, he scoops up hurt teammates and carries them to safety.

He was sent away from home for caring too much. But among the Guardians? Caring too much is the most important superpower there is.

I AM GROOT!
