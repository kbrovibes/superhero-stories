• The Milano is Star-Lord's ship — patched in 14 places, with a cassette deck and a stubborn engine fault — named after Earth actress Alyssa Milano
• Groot sleeps in the cargo bay because it's the only room tall enough; he roots lightly into the metal deck at night
• His roots actually reinforce the hull in his corner, making it denser and more corrosion-resistant than anywhere else on the ship
• From the porthole he watches the galaxy: Xandar's double sunset, a sweeping pulsar, the ribbon nebula of the Kyln region
• The galaxy is enormous — the Nova Corps, Kree Empire, and Sovereign each claim their piece; thousands of worlds float between
• Groot was cast out from Planet X and now travels this immense universe with the Guardians
• He found, against all odds, exactly where he belongs — a battered ship, loud friends, and whatever comes next
• Rocket knows exactly what Groot is thinking, even when no one else does
