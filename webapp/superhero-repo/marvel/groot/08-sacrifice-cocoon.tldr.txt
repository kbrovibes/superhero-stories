• The Dark Aster is crashing toward Xandar's surface with all the Guardians still inside — no weapon or plan can stop it
• Groot spreads his arms and grows a dense interlocked shell of living wood around all of his friends
• Rocket grabs Groot's arm and tries to stop him — Groot says "I am Groot," quiet and certain, looking right at Rocket
• Rocket understands immediately; Groot always regrows, but the action is still an act of total courage
• The crash happens — the bark absorbs the impact; the outer layers break apart but the innermost shell holds
• All the Guardians survive; around them in the wreckage are the scattered pieces of Groot
• Rocket sits in the debris for a while, then carefully gathers every fragment — every twig, every chip of bark
• He plants them in a small pot; Groot grows back, as he always does — but the courage of those three words never leaves
