• Groot's greatest power is regeneration — he regrows any broken branch and can even sprout a whole new body from a single twig
• His roots pull nutrients from nearly any surface, even asteroid rock and metal space-station floors
• On rich soil like the forested moons of the Pama system, he grows noticeably taller and stronger within hours
• His strength can bend steel beams and punch through armored ship hulls — but he also has surgeon's precision with single vines
• His consciousness is spread throughout his whole body from root to crown, not just in his head
• This distributed mind is why emotions move through him like seasons through a forest — completely, deeply, everywhere at once
• He once built a protective bark shell around an injured bird to keep it warm and still until it healed
• He is among the strongest beings in the galaxy and also among the gentlest — which makes him truly remarkable
