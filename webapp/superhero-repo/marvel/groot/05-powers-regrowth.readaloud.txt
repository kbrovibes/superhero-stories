So you want to know what Groot can ACTUALLY do? Buckle up, because the list is AMAZING!

First — STRENGTH. Groot is enormously strong. We're talking bend a steel beam, punch through a spaceship hull rated for heavy bombardment strong. The kind of strong where you look at a door and think — probably I should use the door — but if you needed to, you could go STRAIGHT THROUGH the wall. Groot goes through the wall. He doesn't even apologize.

But ALSO — and this is the cool part — he can be incredibly precise. He grows a single vine as thin as a hair and threads it through a tiny lock, feeling each tumbler turn from the inside. CLICK. One moment he is a battering ram smashing through the hull of an enemy ship. The next moment he is threading a single root through a keyhole to rescue something tiny and irreplaceable from a deep narrow gap.

Storm one moment. Surgeon the next. BOTH are Groot. With the same hands!

Second — REGENERATION! This is his SUPERPOWER! Break a branch? SPROINK — grows back! Crack his bark? CREAK CREAK — new layers form within days! On rich soil like the forested moons of the Pama system, Groot gets noticeably TALLER in just a few hours. His chest glows brighter! His branches lengthen and thicken! Even on bare asteroid rock with almost no soil at all, he keeps his quiet biology going — drawing nutrients from whatever is there, because he is UNSTOPPABLE.

And here is the most extraordinary part: if somehow, somehow, Groot was reduced to just a single twig — ONE TWIG — given enough time, a whole new Groot would grow from it. He CANNOT be permanently stopped. He is literally made of renewal!

Third — and this is really interesting — his MIND is spread through his WHOLE body. From the tips of his roots to the crown of his head. Which means a bonk on the head barely bothers him! And it means he FEELS things completely, everywhere at once. Joy rushes through him like sap in spring. Sadness settles through him like autumn settling over a whole forest.

This is why, once, when an injured bird fell onto the Milano's deck, Groot built it a little bark shelter — warm, airy, perfectly fitted — and sat next to it ALL NIGHT with one finger resting on the outside, feeling the tiny heartbeat through his own living wood. Waiting. Completely patient. Completely happy to wait all night for one small creature to be okay.

Storm AND surgeon. Power AND tenderness. Impossible strength and the warmest heart in the galaxy.

ALL GROOT.

I. Am. GROOT!
