• Groot is a living tree-creature from Planet X, home of the ancient Flora Colossi, whose only words are "I am Groot"
• He has glowing violet eyes, bark for skin, and can stretch and regrow his branches — but his greatest quality is his kindness
• The elders of Planet X exile Groot for sheltering a small human child in the sacred groves
• Groot drifts through the galaxy, working cargo jobs at space stations, until he meets Rocket on the planet Xandar
• Rocket is a sharp-witted, mechanical bounty hunter who becomes Groot's best friend within a week
• Together they join Star-Lord, Gamora, and Drax to form the Guardians of the Galaxy
• In every battle Groot protects his teammates — growing walls of bark, stretching bridges, carrying the hurt
• He was exiled for caring too much; among the Guardians, that same quality makes him the heart of the team
