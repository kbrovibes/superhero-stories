• Thanos, the Mad Titan, commands the Black Order and plans to collect all six Infinity Stones to control the universe
• His generals — Corvus Glaive, Proxima Midnight, and Ebony Maw — attack the sector near Xandar, forcing the Guardians to respond
• The Guardians race to Knowhere, the ancient mining colony carved inside a giant Celestial skull
• Proxima Midnight corners Gamora in a narrow tunnel — Groot smashes through the wall to rescue her
• Rocket fires two blasters to seal the tunnel, giving the team a chance to regroup
• The Guardians cannot defeat Thanos outright — he is too powerful — but they protect what they came to protect
• They escape on the Milano, battered but together
• Back on the ship Groot quietly regrows his broken branches — steady and calm after the chaos
