• Taneleer Tivan, the Collector, lives in Knowhere and fills his vast museum with rare alien specimens and artifacts
• He hears that Groot — a rare living Flora Colossi — is nearby and sends an invitation to the Guardians
• The Guardians visit the Collector's museum; Tivan offers any price they name in exchange for Groot
• Groot notices a small bird-creature imprisoned in a cage nearby, its eyes wide with misery
• Without hesitation, Groot breaks the cage open — the bird flies free to the vaulted ceiling, singing
• Three guards draw weapons; Groot spreads his arms wide in front of his friends and stands firm
• Rocket reminds Tivan exactly what their weapons can do, and Tivan decides to let them go
• The Guardians leave intact; Groot was never for sale — some things in the galaxy cannot be owned
