In the whole galaxy — in ALL the galaxies — there is no friendship quite like the friendship between Groot and Rocket.

Let me paint the picture. Groot: enormous. Bark skin. Speaks three words. Patient as an ancient forest. Slow to anger. Kind to every small creature he meets.

Rocket: the size of a large house cat. Mechanical parts fitted here and there. Cannot stop talking. Has a plan for everything. Slightly suspicious of everyone, always.

These two are BEST FRIENDS. Completely.

Rocket came from somewhere painful — a place and a past he never talks about. But he trusted Groot quickly. Right away, almost. Because Groot never wanted anything from him. He just walked alongside him and meant it.

And Rocket? Rocket is the ONLY being in the entire galaxy who understands every single thing Groot says. "I am Groot" — three words — but in Rocket's ears it means a thousand different things! He knows the difference between the LOW slow "I am Groot" that means danger is near and the BRIGHT quick one that means "I just saw something fascinating!" He knows the soft one Groot uses after a hard fight — "I am Groot" — which means: are you okay?

In a battle, they don't need to speak at all! Groot makes a wall of bark — WHOMP — and Rocket swings up onto his shoulder — CLUNK — and takes the shot before the enemy even knows where to look. PZWOOM! Done. No planning. No discussion. Just two friends who know each other that completely.

Now. Here is the part that might make you feel something in your chest.

After one very, very hard battle over Xandar, Groot was reduced to just a handful of small twigs. That's it. No voice. No eyes. Just twigs.

Rocket gathered every single piece. Put them carefully into a small pot of soil. And then…

He talked to the pot.

Every day.

He argued with it. He complained to it. He told it what was happening on the Milano. At least once — at least once — he apologized to it. For something. We don't know exactly what. But he did.

Months passed. And then one morning — one very important morning — the tiniest green shoot appeared above the soil.

Rocket called everyone. Ran through the ship. "GET IN HERE! EVERYONE! RIGHT NOW!"

And they all crowded into the cargo bay and looked at the tiny green shoot.

And Rocket, who was not usually the kind of person who got emotional about things, looked at it for a long time.

That's a best friend. That's what a best friend does.

I am Groot. And Rocket knows exactly what that means.
