Oh, this is a big one! Are you ready? Because the villain today is the most dangerous enemy the Guardians have EVER faced, and his name is RONAN THE ACCUSER!

Ronan is a Kree warrior — tall, armored in black from head to toe — who carries an enormous stone hammer called the Cosmi-Rod that can level mountains. And here's the really scary part: Ronan has found the POWER STONE. One of the six Infinity Stones! It glows PURPLE and it is pure, raw, unstoppable force. Ronan has set it into the Cosmi-Rod and pointed his warship — the Dark Aster, a black floating fortress the size of a city — straight at the planet Xandar, where millions of people are going about their ordinary lives, not yet knowing what is coming through the clouds.

Not if the Guardians can help it!

The Milano shoots straight at the Dark Aster — WHOOOOSH! Groot goes first. He drives his enormous bark fists into the hull — CRASH CRASH CRASH — tearing the metal plating open like wrapping paper. In they go! Inside, Rocket fires six different weapons he built from salvaged ship parts. BZZT! BZZT! PZWOOM! Star-Lord, Gamora, and Drax fight corridor by corridor through Ronan's Sakaaran soldiers. The whole ship is noise and chaos and motion.

Then — the bridge. Ronan. The Power Stone blazing in his hammer like a purple star, lighting the whole room with terrible, beautiful energy.

He is TERRIFYINGLY powerful. He swings the Cosmi-Rod — BOOM — and the shockwave sends the whole team sideways. But Groot gets up. CRACK — knocked into a wall. Gets up. BOOM — knocked flat. Gets up.

A tree. Does not. Stay. Down.

Then — the greatest, most impossible moment in Guardian history. All five of them reach for the Power Stone together. All five hands at once — four regular hands and one enormous set of bark-covered fingers. The energy ROARS through all of them. It crackles. It burns. It is too much for any single living being to survive. But spread between five people who would do anything for each other?

Groot holds on.

FLASH! Ronan is defeated! Xandar is saved! The Nova Corps arrives with their ships and their shields and officially declares the Guardians of the Galaxy allies of the planet they just protected!

Groot stands in the rubble after it's all over. The dust is still settling. The sky is just starting to clear. He bends down and picks up one small yellow flower petal that somehow survived the entire battle — just holds it very gently, in his enormous bark fingers, and looks at it for a long moment.

"I am Groot," he says quietly.

Rocket, who is completely out of ammunition and grinning despite every ounce of effort not to: "Yeah. We did."
