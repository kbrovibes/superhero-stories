• Arthur Douglas and his family are attacked by Thanos in the Nevada desert, and Arthur's life is taken
• A cosmic being named Mentor rescues Arthur's spirit and places it in a powerful new body built for battle
• Arthur becomes Drax the Destroyer — immensely strong, covered in green ritual tattoos, and focused on one mission: stop Thanos
• Drax wakes on the planet Titan with no memory of his old life, only the burning need for justice
• He learns his daughter Heather survived and was raised by Isaac, growing up to become the hero Moondragon
• Drax takes everything people say completely literally — he would look for falling cats if someone said it was "raining cats and dogs"
• He eventually joins the Guardians of the Galaxy and fights alongside Star-Lord, Gamora, Rocket, and Groot
• His tattoos mark every promise kept — he is a destroyer who fights only for those he loves
