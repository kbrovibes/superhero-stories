• Drax's tattoos are ritual Kylosian warrior markings applied on Titan before his training began
• Mentor insisted the marks came before training — a warrior who fights without honoring what they fight for is only an animal with weapons
• The first marks, across his shoulders, are Marks of Becoming, honoring the ordinary human life of Arthur Douglas
• The marks on his forearms are Oaths of Protection, a formal vow to defend those who cannot defend themselves
• Two matching marks above his heart honor Yvette and Heather — Drax chose that location himself
• Drax never wears a shirt — not vanity, he simply does not think about clothing
• On an ice planet called Berhert, Gamora suggests a jacket; Drax says "I do not feel cold" and walks into the snow
• The tattoos are the most permanent thing about him — his history written on the outside so he can never lose it
