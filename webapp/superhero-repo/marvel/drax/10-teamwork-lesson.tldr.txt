• The Guardians answer a distress call from Vesper Station, a mining settlement threatened by a syndicate called the Sovereignty
• The mission splits: Gamora and Rocket handle the weapons array; Star-Lord and Nebula fly interference outside
• Drax is left inside the station with forty-three miners and twelve frightened children
• He discovers his most useful quality here is not his strength — it is his absolute honesty and calm
• A six-year-old named Mirava is crying; Drax sits on the floor beside her and names all five Guardians and what each one is doing to protect them
• He promises: "I am here, I will not move, nothing will get past me" — Mirava stops crying
• Rocket finds Drax on the floor, Mirava asleep against his arm, the other children calm around them
• Mirava grows up to be a mining engineer and names her first drill the Destroyer — she is never afraid of the dark
