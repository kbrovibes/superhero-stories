• Heather Douglas survived the Nevada attack and was found by Isaac, an elder of Titan's Eternals, who raised her there
• Heather trained her mind at the Monastery of the Dragon of the Moon, developing telepathy and telekinesis
• She became the hero Moondragon — shaved head, dark clothing, extraordinary mental powers
• Drax and Heather reunite on Titan — no big embrace, just two people unsure what to do with each other
• In battle together they are extraordinary — she senses his moves before he makes them
• They argue often: her philosophical abstractions puzzle him; his literal thinking tries her patience
• Drax never uses her hero name; she is always "Heather" to him, though she pretends to find this annoying
• Their reunion fills in slowly over months — two people from the same broken beginning, each remarkable in a different way
