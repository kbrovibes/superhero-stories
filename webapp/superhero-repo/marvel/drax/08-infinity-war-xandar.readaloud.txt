ALL RIGHT. Get ready. This is the big one. The Battle of XANDAR!

The planet Xandar was beautiful — golden towers, sparkling domes, the headquarters of the Nova Corps gleaming in the afternoon sun. Normal, ordinary day. People going about their lives. And then?

RONAN. In the Dark Aster. With the POWER STONE.

The Dark Aster was enormous, and it came down through Xandar's sky like a metal mountain falling in slow motion. Everyone on the ground looked up. This was very, very bad.

But here came the Milano — the Guardians' battered, patched, barely-aerodynamic ship — flying straight toward Ronan's entire fleet. WHOOOOSH!

Did the Guardians have a good plan? A complete plan? A plan with all the parts?

Not really. But they had each other. And sometimes that is enough.

Rocket screamed out of the Milano in a small fighter jet and went straight for Ronan's warships. ONE RACCOON, entire enemy fleet, zero hesitation. BLAM! KABOOM! Rocket flew like someone who had decided that fear was simply not going to be in this story today.

Gamora fought her way through soldier after soldier, cutting toward the orb. Groot stretched his branches through a corridor and held it solid so people could escape underneath him. A living barricade! HOLD, GROOT, HOLD!

And Drax? Drax planted himself at the forward position and became a wall.

This was not the Drax from Knowhere who charged alone and got dunked in yellow liquid. This was Drax who had LEARNED something. He fought with the team around him. He hit Ronan hard enough — CRACK — to actually split the Universal Weapon's staff. CRACK! Ronan skidded backwards and PAUSED. A real pause! For the first time in the whole battle!

And then. The most unexpected thing in the history of space battles.

Star-Lord started dancing.

Just — dancing. In front of Ronan. Right in Ronan the Accuser's face. Peter Quill pointed finger guns and shuffled his feet to music only he could hear.

Ronan stopped. Every Kree soldier on the ship stopped. The whole Dark Aster seemed to stop.

And in that pause — GRAB — the Power Stone was taken, and all five Guardians held it together, the purple energy blazing through their arms, each one of them pushing BACK against the impossible power —

And they turned it on Ronan. Together.

When the dust settled, Drax stood in the golden Xandar light and looked at a sky that was empty of warships. He breathed in. He breathed out.

Still standing.

Still ready for what came next.

GUARDIANS OF THE GALAXY, everyone!
