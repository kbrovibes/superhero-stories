• Drax carries two simple polished daggers — no gadgets, no energy cells, just balanced blades forged on Titan
• A Titan metalsmith named Peliali crafted them from a special alloy that cuts through materials ordinary steel cannot
• Drax throws with perfect accuracy from thirty meters — he hit every cup Rocket set up without missing once
• Each dagger has a private name: Sorrow and Purpose — reminders of what he lost and what he chose to do about it
• The names are Drax's private tradition — he never explains them to the other Guardians
• Over the years, the grief behind those names became quiet and steady, like the weight of the blades themselves
• Fighting with daggers requires a moment of inner stillness to aim true — a lesson the blades have taught Drax throw by throw
• The daggers represent restraint: even the strongest warrior can choose precision over brute force
