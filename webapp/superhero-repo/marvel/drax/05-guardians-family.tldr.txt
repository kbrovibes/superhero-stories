• The Guardians meet inside the Kyln prison — an undignified start for a team of heroes
• Drax is already in prison waiting for someone connected to Thanos; he initially considers Gamora an enemy
• Together they escape the Kyln and survive chaos on Knowhere and then plan to defend Xandar together
• When Groot sacrifices himself to protect the group as their ship crashes, Drax holds on and understands exactly what is happening
• Groot grows back from a single twig, and Drax watches over that tiny plant with the same care he once gave his daughter Heather
• Gamora teaches Drax when to pause; Star-Lord teaches him when to laugh (he rarely agrees the joke was funny)
• Rocket shows Drax that strength does not always look like muscles — a quiet and important lesson
• Drax gives the Guardians absolute certainty: when things get bad, he will be standing in front of them, daggers out, unmovable
