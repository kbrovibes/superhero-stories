• Ronan the Accuser commands the warship Dark Aster and wields the Universal Weapon, a crackling stone war hammer
• Ronan betrays Thanos and takes the Power Stone — an Infinity Stone powerful enough to destroy an entire planet — aiming to destroy Xandar
• Drax, consumed by his need for revenge, loudly announces his location to Ronan from inside the Kyln prison
• On Knowhere, the head of an ancient Celestial floating in space, Drax secretly contacts Ronan and challenges him to a fight
• Drax battles Ronan alone with his daggers and immense strength — and loses, sinking into the pools of Knowhere
• Groot rescues Drax from the liquid, and the Guardians are frustrated but relieved
• Drax admits he was wrong to act alone — a hard but honest lesson for the galaxy's strongest warrior
• The rematch comes later, over Xandar, with the full Guardians team — and this time they win
