• Joaquin Torres: a sharp, funny Air Force lieutenant stationed in Tunisia, chasing tips on the Flag Smashers when Sam first found him
• Could fly anything — once landed a helicopter on a runway with only one working rotor
• Took a beating from a Flag Smasher and asked, from his hospital bed, "So when are we going back out?"
• When Sam became Captain America, he kept Joaquin close — slow trust, takeout containers, shared silences
• Sam took Joaquin to Wakanda and introduced him to the Royal Engineering Corps; took him to Delacroix to meet Sarah Wilson
• Trained Joaquin in vibranium wings for almost a year — patient teacher, good student
• The day Sam handed the wings over: new sleeker suit in green and gold, Air Force pin tucked into the collar
• Joaquin pulled on the helmet, looked at himself, said quietly: "Oh, this is gonna be a problem"
• They flew together as partners now, not student and teacher — Sam with the shield, Joaquin with the Redwing drone
• Sam finally understood what Steve had felt: some gifts, given at the right time, fly even higher than they did before
