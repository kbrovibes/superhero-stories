• President Thaddeus Ross: former soldier, longtime Hulk hunter, iron-gray hair, suit instead of uniform, temper still simmering
• In Sam's first months as Captain America, the two had to figure out how to work together
• A villain called the Leader had spent years engineering a crisis to push Ross past his limit
• A terrible evening: an attack on a White House state dinner, rumors of war, the Leader's old experiments finally triggered inside Ross
• The President of the United States transformed, on camera, into the Red Hulk — eight feet tall, deep crimson, eyes like furnace doors
• Sam did not panic — cleared civilians, sent Joaquin Torres to redirect news helicopters, picked up the shield
• Used the vibranium shield as a wall, not a weapon — deflected every blow, never tried to hurt Ross
• Ended the fight with a conversation, not a knockout: "Mr. Ross. Look at me. You can come back from this"
• Red Hulk shrank, breathed; Thaddeus Ross sat on the lawn in his torn suit; Sam knelt beside him
• The cameras caught everything — that was the moment the country understood what Sam meant by picking up the shield
