• Sam Wilson: grew up in Harlem, became an Air Force pararescue specialist
• Flew the EXO-7 Falcon — mechanical wings unfolding from a metal backpack, enabling high-speed gliding and diving
• Flew night rescue missions into mountain valleys to recover injured soldiers
• Defining loss: wingman Riley killed by a rocket-propelled grenade in mid-flight; Sam powerless to help
• Returned to Washington D.C., counseled veterans at the VA Hospital, jogged the National Mall
• Met Steve Rogers — "On your left" — on a morning run; instant friendship over shared grief
• Steve told Sam about Bucky; Sam told Steve about Riley; deep bond formed
• HYDRA targeted Nick Fury and planned to weaponize S.H.I.E.L.D. for Project Insight — global assassination
• Sam retrieved the EXO-7 wings from a museum vault and joined Steve without hesitation
• Battle of the Triskelion: Falcon replaced targeting chips on three Helicarriers, helped end Project Insight, stood by Steve Rogers as a full Avenger
