• Sam Wilson originally handed the shield to the Smithsonian Institution — he did not believe he was the right person to carry it
• Believed the shield belonged to Steve Rogers and a different kind of man; saw himself as a pararescue, a VA counselor, a brother
• After John Walker bloodied the shield in Latvia and the Flag Smashers nearly destroyed a global council, the country demanded an answer
• Bucky Barnes flew to Delacroix, Louisiana and delivered a long flat case to the Wilson family porch — the shield, repolished by hand
• A new red, white, and blue suit arrived as a gift from the King of Wakanda — forged from a sketch by Shuri
• Sam put the suit on in Sarah Wilson's bathroom mirror; Sarah told him not to get it dirty — first real laugh in months
• Flew to New York, burst through the Manhattan skyline in red, white, and blue, cameras following — the new Captain America
• Did not try to be Steve; felt like himself, finally — shield on arm, wings catching wind
• Did not inherit the role — earned it the long way, by being the most patient man in any room
