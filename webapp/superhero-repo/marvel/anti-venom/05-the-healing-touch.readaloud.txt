Quick question! What's the coolest superpower you can think of? Super strength? Flying? Zapping lasers?

Well, Anti-Venom has the RAREST power of all. Ready for it?

He can HEAL people. With just a touch!

Most heroes are famous for how HARD they can punch. POW! But Eddie Brock is famous for something way more special — how GENTLY he can make people well.

Here's how it works. Deep inside the white goo are millions of tiny helpers called antibodies. Antibodies are the little heroes ALREADY inside your body, fighting off sniffles and germs right now! And Anti-Venom's antibodies are super-duper powerful.

When Eddie lays his big glowing white hands on somebody — WHOOSH — those antibodies pour in and go hunting!

They can wash away a bad illness. GONE! They can pull yucky poison right out of the blood — like a magnet grabbing metal — ZWIP! They can chase off diseases and leave a person cleaner and healthier than before!

It's the very same gift Martin Li once gave to Eddie. And now Eddie passes it on, hand to hand.

But here's my favorite part. Eddie doesn't save this power for big flashy battles. Nope!

He uses it in the quiet corners of New York, where nobody's watching.

In a little shelter, he pressed his palm to a sick woman's forehead — and by morning, her fever was ALL better!

In a dark alley, he found a man who'd swallowed something poisonous. Eddie's white light pulled the poison right out — and the man coughed, blinked, and sat up. "I'm… I'm okay!"

Soon the whole neighborhood was whispering about the mysterious white healer. And you know what Eddie figured out? Helping one ordinary person felt even BETTER than beating a giant villain. No headlines. No cheering crowds. Just somebody who hurt… and then didn't anymore.

But — uh oh — there's a catch.

The healing isn't endless. Every time Eddie cures someone, a little bit of his power drains away, like pouring water out of a pitcher. Glug, glug, glug. Heal too many people too fast, and the white glow starts to flicker and go dim! Then Eddie has to rest before he can help again.

So every single healing is a choice. And every choice matters.

Isn't that something? The big scary-looking guy with the giant white eyes…

His strongest power is being KIND.

So the next time your tummy feels better, or your scrape stops stinging, give a little thank-you to the tiny antibody helpers inside YOU. They're doing Anti-Venom's favorite job every single day!

Now THAT'S a superhero.
