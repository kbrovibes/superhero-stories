Okay, pop quiz! What do you get when you mix a healing light with a little bit of leftover symbiote goo?

You get the ONE symbiote that ALL the other symbiotes are scared of!

Let me explain. Symbiotes — like Venom and Carnage — all come from a faraway world. They wrap around a person and share cool powers: super strength, wall-crawling, stretchy webs. They're like one big goo family.

But Anti-Venom? He's the odd one out. Because Anti-Venom is the ANTIDOTE. The cure. The natural enemy of every other symbiote!

Here's why. When Martin Li's healing light mixed with the bits of Venom in Eddie's blood — ZAP — it filled the white goo with special antibodies built to fight OTHER symbiotes!

So when Anti-Venom touches black goo? FSSSS! The black goo hisses and melts and yanks itself away! That's why big scary Venom AND big scary Carnage both go, "Uh oh — RUN!" when Eddie shows up.

But wait, there's MORE!

Eddie kept all the classic goo powers too. Watch this!

Super strength! He can bend a steel door — CREAK, SNAP — or lift a heavy beam off somebody who's trapped. HRRRNGH!

Wall-crawling! He climbs straight up a skyscraper, sticking to glass and brick like a spider. Up, up, up!

Webs! THWIP! Strong white webbing shoots from his hands so he can swing across the city or catch a running crook!

And — ooh, this one's sneaky — camouflage! He can change the color of his goo and flatten right against a wall, blending into the shadows until he VANISHES. Now you see him… now you don't!

Pretty amazing, right?

But here's the thing every hero has — a limit. And Eddie's is a big one.

His healing, curing, symbiote-melting power? It is NOT endless.

Every time he uses it, the white glow inside him runs a little lower — like a flashlight when the battery gets tired. Use it too much, too fast, and — flicker, flicker — the light sputters and fades! Then Eddie has to rest until it slowly fills back up.

So Anti-Venom has to be CLEVER. He can't just zap every single problem. He has to pick his moments and save his strength for when it really, really counts.

And you know what? That makes his power extra special. It's not just a weapon.

It's a treasure. And Eddie never, ever wastes it.

So let's remember all of it! The antidote that melts other symbiotes — FSSS! The super strength — HRRRNGH! The wall-crawling, the webs, the sneaky camouflage. And one careful, precious limit that keeps him wise.

That's Anti-Venom. The symbiote who cures. Pretty cool, huh?
