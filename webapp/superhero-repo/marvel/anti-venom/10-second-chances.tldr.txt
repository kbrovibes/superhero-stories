• The lesson of Anti-Venom: everyone deserves a second chance, and healing is the greatest power
• Eddie began as Venom, a creature the whole city feared, sure his story could only end in darkness
• Kind Martin Li saw him as a person worth saving and gave him a second chance
• That mercy cured Eddie, created Anti-Venom, and turned a villain into a Lethal Protector
• Eddie fought to cure, not destroy — Mac Gargan, Mister Negative, and all of Spider-Island
• The point of a second chance is to pass it on: you heal because you were healed
• When Eddie gave up his symbiote, the power lived on through Flash Thompson as Agent Anti-Venom
• The gift passed hand to hand — Martin Li to Eddie to Flash — like a candle lighting a candle
