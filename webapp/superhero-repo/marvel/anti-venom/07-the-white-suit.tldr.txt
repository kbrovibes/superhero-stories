• At a glance Anti-Venom looks like Venom — but he is the mirror-opposite in every way
• Where Venom is black, Anti-Venom is glossy, gleaming white like snow or pearl
• Swirling black tendrils and veins curl along his arms and shoulders like inked branches
• A black spider shape spreads across his white chest — Venom's emblem, flipped
• He has huge oval white eyes rimmed in black and a wide, toothy, friendlier grin
• The white color is the sign of the healing Lightforce and the antibodies inside him
• The living goo stretches, shields, grabs, and shifts color to vanish into shadows
• Eddie is proud of the white suit because it shows who he became — mercy instead of anger, a guardian in the color of light
