• Eddie Brock had once been Venom, the black symbiote, before walking away to seek a quieter life
• Eddie fell ill with a serious sickness that no hospital doctor could cure
• He met Martin Li, who ran the F.E.A.S.T. soup kitchen in Chinatown and carried a healing white glow called the Lightforce
• Martin's Lightforce touched Eddie and washed the bad illness away
• The light mixed with tiny leftover traces of the old Venom symbiote still hidden in Eddie's blood
• A brand-new white symbiote bloomed — black tendrils, a black spider mark, huge white eyes: Anti-Venom
• Anti-Venom could heal sickness, wash out poison, and dissolve any other black symbiote goo
• Eddie, once feared, chose to use his second chance to protect innocent New Yorkers as a Lethal Protector
