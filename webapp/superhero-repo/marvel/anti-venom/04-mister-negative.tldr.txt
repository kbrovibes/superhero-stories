• Eddie owed everything to Martin Li, whose healing Lightforce cured him and created Anti-Venom
• Martin ran the F.E.A.S.T. shelter and seemed the kindest man in New York
• But Martin had a hidden shadow-self: the silver-haired crime boss Mister Negative
• Mister Negative used cold Darkforce to corrupt, and led a gang called the Inner Demons
• Anti-Venom fought the Inner Demons in the tunnels beneath the city and webbed them up
• Facing Mister Negative was hard — Eddie could not bring himself to hate the face that had healed him
• Eddie fought without hate, blocked the Darkforce, and scattered the crew; Mister Negative slipped away
• Eddie learned people are complicated — one person can hold both a light and a shadow
