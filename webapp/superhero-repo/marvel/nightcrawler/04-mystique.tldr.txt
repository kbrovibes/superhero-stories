• Kurt is still and quiet; he has suspected the truth for a long time
• He tells Mystique he has no anger, but that Margali Szardos is his true mother in every way that matters
• Mystique's hard expression softens; she and Kurt shake hands — not forgiveness, but honest acknowledgment
• Kurt returns to his teammates feeling more himself, not less
