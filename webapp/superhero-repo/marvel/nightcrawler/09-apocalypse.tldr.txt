• Apocalypse — En Sabah Nur, the oldest mutant alive — seizes a communications tower in Cairo, Egypt
• The X-Men fly south in the Blackbird; the tower is ringed with energy cannons and guarded by four Horsemen
• Storm and Cyclops draw the Horsemen away; Jean Grey fogs the operators; Kurt threads through the cannon ring
• The cannons track mass — his Brimstone Dimension hops fall below the threshold; he slips through
• Inside, Apocalypse is waiting — seven feet tall, ancient, and has already sensed Kurt's arrival
• Kurt can't fight him alone; he buys ninety seconds by talking about Xavier's vision and the children at the school
• At seventy-eight seconds, Storm's lightning destroys the broadcast equipment through the roof array
• Apocalypse withdraws with something almost like respect
• Flying home Kurt reflects: standing armed with only words — and the words being enough — is sometimes the whole lesson
