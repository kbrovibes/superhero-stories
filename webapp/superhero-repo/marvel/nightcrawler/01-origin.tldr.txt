• Kurt Wagner is born in Bavaria, Germany, with indigo skin, yellow eyes, three-fingered hands, a long tail, and the power to teleport with a loud BAMF
• His mother Mystique gives him up; a kind circus woman named Margali Szardos raises him with her traveling troupe
• Kurt becomes the star acrobat of the Munich circus, billed as "the Incredible Nightcrawler"
• A frightened mob corners him in a town near Stuttgart, calling him a monster
• Professor Charles Xavier arrives and tells Kurt he is not a monster but a gift to the world
• Xavier invites Kurt to his school in Westchester, New York — Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters
• Kurt joins the X-Men and trains alongside Wolverine, Storm, and Colossus in the Danger Room
• Away from battle, Kurt reads poetry, practices swordsmanship, and leads chapel prayers
• He has found a family — and a purpose as one of Earth's bravest defenders
