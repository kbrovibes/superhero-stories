• Tony Stark recruits Peter Parker during the superhero Civil War; for a solo hero from Queens, mentorship by the world's most famous billionaire is transformative
• Tony gives Peter the "Iron Spider" suit and opens his eyes to the larger world of global heroism
• Tony becomes a father figure — giving Peter guidance, not just gadgets — challenging him to be better and avoid his own mistakes
• During the battle against Thanos, Tony's primary motivation is protecting Peter; losing him in the Blip is devastating
• Peter and Tony's reunion after the Blip is one of the most emotional moments in Avengers history
• After Tony's sacrifice in the final battle, Peter inherits his technology including the "EDITH" AI defense system — along with Tony's enemies
• Peter struggles to define himself as a hero independent of Tony Stark's shadow
• Tony's mentorship transforms Peter from a reckless teenager into a responsible, selfless hero; Peter is Tony's greatest legacy
