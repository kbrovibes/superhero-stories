• Tony Stark recruits Peter Parker into the Avengers, expanding Spider-Man from "Friendly Neighborhood" hero to Earth's Mightiest Hero
• Peter suffers serious imposter syndrome alongside legends like Captain America and Thor
• Spider-Man's role: agile scout and moral compass — he stops to help civilians when others focus on the battle
• His spider-sense detects threats that advanced sensors miss, earning him teammates' respect fast
• Tony Stark sees Peter as the future of the hero business; the Avengers become a second family to Peter
• Peter fights the Black Order on an alien planet, far from Queens — and never loses his sense of humor or his sense of self
• Higher stakes strain Peter's personal life — he struggles to balance Avengers duties with family and school back home
• Peter proves he is more than a sidekick: a vital Avenger who will defend the world whether in his neighborhood or among the stars
