• The Spider-Verse: a vast web of alternate realities, each with its own Spider-Hero — all connected by a shared destiny
• The Inheritors — interdimensional vampires — hunt Spider-Totems across the multiverse, threatening them all
• Peter joins forces with Miles Morales (Spider-Man of the Ultimate Universe), Gwen Stacy (Spider-Woman of her world), and a noir Spider-Man from the 1930s
• Together they form an army of spiders to fight for the survival of their entire species
• Every Spider-Hero has faced personal tragedy and learned that with great power comes great responsibility — the bond that links them all
• Peter sees Miles Morales as a protégé who can carry on his legacy; seeing Gwen alive in another world is a bittersweet comfort
• Peter, as the "original" Spider-Man, grows into his role as leader — using experience and heart to guide the army
• The spiders storm the Inheritors' home world and defeat them; their worlds remain permanently connected afterward
