• Dr. Otto Octavius — brilliant nuclear physicist — builds four mechanical arms controlled by thought to handle radioactive materials safely
• A radiation leak causes an explosion, fusing the arms to his nervous system and warping his mind; the respected scientist becomes Doctor Octopus
• Doc Ock uses his four extra limbs to commit high-tech crimes across Manhattan, striking from multiple angles with terrifying agility
• Spider-Man battles Doc Ock across rooftops and the top of a moving subway train; Peter's spider-sense is the key to survival
• Doc Ock sets elaborate intellectual traps, staying one step ahead through manipulation of technology and surroundings
• Spider-Man uses Octavius's own technology against him — short-circuiting the harness to end the rampage
• Peter sees in the broken scientist a warning of what genius without morality can become
• Doc Ock returns repeatedly with more advanced technology; their rivalry endures as a clash of two brilliant minds
