• During the Secret Wars, Peter discovers a black alien substance that becomes a new costume — the "black suit" — responding to thought and providing limitless webbing
• Back on Earth, Peter realizes the suit is a living alien symbiote slowly making him more aggressive and violent
• Peter uses the high-pitched bells of a church tower to rip the symbiote from his body; it survives and bonds to Eddie Brock
• Eddie Brock — a disgraced journalist who blames Spider-Man for ruining his career — fuses with the symbiote to become Venom
• Because the symbiote was once bonded to Peter, Venom knows all his secrets including his identity and how to bypass his spider-sense
• Venom battles Peter in the dark corners of the city, using shape-shifting to become a ghost Peter cannot catch
• Peter identifies Venom's weakness: sound and fire; he uses science to separate Brock from the symbiote
• Venom evolves over time from pure villain to lethal protector; their relationship shifts from hatred to wary alliance
