• Norman Osborn — wealthy industrialist and father of Peter's best friend Harry — develops the Goblin Formula to enhance human strength and intellect
• The formula's side effect: a fractured psychotic personality; Norman becomes the Green Goblin — flying a high-tech glider, hurling pumpkin bombs
• The Goblin discovers Peter's secret identity and targets those he loves most
• Most devastating: the Goblin kidnaps Gwen Stacy — Peter's first true love — and takes her to the George Washington Bridge
• Gwen falls during the struggle; despite Peter catching her with his webbing, the sudden stop breaks her neck — Peter's most painful failure
• The Goblin tried to goad Peter into rage and cruelty; Peter always found the strength to stay true to his principles
• Even after Norman's apparent death, the Green Goblin legacy continues — his son Harry is driven mad by grief and the Goblin Formula
• Peter is forced to fight his own best friend; the Osborn family becomes the defining curse of his heroic life
