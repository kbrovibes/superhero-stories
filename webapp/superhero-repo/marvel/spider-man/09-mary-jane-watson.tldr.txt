• Mary Jane Watson enters Peter's life with the legendary line: "Face it, Tiger, you just hit the jackpot"
• Initially the outgoing "party girl" of the group; behind the bravado lies a perceptive woman who has faced her own family trauma
• MJ is the first person to truly see and understand the man behind Spider-Man's mask
• She lives with constant fear that Peter may not return each time he swings out the door; she faces threats from his enemies and media scrutiny
• Despite the danger, MJ remains Peter's greatest supporter — making him laugh when the weight of the world feels too heavy
• Their wedding celebrates the resilience of their bond and their belief that a normal life is still possible in an extraordinary world
• Mary Jane proves she is a hero in her own right — showing courage and strength that rivals Peter's own teammates
• Even during separations, their connection remains central to who they both are; she is the light that always brings him home
