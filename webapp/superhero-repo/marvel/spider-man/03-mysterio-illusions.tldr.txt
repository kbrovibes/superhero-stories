• Quentin Beck — failed Hollywood special-effects wizard and stuntman — creates holograms and psychological traps to become the villain Mysterio
• Mysterio wears a green suit and a domed reflective helmet; his goal is to humiliate heroes and prove he controls reality
• He frames Spider-Man for crimes using illusions, turning the city and the media against Peter Parker
• Peter is trapped in a hall of mirrors fighting shadows, ghosts, and hallucinogenic gas — his spider-sense scrambled by high-tech drones
• The confrontation peaks in an abandoned theater where Mysterio conjures a massive holographic monster threatening the city
• Peter closes his eyes and trusts his spider-sense alone — moving through illusions to find the man behind the curtain
• One decisive blow shatters Mysterio's domed helmet; Beck is revealed as a bitter, lonely trickster
• Peter learns to trust his instincts over his eyes — Mysterio proves a hero can be hurt by the right kind of lie
