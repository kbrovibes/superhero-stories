• Peter Parker grows up in Forest Hills, Queens, with Aunt May and Uncle Ben — a shy, brilliant teenager bullied by Flash Thompson
• On a school field trip to a research laboratory, a radioactive spider bites Peter's hand
• Peter gains spider-sense, wall-crawling, and superhuman strength; he hides his identity behind a costume and earns money wrestling as "Spider-Man"
• Peter lets a thief escape, refusing to help a police officer — the same thief later murders Uncle Ben at the Parker home
• Peter tracks the killer to an abandoned warehouse and realizes his inaction caused the worst loss of his life
• Uncle Ben's words echo in his mind: "With great power comes great responsibility"
• Peter becomes the Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man, patrolling Queens and Manhattan against muggers and super-villains
• He juggles heroism with poverty, studies, and caring for Aunt May — honoring Uncle Ben's memory with every patrol
