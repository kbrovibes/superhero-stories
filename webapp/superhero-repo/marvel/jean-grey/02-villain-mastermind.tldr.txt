• Mastermind — real name Jason Wyngarde — has the mutant power to create convincing illusions in any person's mind
• He targets Jean Grey, slowly weaving false images around her: wrong hallways, strange shadows, doors that don't exist
• Professor Xavier notices Jean's faraway look and gathers the X-Men, warning them her mind may be under attack
• Scott Summers rushes to help; Jean hears his voice but Mastermind traps her in a maze of mirrors inside an illusion
• Jean closes her eyes, ignores the false images, and reaches outward with her telepathy instead
• She finds the thin thread of Mastermind's concentration and pushes against it — shattering his focus like a stone through glass
• The illusion collapses; Mastermind retreats, stunned that Jean beat his trick from the inside out
• Jean learns she doesn't need to see through a lie — she only needs to find what is real beneath it
