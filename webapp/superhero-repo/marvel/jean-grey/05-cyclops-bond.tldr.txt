• Scott Summers has been at the Xavier Institute longer than Jean; he becomes a steady, familiar presence from her first day
• His ruby-quartz visor contains his optic beams; Jean never sees it as a barrier — it is simply part of who Scott is
• They train together in the Danger Room; Jean's telepathic warnings and Scott's precision make them an unstoppable team
• They develop a silent shorthand — a glance, a nod — that other X-Men can barely follow
• Jean is drawn to Scott because he is never unsettled by her telepathy; he simply goes on being himself around her
• Scott is more open with Jean than anyone else, as if her gift gives him permission to be honest
• Jean could have read his feelings with her telepathy — but she never does; some things belong to the person feeling them
• One quiet evening in the Institute's garden, Scott tells her how he feels; Jean tells him exactly the same
