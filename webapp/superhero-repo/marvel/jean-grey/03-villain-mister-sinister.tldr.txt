• Mister Sinister — real name Nathaniel Essex — is a coldly brilliant scientist obsessed with mutant genetics
• He wears a black coat and has a glowing red diamond on his pale forehead; he never shouts, which makes him more frightening than villains who do
• His obsession is Scott Summers and Jean Grey, whose genetic profiles he has studied for years believing they are the most powerful mutants alive
• He sends his engineered soldiers the Marauders into the Morlock Tunnels beneath New York City to intercept the X-Men
• Jean senses the ambush before it arrives and warns Scott, giving the team time to regroup
• In the dark narrow tunnels, Jean uses telekinesis to slam stone passages shut, blocking Marauder after Marauder
• Sinister appears at the tunnel's end and calmly tells Jean he means only to study her; she sends a firm telepathic message: leave
• Sinister retreats — for now — and Jean knows he will return someday, but she will be ready
