• A reactivated sentinel robot is cutting off power on the island nation of Genosha, trapping civilians underground in collapsed transit stations
• Professor Xavier briefs the team: Scott, Jean, Storm, Iceman, and Beast each have a specific role
• Storm grounds the sentinel's sensors, Iceman freezes its joints, Beast severs the signal, Scott hits its optical systems
• Jean's role is the hardest: go underground alone, find the trapped people, and bring them out safely
• Using telepathy as a map, Jean locates forty-three civilians two levels down in a flooded, dark platform
• She sends a wave of calm into the crowd first, then finds an alternate passage and begins leading everyone out
• She levitates the injured on telekinetic stretchers and holds up a groaning passage ceiling to keep the route clear
• The sentinel falls just as Jean leads the last person out; all forty-three rescued; the team reunites in the wreckage
