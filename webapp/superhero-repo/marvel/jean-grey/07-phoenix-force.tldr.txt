• The Phoenix Force is ancient cosmic energy — pure potential, the force of life and renewal — that has existed since before Earth
• Jean has always felt something vast at the edge of her awareness; Professor Xavier shielded her from it, believing she was not yet ready
• During a crisis in deep space, the Phoenix Force offers itself to Jean: accept and save her teammates, or hold back
• Jean chooses her friends and accepts the Phoenix Fire; she feels the edges of the galaxy
• Her hair blazes and her eyes burn gold; the danger is swept away by clean, vast fire
• The bravest moment is when Jean steps back from the power and returns to being fully herself
• The Phoenix Force stays with her as a warm ember she carries carefully, knowing its value and weight
• She tells the X-Men everything; they look at her the same way they always have — with trust and certainty
