• Jean Grey grows up in Annandale-on-Hudson, New York, with no idea she is a mutant
• When her best friend Annie Richardson is hurt in an accident, Jean's telepathy bursts open without warning, flooding her with Annie's fear and pain
• Her frightened parents contact Professor Charles Xavier, who offers Jean a place at his Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters in Westchester
• Jean arrives at the Xavier Institute and befriends Scott Summers and Hank McCoy, training daily in the Danger Room to gain control
• She discovers a second power — telekinesis — that feels as natural as using her own hands
• Professor Xavier teaches her to focus her telepathy like a signal instead of letting it overwhelm her like a flood
• Jean dons the gold-and-blue X-Men uniform and takes the name Marvel Girl, ready to protect a world that is still learning not to fear mutants
