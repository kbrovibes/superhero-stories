• Jean can feel every mind on her city block at once — learning to manage this is the most important training she does
• Professor Xavier works with her in his shielded study, using Cerebro's auxiliary dampener to block outside interference
• He teaches her to hold one mind at a time: a bird, a student upstairs — one thread, held softly
• Jean keeps a journal; early entries describe overwhelm, later ones show steady improvement
• The hardest lesson is empathy without losing herself: feeling another person's fear while staying clear on who she is
• She learns to scan buildings for hostile intent and project a calm, warm presence into panicked crowds
• She can speak directly into a teammate's mind across great distances and shield her own thoughts from other telepaths
• Jean believes telepathy, used with care, is one of the kindest gifts a person could have
