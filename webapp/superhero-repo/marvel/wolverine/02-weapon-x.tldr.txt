• A secret government program called Weapon X had been studying Logan for years
• Scientists capture Logan and bring him to a facility in the Canadian Rockies
• They have discovered adamantium — a near-indestructible metal — and plan to bond it to his skeleton
• The procedure is painful, but Logan's healing factor keeps him alive throughout
• When finished, every bone and claw is coated in adamantium — nearly unbreakable
• Logan escapes into the wilderness, but the trauma leaves his memories scrambled
• He does not even remember his own name for a time
• Professor Charles Xavier and the X-Men patiently help Logan reclaim himself
• Logan realizes the adamantium belongs to him now — not to any program or anyone else
