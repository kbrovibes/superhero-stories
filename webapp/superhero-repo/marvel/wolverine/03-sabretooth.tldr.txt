• Victor Creed — Sabretooth — has claws and a healing factor like Logan, but fights for domination instead of protection
• Their rivalry stretches back decades; Creed enjoys taunting Logan about his painful past
• Sabretooth attacks Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters, smashing through the gate with a roar
• Students scatter and alarms ring; Logan had already smelled Creed coming a minute before the attack
• Their battle tears up the lawn and brings down part of the garden wall — metal claws clashing with an enormous crash
• The difference between them is not strength — it is purpose: Sabretooth wants to prove superiority; Logan only wants to protect
• Logan drives Sabretooth back step by step until Creed retreats into the trees
• Logan retracts his claws and quietly checks that every student is safe
• That calm, careful question — "Is everyone all right?" — is what makes him a hero
