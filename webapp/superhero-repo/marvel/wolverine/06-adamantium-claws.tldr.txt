• Wolverine's claws are three on each hand, emerging with the famous SNIKT sound from between his knuckles
• They began as natural bone mutations — appearing the same night his healing factor first activated
• For decades Logan used them only as a last resort and kept them secret
• The Weapon X program coated his entire skeleton — including the claws — in adamantium, an almost-indestructible metallic alloy
• After the bonding, the claws transformed from ivory bone to gleaming silver metal, capable of scoring steel
• Logan has used the claws to free allies, cut through locked doors, deflect attacks, and clear obstacles
• Every extension of the claws is a deliberate choice, not an accident
• Professor Xavier never trained Logan to be ashamed of them — he trained him to understand good judgment
• The sound SNIKT in the halls of Xavier's school means: a guardian is present
