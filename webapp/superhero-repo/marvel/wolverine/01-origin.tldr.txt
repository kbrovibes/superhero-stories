• James Howlett grows up sickly on an estate in Alberta, Canada in the 1880s, befriended by a girl named Rose
• A violent confrontation triggers his mutation — three bone claws burst from each hand for the first time
• James discovers his healing factor, which closes wounds at extraordinary speed
• He flees and takes the name Logan, working in the lumber camps of British Columbia
• Logan's senses sharpen over time — tracking by scent, hearing a whisper from far away, seeing in near-darkness
• He spends decades traveling and fighting in great wars, protecting ordinary soldiers
• His bones and instincts are stronger than any normal human's, though no one fully understands why
• Professor Charles Xavier finds Logan and sees deep goodness behind the rough exterior
• Xavier invites Logan to join the X-Men; Logan accepts, thinking of every person he could not protect before
