Okay — time to meet the toughest, meanest, most frustrating enemy in all of Wolverine's world!

His name is Victor Creed. But you can call him Sabretooth — because that is exactly what he is like. Big. Ferocious. With claws just as sharp as Logan's and a healing factor just like Logan's and a nose that can track a scent through a thunderstorm.

The difference? Logan uses his gifts to protect people. Sabretooth uses his to scare them.

And here is the worst part — Sabretooth has known Logan for longer than almost anyone. He keeps showing up at the worst moments, like a storm cloud that follows you on a picnic. He thinks making Logan angry gives him an advantage. He has never been more wrong.

One day, Sabretooth showed up somewhere he absolutely was NOT welcome — the grounds of Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters! CRASH — through the garden gate he came, snarling and growling, big enough to block out the sunlight.

Students ran! Alarms rang — BWEEEEP BWEEEP! The X-Men came running — but Logan?

Logan was already waiting.

See, Logan had smelled Creed coming from a full minute away. Because Logan's nose is THAT good.

SNIKT! Three claws out on each hand, gleaming in the afternoon light. Logan planted his feet.

And then the fight began.

CLANG! Metal claws hit metal claws with a shriek like two trains colliding! WHAM — they crashed through the rose bushes! CRASH — a section of the garden wall came tumbling down! Back and forth, strength against strength, back and forth!

But here is the thing about Logan and Sabretooth. Their strength is about equal. Their healing is about equal. Their senses are about equal.

What is NOT equal — is what they are fighting FOR.

Sabretooth wanted to WIN. To feel superior. To prove something about himself.

Logan just wanted to protect the kids behind him.

And that made ALL the difference.

Logan pushed. Creed stumbled. Logan pressed forward. Creed growled. Step — by — step — until Sabretooth was back at the tree line, snarling and furious, and then with one final sneer he vanished into the dark forest.

Logan stood at the garden edge. Claws still out. Watching.

Then — SNIKT — in they went. He turned around.

"Is everyone all right?"

They were. Because he was there.

SNIKT!
