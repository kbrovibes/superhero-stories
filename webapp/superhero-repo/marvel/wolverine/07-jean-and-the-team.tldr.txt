• Jean Grey is a powerful telepath and telekinetic — and among Logan's closest friends on the X-Men
• She sees Logan clearly and never uses her telepathy as leverage over him
• Jean is the team's heart: she coordinates everyone silently in a fight, notices when teammates struggle, and shields others without being asked
• Logan and Jean's gifts complement each other — his senses catch what she can't yet perceive; her telepathy gives him information no tracking can
• He goes first into dark situations; she keeps the team connected like a lighthouse
• Their best moment comes during a structural collapse in Manhattan's Flatiron District
• Logan cuts through load-bearing obstructions while Jean holds the compromised sections up with telekinesis
• They move without speaking, reading each other perfectly — and get all thirty trapped people out safely
• Afterward, she puts her hand briefly on his shoulder; no words are needed
