• Professor Xavier holds an annual Gathering at Xavier's School — a reflection on what the year taught everyone
• This year, he asks Logan to speak
• Logan tells the story of an early mission: the X-Men helped Dr. Moira MacTaggert on Muir Island, Scotland, rescuing three researchers trapped by a facility failure
• Cyclops organized instantly; Storm stabilized the weather; Jean Grey linked the team telepathically; Logan went into the most compromised section alone
• What Logan does not say aloud: he was afraid of getting it wrong, of letting his team down
• What he did: followed his training and his team, used his claws only when needed, and got all three people out safely
• Dr. MacTaggert shook his hand in the rain and thanked him; Logan didn't quite know what to do with that kindness
• He tells the students: he spent years believing his only value was enduring damage — the X-Men showed him that's only meaningful because of who it protects
• "It is never the point by itself. The point is the people."
