SNIKT!

That sound. Do you hear it? Three letters, one syllable, and yet it might be the most famous sound in all of comics! That is the sound of Wolverine's claws coming out — and today, we are going to learn everything about them!

Three claws on each hand. They live right there, between the knuckles, coiled like springs in the dark, until Logan decides to extend them. Then — SNIKT! — out they come! Bright, sharp, gleaming silver in any light. Six claws total. Three on the left, three on the right.

But here is something really interesting. Those claws did not start out as metal!

When Logan's powers first appeared, way back on that stormy night in Alberta, Canada — his claws were BONE. Natural bone, as hard as the skeleton they grew from, but still bone. For many years, those bone claws were Logan's secret. He kept them hidden. He used them only when he absolutely had to.

Bone is tough. Bone is impressive. But bone is not ADAMANTIUM.

Adamantium is one of the strongest materials in the entire Marvel Universe — a dense metallic alloy that almost nothing can cut, bend, or break. The Weapon X program coated every single bone in Logan's body with it. His ribs. His spine. His skull. And — yes — every one of those six claws.

When the process was finished, the claws came out silver. SNIKT — gleaming and permanent and nearly indestructible.

Since then, Logan has used those claws to help people in SO many ways. He has sliced clean through a metal door that no key could open! He has cut ropes to free teammates who were stuck. He has held his arms up like metal shields when energy blasts came flying. He once cleared a fallen tree from a mountain road in two minutes flat — faster than any chainsaw!

And every single time — every SNIKT — is a CHOICE.

Logan decides when those claws come out. He decides why. He is never reckless about it. Professor Xavier helped him understand something important: a powerful tool in careful hands is not scary. It is protective.

So the next time you hear SNIKT echoing through the halls of Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters, do not be frightened. That sound means one thing and one thing only.

Wolverine is there. Everyone is safe.

SNIKT!
