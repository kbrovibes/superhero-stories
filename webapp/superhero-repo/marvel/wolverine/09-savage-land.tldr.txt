• The Savage Land is a hidden prehistoric jungle in Antarctica — warm, lush, and full of ancient creatures
• The X-Men deploy after researchers from their network go silent inside the Savage Land
• Wolverine, Cyclops, Jean Grey, and Storm enter the jungle by foot; electronic instruments fail in the volcanic interference
• Logan's senses — tracking by scent, navigating by sound — become the team's only reliable guide
• The researchers are found penned in a canyon by Garokk, an ancient being who rules the central plateau
• Logan plans around the problem: while Storm creates a weather distraction overhead, Logan finds the unguarded eastern access
• He leads the researchers out in single file through near-total darkness, guided by scent and memory
• Jean maintains a psychic veil over the group to mask their movement
• All researchers reach the Blackbird safely at dawn — patient strategy beats head-on force
