Take a deep breath. This is one of the hardest stories in Logan's life — but it is also one of his bravest. Ready?

Somewhere in the frozen mountains of Canada, a secret program called Weapon X had been watching Logan for a very long time. They had heard the stories — a man who never stayed wounded, who never grew old, who seemed almost impossible to stop. And they had a plan.

They captured Logan and brought him to a cold, gray laboratory hidden in the Canadian Rockies. The walls were metal. The air was cold. The scientists were not kind. They had discovered a metal called adamantium — harder and tougher than almost anything else on Earth — and they were going to bond it to Logan's skeleton. Every bone. Every claw.

It was a very hard thing to go through. VERY hard. But here is what those scientists did not understand: Logan was braver than anyone they had ever met. His healing factor — the power in his blood that fixes his body — was working with everything it had. It was the hardest thing Logan had ever endured, and he endured it.

SNIKT! When he finally opened his eyes, his skeleton was wrapped in gleaming adamantium. His three claws on each hand were no longer just bone. They were metal now — bright, sharp, and his. SNIKT, SNIKT, SNIKT — stronger than steel, harder than diamond.

But Weapon X had also scrambled his memories. Logan ran into the wilderness not knowing who he was. He ran through the snow with nothing but his claws and his instincts and the cold Canadian night around him.

Here is the part where the story turns warm again.

Professor Charles Xavier found him. Xavier was a kind man with a school in New York called Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters, and he looked at the wild-eyed, confused man in the snow — and he saw someone worth saving.

With patience, kindness, and time — the X-Men helped Logan put himself back together.

The adamantium stayed. But now it was Logan's — all his, no one else's. He could extend those claws whenever HE chose. He could fight for people HE cared about.

That is what Weapon X never understood. You can change a person's bones. You cannot change their heart.

SNIKT!
