Have you ever heard of a hidden jungle in the middle of Antarctica?

I know, I know — Antarctica is supposed to be nothing but ice and snow! But deep inside a giant volcanic crater, warmed by heat from the earth itself, is a secret place called the Savage Land. And it is PREHISTORIC — towering trees, warm rivers, and creatures that vanished from the rest of the world sixty-five million years ago!

The X-Men had studied it on maps. But maps and reality? Very different things!

A distress beacon went off deep inside the Savage Land — researchers connected to Xavier's network had gone silent. Wolverine, Cyclops, Jean Grey, and Storm deployed from the Blackbird and landed in the jungle.

WHOOOMP — the forest closed around them like a green wall.

Inside the Savage Land, all the electronics stopped working! The volcanic interference scrambled every instrument. Cyclops's tactical maps — useless. The radio — fizzing. The team looked at each other.

Then Logan stepped forward. He took a breath through his nose.

"River's three miles north," he said. "The researchers went this way — two days ago. And something BIG is moving parallel to us, fifty yards east. Don't worry about the east."

His senses were the team's map now. He navigated without hesitation — through rivers, around obstacles, under the canopy where the light barely reached. Cyclops followed. Storm followed. Jean followed.

They found the researchers — but the researchers were TRAPPED! In a riverbed canyon, penned in by the forces of Garokk, an ancient ruler of the central plateau. The canyon walls were too steep and too guarded.

But Logan didn't try to fight through. He thought.

While Storm swooped overhead and called up a dramatic weather event over the plateau — BOOM CRACK — drawing all attention upward — Logan slipped along the canyon rim. Quietly. Carefully. He found the unguarded eastern access in the dark.

Then, in near-total darkness, guided entirely by scent and memory — he led the researchers out. One by one. Single file. Silent.

Jean wrapped them all in a psychic veil — soft, invisible — making them nearly undetectable to anyone who might be looking.

DAWN. The Blackbird. Every researcher. Safe and accounted for!

Logan stood at the jungle's edge, watching the Savage Land stretch away behind them — magnificent and ancient and impossible.

Some problems you solve by charging in. Some problems you solve by going around.

Wolverine knows the difference.

SNIKT — mission complete!
