• Thanos and the Black Order attack the refugee ship carrying Asgardian survivors; Loki and Heimdall are killed
• Thor is left for dead in the vacuum of space; the Guardians of the Galaxy rescue him
• Burning with the desire for vengeance, Thor travels to Nidavellir to forge Stormbreaker with Eitri, Rocket Raccoon, and Groot
• Thor endures the full power of a dying star to hold the forge's iris open long enough to pour the Uru
• Thor's arrival at Wakanda — descending in a pillar of Bifrost light, Stormbreaker crackling — turns the tide of the battle against Thanos's army
• Thor drives Stormbreaker into Thanos's chest — nearly killing him; but grief-driven vengeance causes a fatal mistake
• Thanos taunts: "You should have gone for the head" — and snaps his fingers, wiping out half of all life; Thor failed at the last moment
• The following five years: Thor retreats into grief and isolation in New Asgard, consumed by self-blame
• A conversation with his mother Frigga during the Time Heist helps Thor rediscover himself; he returns to fight in the final battle alongside Captain America and Iron Man
