• Jane Foster — brilliant astrophysicist and Thor's former love — is diagnosed with terminal cancer
• She feels a mysterious pull toward the shattered remains of Mjolnir, left in the ruins of Asgard
• When Jane reaches out to the hammer, it reforms itself and grants her the power of Thor; she becomes the Mighty Thor
• Each transformation temporarily holds the cancer at bay — but the magic purges chemotherapy from her system, allowing the cancer to advance faster
• Jane does not stop; she believes the world needs a Thor more than she needs to be saved
• She brings her scientific mind to the role — using Mjolnir's properties in inventive ways no one has seen before
• Thor, initially shocked, watches with growing admiration and deep love; he sees in Jane the truest definition of worthiness
• The final battle: against Gorr the God Butcher; Jane uses the last of her strength to shatter the Necrosword — knowing the transformation will kill her
• Jane dies in Thor's arms; her spirit ascends to Valhalla; her legacy is permanently written into Asgardian history
