• After Ragnarok and the Infinity War, Asgardian survivors establish New Asgard — a small coastal fishing village in Tønsberg, Norway
• Tønsberg has ancient Norse history; the Asgardians blend their ancient traditions with modern Earth life
• King Valkyrie manages day-to-day leadership while Thor retreats into grief, isolation, and inactivity — a god lost in sadness
• Thor gains weight and withdraws from heroism; the village grows in strength around him — a testament to the Asgardian spirit
• New Asgard becomes a popular tourist destination; humans and Asgardians live together in peaceful coexistence
• The Asgardians contribute to their new home using advanced technology and their unique abilities
• Thor eventually finds his way back to purpose and passes the crown to Valkyrie, recognizing her as the leader this new world needs
• New Asgard proves that home is wherever you find the courage to start again — the legacy of Odin's realm lives in the people, not the place
