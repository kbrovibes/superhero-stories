• Mjolnir: forged in the heart of a dying star by the dwarves of Nidavellir from mystical Uru metal — a weapon of worthiness as much as power
• Mjolnir's enchantment allows only the worthy to lift it; it channels the raw energy of the storm
• Hela destroys Mjolnir with one hand during her return; Thor must find a new path
• Thor travels to Nidavellir with Rocket Raccoon and Groot to forge a "kings' weapon" capable of killing a god
• Eitri — dwarf king, sole survivor of Thanos's purge of Nidavellir — is the only one who can forge the weapon
• To pour the molten Uru, Thor must hold open the dying star's iris with his own body — enduring lethal levels of stellar energy
• Stormbreaker: a massive battle-axe, even more powerful than Mjolnir — it can summon the Bifrost to travel across the universe
• Groot provides the handle: reaching into the molten metal and severing his own arm to bind the axe together — the handle of Stormbreaker is made from Groot's sacrifice
• Stormbreaker proves the only weapon capable of truly wounding Thanos when Thor arrives at Wakanda
