• Hela — Goddess of Death and Odin's firstborn child — was once his executioner, commanding his armies as they conquered the Nine Realms
• When Odin turned toward peace, Hela refused to stop; Odin imprisoned her and erased her from Asgardian history
• With Odin's death, her prison's seal breaks; Hela returns and destroys Mjolnir with one hand — the first time anyone has done so
• She exiles Thor and Loki to the distant planet Sakaar; she marches into Asgard and decimates its army
• Hela resurrects an undead legion of Asgardian warriors and her giant wolf Fenris; she is virtually invincible while connected to the realm
• Thor, having lost his hammer, discovers his power comes from within — not from Mjolnir; he becomes a living bolt of lightning
• Thor realizes he cannot defeat Hela while she draws strength from Asgard; he makes the hardest choice a king can make
• Thor orchestrates Ragnarok: the resurrection of the fire demon Surtur, whose massive blade destroys Asgard — ending Hela's power and fulfilling prophecy
• Thor escapes with the Asgardian survivors on a ship: "Asgard is not a place; it's a people"
