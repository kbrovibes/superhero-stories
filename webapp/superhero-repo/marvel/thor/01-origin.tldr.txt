• Thor — son of Odin the All-Father and Queen Frigga — is raised as a prince and warrior in Asgard, the golden realm at the center of the Nine Realms
• His companions are the warrior Sif and the Warriors Three; he views war as a game and power as a birthright
• Defying Odin's orders, Thor leads an unauthorized attack on Jotunheim, home of the Frost Giants — threatening to restart an ancient war
• Odin punishes Thor: strips him of his power and armor and exiles him to Earth (Midgard), casting the worthiness enchantment on Mjolnir
• Mjolnir's enchantment: "Whosoever holds this hammer, if he be worthy, shall possess the power of Thor"
• Thor lands in the New Mexico desert as a powerless mortal; he meets astrophysicist Jane Foster, who helps him understand the human world
• The Destroyer — a massive Asgardian automaton sent by Loki — attacks a New Mexico town; Thor offers his own life to protect the people
• Thor's selflessness makes him worthy — Mjolnir returns to his hand; he destroys the Destroyer and earns his power back
