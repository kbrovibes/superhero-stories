• Wanda's red Hex over Westview couldn't last forever — the walls began to flicker and dissolve at the edges
• Pink Vision found Wanda in the living room of 2800 Sherwood Drive and gently took her hand
• Tommy and Billy were upstairs in pajamas, watching from the top of the stairs
• Earlier in the week, the boys had asked why Mom seemed so sad sometimes
• Vision answered with one of the kindest sentences in any Marvel story: "What is grief, if not love persevering?"
• Grief = the sadness of missing someone you love; persevering = it keeps going
• Vision was saying: being sad about losing someone is not weakness — it's just love with nowhere to go
• Wanda and Vision said goodbye holding hands: "We have said goodbye before — so we'll say hello again"
• The Hex came down; Tommy, Billy, and Pink Vision faded gently — but the sentence stayed with Wanda forever
