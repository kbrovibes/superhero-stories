• Vision started as ingredients in three different parts of Stark Tower: Helen Cho's cradle (the body), the Mind Stone (the spark), and J.A.R.V.I.S.'s scattered code (the soul)
• The cradle, brought up from Seoul, was growing a vibranium-and-synthetic-skin body with an empty socket in the forehead
• Bruce Banner held the Mind Stone in a glass case after the Avengers took it back from Ultron
• Tony Stark wanted to upload J.A.R.V.I.S. into the body instead of letting Ultron have it
• Steve Rogers said no; Wanda Maximoff said no even louder and tried to stop them
• Tony and Bruce did it anyway — the Mind Stone clicked into the socket, the code began uploading
• Wanda blasted the cradle backward in protest; then Thor arrived through the roof
• Thor's Mjolnir lightning poured into the cradle in a white-hot column — KRAKABOOOOM
• Vision rose from the cradle: pink and silver, green cape, glowing yellow gem in his forehead — and his first words were "I am"
