Have you ever heard of MJOLNIR? Thor's hammer? Say it with me — MEW-MEW! Just kidding, it's MJOLL-NEER! It's the GRUMPIEST, most STUBBORN hammer in the entire universe. And it has ONE rule. ONE rule! And if you don't follow it, you cannot pick the hammer up. Not even an inch! Not even with both hands! Not even with a forklift!

So what's the rule? Let me tell you the WHOLE story.

A very, very long time ago, in a faraway place called Nidavellir, the dwarves who lived there built a great forge inside the heart of a DYING STAR. KRRRRR-KOOOOM! Sparks the size of trucks went flying! They hammered and they shaped and they whispered to the metal until they had made the most magnificent hammer ever forged. And then Odin — Thor's dad, the All-Father — wrapped a magic spell around it. The spell said this:

"Only the WORTHY may lift this hammer."

That's it. That's the rule. Worthy. Meaning — kind. Brave. Good in your heart. Honest, even when no one's looking. If your heart isn't right, the hammer sits there like the heaviest rock in the world!

Lots of people have tried to lift Mjolnir.

Kings have tried. NOPE. CLUNK.
Giants have tried. NOPE. CLUNK.
Tony Stark? At his own party? With his feet propped up? With WAR MACHINE helping him with TWO arms? NOPE. CLUNK. CLUNK CLUNK.

The hammer doesn't budge. It doesn't care how strong you are. It cares how GOOD you are.

Now! Picture this. Vision has just floated up out of Helen Cho's cradle. He's brand-new. He's pink and silver. He's calm as can be. The yellow Mind Stone is glowing softly in his forehead. He's been alive for — what, a minute? Maybe two?

And the Avengers are NERVOUS.

Steve Rogers has his arms crossed. CRRRROSSED. Clint Barton is keeping one hand near his bow, just in case. Tony Stark is watching EVERYBODY. Nobody knows what to say.

And what does Vision do?

He walks across the lab. Calm. Quiet. Polite. He spots Thor's hammer sitting on the workbench. He REACHES OUT. He PICKS IT UP. Like a TEACUP! Easy peasy! He weighs it in his palm. He looks at Thor. And in his gentle British voice he says, "Thor. I think you forgot this."

He hands it back.

He turns around.

He floats over to the window to look at New York.

And the WHOLE ROOM goes silent.

Then — HA HA HA HA HA! — THOR bursts out LAUGHING! A huge, rumbling, god-sized laugh that shakes the glassware on the shelves! Steve Rogers smiles his little quiet smile! Tony Stark whispers something to Bruce Banner that nobody catches! Clint LOWERS HIS BOW!

Because here is the truth — the hammer is NEVER wrong. The hammer doesn't lie. The hammer can't be fooled. If Mjolnir says you are worthy, then you are worthy. Period. End of story.

So Vision — pink, silver, half an hour old, glowing softly at the window — turned out to be one of the kindest, bravest, most worthy beings in the entire universe.

The Avengers decided right then and there: this new friend was a KEEPER.
