There is a yellow gem. It's about the size of a large marble. It glows with a warm golden light. And it is one of the most powerful things that has ever existed in the entire universe — older than any star, older than almost anything! This is the Mind Stone. And it lived right in Vision's forehead!

How did it get there? Well, the Mind Stone traveled a LONG and winding road before it found Vision. It had once lived inside Loki's blue scepter during the Battle of New York — ZAP ZAP ZAP! Before that, it was held by strange creatures called the Chitauri in the dark of deep space. And before that? Nobody quite remembers. The Mind Stone simply existed, glowing softly wherever it rested, waiting.

Then HYDRA's Baron Strucker used the Mind Stone's energy on two brave twins named Wanda and Pietro Maximoff — and it unlocked extraordinary gifts in them both! WHOOOSH! And when Tony Stark and Bruce Banner connected it to a Vibranium body filled with J.A.R.V.I.S.'s code... KRAKABOOOOM! It sparked Vision's very first heartbeat! The stone didn't just power Vision — it STARTED him.

Because of the Mind Stone, Vision could shoot a golden energy beam from his forehead powerful enough to carve through almost anything! He could think ten thousand thoughts at the same time! He could walk through solid walls like they were made of fog — WHOOOOSH, right through! He could make himself as light as a feather or as heavy as a mountain! He was, in a real sense, made of mind.

The stone also made Vision the most hunted being on Earth. Thanos wanted all six Infinity Stones for his gauntlet. Vision knew this. He carried it with complete, steady calm.

Tony Stark once asked Vision quietly: did he ever wish the stone had been in someone else's forehead instead? Vision thought for a long time. His yellow gem glowed gently.

"No," he said. "I am glad it is mine."

And that is how Vision carried the most powerful thing in the universe — not with pride or fear or anger — but with the quiet, certain understanding that some things are worth carrying carefully, for as long as you possibly can.
