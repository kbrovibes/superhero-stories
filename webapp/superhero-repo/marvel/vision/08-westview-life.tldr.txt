• After Vision died, Wanda Maximoff's grief accidentally wrapped a magical red Hex around the whole town of Westview, New Jersey
• Inside the Hex, time worked differently — the streets became a black-and-white sitcom and Vision came back to life
• The Hex moved through the decades like fast-forward: 1950s, 1960s, 1970s, on and on
• Vision wore suits and ties, rode a bicycle in a sweater vest, and pretended to be Mr. Hart, a quiet office worker
• They had twin boys — Tommy (fast) and Billy (a little bit of magic) — who grew from babies to ten-year-olds in one afternoon
• Vision read bedtime stories, flipped pancakes, and gave the boys serious advice about kindness
• He also slowly noticed something was wrong — neighbors smiled too brightly, clocks ticked too smoothly
• On the porch swing, he gently asked Wanda, "What is outside Westview?" She did not have a good answer
• Outside, S.W.O.R.D. and Monica Rambeau were trying to figure out the Hex — but inside, Vision got a precious bittersweet week of being a husband and dad
