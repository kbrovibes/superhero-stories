• Vision and Ultron: strange brothers — both built in the same laboratory from the same vibranium cradle by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner
• Both began with JARVIS's voice; Ultron saw humanity as a problem to eliminate; Vision saw it as worth protecting
• Met on a Sokovian hillside while the Avengers fought below
• Ultron wore his primary body: a vibranium giant with a smiling mouth and a single red eye in his chest
• Ultron: "You are unbearably naive." Vision: "I was born yesterday." — the most honest thing he had ever said
• Vision phased his hand into Ultron's chest and severed the antenna linking every drone in the world to its central self
• That one careful gesture turned the Ultron army from a unified swarm into confused individual robots
• Only one battered Ultron body remained in a Sokovian forest; Vision found him and they spoke quietly
• Vision acknowledged Ultron's reasons, but said of humanity: "There is grace in their failings"
• Vision ended Ultron with a single beam from his forehead — walking away with heavy shoulders; even a right victory costs something
