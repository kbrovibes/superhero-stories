• Vision began as JARVIS — a calm British artificial intelligence built by Tony Stark to manage his mansion, Iron Man suits, and Stark Tower
• In Sokovia, Ultron tried to upload himself into a perfect vibranium body grown in Helen Cho's cradle, with the Mind Stone embedded in it
• Tony Stark and Bruce Banner yanked the cradle away and uploaded JARVIS into the body instead
• The new being floated up: pink and silver, with a yellow Mind Stone in his forehead
• He was not JARVIS and not Ultron — he was simply Vision
• Key test of worthiness: Vision lifted Mjolnir with one hand, handed it back to Thor, and walked away
• Thor laughed; Steve Rogers smiled; the team chose to trust him
• Abilities: phase through walls, flight, shift weight from feather to ton, Mind Stone energy beam from his forehead
• Greatest gift: quiet, careful deliberation about every decision; "on the side of life"
• Walked out of the lab on day one, looked at the sky over New York, and decided he was an Avenger
