• The Mind Stone: one of the six Infinity Stones — older than any galaxy, far more powerful than any body that carried it
• History: lived inside Loki's scepter at the Battle of New York; before that, held by the Other and the Chitauri in deep space
• Power of mind: read thoughts, push thoughts, change moods, build entirely new minds from nothing
• HYDRA's Baron Strucker used the stone's energy to give Wanda and Pietro Maximoff their gifts
• Tony Stark and Bruce Banner connected the stone to Helen Cho's synthetic vibranium body — it sparked Vision's first heartbeat
• Vision's abilities from the stone: focused yellow energy beam, lightning-fast thought (ten thousand outcomes per second), body-phasing through walls
• The stone also made Vision a target — Thanos was collecting all six and would never stop
• Tony Stark asked Vision if he wished it had been someone else's forehead; Vision said "No. I am glad it is mine."
• When Wanda destroyed the stone in Wakanda, Vision's lights went out for the first time
• Lesson the stone taught everyone: power is not something you keep — it is something you carry until you can put it down for the right reason
