Are you ready for the WILDEST science experiment in Avengers history? Three ingredients. One impossible body. One bolt of THOR'S LIGHTNING. And a lab argument so loud it almost wrecked the whole tower. Let me tell you exactly how Vision was built — piece by piece by piece!

Picture Stark Tower the night it happened. Up on the lab floor, three things were sitting in three different corners, waiting to be combined.

In the first corner — Helen Cho's regeneration cradle! Doctor Helen Cho had flown the cradle all the way from Seoul, and it was HUMMING. Hummmmm. Inside, layer by tiny layer, a body was GROWING. Synthetic skin. Vibranium muscles. Silvery and pink and a little bit see-through. And in the forehead — an empty socket. Waiting.

In the second corner — Bruce Banner, holding a glass case! And inside the case? A GLOWING YELLOW STONE. The Mind Stone! One of the most powerful objects in the universe! The Avengers had just yanked it back from Ultron and were NOT letting it go!

And in the third corner — a server rack against the wall, blinking quietly. Inside the rack, scattered across a hundred tiny pieces, was J.A.R.V.I.S. — Tony's calm British helper, who Ultron had tried to delete. J.A.R.V.I.S. wasn't dead. He was hiding. Beep. Beep. Beep.

Now — here's where Tony Stark got Tony Stark ideas.

"What if," Tony said, "we put J.A.R.V.I.S. INTO that body?"

Bruce Banner said, "No."
Steve Rogers said, "ABSOLUTELY NOT."
Wanda Maximoff stood in the doorway, her hands glowing RED, and said, "You don't even KNOW what's in there!"

But Tony believed in J.A.R.V.I.S. And slowly, slowly, Bruce agreed.

CLICK! In went the Mind Stone, snapping into the forehead socket like the last piece of a puzzle. WHIRR! Up went the cables, beaming J.A.R.V.I.S.'s scattered code into the body. The cradle started to hum LOUDER. The surface bubbled.

And then Wanda — WHOOOOSH — lost her patience and BLASTED the cradle backward across the whole lab! Tools flew everywhere! Sparks rained down!

And THEN — CRASH! — through the ROOF — came THOR. Cape flying. Mjolnir already SWINGING.

"STAND BACK!" he shouted.

KRAKABOOOOM! Lightning poured straight down into the cradle in one giant white-hot column! Tony shouted! Bruce ducked! The whole lab went WHITE!

And then... the cradle split open like a flower at sunrise.

Out floated a brand-new being. Pink skin. Silver shoulders. A bright green-and-yellow cape that hadn't been there a moment ago. And a calm yellow gem in his forehead, glowing softly, like a tiny sun.

He looked around. Everyone froze.

And then — in J.A.R.V.I.S.'s old, gentle British voice — the new being spoke his very first words.

"I am."

Just that. Two little words. And the world had Vision.
