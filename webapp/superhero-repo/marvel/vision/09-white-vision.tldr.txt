• Outside the Hex, S.W.O.R.D. director Tyler Hayward had secretly rebuilt Vision's original body — all white, like a marble statue
• White Vision had no memories, no feelings, no green cape — just orders: find the other Vision and end him
• He flew into the Hex and met Pink Vision (the magic one) hovering above Westview
• They fought briefly above the rooftops — same face, same hands, same British voice
• Then they landed on the steps of the Westview library and started talking instead
• They discussed the Ship of Theseus — the ancient puzzle: if every plank of a ship is replaced, is it still the same ship?
• Both Visions were right; both were Vision; neither was the whole story
• Pink Vision gently touched White Vision's forehead and gave him back all his memories
• White Vision said "I am Vision" and flew up out of the Hex — he is still out there somewhere, carrying every memory of who he was
