• Scott Summers and his brother Alex survive a plane crash over Alaska when their parents give up the only parachute to save the boys
• Scott ends up alone in an orphanage in Omaha, Nebraska, separated from Alex
• As a teenager, Scott discovers his eyes fire uncontrollable red optic blasts that can shatter walls
• Only lenses made of ruby-quartz can hold the energy back — Scott wears a curved visor every waking moment
• Professor Charles Xavier finds Scott and invites him to the Xavier School for Gifted Youngsters in Westchester, New York
• Scott trains in the Danger Room, learning to aim and control his optic blasts with growing precision
• Xavier asks Scott to lead the first team of X-Men — Scott takes the name Cyclops and accepts
• Cyclops becomes the X-Men's steady, disciplined field commander — the hero his teammates count on when things get hard
