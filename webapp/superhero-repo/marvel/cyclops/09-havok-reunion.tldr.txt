• After years of searching old records and forwarding addresses, Scott finally locates his brother Alex in Pasadena, California
• Alex grew up with a good family, is confident and easygoing, and carries a very different power: concussive energy rings that spread outward in wide waves
• He takes the name Havok
• They meet outside a coffee shop on a gray morning — two tall young men with the same jaw, one in a red visor, one squinting in the sun
• Alex says "You look like Dad"; Scott says "So do you"
• They talk for four hours, filling in each other's stories piece by piece, like restoring something fragile
• Scott invites Alex to the Xavier School; Alex enrolls and becomes a field member of the X-Men
• In the field, Scott's tight focused beam and Alex's wide energy rings can bracket enemies together — complementary powers built on the choice to be brothers
