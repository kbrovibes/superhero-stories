• Apocalypse — real name En Sabah Nur — is an ancient mutant born in Egypt who believes only the strongest deserve to survive
• He collects powerful mutants and turns them into his Four Horsemen, warriors bound to his service
• Apocalypse targets New York City; Cyclops studies everything about him and forms a careful plan
• The X-Men meet the Horsemen on Manhattan rooftops in a blazing battle
• Cyclops directs his teammates with short clear commands while firing precise optic blasts
• Apocalypse appears on his armored transport — twelve feet tall, blue-skinned, ancient and imposing
• Cyclops holds him off with a sustained blast while Jean Grey and Storm combine their powers
• The combined assault forces Apocalypse to retreat; every X-Man makes it home safely
