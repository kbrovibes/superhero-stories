• Scott's ruby-quartz visor is not just protective gear — without it, his optic blasts fire continuously and uncontrolled
• Ruby quartz is a rare dense crystal that absorbs concussive energy rather than transmitting it
• Xavier found a deposit in Madripoor and had craftsman Henry Larner spend three weeks shaping the lenses
• The visor has a slider on the right temple: fully down = tight pencil-beam for precision; fully up = wide dispersed spread; middle = fist-width power blast
• Scott has never seen full color — through ruby quartz, every sky looks amber and every face is tinged with rose
• Jean Grey describes blue to him: cool and wide and open, like a long slow breath
• Scott says that sounds nice — and means it without sadness; ruby-quartz has always been his world
• The visor is the first thing he touches each morning — a symbol not of limitation but of discipline and choice
