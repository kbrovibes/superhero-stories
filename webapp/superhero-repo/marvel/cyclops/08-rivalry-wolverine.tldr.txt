• Wolverine — Logan — arrives at the Xavier School: stubborn, instinct-driven, no patience for plans
• He calls Scott "Summers" with just enough emphasis to mean something; Scott responds "Logan" with none
• They disagree on everything tactical: Logan reacts fast on the move; Scott maps every exit before entering
• Their arguments fill training rooms and hallways — other X-Men learn to be elsewhere for the first ten minutes
• At the Weapon X facility in Alberta, the corridor doors start sealing ahead of schedule, trapping Scott, Logan, and two rescued mutants
• Logan spots the locking pin — a ten-centimeter target sixty meters away — and says "Tell me you can hit that"
• Scott narrows his visor to minimum aperture and fires — CRACK — the pin shears clean, the door stays open
• Logan sprints the kids out; they escape with four seconds to spare; outside, Logan says "Good shot" — from Wolverine, enormous praise
