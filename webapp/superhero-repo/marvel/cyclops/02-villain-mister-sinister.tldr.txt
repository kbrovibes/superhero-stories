• Mister Sinister — real name Nathaniel Essex — is an ancient scientist obsessed with mutant genetics who has studied the Summers family for years
• Sinister deploys Sentinel robots against the X-Men to capture their genetic material for study
• He sends his team the Marauders to intercept the X-Men in the Morlock Tunnels beneath New York City
• Cyclops leads his teammates through the dark tunnels, keeping everyone moving and focused
• The X-Men confront Sinister in an abandoned laboratory — he wears a dark costume with a diamond mark on his forehead
• Cyclops fires a full-force optic blast at Sinister, but Sinister simply absorbs the energy and smiles
• Jean Grey hits Sinister with a telekinetic wave from behind, sending him crashing through a wall
• The X-Men escape; Cyclops stays up late in the Danger Room, preparing for the next encounter
