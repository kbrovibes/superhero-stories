• The Xavier School for Gifted Youngsters is a manor house in Westchester, New York, built by Professor Xavier for young mutants who had nowhere else to go
• Xavier is a telepath who spent years locating gifted young people and offering them education and community
• Scott arrives first and helps design the curriculum and run the Danger Room training sessions
• The Danger Room is an underground chamber where students practice their powers against simulated scenarios
• Scott learns to plan around each person's strengths, not the mission's demands — a lesson he keeps for life
• Outside training: Hank McCoy debates literature at breakfast, Ororo's garden is always perfect, Bobby Drake leaves ice sculptures in the hall
• Scott walks the grounds at dusk and understands what Xavier built: not just a team, but a community
• The heroism is a consequence of the people — good training and genuine friendship together
