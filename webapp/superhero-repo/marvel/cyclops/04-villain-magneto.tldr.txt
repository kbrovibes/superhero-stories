• Magneto opens the dome, surprised someone arrived unarmed
• For two hours Scott listens to Magneto's history: the losses, the loneliness, the logic that led him here
• Scott disagrees with the dome plainly, but tells Magneto that Professor Xavier never stopped believing he could come back
• Magneto goes still at those words; his magnetic field surges once, then quiets
• Magneto lowers the dome — not because he was defeated, but because someone treated him as more than a problem
• The people of Genosha walk out into sunlight; Scott and Magneto stand side by side — not friends yet, but something more than enemies
