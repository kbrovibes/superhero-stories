• Rocket and Groot were partners long before they met the rest of the team — a bond built over years of bounty work
• Rocket is guarded and sharp with everyone; around Groot he is observably gentler without ever saying so
• On Berhert station, they go off alone to find an ignition coil for the damaged Milano
• They return after loud crashes with the part — and Groot brings Rocket a small purple flower, which Rocket keeps on the dashboard for weeks
• During the Kyln breakout, Groot holds a massive containment gate alone so Rocket can escape
• After the Dark Aster crash on Xandar, Rocket finds Groot's tiny sprout in the rubble and holds it quietly
• Drax places a hand on Rocket's shoulder and walks away — Rocket doesn't pull back
• The pair's friendship quietly teaches the whole team what loyalty looks like
