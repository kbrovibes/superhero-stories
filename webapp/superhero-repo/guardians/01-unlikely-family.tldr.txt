• Peter Quill — the self-named Star-Lord — steals the Orb from the ancient planet Morag, not knowing it holds the Power Stone
• Ronan, a fanatical Kree warlord working for Thanos, wants the Orb for world-destroying power
• On Xandar, home of the Nova Corps, Gamora, Rocket, and Groot all converge on Peter in a chaotic brawl
• The Nova Corps arrests all four; in prison they meet Drax, a tattooed strongman who lost his family to Ronan
• Rocket devises a breakout using a guard's prosthetic leg and the prison watchtower controls
• The five escape together aboard the Milano, Peter's battered personal ship
• Despite mistrust and very different personalities, they act like a team almost without meaning to
• The galaxy is hunting them, the Dark Aster looms behind them, and the Orb is still in Peter's satchel
