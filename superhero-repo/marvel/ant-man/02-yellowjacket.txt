Ant-Man often finds himself facing opponents who use similar technology for the wrong reasons. One of his most persistent rivals is Darren Cross, who created the Yellowjacket suit. Cross was once a student of Dr. Hank Pym, but he became obsessed with the Pym Particle technology. He felt jealous that Dr. Pym wouldn't share the secrets of the shrinking suit with him, and this jealousy led him to create a dangerous version of his own.

The Yellowjacket suit is designed for combat, equipped with powerful energy blasters and mechanical stingers. Unlike Scott Lang, who uses the Pym Particles to help people, Darren Cross wanted to use them to create a tiny army for his own gain. The suit itself was often unstable, causing Darren’s mind to become confused and his actions to become more unpredictable. This made him a very dangerous opponent for Ant-Man to handle.

In their many encounters, Ant-Man has to use his cleverness and his tiny friends to outsmart Yellowjacket. Since their suits have similar powers, the battles often involve shrinking and growing at high speeds. Ant-Man uses his ability to communicate with ants to create obstacles for Cross, while Yellowjacket relies on his suit's advanced weaponry. These fights are like a high-speed game of tag where the players are smaller than a blade of grass.

Despite the power of the Yellowjacket suit, Scott Lang always manages to prevail by staying calm and focused. He knows that being a hero isn't about having the best weapons, but about using your heart and mind to do what's right. By protecting the world from the misuse of Pym Particles, Ant-Man ensures that this incredible technology is only used to build a better future for everyone, including his daughter.
