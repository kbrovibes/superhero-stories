Stephen Strange was once a world-renowned but incredibly arrogant neurosurgeon whose life changed forever after a tragic car accident severely damaged his hands. Unable to perform the delicate surgery that defined his identity, he spent his entire fortune seeking a experimental cure. His desperate search eventually led him to the mysterious enclave of Kamar-Taj in Kathmandu, Nepal, where he encountered the Ancient One. Instead of physical healing, he was shown the vast world of magic and the existence of the mystic arts.

Under the patient but firm guidance of the Ancient One and the disciplined librarian named Wong, Stephen began his rigorous training. He learned that our world was protected by a mystical shield and that he had a much greater purpose than his own ego. He practiced complex spells for countless hours, learning to move his hands in precise patterns to manifest glowing energy shields, magical whips, and protective runes. It was a difficult transition from a man of science to a master of sorcery, but his disciplined mind and photographic memory helped him excel faster than anyone expected.

Eventually, Stephen proved his bravery and was entrusted with powerful relics. He became the guardian of the New York Sanctum Sanctorum, a magical house that serves as a vital gateway to other dimensions. He donned the sentient Cloak of Levitation, which chose him as its master, and began wearing the Eye of Agamotto around his neck. Through courage and wisdom, Stephen Strange rose to become the Sorcerer Supreme. He no longer used his hands to save lives in a hospital; instead, he used his powerful magic to protect the entire Earth from terrifying mystical threats. His journey shows that even when one path is blocked, a magical new destiny may be waiting.
