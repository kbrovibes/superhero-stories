It was a quiet afternoon at the training facility when a friendly debate broke out between Thor, the God of Thunder, and the Incredible Hulk. They were discussing who among them possessed the greatest raw strength. Tony Stark, always one to encourage a bit of competition, suggested a series of tests to settle the matter once and for all. The rest of the team gathered around, eager to see two of Earth's mightiest heroes push their limits in a safe environment.

The first challenge involved lifting massive vibranium blocks that Captain America had helped secure. Thor went first, his muscles tensing as he hoisted the enormous weight above his head with a triumphant shout. Not to be outdone, the Hulk stepped forward and lifted two blocks at once, his green skin practically glowing with effort. The ground shook slightly under their feet, but the facility's reinforced flooring held firm. Both heroes grinned, clearly enjoying the chance to show off their power.

Next, they moved to a specialized punching machine designed by Tony to measure kinetic force. Thor swung Mjolnir with a crackle of lightning, sending the meter into the red zone. The Hulk followed with a massive fist, striking the pad with such force that the machine let out a loud beep before resetting. It was impossible to declare a clear winner, as both results were off the charts. The team cheered, impressed by the incredible display of strength from both teammates.

In the end, Steve Rogers stepped in to remind them that while strength was important, it was how they used it together that mattered most. Thor and the Hulk bumped fists, acknowledging each other's incredible abilities with mutual respect. The contest ended in a tie, which seemed to satisfy everyone. They headed back to the common area, laughing and sharing stories, reminded once again that their greatest strength lay in their unity and friendship.
