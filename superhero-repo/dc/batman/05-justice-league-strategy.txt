When the world faces big challenges that are too much for one hero to handle, Batman joins his friends in the Justice League. While others might have super-strength or the ability to fly, Batman brings his incredible detective work and tactical planning to the team. He is often called the "World's Greatest Detective" because he can solve almost any mystery by looking for small clues that others might miss.

Before the Justice League goes on a mission, Batman spends hours studying the situation. He looks at maps, analyzes data, and thinks about every possible outcome. He helps the team decide who should go where and what the best strategy is to keep everyone safe. His teammates, like Superman and Wonder Woman, deeply respect his advice because they know he always has a well-thought-out plan ready to go.

Batman also uses his technology to help the League communicate. He designed the team's communication devices and often manages their headquarters, the Watchtower, which orbits high above the Earth. From there, he can see everything that is happening and guide his friends to where they are needed most. His ability to stay calm and focused under pressure makes him a natural leader and a vital part of the group.

The teamwork between Batman and the Justice League shows that everyone has something unique to offer. Even without superpowers, Batman's intelligence and dedication make him just as important as the strongest members. By working together and using Batman's smart strategies, the Justice League is able to protect the entire world and ensure that peace and justice prevail for everyone, everywhere.
