Deep in the hidden jungles of Africa lies a magnificent place called Gorilla City. This is no ordinary city, as it is home to a society of highly intelligent gorillas who have developed advanced technology. The most famous and powerful among them is Gorilla Grodd. Unlike other gorillas, Grodd was changed by a mysterious meteorite that crashed near his home, giving him a super-intelligent mind and incredible mental powers.

Gorilla Grodd is not just strong; he is one of the smartest beings on the planet. He can communicate using his mind and can even control the thoughts of others if they aren't careful. Grodd believes that gorillas are meant to lead the world because of their strength and intelligence. This often brings him into conflict with The Flash, as Grodd sometimes tries to use his technology and mental powers to take over Central City.

When Grodd arrives, he is a formidable opponent. He is much stronger than a human and uses his mental abilities to create illusions or move objects with his mind. The Flash has to use his super-speed to stay one step ahead of Grodd's powerful thoughts. Barry Allen knows that he cannot just rely on his speed to defeat Grodd; he also has to use his own scientific mind to find ways to block Grodd's mental signals.

Despite his tough exterior, Grodd is a complex leader who cares about the future of his city. He is a master of science and has built many amazing machines in Gorilla City. The Flash often tries to show Grodd that humans and gorillas can work together for a better world. Their encounters are a test of both physical speed and mental strength, proving that being a hero requires a big heart and a sharp mind.
