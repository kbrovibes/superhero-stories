Long ago, in ancient times, the Wizard chose a different champion named Teth-Adam to be the guardian of magic. Teth-Adam was given the same incredible powers as Shazam, but he became consumed by his own strength and used his magic to rule with an iron fist. Because of this, the Wizard was forced to take away his powers and exile him. Now known as Black Adam, he has returned to the modern world, believing that he is the only one worthy of the Wizard's gifts. This has created a fierce and ancient rivalry between him and Billy Batson.

Black Adam and Shazam are very similar in their abilities, as they both draw power from magical sources. However, their approaches are very different. While Billy uses his powers to protect and help others, Black Adam believes in using force to maintain order as he sees fit. Their encounters are often spectacular competitions of magic, with lightning bolts flashing across the sky and the ground shaking from their immense strength. Shazam must use all of his courage to stand up to the experience and determination of his ancient predecessor.

During their battles, Black Adam often tries to convince Shazam that being a hero is a waste of time and that he should join him in ruling the world. But Billy, with his pure heart, knows that true power comes from helping others, not controlling them. He uses his wisdom to find ways to outsmart Black Adam, often turning the ancient warrior's own pride against him. Shazam understands that he is not just fighting for himself, but for the legacy of the Wizard and the safety of everyone.

Despite their differences, Shazam often hopes that Black Adam can find his way back to being a true protector. He sees the potential for good in the ancient champion and tries to show him the value of kindness and teamwork. However, Black Adam's stubbornness makes this a difficult task. Shazam remains a steadfast guardian, always ready to defend the world from Black Adam's misguided plans. Their rivalry continues to be a legendary tale of two champions, one from the past and one from the present, fighting for the future of magic.
