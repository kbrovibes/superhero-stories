Arthur Curry has a brother named Orm, who is also a prince of Atlantis. While Arthur loves both the land and the sea, Orm believes that Atlantis should be the most powerful kingdom in the world and stay hidden away from everyone else. This difference in ideas often leads to a rivalry between the two brothers. When Orm takes on the name Ocean Master, he uses his Atlantean strength and magical tools to try and take control of the throne.

Ocean Master is a very skilled warrior who wears a majestic purple and silver suit with a flowing cape. He carries a powerful trident of his own, which can control the currents of the ocean and create massive whirlpools. He is very proud of his Atlantean heritage and wants to make sure his people are safe, but he sometimes forgets that the surface world and the ocean need to work together to be truly strong.

Aquaman and Ocean Master often have to settle their disagreements through tests of strength and wisdom. Arthur tries to teach Orm that being a king means caring for everyone, not just those who live underwater. They might have a race through a field of glowing jellyfish or a challenge to see who can help a stranded whale the fastest. These encounters show that even brothers can have very different ways of looking at the world.

Deep down, Arthur still cares for his brother and hopes that they can one day rule together in peace. He knows that Orm is a brave leader who truly loves Atlantis. By standing his ground and being a fair king, Aquaman shows Ocean Master that kindness and cooperation are more powerful than any trident. Their story is a reminder that family is important, even when you don't always agree on everything.
