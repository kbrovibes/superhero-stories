In the vast history of the Green Lantern Corps, there was once a great leader named Sinestro. He was known for being one of the most skilled and powerful Lanterns, and he was even the one who trained Hal Jordan. However, Sinestro began to believe that the best way to keep order was through fear instead of willpower. He wanted to control people to make sure they followed the rules, which went against the Green Lantern mission of peace.

Because of his actions, the Guardians of the Universe asked Sinestro to leave the Green Lantern Corps. Instead of giving up, Sinestro found a new source of power: a yellow ring that is fueled by fear. He created his own group called the Sinestro Corps, and he became a major rival to Hal Jordan and the other Green Lanterns. The color yellow is the opposite of the green energy of willpower, creating a spectacular rivalry across the stars.

Hal Jordan and Sinestro have had many epic battles in space. When they fight, the sky is filled with bright green and yellow light. Sinestro uses his yellow ring to create scary constructs designed to frighten his opponents. Hal has to use all of his willpower to stay brave and not let fear take over. He knows that as long as he remains courageous, his green ring will always be stronger than Sinestro's yellow one.

Despite their rivalry, Hal and Sinestro have a complicated history. They both want to protect the universe, but they have very different ideas on how to do it. Sinestro is a master strategist who is always looking for new ways to challenge the Green Lanterns. Hal's battles with Sinestro teach him that true strength comes from overcoming fear and standing up for what is right, no matter how scary the situation might seem.
