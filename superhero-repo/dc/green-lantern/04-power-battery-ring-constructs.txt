Every Green Lantern carries a very important tool called a Power Battery, which looks like a beautiful green lantern. This battery is the source of energy for their Power Ring. Once a day, a Green Lantern must place their ring against the battery and recite the Green Lantern Oath to recharge it. This process fills the ring with the green energy of willpower, allowing the hero to continue their mission of protecting the universe.

The most amazing thing a Green Lantern can do with their ring is create "constructs." These are solid objects made entirely of green energy that are as strong as steel. A Green Lantern can create anything they can imagine! Hal Jordan, being a pilot, often creates green airplanes, parachutes, or giant boxing gloves to help him in a fight. Other Green Lanterns might create giant tools, shields, or even complex machines to solve problems.

Creating a construct requires a lot of focus and a very strong imagination. The more a Green Lantern believes in what they are making, the stronger the construct will be. If they get distracted or lose their willpower, the construct might disappear. This is why Green Lanterns spend a lot of time training their minds to stay calm and focused, even when they are in the middle of a big adventure or a rescue mission.

The Power Battery and the ring constructs are a perfect team. The battery provides the energy, and the ring turns that energy into whatever the hero needs. Hal Jordan loves finding creative new ways to use his ring to help people. Whether it's building a green energy dam to stop a flood or creating a giant hand to catch a falling object, the possibilities are endless. It's a job that requires a big heart and an even bigger imagination.
